====== 29. Hadithi  ya Mtumishi Unmerciful ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-01.jpg}}

Siku moja, Petero alimuuliza Yesu, “Bwana, ni mara ngapi nitasamehe ndugu yangu anaponitendea dhambi? Mpaka mara saba? Yesu akasema, “Sio mara saba, lakini saba mara saba!” Kwa hii Yesu alimanisha  kuwa inatupasa kusamehe kila wakati. Kisha Yesu akapeana hadithi inayofuata. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-02.jpg}}

Yesu akasema, “Ufalme wa Mungu ni kama mfalme ambaye alitaka kukagua hesabu vya watumishi wake. Mojawapo wa watumishi wake alikuwa na deni kubwa inayoweza kugharimu mshahara wa miaka 200,000.” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-03.jpg}}

"Kwa sababu yule mtumishi hangeweza kulipa ile deni, mfalme akasema, 'Muuze huyu mtu na jamii yake wawe watumwa ili ukalipe hii deni.'" 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-04.jpg}}

“Yule mtumishi akaanguka kwa mabega yake mbele ya mfalme na kusema, 'Tafadhali kuwa mvumilivu nami, na nitakulipa pesa yote unayo nidai.' Mfalme alimuonea huruma yule mtumishi, kwa hivyo alifutilia mbali ile deni yake yote na kumuachilia.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-05.jpg}}

“Lakini wakati yule mtumishi alitoka kwa mfalme, alienda kwa mtumishi mwenzake aliyekuwa na deni yake iliyotoshana na mshahara wa miezi minne. Yule mtumishi alimkamata mtumishi mwenzake na kusema, 'Lipa deni ambayo ninakudai!'” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-06.jpg}}

“Yule mtumishi mwenzake alianguka kwa magoti na kusema, 'Tafadhali nivumilie, na nitakulipa kile unacho nidai.' Lakini badala ya hiyo, yule mtumishi alimuweka mtumishi mwenzake gerezani  mpaka wakati atalipa ile deni.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-07.jpg}}

“Wengine wa watumishi waliona kile kilifanyika na wakasumbuliwa sana. Walienda kwa mfalme na kumuambia kila kitu.”  

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-08.jpg}}

“Mfalme alimuita yule mtumishi  na kusema, 'Wewe mtumishi mbaya! Nilikusamehe deni yako kwa sababu uliniomba. Ulipaswa kufanya hivyo hivyo.'  Mfalme akawa na hasira kiasi kwamba alimtupa yule mtumishi mbaya gerezani mpaka wakati atamlipa deni yake yote.” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-09.jpg}}


Kisha Yesu akasema, “Hivi divyo Baba yangu aliye binguni atafanyia kila mmoja wenu kama utakosa kumsamehe ndugu yako kutoka kwa moyo wako.” 

//Hadithi Kutoka kwa Biblia Takatifu: Mathayo 18:21-35//
