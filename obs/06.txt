====== 6. Mungu ampatia Isaka ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-01.jpg?direct&}}

Wakati Ibrahimu alikuwa mzee sana, mwanawe Isaka akawa amefika umri wa kuoa. Basi Ibrahimu akamtuma mmoja wa watumishi wake arudi kwenye nchi ambayo jamii yake walipoishi ili amletee mke mwanawe Isaka.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-02.jpg?direct&}}

Baada ya safari ndefu sana kuelekea nchi ambayo **jamaa wa Ibrahimu** walikuwa wanaishi, **Mungu** akamuelekeza yule **mtumishi kwa Rebeka**. Alikuwa **mjukuu wa ndugu wa Ibrahimu**.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-03.jpg?direct&}}

**Rebeka akakumbali** kuacha jamii yake na kurudi na yule **mtumishi** **nyumbani kwa Isaka**. Isaka alimuoa muda tu alipofika.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-04.jpg?direct&}}

Baada ya muda mrefu, Ibrahimu akafa na ahadi zote zile Mungu alikuwa amefanya naye katika agano zikapitishwa kwa Isaka. Mungu alikuwa ameahidi kwamba Ibrahimu atakuwa na uzao usiohesabika, lakini mke wa Isaka, Rebeka, hangepata watoto.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-05.jpg?direct&}}

Isaac prayed for Rebekah, and God allowed her to get pregnant with twins. The two babies struggled with each other while they were still in Rebekah's womb, so Rebekah asked God what was happening. Isaka alimuombea Rebeka,naye Mungu akamuruhusu ashike mimba ya mapacha. Hao watoto wawili walimenyana mmoja na mwingine wakati wangali tumboni mwa Rebeka, naye Rebeka akamuuliza Mungu kile kilikuwa kinaendelea.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-06.jpg?direct&}}

God told Rebekah, "Two nations will come from the two sons inside of you. They will struggle with each other and the older son will serve the younger one." Mungu alimwambia Rebeka, "Mataifa mawili itatoka kutoka kwa wana hao wawili waliomo ndani yako. Watamenyana mmoja kwa mwingine na yule mkubwa atamtumikia yule mdogo."

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-07.jpg?direct&}}

When Rebekah's babies were born, the older son came out red and hairy, and they named him Esau. Then the younger son came out holding on to Esau's heel, and they named him Jacob. Wakati watoto wa Rebeka walipozaliwa, yule mwana mkubwa alitoka akiwa mwekudu na mwenye nywele kwa mwili, na wakamuita jina lake Esau. Halafu yule mwana mdogo alitoka akiwa ameshikilia kisigino cha Esau, naye wakaita jina lake Yakobo.

//Hadithi ya Bibilia Takatifu kutoka Mwanzo 24:1-25:26//
