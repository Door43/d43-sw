====== 18. The Divided Kingdom ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-01.jpg}}

Baada ya miaka mingi, Daudi alifariki na mwanawe Suleimani akaanza kutawala Israeli. Mungu alisema na Suleimani na akamuuliza kile angetaka zaidi ya yote. Wakati Suleimani aliuliza hekima, Mungu alipendezwa sana na akamfanya kuwa mwenye hekima zaidi ya wote ulimwenguni. Suleimani alijifunza mambo mengi na akawa hakimu mwenye hekima sana. Mungu pia alimfanya tajiri sana.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-02.jpg}}

Katika Yerusalemu, Suleimani alijenga Hekalu ambayo baba yake Daudi alikuwa amepanga na kukusanya vifaa. Watu sasa waliabudu Mungu  na kutoa dhabihu kwake katika hekalu badala ya Hema ya Mkutano. Mungu alikuja na alikuwepo hekaluni, na aliishi hapo pamoja na watu wake. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-03.jpg}}

Lakini Suleimani alipenda wanawake wa mataifa mengine. Alikosa kutii Mungu kwa kuoa wanawake wengi, karibu 1,000! Wengi wa hawa wanawake walitoka kwa mataifa ya kigeni na wakaleta miungu yao pamoja na wao na wakaendelea kuiabudu hiyo. Wakati Suleimani alikuwa mwenye umri, yeye pia aliabudu miungu yao. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-04.jpg}}

Mungu alikuwa amekasirika na Suleimani na, kama adhabu kwa kutoaminika kwa Suleimani, aliahidi kugawanya taifa la Israeli kuwa falme mbili baada ya kifo cha Suleimani. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-05.jpg}}

Baada ya Suleimani kufa, mwanawe, Rehoboamu, akawa mfalme. Rehoboamu alikuwa mtu mpumbavu. Watu wote wa taifa la Israeli  walikuja pamoja kumdhibitisha kama mfalme. Walilalamika kwa Rehoboamu ya kuwa Suleimani alikuwa akiwafanyisha kazi nzito na kawafanya walipe kodi nyingi sana. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-06.jpg}}

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-07.jpg}}

Kumi ya zile makabila za taifa la Israeli waliasi kinyume na Rehoboamu. Ni makabila mbili peke yake walikaa wakiwa waaminifu kwake. Haya makabila mawili wakafanyika ufalme wa Yuda. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-08.jpg}}

Zile makabila zingine kumi za taifa la Israeli  ambazo zilimuasi Rehoboamu zikachagua mtu anayeitwa Yeroboamu awe mfalme wao. Wakaanza ufalme wao katika sehemu ya kaskazini ya nchi na wakaitwa ufalme wa Israeli. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-09.jpg}}

Yeroboamu aliasi kinyume na Mungu na akafanya watu watende dhabi. Alitengenezea watu wake sanamu mbili waziabudu badala wa kuabudu Mungu kwa hekalu katika ufalme wa Yuda. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-10.jpg}}

Falme za Yuda na Israeli wakafanyika maadui na mara nyingi wakapigana. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-11.jpg}}

Katika ufalme mpya wa Israeli, wafalme wote walikuwa wabaya. Wengi wa wafalme hawa waliuawa na Waisraeli wengine waliotaka kufanyika wafalme mahali pao. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-12.jpg}}

Wote wa wafalme na wengi wa watu wa ufalme wa Israeli waliabudu sanamu. Ibada yao ya sanamu ilikuwa pamoja na uasherati na wakati mwingine hata kutoa watoto kama dhabihu. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-13.jpg}}

The kings of Judah were descendants of David. Some of these kings were good men who ruled justly and worshiped God. But most of Judah's kings were evil, corrupt, and they worshiped idols. Some of the kings even sacrificed their children to false gods. Most of the people of Judah also rebelled against God and worshiped other gods.

Wafalme wa Yuda walikuwa uzao wa Daudi. Wengine wa hawa wafalme walikuwa watu wazuri waliotawala kwa haki na kuabudu Mungu. Lakini wengi wa wafalme wa Yuda walikuwa wabaya, wafisadi, na waliabudu sanamu. Wengine wa hawa wafalme hata walitoa dhabihu watoto wao kwa miungu ya uongo. Watu wengi wa Yuda pia walimuasi Mungu na kuabudu miungu mingine.
