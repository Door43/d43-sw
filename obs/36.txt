====== 36. The Transfiguration ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-01.jpg}}

Siku moja, Yesu alichukua watatu kati ya wanafunzi  wake, Petero, Yakobo na Yohana, pamoja naye. (Yule mwanafunzi aitwaye Yohana hakuwa yule yule mtu ambaye alimbatiza Yesu). Wakapanda juu ya mlima mrefu peke yao kuomba. 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-02.jpg}}

Yesu alipokuwa akiomba, sura yake ikawa inang'aa kama jua na nguo zake zikawa nyeupe kama mwanga, nyeupe kuliko vile mtu yeyote duniani anaweza kuzifanya. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-03.jpg}}


Kisha Musa na Nabii Elia walijitokeza. Watu hawa waliishi duniani mamia ya miaka kabla ya jambo hili. Walizungumza na Yesu kuhusu kifo chake, ambacho hivi karibuni itafanyika kule Yerusalemu. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-04.jpg}}

Musa na Elia walipokuwa wakizungumza na Yesu , Petro alimwambia Yesu, “Ni vizuri kuwa tuko hapa. Na tutengeneze vibanda vitatu, kimoja chako, kimoja cha Musa, na kimoja cha Elia.” Petro hakujua anasema nini. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-05.jpg}}

Petero alipokuwa akizungumza, wingu nyeupe lilishuka na kuwazunguka, na sauti kutoka kwa wingu ikasema, “Huyu ndiye Mwanangu nimpendaye. Ninafurahishwa naye. Sikilizeni yeye.” Wale wanafunzi watatu wakawa na hofu na wakaanguka chini. 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-06.jpg}}

Wakati huo Yesu akawagusa,  na akasema, “Msiogope. Amkeni.” Baada ya kutazama pande zote, mwenye aliye kuweko ni Yesu pekee. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-07.jpg}}

Yesu na wale wanafunzi watatu walirudi chini ya mlima. Kisha Yesu akawaambia, “Msimwambie mtu bado kuhusu kile kilichotokea hapa. Mimi hivi karibuni nitakufa na kisha kurudi kwa maisha. Baada ya hayo, mnaweza kuwaambia watu.” 

//Hadithi ya Biblia kutoka: Mathayo 17:1-9; Mark 9:2-8; Luke 9:28-36//
