====== 28. Mtawala Kijana Aliyekuwa Tajiri ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-01.jpg}}

Siku moja, kijana mtawala aliyekuwa tajiri akaja kwa Yesu na akamuuliza, “Mwalimu mwema, nifanye nini ili nipate uzima wa milele?” Yesu akamwambia, “Kwa nini unaniita 'mwema'? Kuna mmoja tu aliye mwema, na ni Mungu. Lakini kama unataka kuwa na uzima wa milele, tii sheria za Mungu.” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-02.jpg}}


“Nina haja kutii zipi?” akauliza. Yesu akajibu, “Usiue. Usifanye uasherati. usiimbe. usiongee uongo. Heshimu baba yako na mama yako, na penda jirani yako kama vile unavyojipenda mwenyewe.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-03.jpg}}

Lakini yule kijana akasema, “Nimetii  sheria hizi zote tangu nikiwa kijana mdogo. Je! Bado nina haja ya kufanya nini ili niiishi milele?” Yesu akamtazama na akampenda. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-04.jpg}}

Yesu akajibu, “Kama unataka kuwa mkamilifu, basi enda uuze kila kitu unamiliki na ukatoe pesa hizo kwa maskini, na utakuwa na hazina mbinguni . Kisha njoo unifuate.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-05.jpg}}

Wakati yule kijana alisikia kile Yesu alisema, alikasirika sana, kwa sababu alikuwa tajiri sana, na hakutaka kutoa zile vitu vyote alikuwa anamiliki. Akageuka na akaenda mbali na Yesu. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-06.jpg}}

Kisha Yesu akaambia wanafunzi wake, “Ni ngumu sana kwa watu matajiri kuingia ufalme wa Mungu! Ndio, ni rahisi ngamia kupitia kwa tundu la sindano kuliko tajiri kuingia ufalme wa Mungu.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-07.jpg}}

Wakati wanafunzi walisikia kile Yesu alisema, walishangaa na kusema, “Nani basi anaweza Kuokolewa?” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-08.jpg}}

Yesu aliwatazama wanafunzi na kusema, “Kwa wanadamu hii haiwezekani, lakini kwa Mungu, mambo yote yawezekana.” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-09.jpg}}

Petero akamwambia Yesu, “Tumeacha yote na kukufuata wewe. Malipo yetu yatakuwa nini?” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-10.jpg}}

Yesu akajibu, “Kila mtu ambaye ameacha manyumba, ndugu, dada, mama, watoto, au mali kwa ajili yangu, atapokea mara 100 zaidi na pia atapokea uzima wa milele. Lakini wengi waliyo mbele watukuwa nyuma, na wengi waliyo nyuma watakuwa mbele.” 

//Hadithi Kutoka kwa Biblia Takatifu: Mathayo 19:16-30; Mark 10:17-31; Luke 18:18-30//
