====== 10. Mapigo Kumi ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-01.jpg?direct&}}

Musa na Haruni wakaenda kwa Farao. Wakasema, "Asema hivi Mungu wa Israeli, 'Achilia watu wangu waende?'" Farao hakuwasikiliza. Badala ya kuruhusu Waisraeli wawe huru, aliwalazimisha kufanya kazi ngumu zaidi!

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-02.jpg?direct&}}

Farao aliendelea na kukataa kuwaachilia watu waende, kwa hivyo Mungu akatuma mapigo kumi ya kutisha kule Misri. Kupitia kwa haya mapigo, Mungu alionyesha Farao kuwa yeye ana nguvu kushinda Farao na miungu yote ya Misri.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-03.jpg?direct&}}

Mungu alibadilisha mto wa Nile ukawa damu, lakini Farao bado hangeruhusu Waisraeli waende.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-04.jpg?direct&}}

Mungu alituma vyura kila mahari Misri. Farao akamsihi Musa aondoe zile vyura. Lakini wakati vyura zote zilikufa, Farao akafanya moyo wake mugumu na hangeruhusu Waisraeli kutoka Misri.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-05.jpg?direct&}}

Kwa hivyo Mungu alituma pigo la chawa. Halafu akatuma pigo la nzi. Farao akaita Musa na Haruni na akawaambia kuwa, wakifanya lile pigo lisimame, wana wa Israeli wanaweza toka Misri. Wakati Musa aliomba, Mungu aliondoa zile nzi zote kutoka Misri. Lakini Farao akafanya moyo wake mugumu na hangeruhusu watu kuwa huru.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-06.jpg?direct&}}

Lilofuata, Mungu akafanya mifugo ya Wamisri kuwa na magojwa na ikafa. Lakini moyo wa Farao ukafanyika mugumu, na hangewaruhusu waisraeli kwenda.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-07.jpg?direct&}}

Halafu Mungu akamwambia Musa arushe majivu kwa hewa mbele ya Farao. Alipofanya hivyo, Wamisri wakawa na vidonda vyenye uchungu, lakini Waisraeli hawakuwa nazo. Mungu akafanya moyo wa Farao mugumu, na Farao hangewaruhusu waisraeli kuwa huru.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-08.jpg?direct&}}

**Baadaye**, **Mungu akaleta** **mvua ya mawe** iliyoharibu mimea mingi kule **Misri ** na kuua kila mmoja alieenda nje. Farao akaita **Musa** na **Haruni ** na akawaambia, “**nimetenda dhambi**. **Munaweza enda**.” Kwa hivyo Musa **akaomba** na ile mvua ya mawe ikaacha kunyesha kutoka angani.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-09.jpg?direct&}}

But Pharaoh sinned again and hardened his heart. He would not let the Israelites go free.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-10.jpg?direct&}}

So God caused swarms of locusts to come over Egypt. These locusts ate all the crops that the hail had not destroyed.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-11.jpg?direct&}}

Then God sent darkness that lasted for three days. It was so dark that the Egyptians could not leave their houses. But there was light where the Israelites lived.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-12.jpg?direct&}}

Even after these nine plagues, Pharaoh still refused to let the Israelites go free. Since Pharaoh would not listen, God planned to send one last plague. This would change Pharaoh's mind.

//A Bible story from: Exodus 5-10//
