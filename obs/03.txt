====== 3. Mafuriko ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-01.jpg?direct&}}

Baada ya muda mrefu, watu wengi wakawa wanaishi duniani. Wakawa wamejaa uovu na vurugu. Ikawa mbaya sana mpaka Mungu akaamua kuuharibu ulimwengu wote kupitia kwa mafuriko makubwa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-02.jpg?direct&}}

Lakini Nuhu akapata neema na Mungu. Yeye alikuwa mwenye haki, akiishi kati ya watu walio waovu. Mungu akamwambia Nuhu kuhusu mafuriko aliyokuwa anapanga kuleta. Akamwambia Nuhu alijenge mashua kubwa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-03.jpg?direct&}}

Mungu akamwambia Nuhu aitengeneze mashua kama mita 140 kwa urefu, na mita 23 kwa upana, na mita 13.5 kwenda juu. Nuhu alikuwa aijenge akitumia mbao na atengeneze ghorofa tatu, vyumba nyingi, paa na ndirisha. Ile mashua ingewaweka salama Nuhu, jamii yake, na wanyama wote wa nchi kavu wakati wa mafuriko.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-04.jpg?direct&}}

Nuhu akamtii Mungu. Yeye na wanaye watatu wakajenga mashua kama vile Mungu alivyowaeleza. Ilichukua miaka mingi kujenga mashua, kwa sababu ilikuwa kumbwa mno. Nuhu akawaonya watu kuhusu mafuriko yatakayokuja na akawaambia wamgeukie Mungu, lakini hakumuamini.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-05.jpg?direct&}}

Mungu pia akaamrisha Nuhu na jamii yake wakusanye chakula yao na ya wanyama. Kila kitu kilipokuwa tayari, Mungu akamwambia Nuhu ni wakati wake, mke wake, na wanao watatu, na wake zao kuingia kwa mashua - watu nane kwa jumla.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-06.jpg?direct&}}

Mungu akatuma kutoka kwa kila aina ya mnyama na ndege, mmoja wa kike na mmoja wa kiume kwake Nuhu ili waingie kwa ile mashua na wawekwe salama wakati wa mafuriko. Mungu akatuma kutoka kwa kila aina ya mnyama, saba wa kiume na saba wa kike ndio baadaye zikatumike kumtolea Mungu dhabihu. Walipoingia wote kwa ile mashua, Mungu mwenyewe akafunga mlango.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-07.jpg?direct&}}

Halafu ikaanza kunyesha, na ikandelea, na ikaendelea. Ikanyesha kwa muda wa siku arobanne usiku na mchana bila kukoma! Maji pia ilikuwa inatiririka kutoka kwa ardhi. Kila kitu katika duniani yote ikawa imefunikwa na maji, hata milima iliyo mirefu zaidi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-08.jpg?direct&}}

Kila kitu kilichokuwa kinaishi katika nchi kavu kilikufa, isipokuwa watu na wanyama waliokuwa ndani ya ile mashua. Ile mashua ikaelea juu ya maji na ikalinda wote na kila kitu kilichokua ndani yake isizame.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-09.jpg?direct&}}

Baada ya mvua kusimama, ile mashua ikaelea juu ya maji kwa muda wa miezi tano, na maji yakaanza kudidimia. Halafu siku moja ile mashua ikatua juu ya mlima, lakini dunia ilikuwa bado imefunikwa na maji. Baada ya miezi mitatu zaidi, vilele vya milima vikaonekana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-10.jpg?direct&}}

Baada ya siku arobanne zaidi, Nuhu akatuma ndege anayeitwa kunguru ndio ajue kama maji imekauka. Yule kunguru akaenda huku na huko akitafuta ardhi imekauka, lakini hakupata.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-11.jpg?direct&}}

Baadaye, Nuhu akamtuma nje ndege anayeitwa njiwa. Naye pia hangepata ardhi iliyokauka, na kwa hivyo ikamrudia Nuhu. Wiki moja baadaye akamtuma njiwa tena na ikarudi na tawi la mzeituni katika kinywa chake! Maji ikaendele kupungua na miti ikaanza kumea tena!

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg?direct&}}

Nuhu akangojea wiki ingine na akatuma njiwa mara ya tatu. Wakati huu, njiwa akapata mahali pa kutua na hakurudi. Maji ilikuwa inakauka!

Two months later God said to Noah, "You and your family and all the animals may leave the boat now. Have many children and grandchildren and fill the earth." So Noah and his family came out of the boat.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-14.jpg?direct&}}

After Noah got off the boat, he built an altar and sacrificed some of each kind of animal that could be used for a sacrifice. God was happy with the sacrifice and blessed Noah and his family.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-15.jpg?direct&}}

God said, "I promise I will never again curse the ground because of the evil things that people do, or destroy the world by causing a flood, even though people are sinful from the time they are children."

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-16.jpg?direct&}}

God then made the first rainbow as a sign of his promise. Every time the rainbow appeared in the sky, God would remember what he promised and so would his people.
