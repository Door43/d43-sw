====== 40. Yesu Je Aliyesulubiwa ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-01.jpg}}

Baada ya askari kumdhihaki Yesu, wakampeleka kumsulubisha. Walimfanya abebe msalaba  ambayo atakufa juu yake. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-02.jpg}}

Askari wakaleta Yesu mpaka pahali palipoitwa “fuvu” na wakapigilia misumari kwa mikono na miguu yake kwa msalaba. Lakini Yesu akasema, “Baba, wasamehe, kwa sababu hawajui kile wanachokifanya.” Pilato akaamuru waandike, “Mfalme wa Wayahudi” kwa kibao na akaiweka kwa msalaba juu ya kichwa cha Yesu. 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-03.jpg}}

Askari walicheza kamari kwa ajili ya mavazi ya Yesu. Walipofanya hivyo, walitimiza unabii uliyosema, “Waligawana nguo zangu kati yao, na kuchezea kamali nguo zangu.” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-04.jpg}}


Yesu alisulubiwa kati ya wezi wawili. Mmoja wao alimdhihaki Yesu, lakini mwingine alisema, “Je, wewe hauogopi Mungu? Sisi tuna hatia, lakini huyu mtu hana hatia.” Kisha akamwambia Yesu, “Nikumbuke katika ufalme wako.” Yesu akamjibu, “Leo hii, utakuwa pamoja nami paradiso.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-05.jpg}}

Viongozi wa Wayahudi  na watu wengine katika umati walimdhihaki Yesu. Wakamwambia, “Kama wewe ni Mwana wa Mungu, basi shuka msalabani  na kujiokoa! Kisha tutakuamini.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-06.jpg}}

Kisha anga katika eneo hilo lote ikawa kabisa na giza, hata ingawa ilikuwa katikati ya mchana. Kulikuwa na giza saa sita mchana na giza ikakaa kwa masaa matatu. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-07.jpg}}

Kisha Yesu akasema kwa sauti, “Imekwisha! Baba, naweka roho yangu mikononi mwako.” Kisha akainamisha kichwa, na akatoa roho yake. Alipokufa kukawa na tetemeko la ardhi na ile pazia kubwa iliyo tenga watu kutoka kwa uwepo wa Mungu Hekaluni ikapasuka vipande viwili, toka juu mpaka chini. 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-08.jpg}}

Through his death, Jesus opened a way for people to come to God. When the soldier guarding Jesus saw everything that had happened, he said, "Certainly, this man was innocent. He was the Son of God."


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-09.jpg}}

Kupitia kwa kifo chake, Yesu ilifungua njia wa watu kufika kwa Mungu. Wakati askari aliyekuwa anamlinda Yesu aliona yote yaliyotokea, akasema, “Hakika, huyu alikuwa mtu ambaye hana hatia. Alikuwa Mwana wa Mungu.” 


//Hadithi ya Biblia kutoka: Mathayo 27:27-61; Mark 15:16-47; Luke 23:26-56; John 19:17-42//
