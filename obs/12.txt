====== 12. The Exodus ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-01.jpg?direct&}}

Waisraeli walifurahia sana kutoka Misri. Hawakua watumwa tena, na walikuwa wakienda kwenye nchi ya ahadi. Wamisri waliwapatia Waisraeli chochote walichouliza, hata dhahabu na fedha na vitu vingine vya dhamana. Watu wengine kutoka mataifa mengine walimuamini Mungu na walienda pamoja na Waisraeli walipotoka Misri.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-02.jpg?direct&}}

Mungu aliwaongoza na nguzo refu la wingu ambalo lilienda mbele yao mchana, na ambalo usiku lilifanyika nguzo refu la moto. Mungu alikuwa pamoja nao kila wakati na aliwaongoza waliposafiri. Kile tu walihitajika kufanya ni kufuata.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-03.jpg?direct&}}

Baada ya muda mfupi, Farao na watu wake wakabadilisha mawazo yao na wakataka Waisraeli wawe watumwa wao tena. Mungu akafanya Farao kuwa mkatili ili watu waone kuwa yeye ndiye Mungu mmoja wa kweli, na waelewe kuwa, Yehova, yuko na nguvu kuliko Farao na miungu yake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-04.jpg?direct&}}

Kwa hivyo Farao na jeshi lake wakafuata Waisraeli ili wawafanye watumwa wao tena. Wakati Waisraeli waliona jeshi la Wamisri linakuja, waligudua kuwa wametegwa kati ya jeshi la Farao na bahari ya shamu. Wakaogopa na kulia kwa sauti, “Kwa nini tulitoka Misri? Tunaenda kukufa!”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-05.jpg?direct&}}

Musa akawaambia Waisraeli, “Acheni kuogopa! Mungu atawapigania leo na kuwaokoa.” Halafu Mungu akamwambia Musa, “Waambie watu wasonge kuelekea bahari ya shamu.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-06.jpg?direct&}}

Kisha Mungu akalisongeza lile nguzo la wingu na akaiweka katikati ya Waisraeli na Wamisri kwa hivyo Wamisri hawangeweza kuona Waisraeli.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-07.jpg?direct&}}

Mungu akamwambia Musa ainue mkono wake juu ya bahari na kugawanya maji. Halafu Mungu akafanya upepo usukume maji baharini upande wa kushoto na wa kulia, kwa hivyo njia ikawa katikati ya bahari.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-08.jpg?direct&}}

Waisraeli waliandamana kupitia katikati ya bahari ikiwa nchi kavu na kukiwa na ukuta wa maji katika pande zao zote mbili.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-09.jpg?direct&}}

Na Mungu akainua lile wingu juu na akaliondoa kwa njia ili Wamisri waweze kuona Waisraeli wakitoroka. Wamisri wakaamua kuwafukuza.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-10.jpg?direct&}}

Kwa hivyo wakawafuata Waisraeli katika njia ya katikati ya bahari, lakini Mungu akawafanya Wamisri kuwa na hofu na magari yao kukwama. Wakapasa sauti, “Kimbieni! Mungu anawapigania Waisraeli!”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-11.jpg?direct&}}

After the Israelites all made it safely to the other side of the sea, God told Moses to stretch out his arm again. When he obeyed, the water fell on the Egyptian army and returned to its normal place. The whole Egyptian army drowned.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-12.jpg?direct&}}

When the Israelites saw that the Egyptians were dead, they trusted in God and believed that Moses was a prophet of God.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-13.jpg?direct&}}

The Israelites also rejoiced with much excitement because God had saved them from death and slavery! Now they were free to serve God. The Israelites sang many songs to celebrate their new freedom and to praise God because he saved them from the Egyptian army.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-14.jpg?direct&}}

God commanded the Israelites to celebrate the Passover every year in order to remember how God gave them victory over the Egyptians and rescued them from being slaves. They celebrated by killing a perfect lamb, eating it with unleavened bread.

//A Bible story from: Exodus 12:33-15:21//
