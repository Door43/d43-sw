====== 25. Shetani ana jaribu kumshawishi Yesu ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-01.jpg}}

Mara tu baada ya Yesu kubatizwa, Roho Mtakatifu alimuongoza na kumpeleka nyikani, mahali ambapo alifunga kwa siku arobanne, mchana na usiku. Kisha Shetani akaja kwa Yesu na kumjaribu ili atende dhambi. 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-02.jpg}}

Shetani alimjaribu Yesu kwa kusema, “Kama wewe ni Mwana wa Mungu, badilisha mawe haya yawe mkate ili ukaweze kula!” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-03.jpg}}

Yesu akajibu, “imeandikwa kwa neno la Mungu, 'Watu hawahitaji tu mkate  peke yake ili kuishi, lakini wanahitaji kila neno ambalo Mungu ananena!'“ 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-04.jpg}}

Kisha Shetani akamchukua Yesu kwa kilele cha Hekalu na kusema, “Kama wewe ni Mwana wa Mungu, jitupe chini, kwa sababu imeandikwa, 'Mungu ataamrisha malaika wake kukumbemba ili mguu wako isingonge jiwe.'” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-05.jpg}}

Lakini Yesu  akamjibu Shetani  kwa kunukuu kutoka kwa maandiko. Akasema, “Katika neno la Mungu, ameamuru watu wake, “Msimjaribu Bwana Mungu wenu.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-06.jpg}}

Basi Shetani  akamuonyesha Yesu falme zote za ulimwengu na utukufu wao na kusema, “Mimi nitakupatia hii yote kama utainama na kuabudu  mimi.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-07.jpg}}

Yesu  alijibu ”Enda mbali na mimi, Shetani! Katika neno la Mungu yeye huamuru watu wake, “Abuduni Bwana Mungu wenu peke yake na kumtumikia yeye pekee.” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-08.jpg}}

Yesu hakukubaliana na Majaribu ya Shetani, kwa hivyo Shetani akamuacha. Halafu Malaika wakaja na wakamtunza Yesu. 

//Hadithi Kutoka kwa Biblia Takatifu: Mathayo 4:1-11; Mark 1:12-13; Luke 4:1-13//
