====== Ahab   Ahabu ======

===== Facts: Maarifa =====

Ahab was a very evil king who ruled over the northern kingdom of Israel.

Ahabu alikuwa mfalme muovu sana aliyetawala kaskazini mwa Israeli

  * King Ahab was wicked and encouraged the people of the kingdom of Israel to worship false gods.
  * Mfalme Ahabu aliwashurutisha watu wa ufalme wa Israeli kuiabudu miungu mingine
  * The prophet Elijah confronted Ahab and told him that there would be a severe drought for three and a half years as a punishment for the sins that Ahab caused Israel to commit.
  * Nabii Elia alimuendea mfalme Ahabu na akamuambua kutakuwa na kiangazi kwa muda wa miaka mitatu na nusu kama adhabu ya uovu aliowasababisha wana wa Israeli kutenda
  * Ahab had 450 prophets to the false god named Baal. Elijah challenged those prophets and proved that Yahweh is the only true God.
  * Ahabu alikuwa na manabii 450 wa uongo wa Baali. Nabii Elia aliwatia changamoto hao manabii wa uongo kuwa Yahweh pekee ndiye Mungu wa kweli
