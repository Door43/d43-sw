====== Babylon Babiloni ======

==== (Babylon, Babylonian) (Babiloni, Mbabiloni) ====

===== Facts: Maarifa =====

The ancient city of Babylon was located along the Euphrates River. Babylon was the capital of the Babylonian Empire which was located in the region of what is now the country of Iraq.

Mji wa kale wa Babiloni ulikuwa ukingoni mwa Mto Ufratia. Mji wa Babiloni ndio ulikuwa mji mkuu wa Ufalme wa Kibabilonia ambao uliokuweko sehemu ambazo kwa sasa ni nchi ya Iraqi.

  * Babylon was located in the same region where the Tower of Babel had been built hundreds of years before.
  * Mji wa Babiloni ulikuwa sehemu za nchi sawa na mnara wa Babeli ambao uliojengwa miaka mingi kabla.
  * The Babylonians were a powerful people group that attacked the kingdom of Judah and held some of them captive for 70 years.
  * WaBabiloni walikuwa kundi la watu wenye nguvu walioushambulia ufalme wa Judah kuwafanya baadhi yao mateka kwa miaka 70.
  * Sometimes the word "Babylon" is used to refer to the entire Babylonian Empire. The "king of Babylon" was king not only of that city, but of the whole empire.
  * Wakati mwingine neno "Babiloni" hutumika kwa ujumla kutaja ufalme wa Babilonia. "Mfalme wa Babiloni" alikuwa ni mfalme sio tu wa mji huo, bali alikuwa ni mfalme wa himaya nzima.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:20-06|[20-06]]]** About 100 years after the Assyrians destroyed the kingdom of Israel, God sent Nebuchadnezzar, king of the **Babylonians**, to attack the kingdom of Judah. **Babylon**  was a powerful empire.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:20-07|[20-07]]]** But after a few years, the king of Judah rebelled against **Babylon**. So, the **Babylonians**  came back and attacked the kingdom of Judah. They captured the city of Jerusalem, destroyed the Temple, and took away all the treasures of the city and the Temple.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]** Nebuchadnezzar and his army took almost all of the people of the kingdom of Judah to **Babylon**, leaving only the poorest people behind to plant the fields.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:20-11|[20-11]]]** About seventy years later, Cyrus, the king of the Persians, defeated **Babylon**.
