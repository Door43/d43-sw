====== Bethlehem Bethlehemu ======

===== Facts: Maarifa =====

Bethlehem was a small city in the land of Israel, near the city of Jerusalem. It still exists to the present day.

**Bethlehemu ulikuwa mji mdogo nchini israeli, karibu na mji wa Yerusalemu. Mji wa Bethlehemu uko hadi siku leo.**

   * King David was born in Bethlehem; for this reason it has been called the "city of David."
  * **Mfalme Daudi aizaliwa mji wa Bethlehemu; ni kwa sababu hii mji unaitwa "mji wa Daudi."**
  * The prophet Micah said that the Messiah would come from Bethlehem.
  * **Nabii Mika alinena kwamba mesiah atatoka Bethlehemu.**
  * Bethlehem is the city where Jesus was born.
  * **Bethlehemu ndio mji ule Yesu alikozaliwa**

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** David was a shepherd from the town of **Bethlehem**.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:21-09|[21-09]]]** The prophet Isaiah prophesied that the Messiah would be born from a virgin. The prophet Micah said that he would be born in the town of **Bethlehem**.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:23-04|[23-04]]]** Joseph and Mary had to make a long journey from where they lived in Nazareth to **Bethlehem**  because their ancestor was David whose hometown was **Bethlehem**.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:23-06|[23-06]]]**"The Messiah, the Master, has been born in **Bethlehem**!"
