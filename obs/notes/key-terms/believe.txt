====== believe amini ======

=== (believe, believe in) (amini, amini katika) ===

===== Definition: Ufafanuzi =====

"Believe" can have two related but different senses:

"Amini"  inaweza ikawa na fikira mbili zinazohusiana lakini tofauti:

=== 1. Believe Amini ===

  * To believe something is to accept or trust that it is true.
  * **Kuamini kitu ni kukikubali kwamba ni cha ukweli**
  * To believe someone is to acknowledge that what that person has said is true.
  * **Kumuamini mtu ni kukiri ya kwamba alionena ni ukweli.**
=== 2. Believe in  Amini ndani ya- ===

  * To believe in someone is to trust that that person is reliable. In other words, it is to trust that the person is who he says he is, that he always speaks the truth, and that he will do what he has promised to do.
  * **Kumuamini mtu ni ni kuwa na imani kwamba huyu mtu anaweza kutegemewa. Kwa njia nyingine, ni kuwa na imani kwamba huyu mtu ndiye anayejisema ndiye, kwamba yeye husema ukweli wakati wote, na kwamba anatenda chochote alichoahidi kutenda.**
  * To believe in an idea or principle is to be sure that that idea or principle is the right way to act or live. For example, a person might believe in honesty, and therefore always tell the truth.
  * **Kuamini wazo au kanuni ni kuwa na uhakika wazo hilo au kanuni hiyo ndio njia asili ya kuishi. Kwa mfano, mtu anaweza kuamini uaminifu, na kwa hivyo husema ukweli wakati wote**
  * When a person truly believes in something, the person will act in such a way that shows that belief.
  *

**Wakati mtu anaamini jambo, mtu huyo atakuwa na tabia zinazo onyesha hiyo imani. **

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:03-04|[03-04]]]** Noah warned the people about the coming flood and told them to turn to God, but they did not **believe**  him.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:04-08|[04-08]]]** Abram **believed**  God's promise. God declared that Abram was righteous because he **believed**  God's promise.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:11-02|[11-02]]]** God provided a way to save the firstborn of anyone who **believed in**  him.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:11-06|[11-06]]]** But the Egyptians did not **believe**  God or obey his commands.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:37-05|[37-05]]]** Jesus replied, "I am the Resurrection and the Life. Whoever **believes in**  me will live, even though he dies. Everyone who **believes in**  me will never die. Do you **believe**  this?"
