====== Aaron, Haruni ======

===== Facts: =====

God chose Aaron to be the first high priest for the people of Israel.

**<font inherit/georgia,serif;;inherit;;inherit>// Mungu alimchaguwa Haruni kuwa kuhani mkuu wa wanaisraeli.  //</font>    **
  * Aaron was Moses' older brother.
  * <font inherit/georgia,serif;;inherit;;inherit>** Haruni alikuwa nduguye mkubwa wa Musa  **</font>
  * Aaron helped Moses speak to Pharaoh about letting the Israelites go free.
  * **<font inherit/georgia,serif;;inherit;;inherit>Haruni ndiye aliye msadia Musa kumwomba Farao kuwaachilia huru wana wa Israeli.</font>    **
  * While the Israelites were traveling through the desert, Aaron sinned by making an idol for the people to worship.
  * <font inherit/georgia,serif;;inherit;;inherit>** Haruni alitenda thambi kwa kuwaumbia wana wa israeli sanamu waliyoabudu walipokuwa njagwani.  **</font>
  * God also appointed Aaron's descendants to be the priests for the people of Israel
  * <font inherit/georgia,serif;;inherit;;inherit>** Mungu pia aliwateuwa wazawa wa Aroni kuwa makuhani wa wana wa Israeli.  **</font>

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:09-15|[09-15]]]** God warned Moses and **Aaron**  that Pharaoh would be stubborn.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:10-05|[10-05]]]** Pharaoh called Moses and **Aaron**  and told them that if they stopped the plague, the Israelites could leave Egypt.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:13-09|[13-09]]]** God chose Moses' brother, **Aaron**, and Aaron's descendants to be his priests.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:13-11|[13-11]]]** So they (the Israelites) brought gold to **Aaron**  and asked him to form it into an idol for them!
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:14-07|[14-07]]]** They (the Israelites) became angry with Moses and **Aaron**  and said, "Oh, why did you bring us to this horrible place?"
