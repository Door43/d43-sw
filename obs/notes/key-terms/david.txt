====== David ======

大衛

===== Facts: =====

事蹟

David was the second king of Israel and he served God faithfully.

大衛是以色列的第二個王, 他忠心的事奉神

  * God chose David to become king when he was still a young boy caring for his family's sheep.
  * 當大衛還是年輕的孩子,看管家中的羊群時,神就揀選他作王
  * David became a great fighter and led the Israelite army in battles against their enemies.
  * 大衛是一個偉大的戰士, 他帶領以色列的軍對與敵人爭戰
  * Saul tried to kill David, but God protected him, and made him king after Saul's death.
  * 掃羅想要殺大衛, 但是神保護他, 當掃羅死了之後, 神就立他作王
  * David sinned against God, but he repented and God forgave him.
  * 大衛犯罪得罪神, 但是他悔改, 神就赦免他
  * David's son, King Solomon, built the temple for God that David had planned.
  * 大衛的兒子所羅門王, 按著神指示大衛的計劃, 為神建造聖殿
  * Jesus, the Messiah, is a descendent of David, and is therefore called the "Son of David."
  * 耶穌, 就是彌賽亞, 是大衛的一個後裔, 所以他也被稱為 " 大衛之子"

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:17-02|[17-02]]]** God chose a young Israelite named **David**  to be king after Saul. **David**  was a shepherd from the town of Bethlehem. … **David**  was a humble and righteous man who trusted and obeyed God.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:17-03|[17-03]]]****David**  was also a great soldier and leader. When **David**  was still a young man, he fought against a giant named Goliath.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:17-04|[17-04]]]** Saul became jealous of the people's love for **David**. Saul tried many times to kill him, so **David**  hid from Saul.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:17-05|[17-05]]]** God blessed **David**  and made him successful. **David**  fought many battles and God helped him defeat Israel's enemies.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:17-06|[17-06]]]****David**  wanted to build a temple where all the Israelites could worship God and offer him sacrifices.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:17-09|[17-09]]]****David**  ruled with justice and faithfulness for many years, and God blessed him. However, toward the end of his life he sinned terribly against God.
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** God was very angry about what **David**  had done, so he sent the prophet Nathan to tell **David**  how evil his sin was. **David**  repented of his sin and God forgave him. For the rest of his life, **David**  followed and obeyed God, even in difficult times.
