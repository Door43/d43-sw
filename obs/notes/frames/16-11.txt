===== The Midianite's Dream [16-11] Ndoto ya Wamidiani =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-11.jpg?nolink&}}

That night **God** told **Gideon**, “**Go down** to the **Midianite** camp and when you hear what they say, you will **no longer be afraid**.” So that night, Gideon went down to the camp and heard a Midianite soldier telling his friend about **something he had dreamed**. The man’s friend said, “This dream means that Gideon's army will defeat the Midianite army!” When Gideon heard this, he **worshiped** God.

Usiku huo **Mungu** akamwambia **Gideoni**, "**Enda chini ** kwa kambi ya **Wamidiani ** na ukisikia kile wanasema, **hautaogopa tena**." Kwa hivyo usiku huo, Gideoni akaeda chini kwa kambi na akasikia askari Mumidiani akiambia rafiki yake **kuhusu kitu aliota**. Rafiki ya yule mwanamme akasema, "Hii ndoto inamaanisha kuwa jeshi la Gideoni litashinda jeshi la Midiani!" Wakati Gideoni alisikia haya, **akamuabudu** Mungu.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **Go down**  - The Midianite soldiers were camping out in a valley and were at a lower elevation than the Israelite soldiers.
  * **enda chini**  - Askari Wamidiani walikuwa wameweka Kambi katika bonde iliokuwa mahali chini ya mahala askari Waisraeli walikuwa.
  * **you will no longer be afraid**  – That is, "you will stop being afraid."
  * **hutakuwa na uoga tena**  - Hiyo ni, "utaacha kuogopa."
  * **something he had dreamed**  – That is, "something he had seen in a dream" or, "a dream he had had."
  * **kitu alikuwa ameota**  - Hiyo ni, "kitu alikuwa ameona katika ndoto" ama, "ndoto aliyoiota."
