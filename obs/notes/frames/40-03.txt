===== Gambling for His Clothes [40-03] Kamari kwa ajili ya Nguo Zake =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-03.jpg?nolink&}}

The soldiers **gambled for Jesus' clothing**. When they did this, **they fulfilled a prophecy that said**, “They divided my garments among them, and gambled for my clothing.”

Askari **walicheza kamari kwa ajili ya mavazi ya Yesu**. Walipofanya hivyo, **walitimiza unabii uliyosema**, "Waligawana nguo zangu kati yao, na kuchezea kamali nguo zangu."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **gambled for Jesus' clothing**  - That is, "played a game of chance to win Jesus' clothes." We do not know how they did this, but in some cultures it could be translated as, "tossed stones on the ground to win Jesus' clothing" or, "chose sticks to decide who could take Jesus' clothes."
  * **walicheza kamali kwa ajili ya mavazi ya Yesu**  - Hiyo ni, "walicheza mchezo wa kamali kushinda nguo za Yesu." Hatujui jinsi walivyofanya hili, lakini katika baadhi ya tamaduni inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kurusha mawe juu ya ardhi ili kushinda nguo za Yesu ama, "walichagua vijiti kuamua nani atachukua nguo za Yesu."
  * **they fulfilled a prophecy that said**  - That is, "they did what the scriptures had long ago predicted would happen to the Messiah" or, "they did what a prophet wrote long ago."
  * **wao walitimiza unabii uliosema**  - Hiyo ni, "walivyofanya kile maandiko muda mrefu uliopita ilitabiri kuwa itafanyika kwa Masihi" ama, "walifanya kile nabii aliandika muda mrefu uliopita."
