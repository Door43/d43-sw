===== The Deliverer [16-03] Mkombozi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **God provided a deliverer** who rescued them from their enemies and **brought peace** to **the land**. But then **the people forgot about God** and started **worshiping idols** again. So God allowed the **Midianites**, a nearby enemy people group, to defeat them.

Kisha **Mungu alituma mkombozi** aliyewaokoa kutoka kwa adui zao na **akaleta amani** kwa **nchi**. Lakini **watu wakasahau kuhusu Mungu** na wakaanza **kuabudu sanamu** tena. Kwa hivyo Mungu akaruhusu **Wamidiani**, kikundi cha watu adui waliokua karibu nao, kuwashinda.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **God provided**  - This could be translated as, "God chose" or, "God appointed" or, "God raised up."
  * **Mungu alituma**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mungu alichagua" ama, "Mungu aliteua" ama, "Mungu aliinua."
  * **brought peace**  - This could be translated as, "allowed the people to live without fear" or, "ended the fighting" or, "stopped their enemies from attacking them."
  * **alileta amani**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "akaruhusu watu kuishi bila uoga" ama, "akafikisha vita mwisho" ama, "akazuiliwa maadui wao kuwavamia tena."
  * **the land**  - That refers to Canaan, the Promised Land that God had given to Abraham.
  * **nchi**  - Hii inaelezea kuhusa Kanani, ile Nchi ya Ahadi ambayo Mungu alikuwa amempa Ibrahimu.
  * **the people forgot about God**  - This means, "The people stopped thinking about God and ignored what he had commanded them."
  * **watu walisahau kuhusu Mungu**  - Hii inamaanisha, "Watu waliacha kufikiria kuhusu Mungu na wakapuuza kile Mungu aliwaamrisha kufanya."
