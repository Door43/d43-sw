===== The Treasure in the Field [34-04] Hazina katika Shamba =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-04.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **kingdom of God** is also like **treasure** that someone hid in a field. Another man found the treasure and then **buried it again**. He was so **filled with joy** that he went and sold everything he had and used the money to **buy that field**.”

**"Ufalme wa Mungu** pia ni kama **hazina** ambayo mtu aliificha kwa shamba. Mtu akaipata ile hazina na **akaizika tena**. Akawa **amejawa na furaha kiasi ** ya kuwa alienda na akauza kila kitu alichokuwa nacho na akatumia ile pesa **kununua hiyo shamba."**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus told another story.)
  * (Yesu akapeana hadithi ingine.)
  * **treasure**  - That is, "something that is very valuable."
  * **hazina**  - Hiyo ni, "kitu ambacho ni cha dhamana kubwa."
  * **buried it again**  - It is also possible to add, "so that no one else would find it."
  * **aliizika tena **- Pia unaweza kungezea, "ili mtu mwingine asije akaipata."
  * **filled with joy**  - Another way to say this is, "very happy" or, "excited."
  * **akajawa na furaha**  - Njia ingine ya kusema hii, "akawa na furaha sana" ama, "akawa na musisimuko."
  * **buy that field**  - Some people may want to add, "so that the treasure would be his."
  * **a****kanunua**** ile shamba**  - Inaweza pendeza watu wengine kuongeza, "ili ile hazina iwe yake."
