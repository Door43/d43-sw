===== Leaving the Boat [03-13] Kuacha Mashua =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-13.jpg?nolink&}}

**Two months later** **God** said to **Noah**, “You and your family and all the animals may leave the boat now. **Have many children** and grandchildren and **fill the earth**.” So Noah and his family came out of the boat.

**Miezi miwili baadaye Mungu** akamwambia **Nuhu**, "Wewe na jamii yako na wanyama wote munaweza toka kwa mashua sasa. **Iweni na watoto** wengi na wajukuu wengi na **mjaze dunia.**" Kwa hivyo Nuhu na jamii yake wakatoka kwa mashua.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Two months later**  - This means two months after Noah released the dove from the ark. It may be necessary to say this explicitly if it is not clear.
  * **miezi miwili baadaye**  - hii inamaniisha miezi miwili baada ya Nuhu kuachilia njiwa kutoka kwa mashua. Ni vizuri kueleza hii kinanaga kama haieleweki vizuri.
  * **Have many children**  – To make sure it is clear that this was God's command and desire, you could say, "You must have many children." or, "I want you to have many children."
  * **Iweni na watoto wengi**  - Ndio uhakikishe hii inaeleka vizuri kuwa hii ilikuwa amri na kusudi la Mungu, unaweza sema, "lazima muwe na watoto wengi" ama "ninataka muwe na watoto wengi."
  * **fill the earth**  - If this is not clear, it may be necessary to say, "and fill the earth with people" or, "so that there will be many people living on the earth."
  * **Jazeni dunia**  - kama hii haileweki vizuri, unaweza sema, "na mjaze dunia na watu" ama "ndiyo kuwe na watu wengi wanaishi duniani."
