===== Solomon Built the Temple [18-02] - Suleimani alijenga Hekalu *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-02.jpg?nolink&}}

In **Jerusalem**, **Solomon** built the **Temple** for which his father **David** had planned and gathered materials. People now **worshiped God** and offered **sacrifices** to him at the Temple instead of at the **Tent of Meeting**. God came and **was present in the Temple**, and he lived there **with his people**.

Katika **Yerusalemu**, **Suleimani** alijenga **Hekalu** ambayo baba yake **Daudi** alikuwa amepanga na kukusanya vifaa. Watu sasa **waliabudu Mungu ** na kutoa **dhabihu** kwake katika hekalu badala ya **Hema ya Mkutano**. Mungu alikuja na **alikuwepo hekaluni**, na aliishi hapo **pamoja na watu wake**.

==== Translation Notes: Tafsiri ya Maelezo ====

  * **was present in the Temple**  â€“ That is, "was present in the Temple in a special way." Even though God was also present everywhere else at the same time, he made himself especially available to the people at the Temple.
  * **Alikuwepo hekaluni**  - Hiyo ni, "alikuwepo hekaluni kwa njia ya kipekee." Hata ingawa Mungu alikuwepo kila mahali pengine kwa wakati mmoja, alijidhihirisha hasa kwa watu katika hekalu.
  * **with his people**  - This could be translated as, "in the midst of his people" or, "among his people."
  * **Pamoja na watu wake**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "katikati ya watu wake" ama, "miongoni mwa watu wake."
