===== Paul Healed the Slave Girl [47-05] Paulo Alimponya Yule Msichana Mtumwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-47-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Finally **one day** when the **slave girl** started yelling, **Paul turned to her** and said to the **demon** that was in her, “**In the name of Jesus**, **come out of her**.” Right away the demon left her.

Hatimaye **siku moja** wakati yule **msichana mtumwa ** alianza kutoa sauti kubwa, **Pauo aligeuka kwake ** na kusema na yule **pepo** aliyekuwa ndani yake, "**Katika jina ya Yesu, toka ****ndani yake**." Mara moja pepo likamtoka.

<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri):  **</font>

  * **one day - **  This phrase introduces an event that happened in the past, but does not state the specific time. Many languages have a similar way to begin telling a true story.
  * **Siku Moja - **  Msemo huu unatanguliza tukio ambalo lilifanyika siku zilizopita, lakini haliezei wakati ule haswa. Lugha nyingi ziko na njia sawa ya kuanza kueleza hadithi ya ukweli.
  * **turned to her - **  That is, "turned around and looked at her."
  * **aligeuka kwake- **  Hii ni "aligeuka na kumwangalia yeye."
  * **In the name of Jesus - **  That is, "by the authority of Jesus." Because of Jesus' authority, Paul could command the demon to leave.
  * **Katika jina la Yesu - **  Hiyo ni, "kwa mamlaka ya Yesu." Kwa sababu ya mamlaka ya Yesu, Paulo angeweza kuamuru hiyo pepo itoke.
  * **come out of her -**  That is, "leave her" or, "go away from her."
  * **toka ndani yake- ** Hiyo ni, "mtoke huyu" ama, "enda mbali naye."
