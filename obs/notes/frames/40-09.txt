===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Joseph**  - This was not Mary's husband. It was another man named Joseph.
  * **Yusufu**  - Huyu hakuwa mume wa Mariamu. Alikuwa ni mtu mwingine aitwaye Yusufu.
  * **asked Pilate for Jesus' body**  - That is, "asked Pilate to permit them to take Jesus' body down from the cross."
  * **waliitisha Pilato mwili wa Yesu**  - Hiyo ni, "waliuliza Pilato awaruhusu kutoa mwili wa Yesu juu ya msalaba."
