===== The Earthquake [47-09] Tetemeko la Ardhi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-47-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Suddenly, there was a violent earthquake! All the prison doors **flew open**, and the chains of all the prisoners **fell off**.

Ghafla, kukawa na tetemeko kali la ardhi! Milango yote ya gereza **ikafunguka**, na minyororo ya wafungwa wote **ikaanguka**.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **flew open**  - That is, "became unlocked and suddenly opened wide."
  * **ikafunguka **- Hiyo ni, "ikafunguka na ghafla ikawa wazi."
  * **fell off**  - That is, "suddenly came off" or, "suddenly came apart so that the prisoners were released."
  * **Ikaanguka **- Hiyo ni, "ghafla ikatoka" ama, "ghafla ikavunjika hivyo ya kwamba wafungwa wakaachiliwa."
