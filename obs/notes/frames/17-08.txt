===== David Praised God [17-08] Daudi alisifu Mungu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-08.jpg?nolink&}}

When **David** heard **these words**, he immediately thanked and **praised God** because he had **promised** David t**his great honor** and many **blessings**. David did not know when God would do these things. But as it happened, the **Israelites** would have to wait a long time before the **Messiah** came, almost 1,000 years.

Wakati **Daudi** alisikia **maneno haya**, mara tu alimshukuru **Mungu** na **kumsifu** kwa sababu alikuwa **amemuahidi** Daudi **heshima hii kubwa** na **baraka** nyingi. Daudi hakujua wakati Mungu atafanya mambo haya. Lakini kama ilivyofanyika, ingebidi **Waisraeli** wangoje muda mrefu kabla ya **Masihi** kuja, karibu miaka 1,000.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **these words**  - That is, "the words the prophet had just said." This refers back to the promises in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:17-07|[17-07]]]**.
  * **maneno haya**  - Hiyo ni, "maneno ambayo nabii alikuwa tu ameyasema." Hii inarejelea kwa ahadi zilizoko pale **[17-07]**.
  * **this great honor**  - People who lived later would highly respect David because some of his descendants would be kings over Israel and one of them would be the Messiah.
  * **heshima hii kubwa**  - Watu walioishi baadaye walimheshimu sana Daudi kwa sababu wengine katika uzao wake watakuwa wafalme juu ya Israeli na mmoja wao atakuwa Masihi.
