===== God Created the Land [01-04] Mungu Aliumba Ardhi *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-04.jpg?nolink&}}

On the **third day**, **God spoke** and separated the water from the dry land. He called the dry land “**earth**,” and he called the water “seas.” God saw that what he had **created** was **good**.

**Siku ya tatu**, **Mungu alinena ** na kutenganisha maji na nchi kavu. Akaiita nchi kavu "**ardhi**", na akaita maji "bahari." Mungu akaona ya kwamba **alichokiumba ** ni **kizuri**.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:good|good]]**
==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) ====

  * **third day**  - The next in the series of orderly days in which God prepared the earth for life.
  * **Siku ya tatu **- Iliyofuatilia katika mpangilio wa siku kwamba Mungu aliitayarisha dunia kwa uhai.
  * **God spoke**  - God created the dry land by speaking a command.
  * **Mungu alinena **- Mungu aliumba nchi kavu kwa kutamka amri.
  * **earth**  - This word is used here to refer to dirt or soil, which is what dry land is made of.
  * **ardhi **- Neno hili limetumiwa kumaanisha mchanga, ambao ndio umetengeneza nchi kavu.
  * **created**  - This word is used here in the sense of making something out of nothing.
  * **Aliumba **- Neno hili limetumika kwa mumaanisha kutengeneza kitu kutoka kwa hali ya ukiwa.
