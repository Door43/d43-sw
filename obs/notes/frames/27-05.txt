===== The Robbers [27-05] Majambazi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-05.jpg?nolink&}}

“While the man was traveling, he was attacked by a group of robbers. They took **everything he had** and beat him until he was almost dead. Then they went away.”

"Yule mwanamme alipokuwa anasafiri, alishambuliwa na kikundi cha majambazi. Walichukua kila **kitu alikuwa nacho ** na kumpiga mpaka akawa hali mahututi. Kisha wakatoroka."
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues to tell the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea kuelezea ile mfano).
  * **everything he had**  - That is, "everything he had with him, including the clothes he was wearing."
  * **kila kitu alikuwa nacho**  - Hiyo ni, "kila kichu ambacho alikuwa nacho pale, pamoja na manguo alivyokuwa amezivaa."
