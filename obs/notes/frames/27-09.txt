===== Help for the Victim [27-09] Msaada kwa Muathiriwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-09.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **Samaritan** then lifted the man onto **his own donkey** and took him to a **roadside inn where he took care of him**.”

"Yule **Msamaria** alimuweka yule mwanamme kwa punda yake na akampeleka kwa **nyumba ya wageni iliyo kando ya barabara na akamtuunza pale**."
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:samaria|Samaritan (Msamaria)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues to tell the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea kueleza ile mfano.)
  * **his own donkey**  - Make sure it is clear that "his" refers to the Samaritan.
  * **punda yake mwenyewe**  - Hakikisha ni wazi kuwa, "yake" inaeleza kuhusu yule Msamaria.
  * **roadside inn**  - That is, "a lodging place." This is a place where travelers could get food and stay overnight.
  * **nyumba ya wageni iliyo kando ya barabara**  - Hiyo ni, "makaazi ya wageni." Hapa ni pahali ambapo wasafiri wanaweza pata chakula na kulala.
  * **where he took care of him**  - This could also be translated as, "where he continued to take care of him."
  * **ambapo alimutunza**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "mahali ambapo aliendelea na kumtunza."
