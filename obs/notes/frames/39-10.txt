===== What Is Truth? [39-10] Ukweli ni Nini? =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-10.jpg?nolink&}}

Jesus answered, “**You have said so**, but **my kingdom is not an earthly kingdom**. If it were, **my servants would fight for me**. I have come to earth to tell the **truth** about **God**. Everyone who **loves** the truth **listens to me**.” **Pilate** said, “**What is truth**?”

Yesu akajibu, "**Wewe umesema**, lakini **ufalme wangu si utawala wa kidunia**. Kama ingekuwa, **watumishi wangu wangenipigania**. Nimekuja hapa duniani kusema **ukweli** kuhusu **Mungu**. Kila mtu ambaye **anapenda** kweli **ananisikiliza mimi**. "**Pilato** akasema,**"Kweli ni nini?"**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **You have said so**  - That is, "You have spoken correctly."
  * **Wewe umesema**  - Hiyo ni, "Wewe umesema kwa usahihi."
  * **my kingdom is not an earthly kingdom**  - That is, "my kingdom is not like earthly kingdoms."
  * **ufalme wangu si ufalme wa kidunia**  - Hiyo ni, "Utawala wangu si kama falme za kidunia."
  * **my servants would fight for me**  - That is, "my disciples would fight to protect me" so that I could establish my kingdom.
  * **watumishi wangu wangenipigania mimi - **  Hiyo ni, "wanafunzi wangu wangepigana ili kunilinda" ili nianzishe ufalme wangu.
  * **listens to me**  - This could be translated as, "hears my teaching and obeys me." It includes not just hearing Jesus' words, but also doing what he says.
  * **ananisikiliza mimi**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, “anasikia mafundisho yangu na kunitii mimi.” Hii inajumuisha sio tu kusikia maneno ya Yesu, lakini pia kufanya anavyosema.
  * **What is truth?**  - that is, "Can anyone know what is true?"
  * **Ukweli ni kitu gani?**  - hiyo ni, "Je, mtu yeyote anaweza kujua kweli ni nini?"
