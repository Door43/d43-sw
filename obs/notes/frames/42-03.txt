===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **on the third day**  - Some languages may prefer to say, "on the third day after his death."
  * **siku ya tatu**  - Baadhi ya lugha zinaweza kupendelea kusema, "siku ya tatu baada ya kifo chake."
  * **it was almost evening**  - This can also be translated as, "the day was ending" or, "the sun was going down" or, "it was starting to get dark."
  * **ilikuwa karibu jioni**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "siku ilikuwa inasha" ama, "jua lilikuwa likichwa" ama, "giza ilikuwa inaanza kuingia."
