===== The Betrayer [38-06] Msaliti =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Jesus** said to the **disciples**, “One of you will **betray** me.” The disciples were shocked, and asked who would do such a thing. Jesus said, “The person to whom I give this piece of bread **is the betrayer**.” Then he gave the bread to **Judas**.

Kisha **Yesu** akawaambia **wanafunzi** wake, "Mmoja wenu **atanisaliti**." Wanafunzi walishtuka, na wakauliza ni nani ambaye angefanya jambo kama hilo. Yesu alisema, "mtu ambaye nitampa kipande cha mkate ndiye **msaliti**." Kisha akampa **Yuda** mkate.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **give this piece of bread**  - That is, "hand over this piece of bread."
  * **kumpa kipande cha mkate**  - Hiyo ni, "kupitisha kipande cha mkate."
  * **is the betrayer**  - This could also be translated as, "will betray me" or, "is the one who will betray me."
  * **ni msaliti **- Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "atanisaliti" ama, "ndiye atakayenisaliti."
