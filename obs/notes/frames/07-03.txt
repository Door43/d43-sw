**Isaac** wanted to **give his blessing** to **Esau**. But before he did, **Rebekah** and **Jacob tricked him** by having Jacob **pretend** to be Esau. Isaac was old and could no longer see. So Jacob put on Esauâ€™s clothes and put **goatskins** on his neck and hands.

**Isaka** alipendelea k**umpea**** Esau baraka zake**. Lakini kabla ya kufanya hivyo, **Rebeka** na **Yakobo wakamdanganya** kwa kumfanya Yakobo **ajifanye** kuwa yeye ndiye Esau. ===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **give his blessing**  - It was customary for fathers to formally express their desire for good things to happen to their children. Normally the older son received the best promise. Isaac wanted this extra prosperity to happen to Esau.
  * **Toa baraka zake**  - Ilikuwa desturi kwa baba kueleza rasmi kutamani kwao mambo mazuri yatendeke kwa watoto wao. Kawaida, mzaliwa wa kwanza alipata ahadi iliyo bora zaidi. Isaka angependezwa kufanikiwa kwa ziada ifanyike kwa Esau.
  * **tricked him**  - The word "trick" means to do something to deliberately deceive someone. Rebekah came up with a plan to deceive Isaac into giving Jacob the special blessing instead of Esau.
  * **alimdanganya**  - Neno "ndanganya" linamaanisha kufanya kitu kwa kundanganya mtu kwa kimakusudi. Rebeka alitoka na mpango wa kundanganya Isaka ili ampe Yakobo ile baraka maalum badala ya Esau.
  * **pretend**  - The word "pretend" shows how Jacob would deceive his father (who had very poor eyesight in his old age).
  * **Kujifanya**  - Neno "kujifanya" laonyesha vile Yakobo atadanganya baba yake (ambaye macho yake haikuona vizuri siku za uzee wake).
  * **goatskins**  - The hairiness of the goatskins would make Jacob feel like Esau.
  * **ngozi za mbuzi **- hali ya kuwa na nywele ya ngozi za mbuzi ingemfanya Yakobo ahisi kama Esau.
