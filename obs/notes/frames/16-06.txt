===== Gideon Destroyed the Idol [16-06] Gideoni aliharibu sanamu *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-06.jpg?nolink&}}

**Gideon's** father had an **altar** dedicated to an **idol**. **God** told Gideon **to tear down** that altar. But Gideon was **afraid of the people**, so he **waited until nighttime**. Then he tore down the altar and smashed it to pieces. He built a new altar to God near where the altar to the idol used to be and made a **sacrifice** to God on it.

Babake **Gideoni** alikuwa na madhabahu yaliokuwa yametengwa kwa ajili ya **sanamu**. **Mungu** akamuambia ayabomoe hayo madhabahu. Lakini Gideoni** aliwaogopa watu**, kwa hivyo **akasubiri mpaka wakati wa usiku**. Kisha akayabomoa na kuyaharibu kwa vipande vipande. Akamjengea Mungu madhabahu mapya karibu na pale madhabahu ya sanamu yalipokuwa na akamtolea Mungu dhabihu juu yake.

==== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri ====

  * **to tear down**  - This means, "to violently bring down" or, "to take down and destroy."
  * **kubomoa**  - Hii inamaanisha, " kuangusha chini kwa fujo, ama "kuangusha na kuharibu".
  * **afraid of the people**  - Gideon was afraid that his fellow Israelites who worshiped the same idol would be angry with him.
  * **aliogopa watu**  - Gideoni aliogopa kwamba Waisraeli wenzake ambao waliabudu hiyo sanamu wangemkasirikia.
  * **waited until nighttime**  - Another way to say this would be, "waited until after dark." Gideon smashed the altar at night when everyone was asleep so that no one would see him do it.
  * **Alingojea hadi wakati wa usiku**  - NJia nyingine yakusema ingekuwa,"alingojea mpaka baada ya giza.' Gideoni akaharibu ile madhabahu wakati kila mtu alikuwa amelala wakati ambao hakuna yeyote angemuona akifanya hivyo.
