==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **these nine plagues**  - This means, "these nine disasters that God had caused."
  * **hizi mapigo tisa**  - Hii inamaanisha, "hizi maafa tisa ambazo Mungu alizileta."
  * **Since Pharaoh would not listen**  - This could be translated as, "Since Pharaoh would not do what God was telling him to do" or, "Since Pharaoh refused to obey God."
  * **Maana Farao hangesikiliza**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuona kuwa Farao hangefanya kile Mungu alikuwa anamwambia afanye" ama "kuona Farao alikataa kumtii Mungu."
  * **This would change Pharaoh's mind**  - Another way to say this would be, "This last plague would cause Pharaoh to change how he thought about God and as a result he would let the Israelites go free."
  * **Hii ingebadilisha mawazo ya Farao**- Njia ingine ya kusema hii yaweza kuwa, "Hili pigo la mwisho lingebadilisha vile Farao anafikiria kuhusu Mungu na hii ingemfanya aachilie Waisraeli wawe huru."
