===== The Brothers' Lie [08-03] Uongo wa Ndugu *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Before **Joseph’s** brothers returned home, they tore Joseph’s robe and dipped it in **goat's blood**. Then they showed the robe to their father so he would think that a wild animal had killed Joseph. **Jacob** was very sad.

Kabla ya ndugu za **Yosefu** kurudi nyumbani, walirarua vazi la Yosefu na wakaitumbukiza kwa **ndamu ya mbuzi**. Halafu wakamuonyesha baba yao lile vazi ili baba yao akafikirie kuwa mnyama poli amemuua Yosefu. **Yakobo** akasikitika sana.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **goat's blood**  - The brothers wanted Jacob to think that the blood on the clothes was Joseph's blood.
  * **ndamu ya mbuzi**  - Wale wandugu walitaka Yakobo afikirie kwamba damu iliyokuwa kwa nguo ilikuwa ya Yosefu.
