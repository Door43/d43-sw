===== Israel Defeated the Canaanites [15-11] Israeli walishinda Wakanani *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-11.jpg?nolink&}}

After God defeated those armies, many of the other **Canaanite** people groups gathered together to attack **Israel**. **Joshua** and the Israelites **attacked** and **destroyed them**.

Baada ya Mungu kushinda zile majeshi, wengi wa vikundi vya watu kule **Kanani ** wakashikani pamoja ili washambulie **Israeli**. **Yoshua** na Waisraeli **waliwashambulia** na **kuwaangamiza. **
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:canaan|Canaanite (Kanani)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:israel|Israel (Israeli)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:joshua|Joshua (Yoshua)]]**
==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **attacked and destroyed them**  - This could be translated as, "fought against them and defeated them."
  * **waliwashambulia na kuwaangamiza**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "wakapigana kinyume nao na kuwashinda."
