===== The Two Robbers [40-04] Majambazi Wawili =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-04.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** was **crucified** between **two robbers**. One of them **mocked** Jesus, but the other said, “Do you have no fear of **God**? We are **guilty**, **but this man is innocent**.” Then he said to Jesus, “**Please remember me in your kingdom**.” Jesus answered him, “Today, you will be with me in **Paradise**.”

**Yesu** **alisulubiwa** kati ya **wezi wawili**. Mmoja wao **alimdhihaki** Yesu, lakini mwingine alisema, "Je, wewe hauogopi **Mungu**? Sisi **tuna hatia, lakini huyu mtu hana hatia."** Kisha akamwambia Yesu, **"Nikumbuke katika ufalme wako."** Yesu akamjibu, "Leo hii, utakuwa pamoja nami **paradiso."**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **two robbers**  - This could also be translated as, "two bandits." This refers to criminals who used force or violence to steal things.
  * **wezi wawili**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "majambazi wawili." Hii inaelezea kuhusu wahalifu ambao walitumia nguvu ama vurugu kuiba vitu.
  * **Do you have no fear of God?**  - The robber does not expect an answer to this question; it is a way that some languages use to make a strong statement. If your language does not use questions in this way, then translate this as, "You should fear God!"
  * **Je, huogopi Mungu? **- Yule jambazi hatarajii jibu la swali hili; ni njia ambayo baadhi ya lugha zinatumia ili kutoa tamko kali. Kama lugha yako haitumii maswali kwa njia hii, basi tafsiri hii kama, "Inabidi umuogope Mungu!"
  * **We are guilty but this man is innocent**  - This could be translated as, "You and I have done evil things and deserve to die, but this man, Jesus, has done nothing wrong and does not deserve to die." "We" here includes both robbers, but does not include Jesus.
  * **Sisi tuna hatia lakini mtu huyu hana hatia**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama: "Wewe na mimi tumefanya mambo mabaya na tunastahili kufa, lakini mtu huyu, Yesu, hakufanya chochote kibaya na hastahili kufa." "Sisi" hapa inaongea kuhusu wezi hawa wawili, lakini haiongei kuhusu Yesu.
  * **this man**  - This phrase refers to Jesus.
  * **mtu huyu**  - Msemo huu inanena kuhusu Yesu.
  * **Please remember me**  - That is, "Please accept me" or, "Please welcome me" or, "Please allow me to be with you." "Remember" here does not mean to recall something after forgetting it. Translate this in a way that communicates a humble request.
  * **Tafadhali nikumbuke**  - Yaani, "Tafadhali unikubali" ama, "Tafadhali nikaribishe" ama, "Tafadhali naomba niwe pamoja nawe." "Kumbuka " hapa haina maana kukumbuka kitu baada ya kukisahau. Tafsiri hii kwa njia inayoonyesha ombi la unyenyekevu.
  * **in your kingdom**  - That is, "when you establish your kingdom" or, "when you are ruling as king."
  * **katika ufalme wako**  - Hiyo ni, "wakati wewe utaanzisha ufalme wako" ama, "wakati wewe unatawala kama mfalme."
  * **Paradise**  - This is another name for "heaven."
  * **Paradiso**  - Hili ni jina lingine la "mbinguni."
