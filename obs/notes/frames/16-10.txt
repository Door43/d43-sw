===== Too Many Soldiers [16-10] Askari wengi sana *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-10.jpg?nolink&}}

**32,000 Israelite soldiers came to Gideon**, but **God** told him this was **too many**. So Gideon sent home 22,000 who were afraid to fight. God told Gideon that he still had too many men. So Gideon sent all of them home **except for 300 soldiers**.

**Askari Waisraeli 32,000 walikuja kwa Gideoni**, lakini **Mungu** akamwambia hawa ni **wengi sana**. Kwa hivyo Gideoni akarudisha nyumbani 22,000 waliokuwa na uoga kupigana. Mungu akamwambia Gideoni kuwa bado alikuwa na wanaume wengi sana. Kwa hivyo Gideoni akarudisha wote nyumbani **isipokuwa askari 300**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **32,000 Israelite soldiers came to Gideon**  - Some languages might need to insert the following sentence at the beginning of the story: "Gideon called the Israelites to come fight against the Midianites." See **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:16-08|[16-08]]]**.
  * **Askari 32,000 Waisraeli walikuja kwa Gideoni **- Lugha zingine zaweza hitaji kuongezea sentensi hii katika mwanzo wa hadithi hii: "Gideoni aliwaita Waisraeli waje wapigane kinyume na Wamidiani." Ona **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:16-08|[16-08]]]**.
  * **too many**  - This was more soldiers than God wanted for this fight. If that many solders fought and won, they would think that they won the battle with their own strength, and they would not know that God did it.
  * **wengi sana**  - Hawa walikuwa askari wengi ukilinganisha na wale Mungu alitaka kwa vita hii. Kama askari wengi hivyo wangepigana na washinde, wangefikiria kuwa wameshinda vita kwa nguvu zao, na hawangeweza kujua kuwa Mungu amefanya hivyo.
  * **except for 300 soldiers**  - This sentence could be translated as, "So Gideon allowed only 300 men to stay, and the rest of the men went home."
  * **isipokuwa askari 300 **- Hii sentensi inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Kwa hivyo Gideoni akaruhusu wanaume 300 wakae, na wanaume wengine wote wakaenda nyumbani."
