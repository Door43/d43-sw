===== Joseph Ruled Egypt [08-08] Yosefu alitawala Misri =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-08.jpg?nolink&}}

**Pharaoh** was so **impressed with Joseph** that he appointed him to be the **second most powerful man** in all of **Egypt**!

**Farao** **alivutiwa sana na Yosefu**, mpaka akamteua kuwa mtu **wa pili kwa nguvu na mamlaka** katika **Misri ** yote!

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:pharaoh|Pharaoh (Farao)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:joseph|Joseph (Yosefu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:egypt|Egypt (Misri)]]**
==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **impressed with**  - Pharaoh was amazed by Joseph's wisdom and felt respect for him; he trusted Joseph to make wise decisions that would benefit the people. It may be clearer to say, "impressed with Joseph's wisdom."
  * **Kuvutiwa naye**  - Farao alishangazwa na hekima ya Yosefu na akamuheshimu sana; akamuamini Yosefu kuwa anaweza kufanya uamuzi wa kuwafaidi watu.  Itaeleweka zaidi kusema, "akishangazwa na hekima ya Yosefu."
  * **second most powerful man**  - Pharaoh made Joseph a very powerful and important ruler over all of Egypt. Only Pharaoh was more powerful and important than Joseph.
  * **Mtu wa pili kwa nguvu na mamlaka **- Farao alimfanya Yosefu mtawala mwenye nguvu na wa muhimu katika Misri yote. Ni Farao pekee aliyekuwa na mamlaka na wa umuhimu kuliko Yosefu.
