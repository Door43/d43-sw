===== The Two Spies [15-01] Wapelelezi wawili *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-01.jpg?nolink&}}

**At last it was time for** the **Israelites** to **enter Canaan**, the **Promised Land**. **Joshua** sent **two spies to the Canaanite city of Jericho** that was **protected by strong walls**. In that city there lived a prostitute named **Rahab** who hid the spies and later helped them to **escape**. She did this because she **believed God**. They promised to protect Rahab and **her family** when the Israelites would destroy Jericho.

**Hatimaye ilifika wakati** wa Waisraeli kuingia **Kanani**, ile **Nchi ya Ahadi.** **Yoshua** akatuma **wapelelezi wawili mji wa Yericho kule Kanani** ambao ulikuwa **umelindwa na kuta zenye nguvu**. Katika mji ule aliishi kahaba alieitwa **Rahabu** ambaye aliwaficha wale wapelelezi na baadaye akawasaidia **kutoroka**. Alifanya hivyo kwa sababu **alimwamini Mungu**. Wakaahidi kumlinda Rahabu na **jamii yake** wakati Waisraeli wataharibu Yericho.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezoa ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **At last it was time for**  - "At last" means "finally" or, "after a long wait." To make it clear what "time" refers to, you could say, "after they had wandered in the desert for 40 years, God finally permitted."
  * **hatimaye ikafika wakati wa**  - "Hatimaye" inamanisha "mwisho kabisa" ama, "baada ya kugonja muda mrefu." Diposa iwe wazi "wakati" gani inamaanisha nini, unaweza sema, "baada ya kuzungazunguka katika jangwa miaka arobanne, Mungu hatimaye akaruhusu."
  * **two spies to the Canaanite city of Jericho**  - This could be translated as, "two men to Jericho, a city in Canaan, to find out information about it." Also see the notes for, "spy out the land" in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:14-04|[14-04]]]**.
  * **wapelelezi wawili kwenda Yericho mji ulioko Kanani **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "wanaume wawili kwenda Yericho, mji ulioko Kanani, kutafuta habari kuihusu." Pia angalia maelezo ya, "peleleza nchi" pale** [14-04]. **
  * **protected by strong walls**  - This could be translated as, "completely surrounded by thick, strong walls made of stone to protect it from their enemies."
  * **umelindwa na kuta zenye nguvu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "imezingirwa kabisa na kuta nene za mawe na zenye nguvu ili kuilinda kutoka kwa adui zao."

  * **toroka**  - inawezekana pia kuongezea, "toroka kutoka kwa watu kule Yericho waliotaka kuwadhuru."
  * **her family**  - Rahab asked for protection for her father, mother, brothers, and sisters. Use your word for family that includes these people.
  * **jamii yake**  - Rahabu aliuliza ulinzi kwa baba, mama, ndugu na dada zake. Tumia neno lako la jamii ambalo litajumuisha hawa watu.
