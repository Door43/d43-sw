===== Here Is Water [45-11] Hapa Kuna Maji =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-11.jpg?nolink&}}

As Philip and the Ethiopian traveled, they came to **some water**. The Ethiopian said, “Look! There is some water! **May I be baptized**?” And he told the driver to stop the **chariot**.

Vile Filipo na Muethiopia walikuwa wanasafiri, wakaja pahali **penye maji**. Muethiopia akasema, "Tazama! kunayo maji pale! **Ninaweza kubatizwa**? Na akamwambia dereva asimamishe **gari la farasi**.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:baptize|baptized (kubatizwa)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:chariot|chariot (gari la farasi)]]**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **some water**  - Use a term that means a larger body of water such as a pond, lake, or stream.
  * **penye maji**  - Tumia neno linalomaanisha maji mengi kama ya bwawa, ziwa ama mto.
  * **May I be baptized?**  - This could also be translated as, "Is there any reason that I cannot be baptized?"
  * **Ninaweza kubatizwa**? - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "Kunayo sababu yeyote nisiweze kubatizwa?"
