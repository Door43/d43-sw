===== God Defeated the Midianites [16-14] Mungu aliwashinda Wamidiani *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-14.jpg?nolink&}}

**God confused the Midianites**, so that they started attacking and killing each other. Immediately, **the rest of the Israelites were called** from their homes to come help chase the Midianites. They killed many of them and chased the rest of them out of the Israelites' land. 120,000 Midianites died that day. God had **saved Israel**.

**Mungu aliwafanya Wamidiani kuchanganyikiwa**, kuwa walianza kuvamiana na kuuana wenyewe kwa wenyewe. Mara moja, **Waisraeli wengine waliitwa** kutoka kwa manyumba yao waje wasaidie kukimbiza Wamidiani. Waliuwa wengi wao na kuwafukuza wale wengine kutoka kwa nchi ya Waisraeli. Wamidiani 120,000 walikufa siku hiyo. Mungu akawa **ameokoa Israel.**

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **God confused the Midianites**  - God caused the Midianites to be confused. They wanted to attack the Israelites, but instead, they attacked each other.
  * **Mungu alifanya Wamidiani kuchanganyikiwa**  - Mungu alifanya Wamidiani wachanganyikiwe. Walitaka kushambulia Waisraeli, lakini badala ya hiyo, wakashambuliana moja na mwingine.
  * **the rest of the Israelites**  - This could be translated as, "many other Israelite men." This refers to the soldiers previously sent home in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:16-10|[16-10]]]**.
  * **Waisraeli wengine**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "wanaume wengine wengi Waisraeli." Hii inaongea kuhusu askari waliotumwa nyumbani katika **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:16-10|[16-10]]]**.
  * **were called**  â€“ That is, "were called out" or, "were summoned." This sentence could also be translated as, "Gideon sent messengers to summon many other Israelite men from their homes."
  * **waliitwa**  - Hiyo ni, "waliitwa watoke" ama, "waliamurishwa kuja." Hii sentensi inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Gideoni alituma wajumbe watoe amri wanaume Waisraeli wengine wengi kutoka kwa manyumba yao."
