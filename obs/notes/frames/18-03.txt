===== Solomon Served Other Gods [18-03] Suleimani Alitumikia Miungu Mingine =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-03.jpg?nolink&}}

But **Solomon** loved women from other countries. He **disobeyed God** by marrying many women, almost 1,000 of them! Many of these women came from foreign countries and **brought their gods with them** and continued to **worship** them. When Solomon was old, he also worshiped their gods. Lakini Suleimani alipenda wanawake wa mataifa mengine.  Alimuasi Mungu kwa kuoa wanawake wengi, karibu 1,000! Wengi wa hawa wanawake walitoka kwa mataifa ya kigeni na wakaleta miungu yao pamoja na wao na wakaendelea kuiabudu hio.  Wakati Suleimani alikuwa mwenye umri, yeye pia aliabudu miungu yao.

==== Translation Notes: Tafsiri ya maelezo ====

  * **brought their gods with them**  - They brought their idols and their methods of worshiping idols with them to Israel. walilea miungu yao pamoja na wao - Walileta sanamu zao pamoja na njia zao za kuabudu sanamu kwa Israeli.
