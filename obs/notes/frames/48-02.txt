===== Adam and Eve Sinned [48-02] Adamu na Hawa Walitenda Dhambi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**Satan** spoke through the snake in **the garden** in order to **deceive Eve**. Then she and **Adam sinned** against **God**. Because they sinned, everyone on earth gets sick and everyone **dies**.

**Shetani ** aliongea kupitia kwa nyoka **katika bustani** ndiposa aweze** kum**danganya** **Hawa. Halafu yeye na **Adamu wakatenda dhambi** dhidi ya **Mungu**. Kwa sababu walitenda dhambi, kila mtu ulimwenguni hupata magonjwa na kila mtu **hufariki**.

===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **the garden**  - This refers to the garden created by God where he placed the first man and woman.
  * **bustani**  - Hii inamaanisha ile bustani Mungu aliumba pahali aliwaweka mwanaume na mwanamke wa kwanza.
  * **deceive Eve**  - That is, "lie to Eve." Satan lied by leading Eve to doubt what God had said. By doing this, he tricked her into disobeying God.
  * **kumdanganya Hawa**  - Hio ni, "kudanganya Hawa." Shetani alidanganya kwa kumuongoza Hawa kushuku kile Mungu alikuwa amesema. Kwa kufanya hivi, alimdanganya yeye kwa kumuasi Mungu.
