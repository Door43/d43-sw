===== Pharaoh's Dream [08-06] Ndoto ya Farao =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-06.jpg?nolink&}}

After two years, **Joseph** was still in prison, even though he was **innocent**. One night, the **Pharaoh**, which is what the **Egyptians** called their **kings**, had two **dreams** that **disturbed him greatly**. None of **his advisors** could tell him the **meaning of the dreams**.

Baada ya miaka miwili, **Yosefu** bado alikuwa gerezani, ingawa alikuwa **hana hatia**. Usiku mmoja, **Farao**, ambayo ndivyo **watu wa Misri** waliwaita **wafalme** wao, akaota ndoto mbili **zilizomsumbua sana**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **disturbed him greatly**  â€“ This means that the king felt very afraid and confused (because of what he had seen in the dreams).
  * **ilimusumbua sana **- Hii inamaanisha kuwa yule mfalme aliogopa sana na kuchanganyikiwa (kwa sababu ya kile aliona katika ndoto).
  * **his advisors**  - These were men with special powers and knowledge who could sometimes tell what dreams meant. Some translations refer to them as, "wise men."
  * **washauri wake**  - Hawa walikuwa wanaume waliokuwa na nguvu maluum na maarifa ambao wakati mwingine wngeeleza maana ya ndoto. Tafsiri zingine ninawaita, "wanaume wenye hekima."
  * **meaning of the dreams**  - People in Egypt believed that dreams were messages from the gods telling them about what would happen in the future. God used Pharaoh's dreams to tell him what would happen.
  * **maana ya ndoto**  - watu kule Misri waliamini kuwa ndoto ni ujumbe kutoka kwa miungu zinazowaambia kile kitafanyika siku za usoni. Mungu alitumia ndoto za Farao kimwambia kile kitafanyika.
