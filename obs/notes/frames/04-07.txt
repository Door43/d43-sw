===== Melchizedek [04-07] Melkizedeki =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-07.jpg?nolink&}}

One day, **Abram** met **Melchizedek**, the **priest** of **God Most High**. Melchizedek **blessed** Abram and said, “May God Most High who **owns heaven and earth** bless Abram.” Then Abram gave Melchizedek a tenth of all he owned.

Siku moja, **Abrahamu ** akakutana na **Melkizedeki**, **kuhani** wa **Mungu aliye juu zaidi**. Melkizedeki **akambariki** Abrahamu na kusema, "na Mungu aliye juu zaidi ambaye ni **mwenye** **bingu na nchi** abariki Abrahamu."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Melchizedek**  - Melchizedek was the recognized religious authority in Canaan who received and presented offerings to God.
  * **Melkizedeki**  - Melkizedeki ndiye aliyetaburika kama aliye na malaka ya kindini Kanani na alipokea na kutoa sadaka kwa Mungu.
  * **God Most High**  - The people of Canaan worshiped many false gods. The title, "Most High God" explains that the God that Melchizedek worshiped was far superior to all of them, and was the same God that Abram worshiped.
  * **Mungu aliye juu zaidi**  - Watu wa Kanani waliabudu miungi mingi isiyo ya kweli. Kichwa, "Mungu aliye juu zaidi" inaeleza kuwa Mungu ambaye Melkizedeki aliabudu alikuwa juu hizo miungu zingine zote, na alikuwa yule aliye mwabudu Abrahamu.
