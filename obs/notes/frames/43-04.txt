===== The Crowd Gathered [43-04] Umati Ulikusanyika =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-04.jpg?nolink&}}

When the people in **Jerusalem** heard the noise, **a crowd** came to see what was happening. When the people heard the **believers** proclaiming **the wonderful works of God**, they were astonished that they were hearing these things in their own native languages.

Wakati watu katika **Yerusalemu ** walisikia kelele, **umati ** ukaja kuona kile kilikuwa kinaendelea. Wakati watu waliwasikia waaminio wakitangaza **matendo makuu ya Mungu**, wakashangaa kwa kuwa walisikia mambo haya kwa lugha zao za asili.

===== Important Terms:(Maneno ya Muhimu) =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jerusalem|Jerusalem]](Yerusalemu**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:believer|believers]](waaminio)**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God]](Mungu)**
===== Translation Notes:(Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **a crowd**  - This could be translated as, "a crowd of people" or, "a large group of people."
  * **Umati **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "umati wa watu" au, "kikundi kikubwa cha watu."
  * **the wonderful works of God**  - This could be translated as, "the amazing things that God had done."
  * **matendo makuu ya Mungu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "mambo ya ajabu ambayo Mungu amefanya."
