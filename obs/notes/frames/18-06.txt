===== Rehoboam's Foolish Answer [18-06] Jibu la Rehoboamu la Kijinga *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-06.jpg?nolink&}}

**Rehoboam foolishly answered** them, “You thought my father **Solomon** made you work hard, but I will make you work harder than he did, and I will **punish** you more harshly than he did.”

**Rehoboamu akawajibu kwa ujinga**, "Mlifikiri baba yangu **Suleimani** aliwafanyisha kazi yenu ugumu, lakini nitawafanya mfanye kazi gumu zaidi, na **nitawaadhibu** kwa ukatili zaidi ya alivyofanya.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:rehoboam|Rehoboam (Rehoboamu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:solomon|Solomon (Suleimani)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:punish|punish (adhibu)]]**
==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **foolishly answered**  - Rehoboam's answer was harsh, and caused the people to turn against him.
  * **akajibu kwa ujinga**  - Jibu la Rehoboamu lilikuwa na ukatili, na likafanya watu kumgeuka.
