===== The Sick Woman [32-13] Mwanamke Aliyekuwa Mgonjwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-13.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** returned to the other side of the lake. After he arrived there, a large crowd gathered around him and **pressed in on him**. In the crowd was a woman who had suffered from a bleeding problem for twelve years. She had paid all of her money to doctors so they would **heal** her, but **she only got worse**.

**Yesu** akarudi upande ule mwingine wa bahari. Baada ya kufika pale, umati mkubwa ukakusanyika, wakamzunguka na **wakawa wanamfinya.** Katika ule umati kulikuwa na mwanamke aliyeteseka kwa shinda ya kuvunja damu kwa miaka kumi na miwili. Alikuwa amelipa pesa yake yote kwa madaktari ili **waweze kumponya**, lakini **aliendelea na kuadhirika**.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:heal|heal (ponya)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **pressed in on him**  - That is, "pushed tightly against him" or, "crowded in tightly around him."
  * **wakawa wanamfinya**  - Hiyo ni, "kusukuma kwa mkazo dhidi yake" ama, "wakamzunguka kwa karibu."
  * **she only got worse**  - That is, "her condition only got worse" or, " her health got worse instead of better" or, "instead she became more sick."
  * **aliendelea na kuadhirika**  - Hiyo ni, "hali yake iliendelea kuwa mbaya" ama, "afya yake ilizorota badala ya kuwa bora" ama, "badala ya hiyo alikuwa mgonjwa zaidi."
