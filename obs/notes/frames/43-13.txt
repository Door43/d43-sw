===== The Disciples Met Together [43-13] Wanafunzi Walikusanyika Pamoja =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-13.jpg?nolink&}}

The **disciples** continually listened to the teaching of the **apostles**, spent time together, ate together, and **prayed** with each other. They enjoyed **praising God** together and they shared everything they had with each other. Everyone **thought well of them**. Every day, more people became **believers**.

**Wanafunzi ** waliendelea kuyasikiliza mafundisho ya **mitume**, wakitumia wakati pamoja, wakila pamoja, na w**aliomba**** **pamoja, kila mmoja na mwingine. Walifurahia **kumsifu Mungu** pamoja na walishirikiana kwa kila kitu walichokuwa nacho pamoja na kila mmoja na mwingine. Kila mtu **aliwadhania wao vizuri**. Kila siku, watu zaidi wakawa **waaminio**.

  * **thought well of them**  - That is, "had a positive opinion of them."
  * **Waliwadhania wao vizuri**  - Hio ni, "walikuwa na maoni mazuri kuwahusu."
