===== To Prison [29-06] Gerezani =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-06.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **fellow servant fell on his knees** and said, ‘Please be patient with me, and I will pay the full amount that I owe you.’ But instead, the servant **threw his fellow servant into prison** until he could pay the debt.”

"Yule **mtumishi mwenzake alianguka kwa magoti** na kusema, 'Tafadhali nivumilie, na nitakulipa kile unacho nidai.' Lakini badala ya hiyo, yule mtumishi a**limuweka**** mtumishi mwenzake gerezani ** mpaka wakati atalipa ile deni."
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:servant|servant (mtumishi)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **fellow servant**  - Translate this phrase in the same way as in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:29-05|[29-05]]]**.
  * **mtumishi mwenzake**  - Tafsiri hii msemo sawa na uvyotafsiri pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:29-05|[29-05]]]**.
  * **fell on his knees**  - This phrase has the same meaning as in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:29-04|[29-04]]]**.
  * **alianguka kwa mabega yake **- Huu msemo ina maana sawa na pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:29-04|[29-04]]]**.
  * **threw his fellow servant into prison**  - This could also be translated as, "had the man put into prison." The word "threw" is figurative and means it was done in a forceful way.
  * **alimuweka mtumishi mwenzake gerezani **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "akafanya yule mwanamme awekwe gerezani." Neno "aliweka" linatumiwa kama mfano na linamaanisha ilifanywa kwa njia ya kutumia nguvu.
