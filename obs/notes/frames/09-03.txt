===== Hard Labor [09-03] Kazi Ngumu *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-03.jpg?nolink&}}

The **Egyptians** forced the **Israelites** to build many buildings and even whole cities. The hard work made their lives **miserable**, but **God blessed them**, and they had even more children.

**Wamisri** wakawalazimisha **Waisraeli** kujenga manyumba mengi na hata miji mizima. Ile kazi ngumu ikafanya maisha yao **kuwa duni**, lakini **Mungu akawabariki**, na wakawa na watoto wengi hata zaidi.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **kuwa duni**  - Hii inamaanisha kuwa waliteseka vimbaya kwa sababu ya vile walivyofanyiwa makali na vile walivyolazimishwa kufanya kazi kwa nguvu. Pia wajihisi wamezushwa moyo sana.
