===== God's Commandments [28-02] Amri za Mungu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-02.jpg?nolink&}}

“**Which ones do I need to obey**?” he asked. **Jesus** replied, “Do not murder. Do not commit **adultery**. Do not steal. Do not lie. Honor your father and mother, and love your neighbor **as you love yourself**.”

**"Nina haja kutii zipi?**" akauliza. **Yesu** akajibu, "Usiue. Usifanye **uasherati**. usiimbe. usiongee uongo. Heshimu baba yako na mama yako, na penda jirani yako **kama vile unavyojipenda mwenyewe."**
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:adultery|adultery (uasherati)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:love|love (upendo)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Which ones do I need to obey?**  - That is, "Which laws will be enough for me to receive eternal life?"
  * **Nina haja ya kutii zipi**  - Hiyo ni, "Sheria zipi zitatosha ili niweze kupata uzima wa milele?"
  * **as you love yourself**  - That is, "as much as you love yourself" or, "to the same degree that you love yourself."
  * **kama unavyojipenda mwenyewe**  - Hiyo ni, "sawa na vile unavyojipenda" ama, "kwa kiwango sawa na unavyojipenda mwenyewe."
