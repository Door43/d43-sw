===== Philip Baptized the Ethiopian [45-12] Filipo Alimbatiza Muethiopia =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-12.jpg?nolink&}}

So they went down into the water, and Philip **baptized** the Ethiopian. After they came up out of the water, the **Holy Spirit** suddenly **carried Philip away** to another place where he continued telling people about **Jesus**.

Hivyo wakaenda chini ndani ya maji, na Filipo **akambazita ** Muethiopia. Baada ya wao kutoka kwa maji, **Roho Mtakatifu** ghafla **akamchukua Filipo ** mahali pengine ambapo aliendelea kuambia watu juu ya **Yesu**.

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]](Yesu)**
===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **carried Philip away**  - That is, "took Philip away" or, "snatched Philip away."
  * **akamchukua Filipo**
 - Hio ni, "alimpeleka Filipo mbali" ama, "alinyakua Filipo."
