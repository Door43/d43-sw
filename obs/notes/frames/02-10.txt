===== God Cursed the Woman [02-10] Mungu akamlaani mwanamke *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-10.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** then said to the woman, “I will make **childbirth very painful** for you. You will desire your husband, and he will rule over you.”

Kisha **Mungu** akamuambia mwanamke, "Nitafanya kujifungua mtoto iwe na uchungu sana kwako. Utamtamani mumeo, naye atatawala juu yako."
===== Important Terms: maneno muhimu =====

  * **[[:sw:obe:kt:god]]**


==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **childbirth very painful**  - Some languages may need to express this as a verb. You could say, "I will cause you to have much more pain when you give birth to children."
  * **Kujifungua itakuwa na uchungu sana - **  Lugha zingine zaweza kutumia kielezi hapa, yaani zinaweza eleza hii kwa kutumia maneno yanayoonyesha tendo la kujifungua mtoto. unaweza sema, "nitakufanya uwe na uchungu mwingi zaidi unapojifungua watoto."
