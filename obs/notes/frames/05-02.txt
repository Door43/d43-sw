===== Ishmael Was Born [05-02] - Ishmaeli alizaliwa *F =====

So** Abram married Hagar**. Hagar had a baby boy, and Abram named him **Ishmael**. But **Sarai became jealous of Hagar**. When Ishmael was thirteen years old, **God** again spoke to Abram.

Basi **Abramu alimuoa Hajiri. ** Hajiri akamzaa mtoto wa kiume naye Abramu akampa jina **Ishmaeli. ** Lakini Sarai **alimwonea wivu Hajiri. ** Wakati Ishmaeli alipotimiza umri wa miaka kumi na tatu**, Bwana ** alizungumza na Abramu tena.

===== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri =====

  * **Married **  - Hagar became Abram's concubine–a "second wife" of lower status. Hagar was still Sarai's servant.
  * **Kuolewa- **  Hajiri akawa suria wa Abramu - mke wa pili akiwa hadhi ya chini, hii ni kwa sababu Hajiri alikuwa bado mtumishi wa Sarai.
  * **became jealous of Hagar**  - Sarai was jealous of Hagar because Hagar could have children and Sarai could not
  * **(kumwonea wivu Hagari)**  - Sarai alimwonea wivu Hajiri sababu hangeweza kupata watoto kama Hagari​
