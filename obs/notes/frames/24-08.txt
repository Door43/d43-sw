===== You Are My Beloved Son [24-08] Wewe ni Mwanangu Mpendwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-08.jpg?nolink&}}

When **Jesus** came up out of the water after being **baptized**, the **Spirit of God appeared in the form of a dove** and came down and rested on him. At the same time, God's voice spoke from **heaven**, saying, “You are **my Son whom I love**, and I am very pleased with you.”

Wakati **Yesu** alikuja juu kutoka ndani ya maji baada ya **kubatizwa**, **Roho wa Mungu alionekana akiwa kwa mfano wa jiwa** na akashuka chini na kutua juu yake. Wakati huo huo, Sauti ya Mungu ikanena kutoka binguni, ikisema, "Wewe ni **Mwanangu ninayempenda**, na unanipendeza sana."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **my Son whom I love**  - Make sure the translation of this does not sound like there was another son. It may be necessary to say, "You are my Son. I love you very much and am very pleased with you."
  * **Mwanangu ninayempenda**  - Hakikisha tafsiri ya hii haitaonyesha kama kulikuwa na mwana mwingine. Yaweza kuwa ya muhimu kusema, "Wewe ni mwanangu. Ninakupenda sana na ninafurahishwa sana na wewe."
