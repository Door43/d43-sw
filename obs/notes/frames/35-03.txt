===== The Two Sons [35-03] Wana Wawili =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-03.jpg?nolink&}}

“There was a man who had two **sons**. The younger son told his father, ‘Father, I want **my inheritance** now!’ So the father divided his **property** between his two sons.”

"Kulikuwa na mtu ambaye alikuwa na **wana ** wawili. Yule mwana mdogo akamwambia baba yake, 'Baba, ninataka **urithi ** wangu sasa!' Kwa hivyo baba yake akagawanya **mali ** yake kati ya wana wake wawili."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus tells the story.)
  * (Yesu anaelezea hadithi.)
  * **my inheritance**  - This could be translated as, "the part of your wealth that will be rightfully mine when you die."
  * **urithi wangu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "sehemu ya mali yako ambayo itakuwa haki yangu wakati utakufa."
  * **property**  - That is, "wealth" or, "possessions." This property would probably have included land, animals, and money.
  * **mali**  - Hiyo ni, "utajiri" ama, "milki," Hii mali ingekuwa ni vitu kama ardhi, wanyama na fedha.
