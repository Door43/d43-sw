===== Back to Judea [37-02] Kurudi Yuda =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-02.jpg?nolink&}}

After the two days had passed, **Jesus** said to his **disciples**, “Let’s go back to **Judea**.” “But **Teacher**,” the disciples answered, “Just a short time ago the people there wanted to kill you!” Jesus said, “Our friend **Lazarus has fallen asleep**, **and I must wake him**.”

Baada ya siku mbili kupita, **Yesu** akawaambia **wanafunzi** wake, "Twendeni tena Yuda." "Lakini **Mwalimu**, "wanafunzi akajibu, "Muda mfupi uliopita watu huko walikuwa wanataka kuua wewe!" Yesu akasema, "Rafiki wetu **Lazaro amelala, na mimi lazima nimuamshe kwake."**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Judea**  - This refers to the southern section of Israel, which was settled by the tribe of Judah. Some languages will prefer to say, "the region of Judea."
  * **Yuda**  - Hii inamaanisha sehemu ya kusini ya Israeli, ambayo ilikuwa makazi ya kabila la Yuda. Baadhi ya lugha wanapendelea kusema, "kanda ya Yudea."
  * **has fallen asleep, and I must wake him.**  - Translate this phrase with the normal words for "asleep" and "wake" in your language. Although Jesus was using these words with a different meaning, the disciples did not understand that yet.
  * **amelala, na mimi lazima nimuamshe **- Tafsiri msemo huu kwa maneno ya kawaida kwa ajili ya "usingizi" na "amusha" katika lugha yako. Ingawa Yesu alikuwa akitumia maneno haya na maana tofauti, wanafunzi hawakuwa wameelewa hayo bado.
