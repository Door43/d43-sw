===== Alone with Jesus [36-06] Peke Yao na Yesu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Jesus touched them** and said, “**Do not be afraid**. **Get up**.” When they looked around, the **only one still there was Jesus**.

Wakati huo **Yesu akawagusa, ** na akasema, **"Msiogope. Amkeni."** Baada ya kutazama pande zote, **mwenye aliye kuweko ni Yesu pekee.**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **touched them**  - That is, "put his hand on them." Some languages may prefer to specify where he touched them. If so, this could be translated as, "he touched them on the shoulder" or, "he put his hand on each one's shoulder."
  * **aliwagusa **- Hiyo ni, "kuwekelea mkono wake juu yao." Baadhi ya lugha zinaweza kupendelea kueleza wazi mahali aliwagusa. Kama ni hivyo, hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama , "alisema akiwagusa begani" au, "naye akaweka mkono wake juu ya bega la kila mtu."
  * **Do not be afraid**  - This could also be translated as, "Stop being afraid."
  * **Msiogope**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "Wacheni kuogopa."
  * **Get up**  - This could also be translated as, "Stand up" or, "Please get up." Make sure that it sounds like Jesus spoke this with kindness.
  * **Amkeni **- Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "Simama" au , "Tafadhali amkeni. "Hakikisha itaonekana kama Yesu alizungumza haya kwa wema.
  * **the only one still there was Jesus**  - It is also possible to add, "Moses and Elijah had left."
  * **mwenye aliye kuweko ni Yesu pekee**  - Pia kuna uwezekano wa kuongeza, "Musa na Elia walikuwa wameondoka."
