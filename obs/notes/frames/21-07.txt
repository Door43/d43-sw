===== The Perfect High Priest [21-07] Kuhani Mkuu aliye Mkamilifu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-07.jpg?nolink&}}

**Israelite priests** made **sacrifices** to **God on behalf of** the people **as a substitute for the punishment for their sins**. Priests also **prayed** to God for the people. The **Messiah** would be the **perfect high priest** who would **offer himself** as a **perfect sacrifice** to Go**d. **

**Makuhani wa Israeli ** walifanya **dhabihu ** kwa Mungu **kwa niaba** ya watu **kama kitu ya kusimamia adhabu ya dhambi zao.** Makuhani pia **waliomba ** Mungu kwa ajili ya watu. **Masihi ** alikuwa awe **kuhani mkuu aliyekamilika** ambaye **atajitoa ****mwenyew**e kama **dhabihu kamilifu** kwa Mungu.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **on behalf of**  - This may be translated as, "for the benefit of" or, "in the place of."
  * **Kwa niaba ****ya**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kwa manufaa ya", ama, "badala ya."
  * **as a substitute for the punishment for their sins**  - God allowed people to bring animals to sacrifice for their sin as a reminder of the punishment that they deserved and their dependence on God to remove their sin. God accepted these sacrifices as a temporary covering for sin and did not punish the people for it.
  * **Kama kitu ya kusimamia adhabu ya dhambi zao**  - Mungu aliruhusu watu kuleta wanyama wa kutolewa dhabihu kwa ajili ya dhambi yao kama kumbusho la adhabu iliyostahili kwao na utegemezi wao kwa Mungu kuondoa dhambi yao. Mungu alikubali hizi dhabihu kama kifuniko cha muda kwa ajili ya dhabi na alikosa kuwaadhibu watu kwa sababu hiyo.
  * **perfect high priest**  - Unlike the other high priests, the Messiah would never sin, and he would permanently take away all the sins of the people.
  * **kuhani mkuu mkamilifu**  - Kinyume na walivyokua makuhani wakuu wengine, Masihi kamwe hangetenda dhambi, na alikuwa aondoe kabisa dhambi za watu.

  * **perfect sacrifice**  - That is, "a sacrifice that has no fault or imperfection."
  * **dhabihu kamilifu**  - Hio ni, "dhabihu ambayo haina kosa ama kutokamilika."
