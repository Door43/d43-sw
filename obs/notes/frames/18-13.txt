===== Judah Was Unfaithful [18-13] Yuda hakuwa mwaminifu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-13.jpg?nolink&}}

The **kings** of **Judah** were **descendants** of **David**. Some of these kings were **good** men who **ruled justly** and **worshiped God**. But most of Judahâ€™s kings were **evil**, **corrupt**, and they worshiped **idols**. Some of the kings even **sacrificed** their children to false **gods**. Most of the people of Judah also **rebelled** against God and worshiped other gods.

**Wafalme** wa **Yuda** walikuwa **uzao** wa **Daudi**. Wengine wa hawa wafalme walikuwa watu **wazuri** **waliotawala kwa haki** na **kuabudu Mungu**. Lakini wengi wa wafalme wa Yuda walikuwa **wabaya, wafisadi**, na waliabudu **sanamu**. Wengine wa hawa wafalme hata **walitoa dhabihu** watoto wao kwa **miungu** ya uongo. Watu wengi wa Yuda pia **walimuasi** Mungu na kuabudu miungu mingine.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **ruled justly**  - This means they ruled according to God's laws. This can be translated as, "when they ruled, they did what was right."
  * **alitawala kwa haki**  - Hii inamaanisha kuwa alitawala kulingana na sheria za Mungu. Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "walipotawala, walifanya kilicho cha haki."
  * **corrupt**  - This can be translated as, "did what was wrong in order to get whatever they wanted."
  * **wafisadi**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "walifanya mambo mabaya ili wapate kile walichokitaka."
