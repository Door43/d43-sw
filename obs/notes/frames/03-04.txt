===== Building the Boat [03-04] Kujenga Mashua *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-04.jpg?nolink&}}

**Noah obeyed God**. He and his three sons built the boat just the way God had told them. It took many years to build the boat, because it was so big. **Noah warned** the people about the flood that was coming and told them to **turn to God**, but they did not believe him.

**Nuhu akamtii Mungu**. Yeye na wanaye watatu wakajenga mashua kama vile Mungu alivyowaeleza. Ilichukua miaka mingi kujenga mashua, kwa sababu ilikuwa kumbwa mno. **Nuhu akawaonya** watu kuhusu mafuriko yatakayokuja na akawaambia **wamgeukie Mungu**, lakini hakumuamini.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:noah|Noah (Nuhu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:obey|obeyed (alitii]])**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God (Mungu)]]**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsri): =====

  * **Noah warned**  - Noah told everyone that God planned to destroy the world because of sin.
  * **Nuhu aliwaonya**  - Nuhu aliwaambia watu wote kwamba Mungu amepanga kuharibu dunia kwa sababu ya dhambi.
  * **turn to God**  - This means that they should stop sinning and start obeying God.
  * **Mgeukieni Mungu**  - Hii inamanisha kwamba wanastahili kuacha kutenda dhambi na waanze kumtii Mungu.
