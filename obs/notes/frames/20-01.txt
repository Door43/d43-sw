===== Israel Sinned [20-01] Israeli walitenda Dhambi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-01.jpg?nolink&}}

The **kingdom of Israel** and the **kingdom of Judah** both **sinned** against **God**. They **broke the covenant** that God had made with them at **Sinai**. God sent his **prophets** to **warn them to repent** and **worship** him again, but they refused to **obey**.

**Ufalme wa Israeli ** na **ufalme wa Yuda** wote **walitenda dhambi** kwa **Mungu**. **Walivunja agano ** ambalo Mungu alifanya na wao pale Mlima wa **Sinai**. Mungu alituma **manabii ** wake **wawaonye ili watubu** na **kumuabudu** tena, lakini **walikataa** kutii.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **broke the covenant**  - That is, "disobeyed the commands God had given them in his covenant with them at Mount Sinai."
  * **walivunja agano**  - Hiyo ni, "walikosa kutii amri ambazo Mungu aliwapa katika agano lake nao pale Mlima wa Sinai."
  * **warn them to repent and worship him again**  â€“ Another way to translate this would be, "tell them to stop sinning and to worship Yahweh instead of other gods, or terrible things would happen to them."
  * **waonye watubu na kuniabudu tena**  - Njia ingine ya kutafsiri hii yeweza kuwa, "wambie waache kutenda dhambi na waabudu Yehova badala ya miungu mingine, ama mambo mabaya itafanyika kwao."
