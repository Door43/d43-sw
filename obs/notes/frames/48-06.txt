===== The Great High Priest [48-06] Kuhani Mkuu Mkubwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-06.jpg?nolink&}}

For hundreds of years, **priests** continually offered **sacrifices** to **God** for people to show the punishment that they deserved for their **sins**. But those sacrifices could not take away their sin. **Jesus** is the **Great High Priest**. Unlike other priests, **he offered himself** as the only sacrifice that could take away the sin of all the people in the world. Jesus was the perfect high priest because he **took the punishment** for every sin that anyone has ever committed.

Kwa mamia ya miaka,**Makuhani ** waliendelea kutoa **dhabihu ** kwa **Mungu ** kwa ajili ya watu kuonyesha dhawabu waliostahili kwa ajili ya **dhambi ** zao. Lakini dhabihu hizo hazingeweza kuondoa dhambi zao. **Yesu ** ndiye **Kuhani Mkuu Mkubwa**. Tofauti na makuhani wengine, **alijitoa mwenyewe** kama dhabihu ya pekee ambayo ingetoa dhambi ya watu wote ulimwenguni. Yesu alikuwa kuhani mkuu mkamilifu kwa sababu** alichukua adhabu ** ya kila dhambi ambayo kila mtu aliyewahi tenda.

===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **he offered himself**  - This could also be translated as, "Jesus allowed himself to be killed."
  * **alijitoa mwenyewe**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Yesu aliruhusu yeye mwenyewe auawe."
  * **took the punishment**  - That is, "received the punishment in his own body."
  * **alichukua adhabu**  - Hio ni, "alichukua adhabu hiyo kwa mwili wake mwenyewe."
