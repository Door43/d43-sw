===== The Gnats and the Flies [10-05] Chawa na Nzi *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-05.jpg?nolink&}}

So **God sent a plague of gnats**. Then he sent a plague of **flies**. **Pharaoh** called **Moses** and **Aaron** and told them that if they stopped the plague, the **Israelites** could leave Egypt. When Moses prayed, God removed all the flies from **Egypt**. But Pharaoh **hardened his heart** and would not let the people go free.

Kwa hivyo **Mungu alituma pigo la chawa**. Halafu akatuma pigo la **nzi**. **Farao** akaita **Musa** na **Haruni** na akawaambia kuwa, wakifanya lile pigo lisimame, **wana wa Israeli ** wanaweza toka Misri. Wakati Musa aliomba, Mungu aliondoa zile nzi zote kutoka **Misri**. Lakini Farao **akafanya moyo wake mugumu** na hangeruhusu watu kuwa huru.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **God sent a plague**  â€“ This could be translated as, "God caused there to be a plague" or, "God caused a plague (of gnats) to come over the land of Egypt."
  * **Mungu alituma pigo**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mungu alifanya kuwe na pigo" ama "Mungu alifanya pigo (la chawa) lije katika nchi ya Misri."
  * **gnats**  - These were tiny, biting insects flying in large swarms, annoying and landing on all the people and animals of Egypt.
  * **chawa**  - Hizi ni wadudu wadogo wanaouma watu na wanaruka kwa vikundi, ziliudhi na kutua kwa watu na wanyama wa Misri.
  * **flies**  - These were much bigger flying insects that were both annoying and destructive. There were so many of these flies that they covered everything, even filling the Egyptians' houses.
  * **nzi **- Hizi ni wadudu wakubwa zaidi na wanaoruka lakini pia walikuwa wanaudhi na kuharibu. Hizi zilikuwa nyingi kiasi ya kufunika kila kitu hata manyumba ya Misri.
  * **hardened his heart**  - See note in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:10-04|[10-04]]]**.
  * **alifanya moyo mugumu**  - Ona maelezo katika **[10-04]**
