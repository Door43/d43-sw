===== You Crucified Jesus [43-06] Mlimsulubisha Yesu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-06.jpg?nolink&}}

“Men of **Israel**, **Jesus** was a man who did many mighty **signs and wonders** by the **power** of **God**, as you have seen and already know. But **you crucified him**!”

"Wanaume wa **Israeli**, **Yesu ** alikuwa mtu ambaye alifanya **ishara na maajabu** makuu kwa **nguvu ** za **Mungu**, kama vile mumeona na tayari mwajua. Lakini **nyinyi mlimsulubisha!"**

===== Translation Notes:(Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * (Peter continues preaching to the crowd.)
  * (Petero anaendelea kuhubiria umati.)
  * **Men of Israel**  - For some languages it is better to say, "People of Israel" for it to be clear that this included men and women. This could also be translated as, "My fellow people of Israel" or, "My fellow Jews" to make it clear that Peter was also a Jew and belonged to the "people of Israel."
  * **Wanaume wa Israeli**  - Kwa lugha zingine ni bora kusema, "Watu wa Israeli" ndio iwe wazi ya kwamba ilihusisha wanaume na wanawake. Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Wenzangu wa Israeli" au, Wayahudi wenzangu" ndio iwe wazi ya kwamba pia Petero alikuwa Myahudi na alikuwa mmoja wa "watu wa Israeli."
  * **you crucified him!**  - This could also be translated as, "you caused him to be crucified" or, "because of you, he was crucified." The Jews did not actually nail Jesus to the cross. However, the Jewish leaders caused him to be condemned and many of the people in the crowd had shouted for him to be crucified.
  * **Mlimsulubisha yeye**! - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "mlimfanya asulubiwe", au, Kwa sababu yenu, alisulubiwa." Wayahudi kwa uhakika hawakumpigilia yesu msalabani. Hata hivyo, viongozi wa Wayahudi walimfanya ahukumiwe na watu wengi katika umati walikuwa wanapasa sauti ndio yeye asulubiwe.
