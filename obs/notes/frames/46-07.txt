===== Saul Escaped Damascus [46-07] Sauli Alitoroka Dameski =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-07.jpg?nolink&}}

After many days, the **Jews** made a plan to kill **Saul**. They sent people to watch for him at **the city gates** in order to kill him. But Saul heard about the plan, and his friends helped him escape. One night they **lowered him over the city wall in a basket**. After Saul escaped from Damascus, he continued to **preach** about **Jesus**.

Baada ya siku nyingi, **Wayahudi ** walipanga mpango wa kumuua **Sauli**. Walituma watu kumuangalia **ma****langoni**** mwa mji** ili wamuue. Sauli akasikia kuhusu ule mpango, na marafiki zake wakamsaidia kutoroka. Usiku mmoja **wakamteremsha chini kwa ukuta akiwa ndani ya kikapu**. Baada ya Sauli kutoroka Dameski, aliendelea **kuhubiri ** juu ya **Yesu**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **the city gates**  - The gates were the only normal way into or out of the walled cities.
  * **malangoni ya mji**  - Malango yalikuwa njia za pekee kuingia au kutoka nje za kuta za miji.
  * **lowered him over the city wall in a basket**  - Another way to say this would be, "helped him get into a large basket and lowered the basket with him in it over the city wall."
  * **wakamteremsha chini kwa ukuta akiwa ndani ya kikapu **- Njia ingine ya kusema hii inaweza kuwa, "walimsaidia kuingia kwa kikapu kikubwa na wakateremsha kikapu pamoja na yeye kwa ukuta wa mji."
