===== Killing the Prophets of Baal [19-12] Kuuawa kwa manabii wa Baali =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-12.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Elijah** said, “Do not let any of the **prophets** of **Baal escape**!” So the people **captured** the prophets of Baal. Then Elijah took them away from there and killed them.

Kisha **Elia** akasema, "Msiruhusu yeyote kati ya **manabii** wa **Baali watoroke!**" Kwa hivyo watu **wakawakamata** wale manabii wa Baali. Halafu Elia akawaondoa kutoka hapo na akawaua.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **escape**  - The prophets of Baal tried to run away after their god was proven to be a false god.
  * **kutoroka**  - Manabii wa Baali walijaribu kutoroka wakati mungu wao alithibitishwa kuwa wa uongo.
  * **captured**  – That is, "seized and held onto" or, "took hold of."
  * **walikamatwa**  - Hiyo ni, "kukamata na kushikilia" ama, "waliwashika."
