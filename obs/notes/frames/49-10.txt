===== Jesus Died in Our Place [49-10] Yesu Alikufa kwa Niaba Yetu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-10.jpg?nolink&}}

**Because of your sin**, you are **guilty** and deserve to **die**. **God should be angry** with you, but **he poured out his anger on Jesus** instead of on you. When Jesus died on the **cross**, he **received your punishment**.

**Kwa sababu ya dhambi zako**, una **hatia** na unastahili **kufa.** **Mungu wanapaswa kuwa na hasira** na wewe, lakini **yeye alimwaga hasira yake juu ya Yesu ** badala yako. Wakati Yesu alikufa **msalabani, alipokea adhabu yako.**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Because of your sin**  - This can also be translated as, "Because you have sinned." To make it clear that this is talking about all people, for some languages it may be more clear to translate this as, "Because all people have sinned, they are guilty. They deserve death."
  * **Kwa sababu ya dhambi yako**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Kwa kuwa umetenda dhambi." Ili iwe wazi kuwa hii inazungumza kuhusu watu wote, kwa baadhi ya lugha inaweza kuwa wazi zaidi kutafsiri hii kama: "Kwa vile watu wote wamefanya dhambi, wao wana hatia. Wanastahili kufa."
  * **God should be angry**  - This can also be translated as, "It would be right for God to be angry."
  * **Mungu anapaswa kuwa na hasira**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Itakuwa haki Mungu kuwa na hasira."
  * **poured out his anger on**  - That is, "directed his anger at" or, "placed all of his anger upon" or, "was angry only with."
  * **alimwaga hasira yake juu ya**  - Hiyo ni, "alielekeza hasira yake kwa" ​​ama, "aliweka hasira yake yote juu ya" ama, "alikuwa na hasira tu na."
  * **received your punishment**  - This could also be translated as, "was punished instead of you" or, "was punished for your sin." To make it clear that this applies to everyone, it could also be translated as, "was punished for everyone's sin."
  * **alipokea adhabu yako**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "aliadhibiwa badala yako" ama, "aliadhibiwa kwa ajili ya dhambi zenu." Ili iwe wazi kwamba hii inaongea kuhusu kila mtu, inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "aliadhibiwa kwa ajili ya dhambi ya kila mtu."
