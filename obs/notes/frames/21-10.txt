===== Messiah Will Heal [21-10] - Masihi Ataponya =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-10.jpg?nolink&}}

The **prophet Isaiah** said the Messiah would live in **Galilee**, comfort **broken-hearted people**, and **proclaim freedom to captives** and **release to prisoners**. He also predicted that the **Messiah** would **heal** sick people and those who **could not hear**, **see**, **speak**, **or walk**.

**Nabii Isaya** alisema ya kuwa Masihi angeishi **Galilaya**, atafariji **watu waliovunjika mioyo,** na kutangaza **kuwekwa huru kwa mateka** na **kuachilia waliofungwa**. Pia alitabiri ya kuwa **Masihi angeponya ** watu wagonjwa na wale **hawakuwa wanasikia, kuona, kuongea, ama kutembea**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **broken-hearted people**  - This expression refers to people who are experiencing extreme sadness.
  * **watu walio vunjika mioyo**  - Hii msemo inaeleza kuhusu watu wanaopitia huzuni huzuni iliyokidhiri.
  * **proclaim freedom to captives**  - That is, "tell slaves they could be free." This could also refer to freeing people from slavery to sin.
  * **kutangaza kuwekwa huru kwa mateka**  - Hii ni, "kuambia watumwa ya kuwa wanaweza kuwa huru." Hii pia inaweza kumaanisha, kuweka watu huru kutoka kwa utumwa wa dhambi."
  * **release to prisoners**  - That is, "he will set free those who have unjustly been put into prison." This could also refer to setting people free from the bondage of sin.
  * **Kuachilia wafungwa**  - Hii ni, "ataweka huru wale waliokuwa wamewekwa gerezani kwa pasipo haki." Hii inaweza kumaanisha kuweka watu huru kutoka kwa utumwa wa dhambi.

  * **hawana uwezo wa kusikia, ona, ongea, ama tembea**  - Inaweza kuwa bora kusema, "hawakuwa wanasikia, hawakuwa wanaona, hawakuwa wanaongea, ama hakuwa wanatembea." Baadhi ya lugha zina maneno spesheli ya hali hizi, kama "kiziwi" na "Kipofu."
