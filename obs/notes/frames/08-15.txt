==== Translation Notes (Tafsiri ya Maelezo): ====

  * **covenant promises**  - Long ago God made an agreement with Abraham and promised him that he would give him many descendants; they would possess the land of Canaan and become a great nation. God also promised that all peoples would be blessed through Abraham's line. See also **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:07-10|[07-10]]]**.
  * **ahadi za agano**  - muda mrefu uliyopita Mungu alifanya agano na Ibrahimu na akaahidi atampa uzao mwingi; watamiliki nchi ya Kanani na kuwa taifa kubwa. Mungu pia aliahidi kuwa watu wote watabarikiwa kupitia kwa uzao wa Ibrahimu. Ona pia **[07-10].**
  * **passed on to**  - Another way to say this would be, "passed down to" or, "given to" or, "applied to." God's promise to Abraham was also for Abraham's children, grandchildren and all the rest of his descendants. See also **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:06-04|[06-04]]]**.
  * zikapitishwa kwa - Njia ingine ya kusema hii inaweza kuwa, "kupitishwa chini kwa" ama "kupeanwa kwa" ama "kutumiwa kwa." Ahadi ya Mungu kwa Ibrahimu ilikuwa pia ya watoto wa Ibrahimu, wajukuu wake, na uzao mwingine wake wote. Ona pia **[06-04].**
  * **twelve tribes of Israel**  - God had promised Abraham, Isaac and Jacob that their descendants would become a great nation. God later changed Jacob's name to Israel. The descendants of Jacob's 12 sons became 12 great tribes. These 12 tribes made up the ancient nation called Israel, which was named by Jacob's new name.
  * **makabila kumi na mbili ya Israeli**- Mungu alikuwa amemuahidi Ibrahimu, Isaka na Yakobo kuwa uzao wake utakuwa taifa kubwa. Mungu baadaye alibadilisha jina la Yakobo likawa Israeli. Uzao wa wana 12 wa Yakobo wakafanyika makabila 12 makuu. Hizi makabila 12 zikatengeneza nchi ya kale ilioitwa Israeli, ambalo ni jina mpya la Yakobo.
