===== What Must I Do? [28-01] Nifanye Nini? =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-01.jpg?nolink&}}

**One day**, a **rich young ruler came up to Jesus** and asked him, “**Good teacher**, what must I do **to have eternal life?**” Jesus said to him, “**Why do you call me 'good'**? **There is only one who is good**, **and that is God**. But if you want to have eternal life, **obey God's laws**.”

**Siku moja**, **kijana** **mtawala aliyekuwa tajiri** akaja kwa Yesu na akamuuliza, "**Mwalimu mwema**, nifanye nini **ili nipate uzima wa milele?"** Yesu akamwambia, **"Kwa nini unaniita 'mwema'?** **Kuna mmoja tu aliye mwema, na ni Mungu**. Lakini kama unataka kuwa na uzima wa milele, **tii sheria za Mungu."**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **One day**  - This phrase introduces an event that happened in the past, but does not state the specific time. Many languages have a similar way to begin telling a true story.
  * **Siku moja**  - Huu msemo unatanguliza tukio lililofanyika siku zilizopita, lakini haitaji wakati haswa. Lugha nyingi ziko na njia sawa ya  kuanza hadithi ya kweli.
  * **rich young ruler**  - This man was already a rich and powerful political official, even though he was still young.
  * **Kijana mtawala aliye tajiri**  - Huyu mwanamme tayari alikuwa afisa wa kisiasa mwenye utajiri na nguvu, ingawa alikuwa bado kijana.
  * **came up to Jesus**  - That is, "approached Jesus."
  * **alikuja kwa Yesu**  - Hiyo ni, "alimkaribia Yesu."
  * **Good teacher**  - That is, "righteous teacher." He was not saying that Jesus was simply a skilled teacher.
  * **Mwalimu mwema**  - Hiyo ni, "mwalimu mwenye haki." Hakuwa anasema kuwa Yesu tu ni mwalimu aliye na ujuzi.
  * **to have eternal life**  - That is, "to receive eternal life" or, "to live with God forever." Also consider how "eternal life" was translated on **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]** and see the note about that there.
  * **Kuwa na uzima wa milele**  - Hiyo ni, "kupokea uzima wa milele" ama, "kuishi na Mungu milele." Pia fikiria vile "uzima wa milele" lilitafsiriwa pale** [27-01]**  na angalia maelezo ya neno hilo pale.
  * **Why do you call me 'good'**  - Jesus is not denying that he is God. Rather, he is asking if the ruler understands that Jesus is God.
  * **Kwa nini unaniita 'mwema'**  - Yesu hakatai kuwa yeye ni Mungu. Lakini, anauliza yule mtawala kama anaelewa kuwa Yesu ni Mungu.
  * **There is only one who is good, and that is God**  - Another way to say this would be, "The only one who is truly good is God" or, "God is the only one who is truly good."
  * **Kuna mmoja tu aliye mwema, na ni Mungu**  - Njia ingine ya kusema hii inaweza kuwa, "Mtu peke yake ambaye ni mzuri kwa uhakika ni Mungu" ama, "Mungu ndiye peke yake aliye mwema hakika."
