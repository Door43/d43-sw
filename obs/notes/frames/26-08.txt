===== Jesus Healed the Sick [26-08] Yesu Aliponya Wagonjwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Jesus went throughout the region of Galilee**, and large crowds came to him. They brought many people who were sick or handicapped, including those who could not see, walk, hear, or speak, and Jesus **healed** them.

Kisha **Yesu akaenda kila mahali Galilaya,** na umati mkubwa wa watu ukaja kwake. Walileta watu wengi waliokuwa wagonjwa au walemavu, ikiwemo wale hawawezi kuona, kutembea, kusikia ama kuongea, na Yesu **akawaponya**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **went throughout the region of Galilee**  - That is, "all around in Galilee" or, "from place to place in Galilee."
  * **alienda kila mahali kule sehemu ya Galilaya**  - Hiyo ni, "kila mahali kule Galilaya" au, "toka pahali moja hadi ingine kule Galilaya."
