The people wanted to make **Gideon** their **king**. Gideon **did not allow them to do this**, **but he asked them** for some of the gold rings that each of them had taken from the **Midianites**. The people gave Gideon a large amount of gold.

Watu walitaka kumfanya **Gideoni ** kuwa **mfalme** wao. Gideoni **hakuwaruhusu kufanya hivyo, lakini aliwauliza** baadhi ya pete za dhahabu ambazo kila mmoja wao alikuwa amechukua kutoka kwa **Wamidiani**. Watu walimpa Gideoni dhahabu nyingi mno.
