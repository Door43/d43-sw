===== Stephen Was Stoned [45-04] Stefano Alipigwa Mawe =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-04.jpg?nolink&}}

When the **religious leaders** heard this, they were so angry that they **covered their ears** and **yelled loudly**. They dragged Stephen out of the city and threw stones at him in order to kill him.

Wakati **viongozi wa dini** waliposikia hivi, walikasirika sana na **wakafunga masikio yao** na **wakapiga kelele kwa sauti kubwa**. Walimvuta Stefano nje ya mji na wakamrushia mawe ili wamuue.

===== Important Terms:(Maneno ya Muhimu) =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jewish-leaders|religious leaders]](viongozi wa dini**
===== Translation Notes:(Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **covered their ears**  - This could also be translated as, "They put their hands over their ears." This was to show that they did not want to hear what Stephen said.
  * **walifunga masikio yao**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "Waliweka mikono yao juu ya masikio yao." Hii ilikuwa kuonyesha ya kwamba hawakutaka kusiskia kile Stefano alisema.
  * **yelled loudly**  - They were yelling in anger. Translate this in a way that communicates that they were very upset.
  * **walipiga kelele kwa sauti kubwa**  - Walikuwa wakiguruma kwa hasira. Tafsiri hii kwa njia ambayo inawasiliana ya kwamba walikuwa wamekasirika sana.
