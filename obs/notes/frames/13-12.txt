===== The Golden Calf [13-12] Ndama wa dhahabu *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-12.jpg?nolink&}}

**Aaron made a golden idol** in the shape of a calf. The people began to **wildly worship** the idol and make **sacrifices** to it! **God** was very angry with them because of their **sin** and planned to destroy them. But **Moses prayed** for them, and God **listened to his prayer** and did not destroy them.

**Haruni alitengeneza sanamu ya dhahabu** kwa sura ya ndama. Watu wakaanza** kuiabudu bila mpangilio** ile sanamu na kuitolea **dhabihu**. **Mungu ** alikasirishwa nao kwa sababu ya **dhambi** na akapanga kuwaangamiza. Lakini **Musa akawaombea** na Mungu **akasikia maombi yake ** na hakuwangamiza.

==== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri ====

  * **made a golden idol**  - Aaron took the things made of gold that the people had brought to him, melted them, combined them, and formed them into the shape of a calf.
  * **Alitengeneza sanamu ya dhahabu**  - Haruni alichukua vitu zilizotengezwa na dhahabu alizoletewa, akaziyeyusha, akazichanganya na akatengeneza sura ya ndama.
  * **wildly worship**  - The people were sinning by worshiping the idol and also by doing sinful things as they worshiped it.
  * **kuabudu bila mpangilio - **  Watu walikua wakitenda dhambi kwa kuabudu sanamu na pia kwa kufanya mambo ya dhambi walipoabudu.
  * **listened to his prayer**  - God always hears prayer. In this situation, "listened" means that God agreed to do what Moses asked.
  * **Akasikia maombi yake**  - Mungu husikia maombi kila mara. katika hali hii,"alisikiliza" inamaanisha kwamba Mungu alikubali kile Musa alichomuuliza.
