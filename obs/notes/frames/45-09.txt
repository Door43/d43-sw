===== Philip Met the Ethiopian [45-09] Filipo Alikutana na Muethiopia =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Philip asked the Ethiopian, “Do you understand what you are reading?” The Ethiopian replied, “No. I cannot understand it unless someone explains it to me. Please come and sit next to me. **Was Isaiah writing about** himself or someone else?”

Filipo akamuuliza Muethiopia, "unaelewa kile ambacho unasoma?" Muethiopia akajibu, "Hapana. Siwezi kuielewa bila mtu kunielezea. Tafadhali njoo uketi kando yangu. **Isaya alikuwa anaandikia kuhusu** yeye mwenyewe ama mtu mwingine?
===== Important Terms: (Maneno ya Muhimu) =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:isaiah|Isaiah]](Isaya)**
===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **Was Isaiah writing about**  - This can also be translated as, "Was Isaiah referring to."
  * Isaya alikuwa anaandika kuhusu - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Isaya alikuwa anasema juu ya."
