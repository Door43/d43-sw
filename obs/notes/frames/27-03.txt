===== Who Is My Neighbor? [27-03] Jirani Yangu ni Nani? =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-03.jpg?nolink&}}

But the **law expert** wanted to prove that he was **righteous**, so he asked, “**Who is my neighbor**?”

Lakini yule **mtaalam wa sheria** alitaka kuthibitisha kuwa yeye ni **mwenye haki**, kwa hivyo akauliza, **"Jirani yangu ni nani?"**

  * **[[:sw:obe:kt:righteous]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **law expert**  - That is, "an expert in the Jewish law." See how you translated this term in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]**.
  * **mtaalamu wa sheria**  - Hiyo ni, "mtaalamu wa sheria ya Kiyahudi." Ona ulivyotafsiri hili neno pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:27-01|[27-01]]]**.
  * **Who is my neighbor?**  - This could also be translated as, "What do you mean by 'neighbor'?" or, "Which people are my neighbors?" He knew that he did not love everyone, and was asking which people he needed to love.
  * **Jirani yangu ni nani**? - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "unamanisha nini na 'jirani'?" ama, "watu gani ndiyo jirani zangu?" Alijua kwamba hampendi kila mtu, na alikuwa anauliza ni watu gani anahitajika kupenda.
