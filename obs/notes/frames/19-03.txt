===== God Provided for Elijah [19-03] - Mungu Alipeana kwa ajili ya Elia =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-03.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** told **Elijah** to go to a stream **in the wilderness** to hide from Ahab who wanted to kill him. Every morning and every evening, birds would bring him bread and meat. **Ahab** and his army looked for Elijah, but they could not find him. **The drought** was so severe that the stream eventually dried up.

**Mungu ** alimwambia **Elia ** aende kwa kijito **kilichokuwa jangwani** akajifiche kutoka kwa Ahabu ambaye alitaka kumuua. Kila asubuhi na kila jioni, ndege wangemletea mkate na nyama. **Ahabu ** na jeshi lake walimtafuta Elia, lakini hawangeweza kumpata. **Ukame ** ukawa mkali kiasi ya kuwa mwishowe kijito kilikauka.

==== Translation Notes:(Tafsiri ya maelezo) ====

  * **the wilderness**  - This was a remote location with very few people. This may also be translated as, "desert" or, "the bush."
  * **Jangwa **- Hii ilikuwa pahali ukiwa penye watu wachache sana. Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Ukiwa" ama "mwituni"
  * **The drought**  - This could be translated as, "the lack of rain." This drought was a result of Elijah declaring that there would be no rain.
  * **Ukame **- Hii inaweza tafsiriwa kama, "kukosa mvua." Huu ukame ulisababishwa na usemi wa Elia ya kwamba hakungekuwa na mvua.
