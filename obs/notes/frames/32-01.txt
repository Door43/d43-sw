===== The Country of the Gerasenes [32-01] Nchi ya Wagerasi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-01.jpg?nolink&}}

**One day**, **Jesus** and his **disciples** went in a boat across the lake to the region where **the Gerasene people** lived.

**Siku moja**, **Yesu** na **wanafunzi ** wake wakasafiri kwa mashua kwenda upande ule mwingine wa bahari sehemu **Wagerasi ** waliishi.

===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:disciple|disciples (wanafunzi)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **One day**  - This phrase introduces an event that happened in the past, but does not state the specific time. Many languages have a similar way to begin telling a true story.
  * **Siku moja**  - Huu msemo unatanguliza tukio lililofanyika siku zilizopita, lakini haisemi wakati haswa. Lugha nyingi zina njia sawa ya kuanzisha hadithi ya kweli.
  * **the Gerasene people**  - The Gerasenes lived in an area along the eastern shore of the Sea of Galilee. They are descendants of the Jews, but we know few details about them.
  * **Wagerasi **- Wagerasi waliishi eneo lililoko upande wa mashariki wa pwani ya bahari ya Galilaya. Walikuwa sehemu ya uzao wa Wayahudi, lakini hatujui mengi kuwahusu.
