===== God Provided for the Widow [19-04] - Mungu Alipeana Kwa ajili ya Mjane =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-04.jpg?nolink&}}

So **Elijah** went to a **neighboring country**. A widow and her son in that country had almost run out of food because of **the famine**. But they **took care of** Elijah, and **God provided for them so that their flour jar and their bottle of oil never became empty**. They had food through the whole famine. Elijah stayed there for several years.

Kwa hivyo, **Elia ** akaenda kwa **nchi jirani**. Chakula ya Mjane na mwanawe katika nchi ile ilikuwa karibu kuisha juu ya ule ukame. Lakini **wakamshughulikia ** Elia **na Mungu akapeana kwa ajili yao na ikawa chupa yao ya unga na chupa ya mafuta kamwe haikuwahi kuwa tupu.** Walikuwa na chakula wakati huo wote wa ukame. Elia alikaa pale kwa miaka kadhaa.

==== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) ====

  * **neighboring country**  - This refers to a country that was beside, or shared a border with Israel.
  * **Nchi Jirani**  - Hii inaonyesha nchi iliyokuwa kando, ama iliyopakana na Israeli.
  * **the famine**  - If needed, this could be translated as, "the famine caused by the drought."
  * **Njaa**  - Kama inahitajika, hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "njaa iliyosababishwa na ukame."
  * **took care of**  – This means they gave him a place to stay in their house and provided food for him. It does not mean he was sick.
  * **Kushughulikia**  - Hii inamaanisha walimpatia pahali pa kuishi nyumbani mwao  na wakampatia chakula. Haina maana kuwa alikuwa mgonjwa.
  * **God provided for them…never became empty**  - This could be translated as, "God prevented their flour jar and their bottle of oil from becoming empty" or, "God caused…to never become empty."
  * **Mungu alipeana kwa ajili yao… kamwe haikuwahi kuwa tupu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mungu alizuilia unga wao na chupa ya mafuta yao isiwahi kuisha ama, "Mungu alisababisha…. kamwe isiwe tupu."
  * **flour jar**  - This refers to a clay jar in which the widow kept her supply of flour.
  * **Chupa la Unga**  - Hii inaonyesha chupa ya udongo ile mjane alikuwa akiweka unga wake.
  * **bottle of oil**  - In Israel, olive oil is used for cooking. This could be translated as, "bottle of cooking oil." The widow used the flour and the oil for making bread.
  * **Chupa ya mafuta **- Katika Israeli, mafuta ya mizeituni inatumika kwa upishi. Hii inaweza tafsiriwa kama, "chupa ya mafuta ya kupikia."Huyu mjane alitumia unga na mafuta kutengeneza mkate
