===== The Wicked Servant [29-08] Mtumishi Mbaya =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-08.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **king called the servant** and said, ‘You wicked servant! I **forgave** your debt because you **begged me**. **You should have done the same**.’ The king was so angry that he **threw** the **wicked** servant into prison until he could pay back all of his debt.”

"**Mfalme alimuita yule mtumishi ** na kusema, 'Wewe mtumishi mbaya! **Nilikusamehe** deni yako kwa sababu **uliniomba**. **Ulipaswa kufanya hivyo hivyo.' ** Mfalme akawa na hasira kiasi kwamba **alimtupa** yule mtumishi **mbaya** gerezani mpaka wakati atamlipa deni yake yote."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **called the servant**  - That is, "commanded the servant to come to him" or, "ordered his guards to bring the servant to him."
  * **alimuita yule mtumishi **- Hiyo ni, "aliamuru mtumishu yule aje kwake" ama, "aliamuru walinzi wake wamlete yule mtumishi kwake."
  * **begged me**  - This could be translated as, "pleaded with me" or, "urgently asked me to be merciful."
  * **aliniomba **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "alinisihi mimi" ama, "upesi akaniuliza nione huruma."
  * **You should have done the same**  - That is, "You should have forgiven the man who owed you, just as I forgave you."
  * **ungefanya hivyo hivyo**  - Hiyo ni, "Ungemsamehe huyo mwanamme uliyekuwa unadai, kama tu vile nilikusamehe."
  * **threw**  - That is, "commanded his guards to put." Consider how "threw" was translated in frame **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:29-06|[29-06]]]**.
  * **alimtupa**  - Hiyo ni, "aliamuru walinzi wake wamuweke." Angalia vile "tupa" ilitafsiriwa pale **[29-06]. **
