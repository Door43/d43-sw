===== Wait for the Harvest [50-07] Yasubiri yale Mavuno =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-07.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **servants** responded to their **master**, ‘Should we pull out the weeds?’ The master said, ‘No. If you do that, **you will pull out some of the wheat as well**. Wait **until the harvest** and then gather the weeds into piles to be burned, but bring **the wheat** into my **barn**.’”

"**Watumishi** walimjibu **bwana** wao, 'Je, sisi tunaweza kung'oa magugu?' Bwana akasema, 'Hapana! Nyinyi mkifanya hivyo, **mtang'oa baadhi ya ngano pia. ** Subiri **hadi mavuno** na kisha ukusanye magugu ili zichomwe moto, lakini leta **ngano** **ghalani ** mwangu."`

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues to tell the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea kueleza ile hadithi.)
  * **you will pull out some of the wheat as well**  - That is, "you will accidentally pull out some of the wheat also." It would be too difficult to distinguish young wheat from the weeds, and to pull the weeds without uprooting the wheat.
  * **utang'oa baadhi ya ngano pia**  - Hiyo ni, "kwa ajali utang'oa baadhi ya ngano pia." Itakuwa vigumu sana kutofautisha mimea michanga ya ngano na magugu, na kuvuta Magugu bila kung'oa ngano.
  * **until the harvest**  - That is, "until the time when the wheat is ready to harvest" or, "until the wheat has grown enough to be harvested."
  * **mpaka wakati wa mavuno**  - Hiyo ni, "mpaka wakati ambapo ngano itakuwa tayari kuvunwa" ama, "mpaka wakati ngano imekua na kufikia kiwango cha kuvunwa."
  * **the wheat**  - That is, "the harvested wheat grains."
  * **ngano**  - Hiyo ni, "nafaka ya ngano iliyo vunwa."
  * **barn**  - This refers to the building where the harvested wheat grain was saved and stored. It could also be called a "storehouse."
  * **ghalani**  - Hii ina maana ya jengo ambapo nafaka ya ngano iliyo vunwa inahifadhiwa. Inaweza pia kuitwa "nyumba ya kuhifadhi."
