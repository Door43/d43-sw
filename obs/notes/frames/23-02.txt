===== Take Mary as Your Wife [23-02] - Mchukue Mariamu Kuwa Mke Wako =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-02.jpg?nolink&}}

The **angel** said, “**Joseph**, **do not be afraid to take Mary as your wife**. The baby that is in her **is from the Holy Spirit**. She will give birth to a **son**. Name him **Jesus** (which means, '**Yahweh saves**'), because he will save the people from their **sins**.”**Malaika ** alisema, "**Yusufu, usiogope kumchukua Mariamu kuwa mke wako**. Mtoto aliye ndani yake **ni kutokana kwa Roho Mtakatifu**. Yeye atamzaa **mwana wa kiume**. Jina lake **Yesu ** (Maana yake, '**Yahweh huokoa**'), kwa sababu ataokoa watu kutoka kwa **dhambi ** zao."

===== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri =====

  * **do not be afraid to take Mary as your wife**  - This can also be translated as, "Stop thinking that you shouldn't marry Mary" or, "Do not hesitate to have Mary as your wife."
  * **Usiogope kumchukua Mariamu kuwa mke wako**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Wacha kufikiria ya kuwa haupaswi kumuoa Mariamu" ama, "Usisite kuchukua Mariamu kuwa mke wako."
  * **is from the Holy Spirit**  - That is, "was conceived by a miracle of the Holy Spirit."
  * **ni kutokana kwa Roho Mtakatifu**  - Hio ni, "Mimba yake ilikuwa kwa muujiza wa Roho Mtakatifu."
