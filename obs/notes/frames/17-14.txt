===== Solomon Was Born [17-14] Suleimani alizaliwa *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-14.jpg?nolink&}}

But as **punishment** for **David's sin**, his baby boy died. There was also **fighting in David’s family** for the rest of his life, and **David’s power was greatly weakened**. Though David had been unfaithful to **God**, God was still **faithful** to his **promises**. Later, David and Bathsheba had another son, and they named him **Solomon**.

Lakini kama **adhabu ** kwa **dhambi ya Daudi**, mtoto wake wakiume alikufa. Pia kulikuwa na **mapigano katika nyumba ya Daudi** kwa sehemu ya maisha yake iliyobaki na **nguvu ya Daudi ilipungua sana**. Ingawa Daudi hakuwa mwaminifu kwa **Mungu**, Mungu bado alikuwa **muaminifu** kwa **ahadi** zake. Baadaye, Daudi na Bathsheba wakapata mtoto mwingine, na wakamuita **Suleimani**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **fighting in David's family**  - This "fighting" was very serious. Among other things, one of his sons murdered another son and tried to take David's place as king while David was still reigning. If possible, use a word that conveys the intensity of this conflict within the family.
  * **vita katika familia ya Daudi**  - Haya "mapigano" yalikuwa makali sana. Miongoni mwa mambo mengine, mmoja wa wanawe akamuua mwanawe mwingine na akajaribu kuchukua nafasi ya Daudi bado akiwa anatawala. Ikiwezekana, tumia neno ambalo litaonyesha kiwango cha mgogoro huo katika familia ya Daudi.
  * **David's power was greatly weakened**  - This might be translated as, "David became less powerful" or, "David lost much of his authority."
  * **nguvu ya Daudi ilipungua sana**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Daudi akawa mdhaifu" ama, "Daudi alipoteza mengi ya mamlaka yake."
