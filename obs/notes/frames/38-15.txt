===== Peter Fought for Jesus [38-15] Petero Alipigana kwa Ajili ya Yesu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-15.jpg?nolink&}}

**As the soldiers arrested Jesus**, **Peter pulled out his sword** and cut off the ear of a **servant** of the **high priest**. Jesus said, “Put the sword away! I could ask the **Father** for an army of **angels** to defend me. But I **must obey my Father**.” Then Jesus **healed** the man’s ear. After Jesus was arrested, all the **disciples** ran away.

**Askari walipokuwa wanamkamata Yesu, Petero alitoa upanga wake**, akalikata sikio la **mtumishi** wa **Kuhani Mkuu**. Yesu akasema, "Weka upanga wako mbali! Naweza kuitisha **Baba** jeshi la **malaika** kunilinda mimi. Lakini ni lazima **ni****tii**** Baba yangu**." Kisha Yesu **akaponya** sikio la yule mtu. Baada ya Yesu kukamatwa, **wanafunzi** wote walitoroka.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As the soldiers arrested Jesus**  - That is, "While the soldiers were arresting Jesus."
  * **Askari walipokuwa wanamkamata Yesu**  - Hiyo ni, "Wakati askari walikuwa wakimkamata Yesu."
  * **pulled out his sword**  - That is, "pulled out his sword from the place where he was carrying it."
  * **akatoa upanga wake**  - Hiyo ni, "kutoa upanga wake kutoka mahali alipokuwa anaibebea."
  * **I must obey my Father**  - That is, "I won't do that because I must obey my Father's will and allow myself to be arrested."
  * **Mimi lazima nimtii Baba yangu**  - Hiyo ni, "Mimi sitafanya hivyo kwa sababu ni lazima nitii mapenzi ya Baba yangu na kuruhusu nikamatwe."
