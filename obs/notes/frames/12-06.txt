===== Darkness over the Egyptians [12-06] Ngiza juu ya Wamisri *F =====

Then **God** moved the **pillar of cloud** and placed it between the **Israelites** and the **Egyptians** so the Egyptians **could not see** the Israelites.

Kisha **Mungu** akalisongeza **lile nguzo la wingu** na akaiweka katikati ya **Waisraeli** na Wamisri kwa hivyo Wamisri **hawangeweza kuona** Waisraeli.
