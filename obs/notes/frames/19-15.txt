===== Naaman Was Healed [19-15] Namani aliponywa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-15.jpg?nolink&}}

At first **Naaman** was angry and **would not do it because it seemed foolish**. But later **he changed his mind** and dipped himself seven times in the **Jordan River**. When he came up the last time, his skin was completely **healed**! **God** had healed him.

Kwanza **Namani ** alikuwa na hasira na **hangefanya hivyo maana lionekana jambo la upuzi**. Lakini baadaye **alibadilisha mawazo yake** na akajitumbukiza mara saba kwa **Mto wa Yordani**. Alipotoka kwa maji mara ya mwisho, ngozi yake ilukuwa **imepona** kabisa! **Mungu** alikuwa amemponya.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **would not do it because it seemed foolish**  - Naaman would not do what Elisha said because he knew that washing alone could not heal his disease.
  * **hangefanya hivyo maana lilionekana jambo la upuzi **- Namani hangeweza kufanya kile Elisha alisema kwa sababu alijua kuwa kuoga peke yake haingeweza kuponya ugonjwa.
  * **he changed his mind**  - That is, "he decided to do what Elisha had told him to do."
  * **alibadilisha mawazo yake**  - Hiyo ni, "aliamua kufanya kile Elisha alimuambia afanye,"
