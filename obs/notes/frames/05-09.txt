===== The Ram [05-09] Kondoo wa kiume =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Nearby **Abraham** saw **a ram** that was stuck in a bush. **God had provided the ram** to be the **sacrifice** instead of **Isaac**. Abraham happily offered the ram as a sacrifice.

Hapo karibu Ibrahimu akamuona kondoo wa kiume amekwama katika kichaka. Mungu alikuwa amepeana kondoo awe dhabihu badala ya Isaka. Ibrahimu kwa furaha akamtoa kondoo kama dhabihu.

===== Important Terms: Maelezo Ya Muhimu =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:abraham|Abraham]] - Ibrahimu**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God]] - Mungu**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:sacrifice|sacrifice]] - Dhabihu**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:isaac|Isaac]] - Isaka**
===== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri =====

  * **a ram**  - This is a male sheep. sheep were one of the animals that people could sacrifice to God.
  * **Kondoo wa kiume**  - Huyu ni kondoo wa kiume. Kondoo walikuwa wanyama waliotumiwa na watu kutoa dhabihu kwa Mungu.
  * **God had provided the ram**  - Just at the right moment, God caused the ram to be stuck in the bush.
  * **Mungu alimpeana kondoo wa kiume**  - kwa wakati unaofaa, Mungu alisababisha kondoo kukwama katika kichaka.
