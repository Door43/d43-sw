===== The Birth of Moses [09-05] Kuzaliwa kwa Musa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-05.jpg?nolink&}}

A certain **Israelite** woman gave birth to a baby boy. She and her husband hid the baby **for as long as they could**.

Mwanamke mmoja **Muisraeli** akazaa mtoto mvulana.  Yeye na mume wake wakamficha yule mvulana **kwa wakati ule wote wangeweza**.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:israel|Israelite (Muisraeli)]]**
==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **as long as they could**  - They hid the baby from the Egyptians until it became too difficult to keep him safely hidden in their home.
  * **kwa ule wote wangeweza**  - Walificha yule mtoto kutoka kwa Wamisri mpaka ikawa ngumu kumficha tena na kuhakikisha yuko salama nyumbani kwao.
