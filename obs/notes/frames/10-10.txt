===== The Locusts [10-10] Nzige *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-10.jpg?nolink&}}

So **God** caused **swarms of locusts** to come over **Egypt**. These **locusts** ate all the crops that the **hail** had not destroyed.

Kwa hivyo **Mungu** akafanya **makundi ya nzige** zije **Misri**. Hizi **nzige** zikala mimea yote ambayo haikuwa imeharibiwa na **mvua ya mawe**.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God (Mungu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:egypt|Egypt (Misri)]]**
==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya tafsiri): ====

  * **swarms of locusts**  - Locusts are grasshoppers that fly together in swarms, or large groups, and that can destroy large areas of all kinds of plants and food crops by eating them.
  * **makundi ya nzige**  - Nzige ni panzi zinazoruka pamoja katika vikundi, ama vikundi vikubwa, na ziko na uwezo wa kuharibu eneo kubwa la kila aina ya mimea pamoja na mimea ya chakula kwa kuikula.
  * **hail**  - Hail is like chunks of ice that fall down from the clouds as rain does.
  * **mvua ya mawe**  - Mvua ya mawe ni kama vipande za theluji zinazoanguka kutoka angani kama ianvyofanya mvua.
