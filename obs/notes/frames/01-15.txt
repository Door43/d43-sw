===== Fill the Earth [01-15] Ijaze Dunia *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-15.jpg?nolink&}}

**God made** man and woman **in his own image**. He **blessed** them and told them, “Have many children and grandchildren and fill the earth!” And God saw that everything he had made was **very good**, and he was very pleased with all of it. This all happened on the sixth day of **creation**.

**Mungu alimfanya** mwanaume na mwanamke kwa mfano wake mwenyewe. **Akawabariki ** na akawambia, "iweni na watoto na wajukuu wengi na mkaijaze dunia! Na Mungu akaona kila kitu alichokitengeneza kilikuwa **kizuri sana**, na akaifurahia yote. Hii yote ilifanyika siku ya sita ya **uumbaji. **

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **God made**  - God formed the man and the woman in a very personal way.
  * **Mungu alifanya **- Mungu aliwafanya mwanamme na mwanamke yeye binafsu.
  * **in his own image**  - An image is a physical representation of someone or something. God made people to display or represent some of his qualities and traits, but not to be equal to him.
  * **Kwa sura Yake menyewe**  - Sura ndio inaakilisha mtu ama kitu. Mungu alimuumba mwanadamu ili akaonyeshe ama kuwakilisha sifa zake lakini si mwanadamu awe sawa na yeye.
  * **very good**  - More intense than the, "it was good" statements on previous days. "Very good" refers to all of creation, not just the man and the woman. Everything was exactly as God intended it to be.
  * **Nzuri sana**  - hii inatilia mkazo kuliko msemo ule wa mbeleni, "ni nzuri" katika siku silizopita. Msemo huu "Ni nzuri sana" inahusu vyote vilivoumbwa na si tu mwanamme na mwanamke. Kila kitu kilotekea kama alivyokusudia Mungu.
  * **creation period**  - The six-day period during which God made everything that exists.
  * **Siku za uumbaji**  - Muda wa siku sita Mungu alivyoviumba vitu vyote vilivyomo.
