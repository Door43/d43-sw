===== The Levite [27-07] Mlawi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-07.jpg?nolink&}}

“Not long after that, a Levite **came down the road**. (**Levites were a tribe of Jews** who helped the **priests** at the Temple.) The Levite also crossed over to the other side of the road and **ignored** the man who had been robbed and beaten.”

Baada ya muda mfupi, Mlawi **akawa anateremka katika ile barabara**. (**Walawi lilikuwa kabila la Wayahudi ** ambalo lilisaidia **makuhani** katika hekalu). Yule Mlawi pia akavuka pande ile ingine ya barabara na **akapuuza ** yule mtu ambaye alikuwa ameimbiwa na kupigwa.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues to tell the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea kueleza ile mfano.)
  * **came down the road**  - That is, "was traveling down that same road."
  * **aliteremka katika ile barabara**  - Hiyo ni, "alikuwa anasafiri na kuteremka katika barabara hio hio."
  * **Levites were a tribe of Jews**  - That is, "Levites were from the Israelite tribe of Levi" or, "Levites were from the Israelite clan of Levi."
  * **Walawi walikuwa kabila la Wayahudi**  - Hiyo ni, "Walawi walikuwa wametoka kwa kabila la Levi" ama, "Walawi walikuwa wa kutoka kwa ukoo wa Israeli wa Walawi."
  * **ignored**  - That is, "did not help."
  * **alipuuza**  - Hiyo ni, "hakumsuaidia."
