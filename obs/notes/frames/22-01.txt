===== God Spoke to Zechariah [22-01] Mungu Alinena na Zakaria =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-01.jpg?nolink&}}

In the past, **God** had spoken to **his people** through his **angels** and **prophets**. But then **400 years went by when he did not speak to them**. Suddenly an angel came with a message from God to an old **priest** named **Zechariah**. Zechariah and his wife, Elizabeth, were **godly people**, but she had not been able to have any children.

Katika siku zilizopita, **Mungu** alinena na **watu wake** kupitia kwa **malaika** na **manabii**. Lakini sasa **miaka 400 ikapita bila yeye kuongea nao**. Ghafla malaika akaja na ujumbe kutoka kwa Mungu kwa **kuhani aliyezeeka** anayeitwa **Zakaria**. Zakaria na mke wake, Elisabeti, walikuwa wacha Mungu, lakini hakuwa ameweza kupata mtoto yeyote.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **his people**  - This could be translated as, "his people, the Israelites" or "his people, the Jews." But only include the added information if it is not clearly understood who these people were.
  * **watu wake**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "watu wake, Waisraeli" ama "watu wake, Wayahudi." Lakini ongezea habari zaidi kama haieleweki vizuri hawa watu walikuwa akina nani."
  * **400 years went by**  - Another way to say this would be, "400 years passed" or "there were 400 years." 400 years had passed since the last Old Testament prophet, Malachi.
  * **Miaka 400 ilipita**  - Njia ingine ya kusema hii inaweza kuwa, "miaka 400 ilipita" ama, "kulikuwa na miaka 400." Miaka 400 ilikuwa imepita kutoka wakati wa nabii wa mwisho wa Agano la Kale, Malaki.
  * **when he did not speak to them**  - That is, "during which God did not give any messages to the prophets for his people."
  * **wakati hakunena nao**  - Hiyo ni, "wakati ambao Mungu hakuleta ujumbe wowote kwa manabii kwa niaba ya watu wake."
  * **godly people**  - That is, "people who obeyed God."
  * **watu wacha Mungu**  - Hiyo ni, "watu ambao walimtii Mungu."
