===== Joseph in Egypt [08-04] Yosefu akiwa Misri *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-04.jpg?nolink&}}

The **slave** traders took **Joseph** to **Egypt**. Egypt was a large, powerful country located along the **Nile River**. The slave traders sold Joseph as a slave to a wealthy **government official**. Joseph served his master well, and **God blessed** Joseph.

Wale wafanya biashara na **watumwa**, wakampeleka **Yosefu Misri**. Misri ilikuwa nchi kubwa na yenye nguvu kando na **mto wa Nile**. Wafanya biashara na watumwa wakauza Yosefu kama mtumwa kwa **afisa** tajiri **wa serikali.** Yosefu akamutumikia bwana wake vizuri, na **Mungu akabariki ** Yosefu.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **government official**  - This was a person who was part of the Egyptian government. Another way to say this would be, "a leader in Egypt's government."
  * **afisa wa serikali **- Huyu mutu alikuwa sehemu ya Serikali ya Misri. Njia ingine ya kusema hii yaweza kuwa, "kiongozi katika Serikali ya Misri.
