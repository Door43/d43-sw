===== God Created Sky [01-03] Mungu aliumba anga  *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-03.jpg?nolink&}}

On **the second day** of creation, **God spoke** and **created** the **sky** above the earth. He made the sky by separating the water above from the water below.

**Siku ya pili ** ya uumbaji, **mungu akanena ** na **akaiumba** **anga** juu ya dunia. Akaiumba anga kwa kutenganisha maji ya juu na maji ya chini.
===== Important Terms (Maneno Muhimu) =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God (Mungu)]] **

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **the second day**  - God's work of creation was orderly, purposeful, and sequential. The things he created each day built upon and depended on the acts of the previous days.
  * **siku ya pili **- Kazi ya Mungu ya uumbaji ilikuwa ya mpangilio, lengo, na ya kufuatanisha hatua. Vitu alivvyo viumba kila siku vilitegemea vile alivyo kuwa ameumba siku iliyotangulia.
  * **God spoke**  - God created the sky by speaking a command.
  * **Mungu alinena **- Mungu aliumba anga kwa kutamka amri.
  * **created**  - God made the sky out of nothing.
  * **Akaumba **- Mungu aliumba anga kutoka kwa hali ya ukiwa.
  * **sky**  - This term refers to all of the space above the earth, including the air we breathe and the heavens.
  * **anga **- Neno hili linasimamia nafasi yote juu ya dunia, pamoja na hewa tunayo ipumua na mbingu.
