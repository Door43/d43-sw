===== The Soldier's Testimony [40-08] Ushuhuda wa Askari =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-08.jpg?nolink&}}

**Through his death**, **Jesus opened a way** for people to **come to God**. When the soldier guarding Jesus saw everything that had happened, he said, “Certainly, this man was **innocent**. He was the **Son of God**.”

**Kupitia kwa kifo chake, Yesu ilifungua njia** wa watu **kufika kwa Mungu**. Wakati askari aliyekuwa anamlinda Yesu aliona yote yaliyotokea, akasema, "Hakika, huyu alikuwa mtu ambaye **hana hatia**. Alikuwa **Mwana wa Mungu.**"

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Through his death**  - That is, "By means of his death" or, "By dying."
  * **Kupitia kwa kifo chake**  - Hiyo ni, "Kupitia kwa kifo chake" ama, "Kwa kufa."
  * **opened a way**  - That is, "made it possible."
  * **kufungua njia**  - Hiyo ni, "alifanya hiyo iwezekana."
  * **come to God**  - That is, "come near to God" or, "go near to God" or, "approach God" or, "know God personally." The tearing of the curtain showed that the barrier between God and people had been removed.
  * **kuja kwa Mungu**  - Hiyo ni, "kumkaribia Mungu" ama, "kwenda karibu na Mungu" ama, "kumwendea Mungu" ama, "kumujua Mungu kibinafsi." Kupasuka kwa pazia ilionyesha kuwa kizuizi kati ya Mungu na watu kilikuwa kimeondolewa."
