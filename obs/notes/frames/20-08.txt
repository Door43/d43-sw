==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **To punish**  - Nebuchadnezzar punished the king of Judah by telling his soldiers to do these things.
  * **kuadhibu**  - Nebukadreza aliadhibu mfalme wa Yuda kwa kuwambia askari wake kufanya mambo haya.
  * **in front of him**  - This may be translated as, "where he could see," or "so that he could see it," or "before his eyes."
  * **mbele yake**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "mahali angeona," ama "ili akaweze kuiona," ama "mbele ya macho yake."
  * **made him blind**  - This may also be translated as, "destroyed his eyes."
  * **walimfanya kipofu**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "waliharibu macho yake."
