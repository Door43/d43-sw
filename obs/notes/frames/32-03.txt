===== Breaking the Chains [32-03] Kuvunja Minyororo =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-03.jpg?nolink&}}

This man was so strong that nobody could control him. People had even **fastened** his arms and legs with chains, but he **kept breaking them**.

Huyu mtu alikuwa na nguvu kiasi kuwa hakuna mtu angeweza kumthibiti. Watu hata walikuwa **wanafunga ** mikono na miguu yake na nyororo, **lakini alikuwa anazikata. **

===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **fastened his arms and legs**  - That is, "restrained" or, "bound."
  * **walifunga mikono na miguu yake**  - Hiyo ni, "walimuzuia" ama, "walimfunga."
  * **kept breaking them**  - Some languages may need to say, "kept breaking the chains."
  * **alikaa ana zikata**  - Lugha zingine zitahitaji kusema, "alikuwa anakata nyororo kila wakati."
