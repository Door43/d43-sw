===== The King Heard [29-07] Mfalme Alisikia =====

“Some other **servants** saw **what had happened** and **were greatly disturbed**. They went to the **king** and told him **everything**.”

"Wengine wa **watumishi** waliona **kile kilifanyika** na **wakasumbuliwa sana**. Walienda kwa **mfalme** na kumuambia **kila kitu.**"

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **what had happened**  - That is, "that the servant had refused to cancel the other servant's debt and had put him into prison."
  * **kile kilifanyika**  - Hiyo ni, "Kuwa yule mtumishi alikataa kufutilia mbali deni aliyo kuwa anadai yule mtumishi mwingine na akamuweka gerezani."
  * **were greatly disturbed**  - That is, "deeply grieved" or, "very distressed."
  * **walisumbuliwa****sana**  - Hiyo ni, "kuwa na huzuni kubwa" ama, "wakawa na shinda sana."
  * **everything**–That is, "they told the king what the servant had done to his fellow servant."
  * **kila kitu**  - Hiyo ni, "walimuambia mfalme kile yule mtumishi alifanya na mtumishi mwenzake."
