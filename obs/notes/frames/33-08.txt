===== The Desire for Riches [33-08] Hamu ya Utajiri =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-08.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **thorny ground is a person** who hears **God’s word**, but as time passes, the **cares**, **riches**, and **pleasures of life choke out** his **love** for **God**. As a result, the teaching he heard **does not produce fruit**.”

"Ile **ardhi iliyo na miti yenye miiba ni mtu** ambaye anasikia **neno la Mungu**, lakini muda unapopita, **shuguli, utajiri,** na **raha za maisha husonga upendo** wake kwa **Mungu.** Kitokana na hiyo, mafundishi aliyopata **haizai matunda."**

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:love|love (upendo)]]**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues to explain the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na kuelezea ile hadithi.)
  * **thorny ground**  - See how you translated this term in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:33-04|[33-04]]]**.
  *
  * **is a person**  - See how you translated this comparison in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:33-06|[33-06]]]**.
  *
  * **cares**  - That is, "concerns" or, "needs" or, "problems."
  * **shuguli **- Hiyo ni, "wasi wasi" ama, "mahitaji" ama, "shinda."
  * **riches**  - That is, "desire for wealth."
  * **utajiri -**  Hiyo ni, "hamu ya utajiri."
  * **pleasures of life**  - This could also be translated as, "doing things to get pleasure" or, "focusing on things that give pleasure."
  * **raha za maisha**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kufanya mambo ili upate furaha" ama, "kulekeza nguvu kwa mambo yanayokupa furaha."
  * **choke out**  - That is, "squelch" or, "destroy" or, "overcome." This phrase could also be translated as, "cause him to stop loving God."
  * **husonga**  - Hiyo ni, "finya" ama, "haribu" ama, "shinda." Hii msemo inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kumfanya aache kumpenda Mungu."
  * **does not produce fruit**  - That is, "does not produce spiritual fruit" or "does not behave in a way that shows God's Spirit is working in him."
  * **haizai matunda**  - Hiyo ni, "haizai matunda ya kiroho" ama, "hatendi kwa njia inaoonyesha kuwa Roho wa Mungu anafanya kazi ndani yake."
