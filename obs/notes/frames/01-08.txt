===== God Created the Animals [01-08] Mungu aliumba Wanyama =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-08.jpg?nolink&}}

On **the sixth day** of creation, **God said**, “Let there be **all kinds** of **land animals**!” And it happened just like God said. Some were **farm animals**, some **crawled on the ground**, and some were **wild**. And God saw that **it was good**.

Katika **siku ya sita** ya uumbaji, **Mungu akasema**, "Na kuwe na **kila aina** ya **wanyama wa nchi kavu**!" Na ikawa kama alivyonena Mungu. Wengine wa kawa **wanyama wa kufugwa,** wengine wa **wanaotambaa kwa ardhi**, wengine** wanyama wa porini.** Na Mungu akaona ni nzuri.
===== Important Terms (Maneno muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:good|good]]**
==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **the sixth day**  - The next event in a continuing, orderly progression of days and creative acts.
  * **Siku ya sita**  - Matokeo ya siku iliofuata inaonyesha mwendo taratibu wa masiku na kazi ya uumbaji.
  * **God said**  - God's spoken word is the means by which the animals were created.
  * **Mungu akasema**  - Neno la Mungu lililonenwa ndio njia iliyotumika kuuamba wanyama.
  * **all kinds**  - This indicates a great variety, but also order.
  * **Aina zote**  - Hii inaonyesha aina tofauti tofauti, na pia mpangilio.
  * **land animals**  - Every kind of animal that lived on the land, as opposed to the birds, or animals that live in the seas.
  * **Wanyama wa ardhini**. Kila aina ya mnyama anayeishi katika ardhi, kinyume na ndege ama wanyama wanaoishi baharini.
  * **farm animals**  - The kinds of land animals that commonly live peacefully with people–-as in tame, or domesticated animals.
  * **Wanyama wa Mifugo**  - Hawa ni wanyama wa ardinini wanaishi kwa amani na wanadamu– ni wanyama wanowekwa ndani ya nyumba.
  * **crawled on the ground**  - This probably includes reptiles, and possibly the insects.
  * **Kitabaacho juu ya nchi**  - hii inajumulisha wanyama kama reptilia na wadudu.
  * **wild**  - The kinds of animals that do not normally live peacefully with people, usually because they are afraid of people, or dangerous to them.
  * **Wanyama pori**- hii ni aina ya wanyama wasioshi kwa amani na wanadamu, sababu wanaogopa watu na tena ni hatari kwao.
  * **it was good**  - This phrase is repeated throughout creation to show that every step went exactly according to God's wise plan and purpose.
  * **Ni nzuri **- haya maneno yamerudiwa kwa hadithi ya uumbaji na inaonyesha kila hatua ilienda kulingana na mpango na kusudi la Mungu mwenya hekima.
