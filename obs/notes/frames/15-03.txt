===== Marching around Jericho [15-03] Kuandamana kuzunguka Yericho *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-03.jpg?nolink&}}

**After the people crossed the Jordan River**, **God** told **Joshua how to attack** the powerful city of **Jericho**. The people **obeyed** God. Just as God told them to do, the soldiers and **priests** marched around the city of Jericho **once a day for six days**.

**Baada ya watu kuvuka Mto wa Yordani**, **Mungu** alimwambia **Yoshua jinsi ya kuvamia** mji mwenye nguvu wa **Yericho**. Watu **walimutii** Mungu. Kama vile Mungu aliwaambia wafanye, wale askari na **makuhani** wakaandamana wakizunguka mji wa Yericho **mara moja kila siku, kwa siku sita.**

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **After the people crossed the Jordan River**  – In some languages it is better to say, "The people crossed the Jordan River and then…"
  * **baada ya watu kuvuka Mto wa Yordani**  - Kwa lugha zingine ni bora zaidi kusema, "watu walivuka Mto wa Yordani na…"
  * **how to attack**  – This could be translated as, "exactly what he should do to attack."
  * **jinsi ya kuvamia**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "haswa kile anastahili kufanya ili kuvamia."
  * **once a day for six days**  - That is, they went around the city one time every day for a total of six days.
  * **mara moja kila siku kwa siku sita**  - Hiyo ni, walizunguka mji mara moja kila siku kwa jumla ya siku sita.
