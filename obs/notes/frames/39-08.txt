===== Judas Killed Himself [39-08] Yuda Alijiua =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-08.jpg?nolink&}}

**Peter** went away and **cried bitterly**. Meanwhile, **Judas**, **the betrayer**, saw that the **Jewish leaders had condemned Jesus to die**. Judas became full of sorrow and went away and killed himself.

**Petro** alienda mbali na **akalia kwa uchungu**. Wakati huo huo, **Yuda, msaliti**, aliona kwamba **viongozi wa Wayahudi wamemuhukumu Yesu kufa**. Yuda akiwa amejawa na huzuni, akaondoka na akajiuua.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **cried bitterly**  - That is, "cried, feeling deep sorrow" or, "cried, feeling deep regret."
  * **akalia kwa uchungu**  - Hiyo ni, "akalia, akihisi huzuni ya kina" ama, "akalia, akihisi majuto ya kina."
  * **the betrayer**  - That is, "who had betrayed Jesus" or, "who had helped the leaders arrest Jesus."
  * **msaliti**  - Hiyo ni, "ambaye alimsaliti Yesu" ama, "ambaye alisaidia viongozi kumkamata Yesu."
  * **had condemned Jesus to die**  - That is, "had said that Jesus was guilty and must die."
  * **walimuhukumu Yesu kufa**  - Hiyo ni "walisema kwamba Yesu yuko na hatia na ni lazima afe."
