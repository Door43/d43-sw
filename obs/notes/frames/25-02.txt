===== The First Temptation [25-02] Jaribio la Kwanza =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**Satan tempted Jesus** by saying, “If you are the **Son of God**, **turn these rocks into bread** so you can eat!”

**Shetani alimjaribu Yesu** kwa kusema, "Kama wewe ni **Mwana wa Mungu, badilisha mawe haya yawe mkate** ili ukaweze kula!"

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **turn these rocks into bread**  - This may also be translated as, "make these rocks become bread" or, "supernaturally change these rocks into bread."
  * **badilisha mawe haya yawe mkate**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "fanya mawe haya yawe mkate" ama, "kimuujiza badilisha haya mawe yawe mkate."
  * **bread**  - This could be translated as, "food" for languages where bread is not a common food. In the Jews' culture, bread was the main food.
  * **mkate**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "chakula" katika lugha ambazo mkate si chakula ya kila siku. Katika desturi ya Wayahudi, mkate ndiyo ilikuwa chakula kikuu.
