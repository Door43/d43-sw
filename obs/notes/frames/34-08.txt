===== I Am Good [34-08] Mimi ni Mwema =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-08.jpg?nolink&}}

“‘For example, I **fast** two times every week and I give you **ten percent** of all the money and goods that I receive.’”

"Kwa mfano, **ninafunga kukula chakula** mara mbili kila wiki na ninakupa **fungu** lako la **kumi** ya fedha na mali yote ninayo ipokea."
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:fast|fast (kufunga kukula chakula)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues to tell of the religious leader's prayer.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea kueleza kuhusu maombi ya yule kiongozi wa kindini.)
  * **I fast**  - The religious ruler believed that doing this would earn favor with God.
  * **ninafunga kukula chakula**  - Yule mtawala wa kindini aliamini kuwa kufanya hivyo inampa kibali mbele ya Mungu.
  * **ten percent**  - That is, "one-tenth."
  * **fungu la kumu**  - Hiyo ni. "sehemu moja katika kumi."
