===== Rebekah Was Childless [06-04] Rebeka hakuwa na Mtoto =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-04.jpg?nolink&}}

After a long time, **Abraham** died and all of **the promises that God had made to him in the covenant were passed on** to **Isaac**. God had promised that Abraham would have **countless descendants**, but Isaac's wife, **Rebekah**, could not have children.

Baada ya muda mrefu, **Ibrahimu ** akafa na **ahadi zote zile Mungu alikuwa amefanya naye katika agano zikapitishwa** kwa **Isaka**. Mungu alikuwa ameahidi kwamba Ibrahimu atakuwa na **uzao usiohesabika**, lakini mke wa Isaka, **Rebeka**, hangepata watoto.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **the promises â€¦ were passed on**  - The promises of the covenant that God made with Abraham were not for him only, but also for his descendants.
  * **ahadi...zikapitishwa**  - Zile ahadi  za agano aliezifanya Mungu na Ibrahimu hazikuwa zake peke yake, lakini pia za uzao wake.
  * **countless**  - This could also be translated as, "very many." The word "countless" means there would be so many descendants that people would not be able to count them.
  * **wasiohesabika**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "wengi sana." Neno "wasiohesabika" inamaanisha uzao utakuwa mwingi kiasi kuwa watu hataweza kuwahesabu.
