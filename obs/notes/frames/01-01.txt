===== The Beginning [01-01] Mwanzo B =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-01.jpg?nolink&}}

This is how **the beginning** of everything happened. God **created** the **universe** and everything in it in six days. After God created the earth it was **dark** and **empty**, and **nothing had been formed** in it. But **God's Spirit** was there over the water.

Hivi ndivyo jinsi **mwanzo ** wa vitu vyote ulifanyika. Mungu **aliumba ulimwengu ** na vitu vyote ulimwenguni kwa siku sita. Baada ya Mungu kuumba dunia ilikuwa na **giza ** na ilikuwa **tupu, ** na **hakuna kilicho kuwa kimeumbwa ** ndani yake, lakini **Roho wa Mungu ** alitanda juu ya maji.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **the beginning**  - That is, "the beginning of all things," before anything existed except God.
  * **mwanzo **- Hiyo ni, "mwanzo wa vitu vyote," kabla ya kuweko kwa chochote ila Mungu.
  * **created**  - This is used here in the sense of making something out of nothing.
  * **aliumba **- Hili limetumiwa hapa kwa maana ya kuumba kitu bila kitu chochote.
  * **universe**  - This includes everything that God created on the earth and in the heavens, both visible and invisible things.
  * **ulimwengu **- Hivi vitu vyote mungu alivyoviumba duniani na mbinguni, vinavyoonekana na visivyoonekana.
  * **dark**  - It was completely dark. There was no light at all, since God had not yet created light.
  * **giza **- Kulikuwa na giza. Nuru haikuweko, kwani Mungu hakuwa ameiumba nuru.
  * **empty**  - God had not yet created anything except for the bare earth covered by water.
  * **tupu **- Mungu bado hakuwa ameumba chochote isipokuwa dunia tupu iliokuwa imefunikwa kwa maji.
  * **nothing had been formed**  - There were no distinguishing featuresâ€“just water covering everything.
  * **hakuna kilicho kuwa kimeumbwa**  - Hakukuwa na sifa za kubainisha- maji yalifunika kila kitu.
  * **God's Spirit**  - The Spirit of God, sometimes called the Holy Spirit, was present in the beginning, moving freely over the earth in order to create all that he had planned to do.
  * **Roho wa Mungu**- Roho wa Mungu, wakati mwingine huitwa Roho Mtakatifu, alikuweko kutoka mwanzo, akitandaa juu ya dunia ili aumbe yote aliokuwa amepanga kufanya.
