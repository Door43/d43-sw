===== The People Were Angry [47-06] Watu Walikasirika =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-47-06.jpg?nolink&}}

The men who owned the **slave girl** became very angry! They realized that **without the demon**, the slave girl could not tell people **the future**. **This meant that** people would no longer pay her owners money so that she would tell them what would happen to them.

Watu wale waliomliki yule **msichana mtumwa** wakawa na hasira sana! Walitambua ya kwamba **bila ile pepo**, yule msichana mtumwa hangeweza kuwaambia watu **mambo ya siku sijazo**. **Hii ilimaanisha ya kwamba** watu hawangeweza tena lipa pesa kwa wamiliki wake ndio awaambie mambo itayofanyika kwao.
===== Important Terms: (Maneno ya Muhimu) =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:servant|slave girl]](msichana mtumwa)**
===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **without the demon**  - That is, "without the power from the demon" or, "now that she did not have the demon."
  * **bila ile pepo**  - Hio ni, "bila nguvu kutoka kwa ile pepo" au, "sasa vile hakuwa na ile pepo."
  * **the future**  - That is, "what would happen to them in the future."
  * **siku ****sijazo**  - Hio ni, "kile kingefanyika kwa wao katika siku sijazo."
  * **This meant that**  - This could also be translated as, "Because of this" or, "Therefore."
  * **Hii ilimaanisha ya kuwa**  - Hii ingeweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, " Kwa sababu hii" au "kwa sababu hiyo."
