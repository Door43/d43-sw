===== God Made Man in His Image [01-09] Mungu alimuumba mwanadamu kwa mfano wake *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **God** said, “**Let us** **make** human beings **in our image** to be **like us**. They will have **authority** over the earth and all the animals.”

Kisha **Mungu** akasema, "**Natuwaumbe** wanadamu **kwa mfano wetu**, **wafanane nasi**. Watakuwa na **mamlaka** juu ya dunia na wanyama wote."
===== Important Terms: Maneno Muhimu =====

  * **[[:sw:obe:kt:god]]**





==== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya tafsiri ====

  * **Let us**  - This indicates God's deliberate, willful decision to create man in a particular way for a particular purpose. You could translate this as, "We will make."
  * **Na tufanye **- Hi inaonyesha makusudi ya mungu kuamua kwa mapenzi yake kumuumba mwanadamu kwa njia fulani na kusudi fulani. Unaweza kutafsiri hii kama " Tutatengeneza."
  * **us…our…us**  - The Bible teaches that there is one God, but the Old Testament word "God" is a plural form, and God uses plural pronouns when speaking to himself. Some understand this as a special way of speaking that expresses God's majesty, and others understand this as God the Father speaking to the Son and the Spirit, who are all God.
  * **sisi…yetu..sisi**- Biblia inafundisha kwamba kuna Mungu mmoja, lakini katika Agano la Kale neno "Mungu" limetumika kwa wingi anapoongea juu yake mwenyewe. Wengine wanafahamu hii kama ya kuonyesha ukuu wa Mungu. Na wengine wanaelewa kama Mungu Baba akinena na Mwana na Roho, ambao wote ni Mungu.
  * **in our image**  - An image is a physical representation of someone or something. Humans were made in such a way that we display or represent some of the qualities or traits of God.
  * **Kwa mfano wetu. **  Mfano ni kitu ambacho kinacho fanana na kitu kingine. Binadamu aliuumbwa kwa njia ambayo anaonyesha na kuwakilisha baadhi ya sifa za Mungu.
  * **like us**  - Humans share some of God's characteristics, but not all of his qualities. This phrase should be translated with words that show man is similar to God, but not equal with him or the same as he is.
  * **Kama sisi**  - Binadamu huwa na tabia zingine za kiungu ila sio tabia zote. Haya maneno yanaweza kutafsiriwa na maneno ya kuonyesha binadamu kuwa sawa na Mungu ila hawatoshani na Mungu kama alivyo.
  * **authority**  - God gave people the right and power to manage, guide, and control how the earth and the animals will be used.
  * **Mamlaka **- Mungu aliwapatia binadamu haki na nguvu kusimamia, kuongoza na kuthibiti jinsi dunia na wanyama zitatumika.
