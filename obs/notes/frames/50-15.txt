===== God Will Judge Satan [50-15] Mungu Atahukumu Shetani =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-15.jpg?nolink&}}

When **Jesus** returns, he will completely destroy **Satan** and **his kingdom**. He will throw Satan into **hell** where he will burn forever, along with everyone who chose to follow him rather than to **obey God**.

Wakati **Yesu** atakaporudi, ataharibu **Shetani** kabisa pamoja na **ufalme wake**. Atatupa Shetani katika moto wa **jehanamu** ambako atachomeka milele, pamoja na kila mtu ambaye aliamua kumfuata badala ya **kumtii Mungu**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **his kingdom**  - This could also be translated as, "Satan's evil rule over people" or, "all the evil things he does and the evil people he controls."
  * **utawala wake**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "utawala wa Shetani mbaya juu ya watu" ama, "mambo yote mabaya anayefanya na watu wabaya anayewadhibiti."
  * **rather than**  - That is, "instead of."
  * **badala ya**  - Hiyo ni, "badala ya."
