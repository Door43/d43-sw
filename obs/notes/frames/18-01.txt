===== God Made Solomon Wise [18-01] - Mungu Alifanya Suleimani Mwenye Hekima *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-01.jpg?nolink&}}

After many years, **David** died, and his son **Solomon** began to rule over **Israel**. **God** spoke to Solomon and asked him what he wanted most. When Solomon **asked for wisdom**, God was pleased and made him the wisest man in the world. Solomon learned many things and was a very wise **judge**. God also made him very wealthy.

Baada ya miaka mingi, **Daudi** alifariki na mwanawe **Suleimani** akaanza kutawala **Israeli**. **Mungu** alisema na Suleimani na akamuuliza kile angetaka zaidi ya yote. Wakati Suleimani **aliuliza hekima**, Mungu alipendezwa sana na akamfanya kuwa mwenye hekima zaidi ya wote ulimwenguni. Suleimani alijifunza mambo mengi na akawa **hakimu mwenye hekima sana**. Mungu pia alimfanya tajiri sana.
