===== Not the Messiah [24-05] Siyo Masihi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Some **Jews** asked **John** if he was the **Messiah**. John replied, “I am not the Messiah, but there is someone coming after me. **He is so great** that I am **not even worthy to untie his sandals**.”

**Wayahudi** wengine waliuliza **Yohana** kama yeye alikuwa **Masihi**. Yohana akajibu, "Mimi si Masihi, lakini kuna mmoja atakayekuja baada yangu. **Yeye ni mkuu** **kiasi kuwa** hata **sistahili kufungua kamba za viatu vyake."**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **He is so great**  - This could be translated as, "He is so important."
  * **Yeye ni mkuu sana**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Yeye ni wa muhimu sana."
  * **not even worthy to untie his sandals**  - In other words, "compared to him, I am not important enough to do even the most menial task for him." Untying sandals was a very low job, something a slave would do.
  * **Hata sistahili kufungua kamba ya viatu vyake**  - Kwa maneno mengine, "nikilinganishwa na yeye, mimi si muhimu hata niweze kufanya kazi yake iliyo ya chini zaidi."  Kufungua kamba za viatu ilikuwa kazi ya chini, ni mtumwa angeifanya.
