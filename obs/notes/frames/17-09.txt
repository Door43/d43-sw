===== God Blessed David [17-09] Mungu alimbariki Daudi *F =====

**David ruled with justice and faithfulness for many years**, and **God blessed** him. However, **toward the end of his life he sinned terribly** against God.

**Daudi alitawala na haki na uaminifu kwa miaka mingi,** na **Mungu akambariki**. Hata hivyo, **akielekea mwisho wa maisha yake ** alimtendea Mungu **dhambi vibaya.**

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **David ruled with justice and faithfulness for many years**  - This could be translated as, "When David ruled the people, for many years he did what was right and fair, and he was faithful to God."
  * **Daudi alitawala na haki na uaminifu kwa miaka mingi **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Wakati Daudi alitawala watu, kwa miaka mingi alifanya yaliyo mema na ya haki, na alikuwa muaminifu kwa Mungu."
  * **toward the end of his life**  - This may be translated as, "when David was older" or "later on in David's life."
  * **kuelekea mwisho wa maisha yake**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "wakati Daudi alikuwa na umri" ama, "baadaye katika maisha ya Daudi."
  * **sinned terribly**  - That is, "sinned in a very evil way." David's sin was especially evil.
  * **alitenda dhambi vibaya**  - Hiyo ni, "akatenda dhambi kwa njia mbaya sana." Dhambi ya Daudi hasa ilikuwa mbaya.
