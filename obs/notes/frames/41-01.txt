===== Guards for the Tomb [41-01] Walinzi wa Kaburi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-01.jpg?nolink&}}

After the soldiers **crucified Jesus**, **the unbelieving Jewish leaders** said to **Pilate**, “**That liar**, **Jesus**, **said** he would **rise from the dead** after three days. Someone must guard the **tomb** to make sure that his **disciples** do not steal the body and then say he has risen from the dead.”

Baada ya askari **kumsulubisha Yesu, viongozi wa Wayahudi wasioamini ** wakamwambia **Pilato**, "**Mwongo huo, Yesu**, **alisema** **atafufuka kutoka wafu** baada ya siku tatu. Lazima kuwe na mtu wa kulinda **kaburi ** kuhakikisha kuwa **wanafunzi ** wake hawataiba mwili na kusema yeye amefufuka kutoka wafu."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **the unbelieving Jewish leaders**  - That is, "the Jewish leaders who did not believe in Jesus."
  * **viongozi Wayahudi wasioamini**  - Hiyo ni, "viongozi wa Wayahudi ambao hawakuwa wanamwamini Yesu."
  * **That liar, Jesus, said**  - This could also be translated as, "That man, Jesus, lied and said." They refused to believe that Jesus told the truth about being the Son of God.
  * **Huo mwongo, Yesu, alisema**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama: "Mtu huyo, Yesu, aliongea uongo na kusema. "Walikataa kuamini kwamba Yesu aliwaambia ukweli kuhusu yeye kuwa Mwana wa Mungu.
  * **rise from the dead**  - That is, "come back to life" or, "become alive again."
  * **fufuka kutoka kwa wafu**  - Hiyo ni, "kurudi tena kwa maisha" ama, "kuwa hai tena."
