===== Jeroboam Made an Idol [18-09] Yeroboamu alitengeneza sanamu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-09.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jeroboam rebelled** against **God** and **caused the people to sin**. He built two **idols** for his people to **worship instead of worshiping God at the Temple in the kingdom of Judah**.

**Yeroboamu aliasi** kinyume na **Mungu na akafanya watu watende dhabi**. Alitengenezea watu wake **sanamu** mbili **waziabudu badala wa kuabudu Mungu kwa hekalu katika ufalme wa Yuda**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **caused the people to sin**  - This could be translated as, "led the people to sin" or, "motivated the people to sin." Rehoboam led the people into sin by making idols for them to worship.
  * **alifanya watu watende dhambi**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "akaelekeza watu kwa dhambi" ama, "alitia watu motisha kutenda dhambi." Rehoboamu aliongoza watu kutenda dhambi kwa kuwatengenezea sanamu ili waziabudu.
  * **instead of worshiping God at…Judah**  - This can be translated as, "so that they would not worship God at" or, "instead of going to the kingdom of Judah to worship God at the Temple."
  * **badala ya kumuabudu Mungu…Yuda**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "ili wasimuabudu Mungu katika" ama, "badala ya kwenda kwa ufalme wa ufalme wa Yuda kuabudu Mungu katika Hekalu."
