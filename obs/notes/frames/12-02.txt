===== God Went before Israel [12-02] Mungu alienda mbele ya Israeli *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** led them with a **tall pillar of cloud** that went ahead of them during the day, and which became a **tall pillar of fire** at night. God was always with them and **guided them** as they traveled. All they had to do was follow him.

**Mungu** aliwaongoza na **nguzo refu la wingu** ambalo lilienda mbele yao mchana, na ambalo usiku lilifanyika **nguzo refu la moto**. Mungu alikuwa pamoja nao kila wakati na **aliwaongoza** waliposafiri. Kile tu walihitajika kufanya ni kufuata.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya tafsiri): ====

  * **tall pillar of cloud**  - This could be translated as, "a tall cloud" or, "a cloud shaped like a pillar."
  * **nguzo refu la wingu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "wingu mrefu" ama " wingu lenye umbo wa nguzo."
  * **tall pillar of fire**  - This was a column of fire that hung or floated in the air in front of the Israelites.
  * **nguzo refu la moto**  - Hili lilikuwa safu la moto ambalo lilielea kwa hewa mbele ya Waisraeli.
  * **guided them**  - God showed them the way by making the pillar move along in front of them so they could follow it.
  * **aliwaongoza**  - Mungu aliwaonyesha njia kwa kutengeneza nguzo kuenenda mbele yao diposa waifuate.
