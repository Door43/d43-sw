===== God Raised Jesus [43-07] Mungu Alimfufua Yesu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-07.jpg?nolink&}}

“Although **Jesus** died, **God raised** him from the **dead**. **This fulfills the prophecy which says**, ‘**You will not let your Holy One rot in the grave**.’ We are **witnesses** to the fact that God **raised Jesus to life again**.”

"Ingawa **Yesu ** alikufa, **Mungu alifufua** yeye kutoka kwa **wafu**.  **Hii inatimiza unabii unaosema, " hutaweza kumuacha yule Mtakatifu wako aozee kaburini**.' Sisi ni **mashahidi ** wa ukweli huo ya kuwa Mungu **alifufua Yesu na akawa na uhai tena**."

===== Translation Notes:(Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * (Peter continues preaching to the crowd.)
  * (Petero aendelea kuhubiria umati.)
  * **This fulfills the prophecy which says**  - Another way to translate this would be, "This caused to come true what one of the prophets said long ago."
  * **Hii inatimiza unabii ule unaosema**  - Njia ingine ya kutafsiri hii ni ya kwamba, "Hii inasababisha kutimia kile mmoja wa manabii wa zama za kale walisema."
  * **You will not let your**  - "You" and "your" refer to God the Father. To make that clear, this could be translated as, "You, God, will not let your." Some languages may have a special way of addressing someone, such as, "You, O God."
  * **Hutaweza kumuacha yule wako**  - "Wewe' na "wako" inaongea juu ya Mungu baba.  Kuifanya hiyo kuwa wazi, hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "wewe, Mungu, hutamuacha wako." Baadhi ya lugha zina njia spesheli ya kumtambulisha mtu, kama, "wewe, ee Mungu."
  * **rot in the grave**  - That is, "decay in the tomb" or, "decompose in the grave." This refers to the fact that Jesus did not remain in the tomb very long and is another way of saying that he did not stay dead, but rather came back to life again.
  * **aozee kaburini**  - hio ni, "kuozea kwa kaburi" ama, kuozea kaburini." Hii inaonyesha ya kwamba Yesu hakukaa kwa kaburi kwa muda mrefu na ni njia ingine ya kusema ya kwamba hakukaa akiwa mfu, bali alifufuka na akawa na uhai tena.
  * **raised Jesus to life again**  - That is, "made Jesus alive again."
  * **alimfufua Yesu akawa na uhai tena**  - Hio ni, "alimfanya Yesu kuwa hai tena."
