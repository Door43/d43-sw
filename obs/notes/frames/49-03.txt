===== Jesus the Teacher [49-03] Yesu Mwalimu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-03.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** was also **a great teacher**, and he spoke with authority because he is the **Son of God**. He taught that you need to **love** other people **the same way** you love yourself.

Yesu alikuwa pia mwalimu mkuu, na alinena kwa mamlaka kwa sababu yeye ni Mwana wa Mungu. Alifundisha kwamba unahitaji kupenda watu wengine kama vile unavyo jipenda wewe mwenyewe.

===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god-son|Son of God (Mwana wa Mungu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:love|love (upendo)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **a great teacher**  - That is, "a very important teacher" or, "an excellent teacher."
  * mwalimu mkuu - Hiyo ni, "mwalimu wa muhimu sana" ama, "mwalimu aliye bora."
  * **the same way **  - That is, "as much as" or, "the same amount that" or, "to the same degree that."
  * Hali kadhalika - Hiyo ni, "kama vile" ama, "kiasi kama hicho kwamba" ama, "kwa kiasi hicho hicho."
