===== The Religious Leaders Grumbled [35-02] Viongozi wa Kidini Walinung'unika =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Some **religious leaders** who were also there saw **Jesus** treating these **sinners** as friends, and they began to criticize him to each other. So Jesus told them this **story**.

Baadhi ya **viongozi wa kidini** ambao pia walikuwepo walimuona **Yesu** akitibu **wenye dhambi ** haya kama marafiki, na wakaanza kumkosoa Yesu kwa miongoni mwao. Kwa hivyo Yesus akawaambia **hadithi ** hii.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jewish-leaders|religious leaders (viongozi wa kidini)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:sin|sinners (wenye dhambi)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **story**  - Jesus used this story to teach truths about God's kingdom. It is not clear if the events actually happened or not. If your language has a term that includes both fictional and real stories, you should use it here.
  * **hadithi **- Yesu alitumia hadithi hii kufundisha kweli kuhusu ufalme wa Mungu. Si wazi kama tukio lilifanyika ama la. Kama lugha yako ina neno ambalo inajumuisha hadithi ya kutunga na ya kweli, unaweza kuitumia hapa.
