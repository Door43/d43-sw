===== The People Were Hungry [30-04] Watu Waikuwa na Njaa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-04.jpg?nolink&}}

**Late in the day**, the **disciples** told **Jesus**, “It is late and there are no towns nearby. **Send the people away** so they can go get something to eat.”

**Siku hiyo jioni,** **wanafunzi** wakamwambia **Yesu**, "Tumechelewa na hukuna miji hapa karibuni. **Tuma hawa watu mbali** ili wakatafute kitu ya kukula."
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:disciple|disciples (wanafunzi)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Late in the day**  - This could also be translated as, "When it was almost evening."
  * **Siku hiyo jioni**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Wakati kulikuwa karibu jioni."
  * **Send the people away**  - This could be translated as, "Please tell the people to go to a town" or, "Shouldn't you tell the people to go somewhere?" or, "Let the people go to a town." Make sure it sounds like a polite request, not an order.
  * **Tuma hawa watu waende mbali**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Tafadhali ambia watu waende kwa mji" ama, "Si ungeambia hawa watu waende mahali?" ama, "Achilia watu waende kwa mji." Hakikisha itaonekana kana kwamba ni ombi, badala ya amri.
