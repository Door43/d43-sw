===== The Rain Began [03-07] Mvua ilianza =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Then it began to **rain, and rain, and rain**. It rained for forty days and forty nights without stopping! Water also came gushing up out of the earth. Everything in the **whole world was covered with water**, even the highest mountains.

Halafu ikaanza **kunyesha, na ikandelea, na ikaendelea**. Ikanyesha kwa muda wa siku arobanne usiku na mchana bila kukoma! Maji pia ilikuwa inatiririka kutoka kwa ardhi. Kila kitu katika **duniani yote ikawa imefunikwa na maji**, hata milima iliyo mirefu zaidi.

===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **rain, and rain, and rain**  - This emphasizes that there was an unusual, extreme amount of rain. Other languages may have a different way of emphasizing this.
  * **Mvua, na ikaendelea, na ikaendelea**  - Hii inatia mkazo kuwa ilinyesha mvua iliokithiri, isiyo ya kawaida. Lugha zingine zaweza kuwa na njia tofauti ya kutia huu mkazo.
  * **gushing**  - This indicates that there was a large amount of water coming out.
  * **Kutiririka**  - Hii inaonyesha kuwa maji yaliyotoka yalikuwa mengi sana.
  * **whole world was covered**  - This refers to all the earth being covered with water from the flood.
  * **Dunia yote ilifunikwa**  - Hii inamanisha dunia yote ilifunikwa na maji ya mafuriko.
