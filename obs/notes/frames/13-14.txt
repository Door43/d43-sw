===== Moses Destroyed the Idol [13-14] Musa aliharibu sanamu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-14.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Moses beat the idol into powder**, threw the powder into some water and made the people drink the water. **God** sent **a plague** on the people and many of them died.

Kisha **Musa alipigapiga ile sanamu mpaka ikawa unga**, akarusha ile unga kwa maji na akafanya watu kunywa ile maji. **Mungu** akaleta **pigo** juu ya watu na wengi wao wakafa.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **beat the idol into powder**  - Moses utterly destroyed the idol by pounding it into fine particles.
  * **pigapiga sanamu akawa unga**  - Musa ariharibu kabisa ile sanamu kwa kuisiaga ikafanyika chembe ndogo.
  * **into some water**  - Moses scattered the gold powder over a large expanse of water.
  * **kwa maji **- Musa alitawanya ile unga ya dhahabu kwa eneo kubwa la maji.
  * **a plague**  - This could be translated as, "a terrible disease."
  * **pigo**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "ugonjwa wa kutisha."
