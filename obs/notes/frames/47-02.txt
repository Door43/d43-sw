===== Lydia Was Baptized [47-02] Lydia Alibatizwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-47-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**God opened Lydia's heart** to **believe** the message about **Jesus**, and **she and her family were baptized**. She invited **Paul** and **Silas** to stay at her house, **so they stayed with her and her family**.

**Mungu alifungua moyo wa Lydia** kuamini ujumbe wa **Yesu ** na **yeye pamoja na jamii yake ****wakabatizwa**. Aliwaalika **Paulo ** na **Sila ** wakae katika nyumba yake, **kwa hivyo walikaa naye na familia yake**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **opened Lydia's heart**  - That is, "enabled Lydia."
  * **alifungua moyo wa Lydia**  - Hiyo ni, "alimwezesha Lydia."
  * **she and her family were baptized**  - This can also be translated as, "they baptized Lydia and her family."
  * **yeye na familia yake wakabatizwa**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "walimbatiza Lydia pamoja na familia yake."
  * **so they stayed with her and her family**  - This was the normal custom of the day for people to provide hospitality to visitors in their homes. There were no immoral motives in this arrangement.
  * **Kwa hivyo walikaa naye pamoja na familia yake **- Hii ilikuwa ni desturi ya kawaida ya siku zile kwa watu kutoa ukarimu kwa wageni kwa nyumba zao. Hakukuwa na nia kinyume cha maadili kwa mpangilio huu.
