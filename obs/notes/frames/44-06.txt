===== Peter and John Were Arrested [44-06] Petro na Yohana Walikamatwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-06.jpg?nolink&}}

The leaders of the **Temple** were very upset by what **Peter** and **John** were saying. So they arrested them and put them into prison. But many of the people **believed Peter's message**, and the **number of men** who believed in Jesus grew to about 5,000.

Viongozi wa **Hekalu** walisikitishwa sana na kile **Petero** na **Yohana** walikuwa wakisema. Basi waliwakamata na kuwaweka gerezani. Lakini wengi wa wale watu waliamini **ujumbe wa Petero**, na **idadi ya watu** walio amini Yesu iliongezeka na kufika kama 5,000.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Peter's message**  - That is, "the message that Peter had preached to them."
  * **Ujumbe wa Petero**  - Hiyo ni, "ujumbe ambao Petero alikuwa akiwahubiria."
  * **number of men**  - This was in addition to the woman and children who believed.
  * **idadi ya watu**  - Hii ilikuwa ni pamoja na mwanamke na watoto walio amini.
