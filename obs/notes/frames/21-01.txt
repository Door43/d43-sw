===== Messiah Will Defeat Satan [21-01] Masihi atamshinda Shetani =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-01.jpg?nolink&}}

**From the very beginning**, **God** planned to send the **Messiah**. The first **promise** of the Messiah came to **Adam** and **Eve**. God promised that a **descendant** of Eve would be born who would **crush the snake’s head**. **The snake who deceived Eve was Satan**. The promise meant that the Messiah would defeat Satan completely.

**Kutoka mwanzo kabisa, Mungu ** alipanga kutuma **Masihi**. **Ahadi** ya kwanza ya Masihi ilipeanwa kwa **Adamu** na **Hawa**. Mungu aliahidi kuwa **mzaliwa** wa Hawa atazaliwa anbaye **ataponda kichwa cha nyoka**. **Yule nyoka aliyemndanganya Hawa ni Shetani**. Ahadi ilimaanisha kuwa Masihi atamshinda Shetani kabisa.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **From the very beginning**  - That is, from when the earth was first created.
  * **Kutoka mwanzo kabisa**  - Hiyo ni, kutoka ulimwengu ulipoumbwa.
  * **crush the snake's head**  - Unless the head of a venomous snake is crushed, the snake can still hurt someone. Use a word for "crush" that communicates that its head is destroyed.
  * **ponda kicha cha nyoka**  - Isipokuwa kichwa cha nyoka mwenye sumu kipondwe, yule nyoka bado anaweza kudhuru mtu. Tumia neno la "ponda" ambalo laeleza kuwa kichwa chake kitaharibiwa.
  * **The snake…was Satan**  – Satan spoke to Eve in the form of a snake. This does not mean that he is a snake now. This could be translated as, "The snake…was an appearance of Satan."
  * **Yule nyoka...alikuwa Shetani**  - Shetani aliongea na Hawa akiwa na mfano wa nyoka. Hii haimanishi kuwa yeye ni nyoka leo. Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Yule nyoka...ilikuwa kujitokeza kwa Shetani."
  * **who deceived Eve**  - That is, "who lied to Eve." The snake lied by making Eve doubt what God had said and tricking her into disobeying God.
  * **aliyemndanganya Hawa**  - Hiyo ni, "alimuambia Hawa uongo." Nyoka ilindanganya Hawa na kumfanya awe na shaka na kile Mungu alikuwa amesema na kumhadaa ili akose kumtii Mungu.
