===== Elijah and the Prophets of Baal [19-06] Elia na manabii wa Baali =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-06.jpg?nolink&}}

All the people of the **kingdom of Israel**, including the 450 **prophets** of **Baal**, came to **Mount Carmel**. **Elijah** said to the people, “**How long will you** keep changing your mind? **If Yahweh is God**, serve him! **If Baal is God**, serve him!”

Watu wote wa **ufalme wa Israeli**, pamoja na **manabii ** 450 wa **Baali**, wakaja **Mlima wa Karmeli**. **Elia** akasema kwa watu, "**Ni mpaka lini** mtasitasita kwa mawazo yenu? K**ama**** Yehova ni Mungu**, mtumikieni! **Kama Baali ni Mungu**, mtumikieni!"

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **Mount Carmel**  - See how you translated this in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:19-05|[19-05]]]**.
  * **Mlima wa Karmeli**  - Ona vile ulivyotafsiri hii pale **[19-05]. **
  * **How long will you**  - This is not a real question asking for information. Elijah was rebuking the Israelites for repeatedly changing their minds about whether they would serve Yahweh or Baal. Some languages will have to express this as a statement such as, "Stop changing your minds about whom you will worship!"
  * **mpaka lini **- Hii si swali haswa inayoitisha majibu. Elia alikuwa anakemea Waisraeli kwa kubadilisha mawazo yao kila mara kama watamuabudu Yehova au Baali. Lugha zingine zitaeleza hii kwa kutumia msemo kama, "Wacheni kubadilisha mawazo yenu kuhusu nani mtaabudu."
  * **If Yahweh is God…If Baal is God**  - This does not mean that Elijah was undecided. He knew that Yahweh is the real God. He wanted the people to understand that when they worship false gods, they are rejecting Yahweh as the only true God. Translate this in a way that shows that the people had to make a choice.
  * **Kama Yehova ni Mungu****…kama Baali ni Mungu**  - Hii haimaanishi kuwa Elia hakua ameamua. Alijua kuwa Yehova ndiye Mungu wa kweli. Alitaka watu waelewe kuwa wanapoiabudu miungu isiyo ya kweli, wanamkataa Yehova kama Mungu peke yake aliye wa kweli. Tafsiri hii kwa njia ambayo inaonyesha kuwa watu lazima wangeamua.
