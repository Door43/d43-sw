===== The Proud Will Be Humbled [34-10] Wenye Kiburi Watashushwa =====

**{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-10.jpg?nolink&}}**

Then **Jesus** said, “I tell you the **truth**, **God** heard the **tax collector's prayer** and **declared him to be righteous**. But he did not like the **religious leader's** prayer. God **will humble** everyone who is **proud**, and he will lift up whoever **humbles himself**.”

Kisha **Yesu** akasema, "Nawambieni **ukweli**, **Mungu** alisikia **maombi ya yule mtoza ushuru** na **kumtangaza mwenye haki.** Lakini hakupendezwa na maombi ya yule **kiongozi wa kindini.** Mungu **atashusha** kila mtu ambaye ni mwenye **kiburi**, na atainua kila mtu **ambaye ananyenyekea."**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **declared him to be righteous**  - That is, "considered him to be a righteous person." Even though the tax collector was a sinner, God was merciful to him because of his humility and repentance.
  * **alitangaza kuwa ni mwenye haki **- Hiyo ni, "alitambua yeye kuwa mwenye haki." Hata ingawa mtoza ushuru alikuwa mwenye dhambi, Mungu alimuonea huruma kwa sababu ya unyenyekevu na kutubu kwake.
  * **will humble**  - This could also be translated as, "will give a lower status to" or, "will cause to not be important." It could be translated figuratively as, "will bring down."
  * **atashusha**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "atawapa kiwango cha chini" ama, "atawafanya wasiwe wa muhimu." Inweza kutafsiriwa kwa mfano kama, "ataleta chini."
  * **will lift up**  - That is, "will give a higher position to" or, "will honor."
  * **atainua**  - Hiyo ni, "atampa nafasi ya juu" ama, "atamheshimu."
  * **humbles himself**  - That is, "chooses to behave in a humble way" or, "has a humble attitude about himself."
  * **anaye nyenyekea **- Hiyo ni, "anachagua kuishi katika njia ya unyenyekevu" ama, "anajitazama kwa unyenyekevu."
