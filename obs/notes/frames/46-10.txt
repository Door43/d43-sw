===== Barnabas and Saul [46-10] Baranaba na Sauli =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-10.jpg?nolink&}}

**One day**, while the Christians at Antioch were **fasting** and **praying**, the **Holy Spirit** said to them, “**Set apart for me Barnabas and Saul to do the work I have called them to do**.” So **the church** in Antioch **prayed** for **Barnabas** and **Saul** and **placed their hands on them**. Then they **sent them out** to **preach** the **good news** about **Jesus** in many other places. Barnabas and Saul taught people from different people groups, and many people **believed** in Jesus.

**Siku moja**, wakati Wakristo wa Antiokia walikuwa **wanafunga** na **kuomba**, **Roho Mtakatifu** akawaambia, **"Nitengeeni Baranaba na Sauli ili waifanye kazi ambayo nimewaita kufanya."** Kwa hivyo **Kanisa** la Antiokia **likaomba** kwa ajili ya** Baranaba** na **Sauli** na **wakawawekelea mikono**. Kisha **wakawatuma nje kuhubiri habari njema** kuhusu **Yesu** katika sehemu nyingi zinginezo. Baranaba na Sauli walifundisha watu kutoka makundi tofauti, na watu wengi **walimwamini** Yesu.

===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **One day**  - This phrase introduces an event that happened in the past, but does not state the specific time. Many languages have a similar way to begin telling a true story.
  * Siku Moja - Hii maneno inatanguliza tukio ambalo lilifanyika hapo zamani, lakini haielezei wakati hasa. Lugha nyingi zina njia sawa ya kuanzisha kupeana hadithi ya kweli.
  * **Set apart for me …**  - This sentence could also be translated as, "Appoint Paul and Barnabas to do the special work I have chosen them to do."
  * Nitengeeni… - Mstari huu unaweza tafsiriwa kama, "Teueni Paulo na Baranaba wafanye kazi spesheli nimewachagulia kufanya."
  * **the church**  - This could also be translated as, "the believers" or, "the Christians."
  * Kanisa - Hii inaweza tafsiriwa kama, "waaminio" au, "Wakristo."
  * **placed their hands on them**  - This could also be translated as, "blessed them with the power and authority of the Holy Spirit by putting their hands on them" or, "put their hands on them as a sign of their unity in the Spirit." Some languages may have to say where they placed their hands. If so, you could say they placed their hands on their head, shoulder or back.
  * Waliwawekelea mikono - Hii inaweza tafsiriwa kama, "waliwabariki na nguvu na mamlaka ya Roho Mtakatifu kwa kuwawekelea mikono" ama, kuweka mikono juu yao kama ishara ya umoja wao ndani ya Roho."Baadhi ya lugha zinaweza kusema mahali waliwawekelea mikono. Kama ni hivyo, unaweza sema waliwawekelea mikono kwa kichwa, mabega ama mgongo.
  * **sent them out **  - That is, "sent them away" or, "sent them on their journey."
  * Waliwatuma nje - Hio ni "waliwapatia ruhusa waende" au, "waliwatuma waende na safari yao."
  * //A Bible story from//  - These references may be slightly different in some Bible translations.
  * Hadithi ya Biblia kutoka - Nukuu hizi zinaweza kuwa na utofauti kidoto na tafsiri zingine za Biblia.
