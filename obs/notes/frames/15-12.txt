===== Dividing the Promised Land [15-12] Kugawanya Nchi ya Ahadi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-12.jpg?nolink&}}

After this battle, **God gave each tribe of Israel its own section of the Promised Land**. Then God **gave Israel peace along all its borders**.

Baada ya hiyo vita, **Mungu aliwapa kila kabila la Israeli sehemu yao la ile Nchi ya Ahadi**. Kisha **Mungu akawapa Israeli amani katika mipaka yao yote. **

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **God gave…Land**  – this could be translated as, "God appointed each tribe its own plot of land" or, "God decided which part of the Promised Land each tribe of Israel would live on."
  * **Mungu aliwapa...Nchi **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mungu aliwachagulia kila kabila eneo la shamba" ama "Mungu aliamuwa ni sehemu gani ya Nchi ya ahadi kila kabila la Israeli litaishi."
  * **gave Israel peace along all its borders**  – This could be translated as, "God allowed the Israelites to experience peace with the other people groups that surrounded them" or. "the other countries around Israel."
  * **aliwapa Israeli amani katika mipaka yao yote**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mungu aliruhusu Waisraeli kuzoea amani na vikundi vingine vya watu waliowazingira" ama, "nchi zingine ambazo ziliwazunguka Israeli."
