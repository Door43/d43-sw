===== Baal Did Not Answer [19-08] Baali hakujibu =====

**{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-08.jpg?nolink&}}**

Then the **prophets** of **Baal prayed to Baal**, “Hear us, Baal!” All day long they prayed and **shouted** and even **cut themselves with knives**, but **there was no answer**.

Kisha **manabii ** wa **Baali wakaomba Baali**, "Tusikie, Baali!" Kwa siku yote waliomba na **kupiga kelele** na **hata wakajikata visu**, lakini **hakukuwa na jibu**.

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:prophet|prophets (manabii)]]**

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **prayed to Baal**  - the prophets of Baal asked Baal to send fire onto the bull they prepared as a sacrifice.
  * **waliomba Baali **- Manabii wa Baali waliuliza Baali atume moto kwa ngombe waliyemtayarisha kama dhabihu.
  * **shouted**  - They yelled or called out loudly to Baal.
  * **walipiga kelele**  - Walipasa sauti ama kumuita Baali kwa sauti kubwa.
  * **cut themselves with knives**  - They injured themselves with knives as an extreme way to show their devotion to Baal, hoping that this would persuade him to listen to them.
  * **walijikata na visu**  - Walijijeruhi na visu ikiwa njia iliokithiri ya kuonyesha kujitoa kwao kwa Baali, wakitumaini kuwa hii itamshawishi kuwasikiliza.
  * **there was no answer**  - There was no response of any kind to their shouting, and no fire came to burn up the sacrifice.
  * **hakukuwa na jibu**  - Hakukuwa na jibu ya aina yoyote kwa kupiga makelele kwao, na hakuna moto ulikuja kuteketeza ile dhabihu.
