===== God's Kingdom of Light [49-15] Ufalme wa Mungu wa Nuru =====

If you **believe** in **Jesus** and what he has done for you, you are a **Christian**! **God** has taken you out of **Satan's kingdom of darkness** and put you into **Godâ€™s kingdom of light**. God has taken away your old, sinful ways of doing things and has given you new, righteous ways of doing things.

Kama unamuamini Yesu na kazi aliofanya kwa ajili yako, wewe ni Mkristo! Mungu amekutoa kwa ufalme wa Shetani wa giza na kukuweka katika ufalme wa Mungu wa mwanga. Mungu ameondoa njia zako za zamani za dhambi za kufanya mambo na akakupa njia mpya ya haki ya kufanya mambo.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Satan's kingdom of darkness**  - "Darkness" is used here to refer to sin and everything that is evil. This could be translated as, "Satan's evil rule over people, which is like darkness."
  * Ufalme wa Shetani wa giza - "Giza" limetumika hapa kumaanisha dhambi na kila kitu ambacho ni mbaya. Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "utawala mbaya wa Shetani  juu ya watu, ambayo ni kama giza."
  * **God's kingdom of light**  - "Light" here refers to God's holiness and goodness. This could be translated as, "God's righteous rule over people, which is like light." The Bible often compares evil to darkness, and goodness to light.
  * Utawala wa Mungu wa mwanga - "Nuru" hapa inaeleza kuhusu utakatifu na wema wa Mungu. Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "utawala wa Mungu mwenye haki juu ya watu, ambayo ni kama mwanga." Biblia mara nyingi inalinganisha mabaya na giza, na wema na mwanga.
