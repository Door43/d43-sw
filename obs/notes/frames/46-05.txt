===== Ananias Baptized Saul [46-05] Anania Alimbatiza Sauli =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-05.jpg?nolink&}}

So Ananias went to **Saul**, placed his hands on him, and said, “**Jesus**, who appeared to you on your way **here**, sent me to you so that you can **regain your sight** and be filled with the **Holy Spirit**.” Saul immediately was able to see again, and Ananias **baptized** him. Then Saul ate some food and **his strength returned**.

Hivyo Anania alienda kwa **Sauli**, akawekelea mikono juu yake, na akasema, "**Yesu**, aliyekutokea ukiwa njiani ukija **hapa**, alinituma kwako ili uweze **kurejesha** **kuona kwako ** na ujazwe na **Roho Mtakatifu**."  Mara Sauli akaweza kuona tena, naye Anania **akambatiza**.  Halafu Sauli akala chakula na **nguvu zake zikarudi**.

===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **here**  - That is, "to Damascus."
  * **hapa **- Hio ni, "kuelekea Damesiki."
  * **regain your sight**  - This could also be translated as, "be able to see again."
  * **kurejesha kuona kwako**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "uweze kuona tena."
  * **his strength returned**  - This could also be translated as, "he became strong again" or, "he felt better."
  * **Nguvu zake zikarudi**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "akawa na nguvu tena" ama, "akajisikia vyema."
