===== The Gibeonites in Danger [15-07] Wagibeoni Hatarini *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-07.jpg?nolink&}}

The **Israelites** were angry when they found out that the **Gibeonites** had deceived them, but they kept the **peace** treaty they had made with them because it was a **promise** before God. Sometime later, the **kings** of another people group in **Canaan**, the **Amorites**, heard that the Gibeonites had made a peace treaty with the Israelites, so they combined their armies into one large army and attacked **Gibeon**. The Gibeonites **sent a message to Joshua asking for help**.

**Waisraeli ** walipatwa na hasira walipogundua kuwa **Wagibeoni** waliwadanganya, lakini waliweka mkataba wa **amani** walioifanya na wao kwa sababu ilikuwa **ahadi** mbele za Mungu. Baadaye, **wafalme** wa kikundi kingine cha watu kule **Kanani**, **Waamori,** wakasikia kuwa Wagibeoni wamefanya mkataba wa amani na Waisraeli, kwa hivyo wakaunganisha majeshi yao ikawa jeshi moja kubwa na wakavamia **Gibeoni**. Wagibeoni **walituma ujumbe kwa Yoshua wakiitisha msaada**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **sent a message to Joshua asking for help**  - This could be translated as, "sent some of their people to tell Joshua that they needed the Israelites to help defend them against their enemies."
  * **alituma ujumbe kwa Yoshua akiitisha msaada**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "walituma wengine kati ya watu wao ili wamwambie Yoshua kuwa wanahitaji msaada wa Waisraeli kujilinda kutoka kwa adui zao.
