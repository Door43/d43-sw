===== The House of the High Priest [39-01] Nyumba la Kuhani Mkuu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-01.jpg?nolink&}}

It was now **the middle of the night**. The soldiers led **Jesus** to the house of the **high priest** in order for the high priest **to question him**. **Peter** followed far behind them. When Jesus was taken into the house, Peter stayed outside and warmed himself by a fire.

Ilikuwa sasa **katikati ya usiku. ** Askari walimpeleka **Yesu** nyumbani kwa **kuhani mkuu** ili kuhani mkuu aweze **kumhoji.​ Petero** alifuata nyuma yao. Wakati Yesu alipelekwa kwa nyumba, Petero alikaa hapo nje akiota moto.​

===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:priest-high|high priest (kuhani mkuu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:peter|Peter (Petro)]]**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **the middle of the night**  - This expression means, "halfway through the night" or, "very late at night."
  * **katikati ya usiku**  - Msemo huu una maana, "usiku wa manane" ama, "muda umeenda sana usiku."
  * **to question him**  - That is, "to ask Jesus questions to try to find something they could accuse Jesus of doing wrong."
  * **kumhoji **- Hiyo ni, "kuuliza Yesu maswali ili kujaribu kupata kitu wangeweza kumshtaki Yesu nacho kwa kufanya makosa."
