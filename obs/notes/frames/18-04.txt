===== The Kingdom Would Divide [18-04] - Ufalme Ungegawanyika *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-04.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** was angry with **Solomon** and, **as a punishment for Solomon’s unfaithfulness**, **he promised to divide** the nation of **Israel** into two **kingdoms** after Solomon’s death.

**Mungu** alikuwa amekasirika na **Suleimani** na, **kama adhabu kwa kutoaminika kwa Suleimani, aliahidi kugawanya** taifa la **Israeli** kuwa **falme** mbili baada ya kifo cha Suleimani.

==== Translation Notes: Tafsiri ya Maelezo ====

  * **as a punishment for Solomon's unfaithfulness, he promised to divide**  – This could be translated as, "to punish Solomon for being unfaithful to him, God solemnly said that he would divide."
  * **Kama adhabu kwa kutoaminika kwa Suleimani, aliahidi kugawanya**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuadhibu Suleimani kwa kutoaminika kwake, Mungu alisema kwa umakini kuwa angegawanya."
