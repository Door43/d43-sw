===== Out of the Garden [02-12] Nje ya bustani =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-12.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **God** said, “Now that the human beings have become **like us by knowing good and evil**, they must not be allowed to eat the **fruit** of the **tree of life** and live forever.” So God sent **Adam** and **Eve** away from the beautiful garden. God placed large, powerful **angels** at the entrance to the garden to keep anyone from eating the fruit of the tree of life.

Halafu **Mungu** akasema, "Sasa kuona wanadamu wamekuwa **kama sisi wakajua mema na mabaya**, hawapaswhi kuruhusiwa kukula **tunda** la **mti wa uzima** na kuishi milele." Kwa hivyo Mungu akamtuma **Adamu** na **Hawa** mbali na ile bustani nzuri. Mungu akaweka **malaika** wakubwa na wenye nguvu kwa lango la bustani kuzuilia mtu yeyote asikule tunda la mti wa uzima.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **like us by knowing good and evil**  - Here, this phrase points to a new way that Adam and Eve would be like God. Because they had sinned, they were aware of evil and could experience it. You could say, "because now they knew both good and evil."
  * **Kama sisi kwa njia ya kujua mema na mabaya**  - msemo huu unaelekeza kwa njia mpya ambayo Adamu na Hawa watakuwa kama Mungu. Sababu wametenda dhambi, wameelewa mabaya na wanaweza kuizoea. unaweza sema, "kuona sasa wamejua mema na pia mabaya."
  * **fruit**  - The specific kind of fruit is not revealed, so it is best to translate this using a general word for fruit.
  * **Tunda**  - Aina ya tunda hili haijaelezwa hasa, kwa hivyo vizuri ni kutafsri kutumia neno linalojumlisha matunda.
  * **tree of life**  - This was an actual tree with fruit. See **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:01-11|[01-11]]]**. If a person ate this fruit he would live continually and never die.
  * **Mti wa uzima**  - Huu ulikua mti halisi uliokuwa na tunda. Ona [01-11]. Kama mtu angeikula hii tunda angeshi daima bila kufa.
