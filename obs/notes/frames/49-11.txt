===== The Sinless Sacrifice [49-11] Dhabihu bila Dhambi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-11.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** never **sinned**, but he chose to be **punished** and to **die** as the perfect **sacrifice** to **take away** your sins and the sins of every person in the world. Because Jesus sacrificed himself, **God** can **forgive** any sin, even terrible sins.

Yesu kamwe hakutenda dhambi, lakini yeye aliamua kuadhibiwa na kufa kama dhabihu kamilifu kuondoa dhambi zenu na dhambi za kila mtu duniani. Kwa sababu Yesu alijitoa dhabihu yeye mwenyewe, Mungu anaweza kusamehe dhambi yoyote, hata dhambi ya kutisha.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **take away**  - That is, "remove the penalty of" or, "remove the punishment for." The sacrifice of Jesus causes God to look at our sin as if it never existed.
  * kuondoa - Hiyo ni, "kuondoa adhabu ya" ama, "kuondoa adhabu kwa." Sadaka ya Yesu husababisha Mungu kuangalia dhambi zetu kana kwamba kamwe kuwepo.
