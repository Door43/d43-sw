===== A Descendant of David [21-04] Wa ukoo wa Daudi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-04.jpg?nolink&}}

**God promised King David** that one of his own **descendants** would rule as **king** over God’s people forever. That meant that the **Messiah** would be one of **David’s own descendants**.

**Mungu alimuahidi mfalme Daudi** ya kwamba mmoja wa **ukoo** wake atakuwa **mfalme ** juu ya watu wa mungu milele. Hii iliimaanisha

ya kwamba masihi atakuwa mmoja wa ukoo wa Daudi mwenyewe.

==== Translation Notes: maelezo ya Tafsiri ====

  * **David's own descendants**  - Another way to say this would be, "a direct descendant of David himself."
  * **Wa ukoo wa Daudi mwenyewe**  -  njia nyingine ya kusema haya ni, " wa moja kwa moja kutoka ukoo wa Daudi mwenyewe".
