===== God Gave His Son [49-09] Mungu Alimtoa Mwanawe =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-09.jpg?nolink&}}

But **God loved** everyone in the world so much that he **gave his only Son** so that **whoever believes in Jesus will not be punished for his sins**, **but will live with God foreve**r.

Lakini **Mungu alipenda** kila mtu katika ulimwengu sana hivi kwamba **akamtoa Mwana wake wa pekee** ili kila mtu **amwaminiye Yesu asiadhibiwe kwa ajili ya dhambi zake, lakini wataishi na Mungu milele.**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **gave his only Son**  - This could also be translated as, "offered his only Son to the world as a sacrifice for sins" or, "gave us his only Son to be a sacrifice for our sins."
  * **alitoa Mwana wake wa pekee **- Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "alitoa mwanawe wa pekee kwa ulimwengu kama sadaka kwa ajili ya dhambi" ama, "alitupa mwanawe wa pekee ili awe sadaka kwa ajili ya dhambi zetu."
  * **whoever believes**  - This could also be translated as, "anyone who believes."
  * **yeyote amwaminiye**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "mtu yeyote ambaye anaamini."
  * **whoever believes in Jesus will not be punished for his sins, but will live with God forever**  - This could also be translated as, "When anyone believes in Jesus, God will not punish that person for his sins, but will allow him to live with God forever."
  * **kila mtu amwaminiye Yesu si hataadhibiwa kwa ajili ya dhambi zake, lakini wataishi na Mungu milele**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "Wakati mtu yeyote anamwamini Yesu, Mungu hatamuadhibu kwa ajili ya dhambi zake, lakini atamwezesha kuishi na Mungu milele."
