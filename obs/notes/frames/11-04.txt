===== The Passover Meal [11-04] Mlo wa pasaka =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-04.jpg?nolink&}}

The **Israelites** did everything just as **God** had commanded them to do. In the middle of the night, God went throughout **Egypt** killing **every firstborn son**.

**Wana wa Israeli** walifanya kila kitu kama vile **Mungu** alivyowaamurisha kufanya. Katikati ya usiku, Mungu akapitia **Misri ** akiua kila **mwana aliye mzaliwa wa kwanza**.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:israel|Israelites (wana wa Israeli)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God (Mungu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:egypt|Egypt (Misri)]]**
==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya tafsiri): ====

  * **every firstborn son**  - This means every firstborn son of families who had not made the blood sacrifice, that is, the Egyptians. To make this clear you may want to add, "Every Egyptian firstborn son" (since all the Israelite families had put the blood on their doorposts).
  * **kila mwana aliye mzaliwa wa kwanza**  - Hii inamaanisha kila mwana aliye mzaliwa wa kwanza ambaye hakufanya ile dhabihu ya damu, hiyo ni, Wamisri. Kuhakikisha inaeleweka wazi unaweza ongeza, "Kila mzaliwa wa kwanza Mumsiri" (kwa maana jamii zote za wana wa Israeli wilikuwa wemepaka damu kwa milango yao).
