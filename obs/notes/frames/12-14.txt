===== An Annual Feast [12-14] Karamu ya kila mwaka *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-14.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** commanded the **Israelites** to celebrate **the Passover** every year in order to **remember how God gave them victory over** the **Egyptians** and rescued them from being **slaves**. They celebrated by killing a **perfect lamb**, eating it with **bread that was made without yeast**, and telling about how God had **saved** them.

**Mungu** aliwaamuru **Waisraeli** kusherehekea **Pasaka** kila mwaka ili **wakumbuke vile Mungu aliwapa ushindi juu** ya **Wamisri ** na akawaokoa kutoka kwa kuwa **watumwa**. Walisherehekea kwa kuchinja **kondoo aliye mkamilifu**, na kuikula na **mkate usiyo na chachu**, na kusema vile Mungu alivyo **waokoa**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **the Passover**  - This could be translated as, "the Passover activities" or, "the Passover celebration" or, "the Passover meal."
  * **Pasaka**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "shuguli za Pasaka" ama, "sherehe za Pasaka" ama, "mlo wa Pasaka."
  * **remember how God gave them victory over**  â€“ This could be translated as, "regularly remind themselves of how God defeated." The word "remember" here doesn't just mean to not forget; it also means to formally commemorate something.
  * **kumbuka vile Mungu aliwapa ushindi**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kujikumbusha kila mala vile Mungu alivyo washinda." Neno "kumbuka" hapa haimanishi tu kutosahau; linamaanisha pia kusherehekea jambo kirasmi.
  * **perfect lamb**  - The word "perfect" here refers to a lamb with no disease or anything else wrong with it. Another way to say this might be, "a completely healthy and well-formed lamb."
  * **kondoo aliyemkamilifu**  - Neno "kamilifu" hapa linanena juu ya kondoo ambaye hana magonjwa ama umbaya mwingine wowote. Njia ingine ya kusema hii yaweza kuwa, "kondoo aliyekomaa na mwenye afya."
  * **bread that was made without yeast**  - Another way to say this is, "unleavened bread." See how you translated this in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:11-03|[11-03]]]**.
  * **mkake ambao hauna chachu**  - Njia ingine ya kusema hii ni, "mkate usiotiwa". Ona vile ulivyotafsiri hii pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:11-03|[11-03]]]**.
