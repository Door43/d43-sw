===== I Am the Son of God [39-04] Mimi ni Mwana wa Mungu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Jesus said, “**I am**, and you will see me **seated with God and coming from heaven**.” The **high priest tore his clothes in anger** and shouted to the other **religious leaders**, “We do not need any more **witnesses**! You have heard him say that he is the **Son of God**. **What is your judgment**?”

Yesu akasema, **"Mimi ndiye**, na utaniona mimi **nimeketi na Mungu na ninakuja kutoka mbinguni.**" Kuhani Mkuu akararua nguo zake kwa hasira na kupasa sauti **viongozi** wengine **wa kidini**, "Hatuna haja tena ya **mashahidi! ** Mumesikia akisema eti yeye ni **Mwana wa Mungu.** **Uamuzi wenu ni gani?"**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **I am**  - That is, "I am as you said" or, "I am the Messiah and the Son of God." "I am" is also the name of God (See **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:09-14|[09-14]]]**). By saying simply, "I am" Jesus was also saying that he is God. If possible, translate this so that people will see a similarity between Jesus' answer and the name of God.
  * **Mimi ndiye**  - Hiyo ni, "Mimi ni kama vile wewe umesema" ama,"Mimi ni Masihi na Mwana wa Mungu." "Mimi ndiye" pia ni jina la Mungu (Angalia **[09-14]**). Kwa kifupi akisema, "Mimi ndiye" Yesu alikuwa pia anasema kuwa yeye ni Mungu. Kama inawezekana, tafsiri hii ili watu waone uhusiano kati ya jibu la Yesu na jina la Mungu.
  * **seated with God**  - This could be translated as, "reigning with God." Because God is the ruler over all, people talk about him as sitting on a throne in heaven. By saying that he would be seated with God, Jesus claimed that he had authority to rule with the Father.
  * **ameketi na Mungu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kutawala na Mungu." Kwa sababu Mungu anatawala yote, watu huongea juu yake kama anayekaa kwa kiti cha enzi mbinguni. Kwa kusema kwamba atakuwa ameketi Mungu, Yesu alidai kuwa yeye alikuwa na mamlaka ya kutawala pamoja na Baba.
  * **seated with God and coming from heaven**  - This could be translated as, "sitting beside God and then coming from heaven."
  * **ameketi na Mungu na kuja kutoka mbinguni **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "ameketi kando ya Mungu na kisha atakuja kutoka mbinguni."
  * **tore his clothes in anger**  - The Jews would tear their clothes to show grief or anger. If tearing the clothes means something else in your language, you may want to substitute a phrase such as, "he was extremely angry."
  * **akararua mavazi yake kwa hasira**  - Wayahudi walikuwa wanararua nguo zao kuwaonyesha huzuni au hasira. Kama kurarua nguo inamanisha kitu kingine katika lugha yako, unaweza kubadilisha msemo na mwingine kama, "alikuwa na ghadhabu sana."
  * **What is your judgment?**  - That is, "What is your decision?" or, "Tell us what have you decided: Is he innocent or guilty?" The chief priest wanted the religious leaders to condemn Jesus for claiming to be equal with God.
  * **Uamuzi wenu ni gani?**  - Hiyo ni, "ni uamuzi wenu ni nini? " ama, "Twambie umeamua nini?: Je, yeye ana hatia au la?" Kuhani mkuu alitaka viongozi wa kidini kumhukumu Yesu kwa ajili ya kudai kuwa yeye ni sawa na Mungu.
