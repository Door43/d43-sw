===== What Should We Do? [43-10] Tufanye nini? =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-10.jpg?nolink&}}

The people listening to **Peter** were **deeply moved by** the things that he said. So they asked Peter and the **disciples**, “**Brothers**, what should we do?”

Watu waliokuwa wakimsikiliza **Petero walisukumwa kwa undani na** mambo aliyoyasema.  Kwa hivyo wakamuuliza Petero na **wanafunzi**, "**Ndungu**, tufanye nini?"
===== Important Terms:(Maneno ya Muhimu) =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:peter|Peter]](Petero)**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:disciple|disciples]](wanafunzi)**
===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **deeply moved by**  - That is, "very troubled by" or, "felt very sad when they heard." To be "moved" is to feel strong emotions.
  * **Kusukumwa kwa undani na**  - Hio ni, "kusumbuliwa sana na" ama, "walihusunika sana waliposikia." kuwa na "msukumo" kuhisi hisia kali.
  * **Brothers**  - This was a normal way for a Jew to address fellow Jews. It could also be translated as, "Friends."
  * **Ndugu **- Hii ilikuwa ni hali ya kawaida kwa Wayahudi kutabulisha Wayahudi wenzao.  Inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Marafiki".
