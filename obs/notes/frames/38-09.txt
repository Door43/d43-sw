===== Jesus Prayed for Peter [38-09] Yesu Aliomba kwa Ajili ya Petero =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-09.jpg?nolink&}}

**Peter** replied, “Even if all the others **abandon you**, I will not!” Then **Jesus** said to Peter, “**Satan** wants **to have all of you**, but I have **prayed** for you, Peter, **that your faith will not fail**. Even so, tonight, **before the rooster crows**, three times you will deny that you even know me.”

**Petro** akawaambia, "Hata kama wengine wote **watakuacha**, mimi siwezi!" Kisha **Yesu** akamwambia Petro, "**Shetani** anataka kuwa na **nyinyi wote**, lakini **nimekuombea** wewe Petro, **kwamba imani yako haitaisha**. Hata hivyo, usiku huu **kabla jogoo hajawika**, mara tatu utakuwa umekana kwamba, hata hunijui."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **abandon you**  - Check to see how you translated this in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:38-08|[38-08]]]**.
  * **kuachana na wewe **- Angalia ili uone vile ulivyotafsiri hii pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:38-08|[38-08]]]**.
  * **to have all of you**  - That is, "to completely control you" or, "to get you to serve him completely." The word "you" is plural in this phrase. All of the words "you" and "your" that follow are singular.
  * **kuwa na nyinyi wote**  - Hiyo ni, "kuwadhibiti nyinyi kabisa" ama, "kuwafanya nyinyi kumtumikia kabisa." Neno "nyinyi" ni wingi katika msemo huu. Yote ya maneno "wewe" na "yako " inayofuata ni umoja.
  * **that your faith will not fail**  - That is, "that you will not stop believing in me."
  * **kwamba imani yenu haitaisha**  - Hiyo ni "kwamba hamutaacha kuniamini mimi."
  * **before the rooster crows**  - Roosters normally crow at the first light of the new day. If that is not clear, it may be helpful to say, "Before the rooster crows at dawn tomorrow" or, "before the rooster crows tomorrow morning."
  * **kabla ya jogoo kuwika**  - Kwa kawaida jogoo huwika katika mwanga wa kwanza wa siku mpya. Kama hiyo si wazi, inaweza kuwa na manufaa kusema, "Kabla jogoo hajawika alfajiri kesho" ama, "kabla jogoo hajawika kesho asubuhi."
