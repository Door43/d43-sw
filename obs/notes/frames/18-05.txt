===== Rehoboam Became King [18-05] Rehoboamu Akawa Mfalme *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-05.jpg?nolink&}}

After **Solomon** died, his son, **Rehoboam**, became **king**. Rehoboam was a foolish man. All the people of the **nation of Israel** came together to **confirm him as king**. They complained to Rehoboam that Solomon had made them do a lot of hard work and pay a lot of taxes.

Baada ya **Suleimani** kufa, mwanawe, **Rehoboamu**, akawa **mfalme**. Rehoboamu alikuwa mtu mpumbavu. Watu wote wa **taifa la Israeli ** walikuja pamoja **kumdhibitisha kama mfalme**. Walilalamika kwa Rehoboamu ya kuwa Suleimani alikuwa akiwafanyisha kazi nzito na kawafanya walipe kodi nyingi sana.

==== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri ====

  * **confirm him as king**  - That is, "tell him that they were glad he was king and that they would do what he said."
  * **Kumthibitisha yeye kama mfalme**  - Hiyo ni, "kumwambia ya kwamba walifurahia kuwa yeye ni mfalme na kuwa wangefanya kile atasema."
