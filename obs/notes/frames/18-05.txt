===== Rehoboam Became King [18-05] Rehoboamu Akawa Mfalme =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-05.jpg?nolink&}}

After **Solomon** died, his son, **Rehoboam**, became **king**. Rehoboam was a foolish man. All the people of the **nation of Israel** came together to **confirm him as king**. They complained to Rehoboam that Solomon had made them do a lot of hard work and pay a lot of taxes.

Baada ya Suleimani kukufa, mwanawe, Rehoboamu, akawa mfalme. Rehoboamu alikuwa mtu mpumbavu. Watu wote wa taifa la Israeli walikuja pamoja kumudhibitisha kama mfalme. Waliteta kwa Rehoboamu ya kuwa Suleimani alikuwa akiwafanya wafanye kazi nzito na kulipa kodi nyingi sana.

==== Translation Notes: Tafsiri ya Maelezo ====

  * **confirm him as king**  - That is, "tell him that they were glad he was king and that they would do what he said."
  * Kudhibitisha yeye kama mfalme - Hiyo ni, "kumwambia ya kwamba walifurahia yeye kuwa mfalme na eti wangefanya kile angesema.
