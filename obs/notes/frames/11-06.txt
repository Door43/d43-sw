===== Death of the Firstborn [11-06] Kifo cha mzaliwa wa kwanza *F =====

But the **Egyptians did not believe God or obey his commands**. So God **did not pass over** their houses. God killed every one of the Egyptians' firstborn sons.

Lakini **Wamisri hawakuamini Mungu au kutii amri zake**. Kwa hivyo Mungu **hakupita ** nyumba zao. Mungu aliua kila mmoja wa wazaliwa wa kwanza Wamisri.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **did not believe God or obey his commands**  â€“ In some languages it may be more natural or clear to say, "did not believe God and so they did not obey his commands."
  * **Hawakuamini Mungu au kutii amri zake**  - Kwa lugha zingine inaweza kuwa rahisi au wazi zaidi kusema, "hawakuamini Mungu na kwa hivyo hawakutii amri zake."
  * **did not pass over**  - He did not pass by their houses. Rather he stopped at each house and killed their eldest son.
  * **hakupita**  - Hakupita manyumba zao. Badala ya hiyo alisimama kwa kila nyumba na akaua mwanao mzaliwa wa kwanza.
