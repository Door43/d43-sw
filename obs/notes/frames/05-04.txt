===== God Promised Isaac's Birth [05-04] Mungu aliahidi kuzaliwa kwa Isaka * F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-04.jpg?nolink&}}

“Your wife, **Sarai**, will have a son—he will be the **son** of **promise**. Name him **Isaac**. **I will make my covenant with him**, and he will become a great nation. I will make **Ishmael** a great nation, too, but my covenant will be with Isaac.” Then **God** changed **Abram's** name to **Abraham**, which means “**father of many**.” God also changed Sarai's name to **Sarah**, which means “**princess**.”

"Mke wako, **Sarai**, atakuwa na mwana–atakuwa yule **mwana** wa agano. Muite jina lake **Isaka**. Nitafanya agano langu naye, na atafanyika taifa kubwa. Nitamfanya** Ishmaeli ** taifa kubwa pia lakini agano langu nitafanya na Isaka." Halafu Mungu akabadilisha jina la Abramu likawa **Ibrahimu**, ambayo inamaanisha "**baba wa wengi**." Mungu pia akabadilisha jina la Sarai likawa **Sara,** linaomaanisha "**binti wa mfalme**."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (God continues to speak to Abram.)
  * (Mungu anaendelea kunena na Abramu.)
  * **son of promise**  - Isaac would be the son that God promised to Sarai and Abram. He would also be the son that God would use to give Abram many descendants.
  * **mwana wa ahadi **- Isaka ndiye atakuwa mwana aliemuahidi Mungu kwa Sarai na Abramu. Yeye pia atakuwa mwana ambaye Mungu atatumia kumpea Abramu vizazi vingi.
  * **I will make my covenant with him**  - This would be the same covenant that God made with Abram.
  * **Nitafanya agano langu naye**  - Hii itakuwa ni ile tu agano Mungu alifanya na Abramu.
  * **father of many**  - As God had promised, Abraham would become the ancestor of many people who would even become many nations.
  * **Baba wa wengi **- Kama alivyoahidi Mungu, Ibrahimu atakuwa baba wa watu wengi na kufanyika pia mataifa mengi.
  * **princess**  - This word means a daughter of a king. The names Sarai and Sarah apparently both meant "princess." But God changed her name to signify that she would be the mother of many nations, and some of her descendants would be kings.
  * **binti wa mfalme**  - hili neno linamaanisha binti wa mfalme. Majina Sarai na Sara zote mbili zinamaanisha "binti wa mfalme." Lakini Mungu alibadilisha jina lake ikiwa ishara ya kuwa atakuwa mama wa mataifa mengi, na wengine kati ya watu wa uzao wake watakuwa wafalme.
