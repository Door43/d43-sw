===== The People Attacked the Believers [45-06] Watu Walishambulia Waaminio =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-06.jpg?nolink&}}

A young man named **Saul** agreed with the people who killed Stephen and **guarded their robes** while they threw stones at him. That day, many people in **Jerusalem** started **persecuting** the followers of Jesus, so the **believers** fled to other places. **But in spite of this**, they **preached** about Jesus everywhere they went.

Kijana aliyeitwa **Sauli ** alikubaliana na watu waliomuua Stefano na **kulinda mavasi yao** walipokuwa wakimrushia mawe. Siku hiyo, watu wengi katika **Yerusalemu ** walianza **kuwatesa ** wafuasi wa Yesu, kwa hivyo **waaminio** walitorokea mahali kwingineko. **Lakini licha ya hii,** **walihubiri ** kumhusu Yesu kila mahali walipoenda.

<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri):  **</font>

  * **guarded their robes - ** This could be translated as, "watched over their coats." He was probably making sure they didn't get stolen or damaged.
  * **kulinda mavasi yao - ** Hii inaweza tafsiriwa kama, "kuangalia mavasi yao." Pengine alikuwa anahakikisha ya kuwa haikuibiwa ama kuharibiwa.
  * **But in spite of this - ** The Jewish leaders thought they could stop the spread of Jesus' teaching by persecuting his followers. Instead, this caused them to scatter and spread the message even more widely.
  * ****lakini licha ya hii - ****Viongozi wa Wayahudi walidhania wangeweza kuzuia kuenea kwa mafundisho ya Yesu kwa kuwatesa wafuasi wake. Badala ya hiyo, hii iliwafanya watawanyike na kueneza ujumbe hata zaidi kwa kiasi kikubwa. ****
