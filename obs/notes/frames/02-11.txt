===== God Cursed the Man [02-11] Mungu alilaani Mwanamme =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-11.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** said to the man, “You listened to your wife and **disobeyed** me. Now the **ground is cursed**, and you will need to work hard to grow food. Then **you will die**, and your body will **return to dirt**.” The man named his wife **Eve**, **which means** “**life-giver**,” because she would become the **mother of all people**. And God clothed **Adam** and Eve with animal skins.

**Mungu** akasema kwa mwanamme, "Ulimsikiliza mke wako na **ukaniasi**. Sasa **ardhi imelaaniwa**, na utahitaji kufanya kazi kwa bidii kukuza chakula. **Halafu utakufa**, na mwili wako **utarudi mavumbini**." Mwanamme akamuita mke wake **Hawa, inayomaanisha "mtia-uzima**," kwa sababu atakuwa **mama wa watu wote**. Na Mungu akamvarisha **Adamu** na Hawa ngozi za wanyama.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **ground is cursed**  - As punishment for Adam's disobedience, the earth would no longer be fruitful. Adam would have to work very hard to raise enough food to eat.
  * **Ardhi imelaaniwa**  - kama adhabu kwa sababu ya kutotii kwa Adamu, ardhi haitazaa matunda. Adamu atalazimika kufanya kazi kwa bidii sana kupata chakula ya kutosha ya kukula.
  * **you will die**  - The ultimate punishment for their disobedience was death. Spiritual death is our separation from God. Physical death is our separation from our body.
  * **Utakufa**  - Adhabu ya mwisho kwa sababu ya kutotii ilukwa ni kifo. Kifo ya kiroho ni kutenganisha na Mungu. Kifo cha mwili ni kutenganishwa na mili yetu.
  * **return to dirt**  - God created Adam from the dirt or soil and gave him life. As a result of sin, his life will be taken from him and his body will decompose and become dirt again.
  * **Kurudi kwa uchafu **- Mungu alimuumba Adamu kutoka kwa udongo na akampa uhai. Kwa sababu ya dhambi, uhai wake utatolewa kwake na mwili wake utaoza na kufanyika vumbi tena.
  * **Eve, which means 'life-giver'**  - God gave life to Adam and Eve, and it is passed on to every person through childbirth.
  * **Hawa, inayomanisha "mwenye-uhai"**  - Mungu alitoa uhai kwa Adamu na Hawa, na ikapita kwa kila mtu kupitia kwa kuzaa watoto.
  * **mother of all people**  - This means that she would be the female ancestor of all people. Some languages say "she would be the grandmother of all people."
  * **Mama wa watu wote**  - Hii inamanisha kuwa atakuwa babu wa kike wa watu wote. Lugha zingine ninasema, "atakuwa nyanya wa kila mtu."
