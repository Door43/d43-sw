===== Firstborn Males Would Die [11-01] Wanaume wazaliwa wa kwanza watakufa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-01.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** warned **Pharaoh** that if he did not let the **Israelites** go, then he would kill all the **firstborn males of both people and animals**. When Pharaoh heard this he still refused to **believe** and **obey** God.

**Mungu** alimuonya **Farao** kuwa kama hatawaruhusu **wana wa Israeli ** waende, ataua **wazaliwa wa kwanza wa kiume, wanadamu na wanyama**. Wakati Farao alisikia hivyo bada alikataa **kuamini** na **kutii ** Mungu.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya tafsiri): ====

  * **the firstborn males of both people and animals**  - This could be translated as, "the eldest son in every family and the first male offspring of any of their animals."
  * **wazaliwa wa kwanza wa kiume, wanadamu na wanyama**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "mwana aliye wa kwanza katika kila jamii na uzao wa kwanza wa kila aina ya wanyama wao."
