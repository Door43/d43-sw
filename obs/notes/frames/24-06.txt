===== Jesus Is the Lamb of God [24-06] Yesu ndiye Kondoo wa Mungu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-06.jpg?nolink&}}

The next day, **Jesus** came to be **baptized** by **John**. When John saw him, he said, “Look! **There is** the **Lamb of God** who **will take away** the **sin of the world**.”

Siku iliyofuata, **Yesu ** akaja **kubatizwa** na **Yohana**. Wakati Yohana alimwona, akasema, "Tazama! **Ndiye yule** **Kondoo wa Mungu** **atakayeondoa** **dhambi za ulimwengu.**"

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **There is**  - For some languages this would be, "Here is" or, "That man is."
  * **Ndiye yule**  - Katika lugha zingine hii inaweza kuwa, "Ndiye huyu" ama, "Yule mwanamme ndiye."
  * **Lamb of God**  - This could also be translated as, "the Lamb from God" or, "the sacrifice Lamb that God has provided." Jesus was the perfect sacrifice for sin that God promised to provide. He fulfills the image that was presented by the sacrifice of lambs in the Old Testament.
  * **Kondoo wa Mungu**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kondoo kutoka kwa Mungu" ama, "kondoo wa dhabihu aliyetolewa na Mungu." Yesu alikuwa dhabihu iliyokamilika ya dhambi ambayo Mungu aliahidi kutoa. Anatimiza ile picha iliyoakilishwa na dhabihu za kondoo katika Agano la Kale.
  * **will take away**  - The sacrifice of Jesus causes God to look at our sin as if it never existed.
  * **ataondoa**  - Dhabihu ya Yesu inafanya Mungu kutazama dhambi zetu kana kwambi haikuweko.
  * **sin of the world**  - That is, "the sin of the people in the world."
  * **dhambi ya ulimwengu**  - Hiyo ni, "dhambi ya watu waliyo katika dunia."
