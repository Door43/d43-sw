===== The Religious Leader's Prayer [34-07] Maombi ya Yule Kiongozi wa Kindini =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-07.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **religious leader prayed like this**, ‘Thank you, **God**, that **I am not a sinner like** other men—such as robbers, **unjust men**, **adulterers**, or even like that **tax collector**.’”

"Yule **kiongozi wa kindini akaomba hivi**, "Asante, **Mungu**, kuwa **mimi si mwenye dhambi kama** watu wengine--kama wezi, **watu wenye kudhulumu, wazinifu**, au hata kama huyu **mtoza** **ushuru." **

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na ile hadithi.)
  * **The religious leader prayed like this**  - Another way to say this would be, "This is how the religious leader prayed" or, "The religious leader prayed in this way."
  * **Yule kiongozi wa kindini aliomba hivi **- Njia ingine ya kusema hili inaweza kuwa, "Hivi ndivyo yule kiongozi wa kindini aliomba" ama, "Yule kiongozi wa kindini aliomba hivi."
  * **I am not a sinner like**  - That is, "I am not sinful like" or, "I am righteous, not like."
  * **mimi si mwenye dhambi kama**  - Hiyo ni, "Mimi si mwenye dhambi kama" ama, "mimi ni mwenye haki, siyo kama."
  * **unjust men**  - That is, "men who are not righteous" or, "people who do evil things" or, "law breakers."
  * **watu wa kudhulumu**  - Hiyo ni, "watu wasiyo wenye haki" ama, "watu wanaotenda maovu" ama, "wavunjaji sheria."
