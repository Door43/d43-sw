===== Two Trees [01-11] Miti Miwili =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-11.jpg?nolink&}}

**In the middle** of the **garden**, God planted two special trees—the **tree of life** and the **tree of the knowledge of good and evil**. God told Adam that he could eat from any tree in the garden except from the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. If he ate from this tree, he would **die**.

**Katikati ya bustani** lile, Mungu akapanda miti miwili spesheli–**mti**** wa uzima** na **mtu wa ujuzi wa mema na mabaya**. Mungu akamwambia Adamu anaweza kula kutoka kwa kila mti uliomo bustani, isipokuma mti wa ujuzi wa mema na mabaya. Akikula kutoka kwa mti huu **atakufa**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **in the middle**  - The central location emphasizes the importance of the two trees.
  * **Katikati **- Sehemu ya katikati inatia mkazo umuhimu wa ile miti miwili.
  * **garden**  - An area of land planted with trees and plants for a purpose–usually to produce food or to provide beauty.
  * **Bustani**  - Sehemu ya ardhi iliyopandwa miti na mimea kwa kusudi - mala nyingi inapandwa ikatoe chakula inayotoa na urembo wake.
  * **tree of life**  - Anyone who ate fruit from this tree would never die.
  * **Mti wa uzima**  - Yeyote aliyekula kwa mti huu hangekufa.
  * **tree of knowledge of good and evil**  - The fruit of this tree could enable a person to know both good and evil.
  * **Mti wa ujuzi wa mema na mabaya**- matunda ya mti huu ungewezesha mtu kujua mema na mabaya.
  * **knowledge**  - To know or understand by personal experience.
  * **Ujuzi**  - Kujua ama kuelewa kwa uzoefu wa kibinafsi.
  * **good and evil**  - Evil is the opposite of good. Just as "good" refers to what is pleasing to God, "evil" refers to everything that is not pleasing to God.
  * **Mema na mabaya**  - mabaya ni kinyume cha mema. Kama vile "mema" inahusu yanayomfurahisha Mungu, "mabaya" inahusu yasiyomfurahisa Mungu.
  * **die**  - In this instance, he would die both physically and spiritually.
  * **Kufa**  - Katika mfano huu, mwanadamu angekufa kimwili na kiroho.
