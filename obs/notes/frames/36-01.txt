===== To the Mountain [36-01] Kwa Mlima =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-01.jpg?nolink&}}

One day, Jesus took three of his disciples, Peter, James, and John with him. (The disciple named John was not the same person who baptized Jesus.) They went up on a high mountain by themselves to pray.

Siku moja, Yesu alichukua watatu kati ya wanafunzi wake, Petero, Yakobo na Yohana, pamoja naye. (Yule mwanafunzi aitwaye Yohana hakuwa yule yule mtu ambaye alimbatiza Yesu). Wakapanda juu ya mlima mrefu peke yao kuomba.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **One day**  - This phrase introduces an event that happened in the past, but does not state the specific time. Many languages have a similar way to begin telling a true story.
  * **Siku moja**  - Msemo huu unatanguliza tukio ambalo lilifanyika katika siku zilizopita, lakini haielezi wakati wake haswa. Lugha nyingi zina njia tofauti ya kuanzia hadithi iliyo ya kweli.
  * **James**  - This is a different James than the one who wrote the Book of James in the Bible. To make this clear, some languages may need to use two slightly different names or to spell them differently.
  * **Yakobo**  - Huyu Yakobo ni tofauti na yule alieandika kitabu cha Yakobo iliyo katika Biblia. Ili haya yawe wazi, lugha zingine zaweza kutumia majina mawili iliyo tofauti kiasi au kuiandika majina haya mawili kitofauti.
