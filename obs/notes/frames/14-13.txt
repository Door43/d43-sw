===== Moses Struck the Rock [14-13] Musa alipiga mwamba *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-13.jpg?nolink&}}

Another time when the people did not have any water, **God** told **Moses**, “Speak to the rock, and water will come out of it.” But **Moses dishonored God** in front of all the people **by hitting the rock twice with a stick instead of speaking to it**. Water came out of the rock for everyone to drink, but God was angry with Moses and said, “You will not enter the **Promised Land**.”

Wakati mwingine watu wakawa hawana maji yoyote, **Mungu** akamwambia **Musa**, "Nena na mwamba, na maji itatoka ndani yake." Lakini Musa **alimkosea Mungu heshima** mbele ya watu wote kwa **kupiga mwamba mara mbili na kijiti badala ya kuiongelesha**. Maji ilitoka ndani ya mwamba ili kila mtu anywe, na Mungu akakasirikia Musa na kusema, "hautaingia **Nchi ya Ahadi**."

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **Moses dishonored God**  - This could be translated, "Moses disobeyed God" or, "Moses disrespected God." God had a specific way He wanted Moses to show the people God's power to provide for them. When Moses disobeyed God by doing it in a different way he showed a lack of respect for God.
  * **Musa alimkosea Mungu heshima**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa, "Musa hakutii Mungu" ama, "Musa hakuheshimu Mungu." Mungu alikuwa na njia maalum aliyotaka Musa atumie kuonyesha watu nguvu za Mungu za kukutana na mahitaji yao. Wakati Musa alikataa kutii Mungu kwa kufanya kwa njia tofauti iliyonyesha kukosea Mungu heshima.
  * **by hitting the rock twice with a stick instead of speaking to it**  - This can be translated as, "Moses did not speak to the rock; he hit it twice with a stick."
  * **kwa kupiga mwamba mala mbili na kijiti badala ya kuinenea**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Musa hakuongea na mwamba; aliipiga mara mbili na kijiti."
