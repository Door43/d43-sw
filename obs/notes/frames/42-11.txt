===== Jesus Went to Heaven [42-11] Yesu Alienda Mbinguni =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Forty days after **Jesus** rose from the dead, he told his **disciples**, “Stay in **Jerusalem** until my **Father** gives you **power** when the **Holy Spirit comes on you**.” Then Jesus went up to **heaven**, and **a cloud hid him from their sight**. Jesus sat down **at the right hand of God** to rule **over all things**.

Siku arobaini baada ya **Yesu** kufufuka kutoka kwa wafu, akawaambia **wanafunzi** wake, "Kaeni huko **Yerusalemu** mpaka **Baba** yangu awape ninyi **nguvu** wakati **Roho Mtakatifu** **atakapowashukieni ninyi.**" Yesu alipaa **mbinguni,** na **wingu likampokea kutoka machoni pao.** Yesu akaketi **upande wa kulia wa Mungu** kutawala **juu ya kila kitu.**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **comes on you**  - That is, "comes down upon you" or, "comes onto you."
  * **kuwajilia juu yenu**  - Hiyo ni, "atashuka juu yenu" ama, "atakuja ndani yenu."
  * **a cloud hid him from their sight**  - This could also be translated as, "he disappeared into a cloud."
  * **wingu likampokea kutoka machoni pao **- Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "alitoweka kwa kuingia katika wingu."
  * **at the right hand of God**  - This could also be translated as, "at the right side of God."
  * **upande wa kulia wa Mungu**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "upande wa kulia wa Mungu."
  * **over all things**  - That is, "over everything."
  * **juu ya vitu vyote**  - Hiyo ni, "juu ya kila kitu."
