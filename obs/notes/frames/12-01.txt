===== Israel Left Egypt [12-01] Israeli katoka Misri *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-01.jpg?nolink&}}

The **Israelites** were very happy to leave **Egypt**. **They were no longer slaves**, and they were **going** to the **Promised Land**! The **Egyptians** gave the Israelites whatever they asked for, even gold and silver and other valuable things. Some people from other nations **believed** in **God** and went along with the Israelites as they left Egypt.

**Waisraeli ** walifurahia sana kutoka **Misri**. **Hawakua watumwa tena**, na walikuwa **wakienda** kwenye **nchi ya ahadi**. **Wamisri** waliwapatia Waisraeli chochote walichouliza, hata dhahabu na fedha na vitu vingine vya dhamana. Watu wengine kutoka mataifa mengine **walimuamini** **Mungu** na walienda pamoja na Waisraeli walipotoka Misri.
