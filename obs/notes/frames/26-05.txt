===== The Ministry of Elijah [26-05] Huduma ya Elia =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Jesus** said, “It is **true** that **no prophet is accepted in his own hometown**. During the time of the prophet **Elijah**, there were many widows in **Israel**. But when it did not rain for three and a half years, **God** did not send Elijah to help a widow from Israel, but rather to a widow from a different nation.”

Kisha **Yesu** akasema, "Ni **kweli** kuwa **hakuna nabii anayekubaliwa kwa mji wa kwao nyumbani**. Wakati wa nabii **Elia**, kulikuwa na wajane wengi **Israeli**. Lakini wakati mvua haikunyesha kwa miaka mitatu na nusu, **Mungu** hakutuma Elia kusaidia mjane wa Israeli, lakini badala ya hiyo alimtuma kwa mjane kutoka taifa lingine."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **no prophet is accepted in his hometown**  - This phrase means, "people do not recognize the authority of a prophet that has grown up among them."
  * **hakuna nabii anayekubaliwa katika mjii wa kwao nyumbani **- Huu msemo unamaanisha, "watu hawatambui mamlaka ya nabii ambaye amekua pamoja nao."
