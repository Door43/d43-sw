===== God Will Provide [05-07] Mungu atapeana =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-07.jpg?nolink&}}

As I**braham** and **Isaac walked to the place of sacrifice**, Isaac asked, “Father, we have **wood for the sacrifice**, but where is **the lamb**?” Abraham replied, “**God** will provide the lamb for the sacrifice, my son.”

Wakati** Ibrahimu** na **Isaka walipokuwa wanatembea kuelekea mahali pa kutoa dhabihu**, Isaka akauliza, "Baba, tuko na **kuni za kuchoma dhabihu**, lakini **kondoo** yuko wapi? Ibrahimu akajibu, "**Mungu** atampeana kondoo wa kutoa dhabihu, mwanangu."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **walked to the place of sacrifice**  - God had told Abraham to sacrifice Isaac on a special high hill that was about three days' walk from where they lived.
  * **walitembea kuelekea pahali pa kutoa dhabihu**- Mungu alikuwaa amemwambia Ibrahimu amtoe Isaka kama dhabihu katika mlima mrefu maalum ambayo ingechukua siku tatu kutembea kutoka mahali walipoishi.
  * **wood for the sacrifice**  - For a sacrifice, normally the lamb was killed and then placed on top of wood so that the wood and the lamb could be burned up with fire.
  * **Kuni ya kuchoma dhabihu**
- Kwa miamba ya dhabihu, kawaida kondoo aliuliwa na kuekelewa juu ya kuni ndio ile kuni na kondoo ziteketezwe na moto.

  * **the lamb**  - A young sheep or goat would be the normal animal for a sacrifice.
  * **kondoo**  -mwana kondoo mchanga ama mbuzi angekuwa mnyama wa kawaida wa kutoa dhabihu.
  * **provide**  - Abraham may have believed that Isaac was the "lamb" that God provided, although God fulfilled Abraham's words by providing a ram to sacrifice in Isaac's place.
  * **Kupeana**  - Yawezekana Ibrahimu aliamini kuwa Isaka ndiye kondoo Mungu aliempeana, ijapokuwa Mungu alitimiza maneno ya Ibrahimu kwa kupeana kondoo wa kiume kumtoa dhabihu katiaka nafasi ya Isaka.
