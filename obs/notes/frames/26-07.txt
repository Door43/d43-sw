===== The Attempt to Kill Jesus [26-07] Jaribio la Kumuua Yesu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-07.jpg?nolink&}}

The people of **Nazareth** dragged **Jesus** out of the **place of worship** and brought him to the edge of a cliff to throw him off of it in order to kill him. **But Jesus walked through the crowd** and left the town of Nazareth.

Watu wa **Nazareti ** walimkokota **Yesu** nje  ya pahali pa ibada na wakamleta katika mpaka wa jabali ili wamutupe hapo na kumuua. **Lakini Yesu akatembea akipitia katikati ya umati wa watu ** na akatoka kwa mji wa Nazareti.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **place of worship**  - That is, "the building where the Jewish people gathered to worship God." This can also be translated as "worship building." See how you translated it in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]**.
  * **pahali pa ibada**  - Hiyo ni, "nyumba ambayo Wayahudi wanakusanyika ili waabudu Mungu." Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama "jengo la ibada." Ona ulivyotafsiri hii pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]**.
  * **But Jesus walked through the crowd**  - "But" could be translated with a strong contrast word or phrase such as "But instead" or, "However" to show that the people were not able to do to Jesus what they had planned to do.
  * **Lakini Yesu alitembea akipitia katikati ya umati wa watu**- "Lakini" inaweza kutafsiriwa ukitumia neno kali la kutofautisha kama, "lakini badala yake" ama, "hata hivyo" ili kuonyesha kuwa watu hawakuweza kumfanyia Yesu kile walikuwa wamepanga.
