===== A Baptism of Repentance [24-03] Ubatizo wa Toba =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-03.jpg?nolink&}}

When people heard **John's** message, many of them **repented from their sins**, and John **baptized** them. Many **religious leaders** also came to be baptized by John, but they **did not repent** or **confess their sins**.

Wakati watu walisikia ujumbe wa **Yohana**, wengi **walitubu dhambi zao**, na Yohana **aliwabatiza**. Wengi wa **viongozi wa kindini** pia walikuja kubatizwa na Yohana, lakini **hawakutubu** wala **kukiri dhambi zao**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **repented from their sins**  - This could also be translated as, "repented concerning their sins" or, "changed their minds about their sins" or, "turned away from their sins."
  * **walitubu dhambi zao**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "walitubu kuhusu dhambi zao" ama, "walibadilisha mawazo yao kuhusu dhambi zao" ama, "waligeuka mbali na dhambi zao."
  * **did not repent**  - That is, "did not turn away from their sins."
  * **hawakutubu**  - Hiyo ni, "hawakugeukia dhambi zao."
  * **confess their sins**  - To confess is to acknowledge that something is true. These leaders did not want to acknowledge that they had sinned. This may be translated as, "confess that they had sinned."
  * **kiri dhambi zao**  - Kukiri ni kukubali kuwa jambo ni kweli. Hawa viongozi hawakutaka kukubali kuwa wametenda dhambi. Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kiri kwamba wametenda dhambi."
