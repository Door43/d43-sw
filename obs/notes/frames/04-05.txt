===== To Canaan [04-05] Kuelekea Kanani *F =====

So **Abram obeyed God**. **He took** his wife, **Sarai**, together with all of his **servants** and everything he owned and went to the land **God showed him**, **the land of Canaan**.

Kwa hivyo **Abramu akamtii Mungu**. **Akamchukua** Mke wake, **Sarai,** pamoja na **watumishi ** wake wote, na mali yake yote na akaenda kwa nchi **aliomuonyesha** **Mungu**,** nchi ya Kanani. **

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **He took**  â€“ Some languages would say, "He brought" here. Others would use two different verbs here, such as, "He caused his wife to come with him" and, "He brought along all of their servants and possessions."
  * **Alichukua**  - Lugha zingine zitasema, "aliwaleta" hapa. Zingine zitatumia maneno mawili hapa, kama hivi, "alimfanya mke wake aje na yeye" na, "alileta pamoja naye watumishi na mali zao zote."
  * **God showed him**  - Somehow God made it clear to Abram where he was to go. The text doesn't tell how God showed him.
  * **Mungu alimuonyesha**  - Kwa namna fulani Mungu alimuonyesha wazi Abramu mahali ya aliokuwa aende. Maandiko haielezi jinsi alivyomueleza.
  * **land of Canaan**  - The name of this land was "Canaan". This could be translated, "the land called Canaan."
  * **Nchi ya Kanani **- Jina ya nchi hii ilikuwa "Kanani." Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "nchi inayoitwa Kanani."
