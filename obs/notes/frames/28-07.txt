===== Who Can Be Saved? [28-07] Nani Anaweza Kuokoka? =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-07.jpg?nolink&}}

When the **disciples** heard what Jesus said, they were **shocked** and said, “Then **who can be saved**?”

Wakati **wanafunzi** walisikia kile Yesu alisema, **walishangaa** na kusema, "**Nani basi anaweza Kuokolewa?**"
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:disciple|disciples (wanafunzi)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:save|saved (kuokolewa)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **shocked**  - That is, "greatly astonished."
  * **walishangaa**  - Hiyo ni, "walitishika sana."
  * **who can be saved**  - This could also be translated as, "If even rich people cannot be saved, how can anyone else be saved?" Many people believed that being rich was a sign of God's favor.
  * **nani anweza kuokolewa**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Kama hata watu matajiri hawawezi kuokolewa, itawezekana je mtu mwingine kuokolewa?"
  * **saved**  - Here it refers to being saved from God's judgment and condemnation for sin and being allowed to be a citizen in God's kingdom.
  * **kuokolewa**  - Hii inaeleza kuhusu kuokolewa kutoka kwa hukumu na lawama ya Mungu kwa sababu ya dhambi na kuruhusiwa kuwa raia wa katika ufalme wa Mungu.
