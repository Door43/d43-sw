===== Remain Faithful [50-04] Iweni Waaminifu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-04.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** also said, “A **servant** is** not greater than his master**. Just as the authorities of this world have hated me, they will torture and kill you **because of me**. Although **in this world** you will **suffer**, be encouraged because I have defeated **Satan**, the one who rules this world. If you **remain faithful to me to the end**, then **God will save you**!”

**Yesu** pia alisema, "**Mtumwa** si **mkuu kuliko bwana wake**. Kama vile watawala wa ulimwengu huu wamenichukia mimi, watawatesa na kuwaua **kwa sababu yangu**. Ingawa **katika dunia hii ** mtapata **mateso**, jipeni moyo kwa sababu mimi nimemshinda **Shetani,** anaye tawala dunia hii. Kama **mtakuwa waaminifu kwangu hadi mwisho**, basi **Mungu atawaokoa!"**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **not greater than**  - That is, "not more important than" or, in this case, "not treated better than."
  * **si mkuu zaidi kuliko**  - Hiyo ni, "si muhimu zaidi ya" ama, katika kesi hii, "hawatendewi bora kuliko."
  * **because of me**  - That is, "because you obey me" or, "because you teach people about me" or, "because you belong to me."
  * **sababu yangu**  - Hiyo ni, "kwa sababu wewe unanitii" ama, "kwa sababu wewe hufundisha watu kunihusu" ama, "kwa sababu wewe ni mali yangu."
  * **in this world**  - This could also be translated as, "in this lifetime."
  * **katika dunia hii **- Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "katika maisha haya."
  * **remain faithful to me**  - That is, "Keep obeying me."
  * **iweni waaminifu kwangu**  - Hiyo ni, "mnitii mimi."
  * **to the end**  - That is, "to the end of your life."
  * **hadi mwisho**  - Hiyo ni, "hadi mwisho wa maisha yako."
  * **will save you**  - This refers to spiritual salvation rather than physical deliverance from harm. It has already been stated that many believers will be killed or tortured.
  * **itakuokoa**  - Hii inamaanisha ukombozi wa kiroho badala ya ukombozi wa kimwili kutokana na madhara. Tayari tumeambiwa kuwa waumini wengi watauawa au kuteswa.
