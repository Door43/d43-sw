===== We Must Celebrate [35-13] Lazima Tusherehekee =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-13.jpg?nolink&}}

“The father answered, ‘My **son**, you are always with me, and everything I have is yours. But **it is right for us** to celebrate, because **your brother was dead**, **but now is alive**. **He was lost**, **but now is found**!’”

"Baba akajibu, "**Mwanangu**, kila wakati uko pamoja nami, na kila kitu niko nacho ni chako. Lakini **ni haki kwetu** kusherehekea, kwa sababu **ndugu yako alikuwa amekufa, lakini sasa yu hai. Alikuwa amepotea, lakini sasa amepatikana!'"**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **it is right for us**  - That is, "it is the right thing for us to do" or, "it is proper for us."
  * **ni haki kwetu**  - Hiyo ni, "ndio jambo la haki kwetu kufanya" ama, "ni sahihi kwetu."
  * **your brother**  - The father refers to his younger son as, "your brother" to remind the older son of his relationship to the younger son and of how he should love his younger brother.
  * **ndugu yako**  - Baba anamuita yule mwana mdogo, "ndugu yako" kumkubusha ndugu mkubwa uhusiano wake na mwanaye aliye mdogo na jinsi anastahili kumpenda ndugu yake.
  * **was dead, but now is alive**  - See how you translated this in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:35-09|[35-09]]]**.
  * **alikuwa amekufa, sasa yu hai **- Ona vile ulivyotafsiri hii pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:35-09|[35-09]]]**.
  * **He was lost, but now is found!**  - See how you translated this in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:35-09|[35-09]]]**.
  * **Alikuwa amepotea, lakini sasa amepatikana!**  - Ona vile ulivyotafsiri hii pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:35-09|[35-09]]]**.
