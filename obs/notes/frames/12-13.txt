===== Israel Rejoiced [12-13] Israeli wakafurahi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-13.jpg?nolink&}}

The **Israelites** also **rejoiced with much excitement** because **God** had **saved** them **from death and slavery**! Now they were **free to serve** God. The Israelites sang many songs to celebrate their new freedom and to **praise God** because he saved them from the **Egyptian** army.

**Waisraeli ** pia **wakafurahi na msisimuko mwingi ** kwa sababu** Mungu amewaokoa kutoka kwa kifo na utumwa**!  Sasa walikuwa **huru kumutumikia Mungu**. Waisraeli wakaimba nyimbo nyingi za kusherehekea uhuru wao mpya na **kusifu Mungu** kwa sababu aliwaokoa kutoka kwa jeshi **la Misri**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **rejoiced with much excitement**  - This could be translated as, "were very happy and they showed it enthusiastically" or, "showed it with their whole hearts" or, "with all their strength."
  * **furahia na msisimuko mwingi**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "walifurahia na wakaionyesha kwa shauku" ama, "waliionyesha na mioyo yao yote" ama, "na nguvu zao zote."
  * **from death and slavery**  - This could be translated as, "from being killed or made slaves by the Egyptians."
  * kutoka kwa kifo na utumwa - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kutoka kwa kuuawa ama kufanywa watumwa na Wamisri."
  * **free to serve**  - God freed, or rescued, the Israelites from being slaves in Egypt so that they could serve him.
  * **Huru kutumika**  - Mungu aliwaweka huru, ama kuwaokoa, Waisraeli kutoka kwa kuwa watumwa Misri ili wakaweze kumutumikia."
  * **praise God**  - In some languages this could be translated as, "lift up God's name" or, "say that God is great."
  * **Sifu Mungu**  - Kwa lugha zingine hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "inua jina la Mungu" ama "sema kuwa Mungu ni mkuu."
