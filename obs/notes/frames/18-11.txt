===== The Evil Kings of Israel [18-11] Wafalme waovu wa Israeli =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-11.jpg?nolink&}}

In the new **kingdom** of **Israel**, all the **kings** were **evil**. Many of these kings were killed by other **Israelites** who wanted to become king in their place.

Katika **ufalme** mpya wa **Israeli**, **wafalme** wote walikuwa **wabaya**. Wengi wa wafalme hawa waliuawa na **Waisraeli** wengine waliotaka kufanyika wafalme mahali pao.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **Israelites**  - Here, "Israelites" refers only to the people living in the northern kingdom of Israel, not to the people living in the southern kingdom of Judah.
  * **Waisraeli **- Hapa, "Waisraeli" inaongea kuhusu watu walioshi katika ufalme wa kaskazini wa Israeli, sio watu walioishi ufalme ulio kusini, ya Yuda.
