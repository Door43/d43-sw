===== The Debt Is Canceled [29-04] Madeni Kufutwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-04.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **servant fell on his knees before the king** and said, ‘Please be patient with me, and I will pay the full amount that I owe you.’ The king **felt pity** for the servant, so he **canceled all of his debt** and let him go.”

"Yule **mtumishi akaanguka kwa mabega yake mbele ya mfalme** na kusema, 'Tafadhali kuwa mvumilivu nami, na nitakulipa pesa yote unayo nidai.' Mfalme **alimuonea huruma** yule mtumishi, kwa hivyo **alifutilia mbali ile deni yake yote** na kumuachilia."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **fell on his knees**  - That is, "quickly knelt down on the ground." This was a way to show his humility and his desire for the king to help him. Make sure it doesn't sound like he fell down accidentally.
  * **alianguka kwa mabega yake**  - Hiyo ni, "kwa haraka alipiga magoti." Hii ilikuwa njia ya kuonyesha unyenyekevu na kutamani kwake kupata msaada wake. Hakikisha haitakaa kana kwamba alianguka ki ajali.
  * **before the king**  - This means, "in front of the king."
  * **mbele ya mfalme**  - Hii inamaanisha, "mbele ya mfalme."
  * **felt pity**  - That is, "felt compassion for" or, "felt sorry for." The king knew that the servant and his family would suffer greatly if they were sold into slavery.
  * **alimuonea huruma**  - Hiyo ni, "aliona huruma kwa" ama, "aliona pole kwa." Mfalme alijua kuwa mtumishi na jamii yake wataambika sana wakiuzwa kwa utumwa."
  * **canceled all of his debt**  - This can be translated as, "said that the servant did not need to pay back any of the money that he owed the king."
  * **alifutilia mbali deni yake yote**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "akasema kwamba yule mtumishi hatahitaji kurundisha pesa ile aliyekuwa anamdai mfalme.
