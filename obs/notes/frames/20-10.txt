===== God Remembered His Promise [20-10] Mungu Alikubuka Ahadi Yake =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-10.jpg?nolink&}}

Even though **God punished** his people for their **sin** by taking them away into **exile**, he **did not forget** them or his **promises**. God continued to **watch over** his people and **speak to them through his prophets**. He promised that, after seventy years, they would return to the **Promised Land** again.

Hata ingawa **Mungu aliadhibu** watu wake kwa sababu ya **dhambi** zao na kuwapeleka **uhamishoni**, **hakuwasahau** wao au **ahadi** zake. Mungu aliendelea **kuwaangalia** watu wake na **kunena nao kupitia kwa manabii wake**. Aliahidi kuwa, baada ya miaka sabini, watarudi kwa **Nchi ya Ahadi**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **exile**  - See how you translated this term in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]**.
  * **uhamisho**  - Ona ulivyotafsiri hili neno pale **[20-09].**
  * **did not forget**  - This could be translated as, "did not ignore" or, "did not neglect." Or the whole phrase could be translated as, "continued to honor his commitment to his people and his promises."
  * **hakuwasahau **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "hakupuuza" ama, "wakuwaachilia."ama msemo wote unaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "aliendelea kuheshimu kujitoa kwa ajiri ya watu wake na ahadi zake."
  * **watch over**  - That is, "take care of."
  * **kuwaangalia**  - Hiyo ni, "kuwatunza."
  * **speak to them through his prophets**  - This could be translated as, "tell his prophets messages that he wanted them to tell his people."
  * **kuongea nao kupitia kwa manabii**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuwapa manabii wake ujumbe aliyotaka waambie watu wake."
