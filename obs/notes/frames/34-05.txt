===== The Perfect Pearl [34-05] Lulu iliyo Kamilifu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-05.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **kingdom of God** is also like a **perfect pearl of great value**. When a **pearl merchant** found it, he sold all that he had and used the money to buy it.”

"**Ufalme wa Mungu** pia ni kama **lulu iliyo kamilifu na yenye dhamana kubwa.** Wakati **mfanya biashara wa lulu** aliipata, aliuza kila kitu ambacho alikuwa nacho na akatumia hiyo pesa kuinunua."
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:kingdom-of-god|kingdom of God (ufalme wa Mungu)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues with another story about the kingdom of God.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi ingine kuhusu ufalme wa Mungu.)
  * **perfect pearl**  - That is, "a pearl without any defects."
  * **lulu iliyo kamilifu**  - Hiyo ni, "lulu ambayo haina kasoro."
  * **pearl**  - If pearls are unknown, this can be translated as, "beautiful stone" or, "beautiful stone-like thing."
  * **lulu **- Kama lulu haijulikani, hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "jiwe zuri" ama, "kitu kizuri ambacho kinakaa kama jiwe."
  * **of great value**  - That is, "that was very valuable" or, "that was worth a lot of money."
  * **ya thamana kubwa**  - Hiyo ni, "iliyo kuwa  ya muhimu sana" ama, "iliyo kuwa na thamani kubwa ya fedha."
  * **pearl merchant**  - That is, "pearl dealer" or, "pearl trader." This refers to a person whose business is buying and selling pearls.
  * **mfanyi biashara wa lulu**  - Hiyo ni, "muuzaji lulu" ama, "mfanyabiashara wa lulu." Hii inaongea kuhusu mtu ambaye biashara yake ni kununua na kuuza lulu.
