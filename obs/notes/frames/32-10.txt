===== Jesus Left [32-10] Yesu Aliondoka =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-10.jpg?nolink&}}

The **people** were very **afraid** and asked **Jesus** to leave. So Jesus got into the boat and **prepared** to leave. The man who used to have the demons **begged to go along with Jesus**.

**Watu waliogopa** sana na wakamuuliza **Yesu** aondoke. Kwa hivyo Yesu akaingia kwa mashua na **akajiandaa** kuondoka. Yule mtu ambaye aliwekwa huru kutoka kwa mapepo **akaomba aende na Yesu.**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **people**  - Some languages may prefer to say, "people from that region" or, "the Gerasene people."
  * **watu**  - Lugha zingine zinaweza pendelea kusema, "watu wa eneo hilo" ama, "Watu wa Gesari."
  * **afraid**  - It may be better to say, "afraid because of what Jesus had done."
  * **waliogopa**  - Inaweza kuwa bora kusema, "waliogopa kwa sababu ya kile Yesu alifanya."
  * **prepared**  - That is, "was getting ready."
  * **alijiandaa**  - Hiyo ni, "alikuwa anajitayarisha."
  * **begged to go along with Jesus**  - That is, "begged Jesus to let him go with him" or, "earnestly asked Jesus if he could go with him."
  * **aliomba kwenda pamoja na Yesu**  - Hiyo ni, "alisihi Yesu amruhusu aende naye" ama, "kwa bidii aliuliza Yesu kama anaweza kuenda na yeye."
