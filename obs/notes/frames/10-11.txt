Then **God sent darkness that lasted for three days**. It was so dark that the **Egyptians** could not leave their houses. But there was light where the **Israelites** lived.

Halafu **Mungu akaleta ngiza kwa siku tatu mfululizo**. Ngiza ilikuwa kubwa kiasi ya kwamba **Wamisri ** hawangetoka kwa manyumba zao. Lakini kulikuwa na mwangaza mahali **W****aisraeli**** ** walikuwa wanaishi.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya tafsiri): ====

  * **God sent darkness**  - God caused darkness to cover or spread over most of Egypt. In other words, God took away the light from this part of Egypt.
  * **Mungu alileta ngiza**  - Mungu alifanya ngiza kufunika ama kuenea karibu kila mahali Misri. Kwa maneno mengine, Mungu aliondoa mwanga kutoka sehemu hiyo ya Misri.
  * **darkness that lasted for three days**  - This darkness was darker than ordinary nighttime darkness, and it continued to be totally dark for three whole days.
  * **ngiza iliyo kaa siku tatu**  - Hii ngiza ilikuwa nyeusi kuliko ngiza wa kawaida ya usiku na iliendelea mfululizo kwa siku tatu.
