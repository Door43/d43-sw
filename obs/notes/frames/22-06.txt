===== Mary and Elizabeth [22-06] Mariamu na Elisabeti =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Soon after the **angel** spoke to **Mary**, she went and visited **Elizabeth**. **As soon as Elizabeth heard Mary’s greeting**, Elizabeth’s **baby jumped inside her**. The women rejoiced together about **what God had done for them**. After Mary visited Elizabeth for three months, Mary returned home.

Upesi tu baada ya **malaika** kunena na **Mariamu**, alienda kumtembelea **Elisabeti**. **Baada tu ya Elisabeti kusikia salamu za Mariamu**, **mtoto wa Elisabeti akaruka ndani yake.** Wale wanawake wakafurahia pamoja kuhusu **kile Mungu amefanyia**. Baada ya Mariamu kumtembelea Elisabeti kwa miezi mitatu, Mariamu akarudi nyumbani.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Elizabeth**  - See the note about Elizabeth on **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:22-05|[22-04]]]**.
  * **Elisabeti **- Ona maelezo kuhusu Elisabeti pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:22-05|[22-04]]]**.
  * **As soon as Elizabeth heard Mary's greeting**  - For some languages it may be better to say, "Mary greeted Elizabeth and as soon as Elizabeth heard her."
  * **Baada ya Elisabeti kusikia salamu za Mariamu**  - Katika lugha zingine itakuwa rahisi kusema, "Mariamu alisalimia Elisabeti na wakati tu Elisabeti alimusikia."
  * **baby jumped inside her**  - The baby moved suddenly inside Elizabeth's womb in response to Mary's greeting to Elizabeth.
  * **mtoto aliruka ndani yake**  - Mtoto alisonga mara moja ndani ya tumbo ya Elisabeti baada ya kusikia salamu za Elisabeti kwa Mariamu.
  * **what God had done for them**  - This refers to the fact that both women were pregnant through God's supernatural intervention. Mary had conceived without a man, and Elizabeth had conceived with Zechariah after she was past the age of childbearing.
  * **Kile Mungu alikuwa amewafanyia**  - Hii inaelezea kuhusu ukweli kuwa wale wanawake wawili walikuwa waja wazito kupitia kwa miujiza ya Mungu. Mariamu alikuwa mja mzito bila mwanamme, na Elisabeti alikuwa amepata mimba na Zakaria akiwa amepitisha umri wa kupata watoto.
