===== A Man with Evil Spirits [32-02] Mtu mwenye Maroho Chafu =====

When they reached the other side of the lake, **a demon-possessed man came running up to Jesus**.

Walipofika upande ule mwingine wa bahari, **mtu aliyepagawa na mapepo akaja kwa Yesu anakimbia. **

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **a demon-possessed man**  - That is, "a man who had demons in him" or, "a man who was controlled by evil spirits."
  * **mtu aliyepagawa mapepo**  - Hiyo ni, "mtu ambaye alikuwa na mapepo ndani yake" ama, "mtu aliyeongozwa na maroho mabaya."
  * **came running up to**  - That is, "ran to" or, "ran and stopped in front of."
  * **akaja kwake anakimbia**  - Hiyo ni, "akakimbia kwa" ama, "akakimbia na kusimama mbele yake."
