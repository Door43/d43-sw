===== I Have Sinned [35-08] Nimetenda Dhambi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-08.jpg?nolink&}}

“The **son** said, ‘Father, I have **sinned against God and against you**. **I am not worthy** to be your son.’”

"Yule **mwana ** akasema, 'Baba, **nimetendea Mungu dhambi na pia wewe nimekutendea dhambi.** Sistahili kuwa mwanao.'"
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:son|son (mwana)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:sin|sinned (kutenda dhambi)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God (Mungu)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **sinned against God and against you**  - This could be translated as, "sinned against God, and I have also sinned against you."
  * **nimetendea Mungu dhambi nawe nimekutendea dhambi**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "nimetendea Mungu dhambi na wewe pia nimekutendea dhambi."
  * **I am not worthy**  - It is also possible to say, "So I am not worthy" or, "As a result, I am not worthy."
  * **Sistahili **- Inawezekana pia kusema, "Kwa hivyo sistahili" ama, "kwa hivyo, sistahili."
