===== Lazarus Was Alive Again [37-10] Lazaro akawa Hai Tena =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-10.jpg?nolink&}}

So **Lazarus came out**! He was still wrapped in **grave clothes**. **Jesus** told them, “Help him take off those grave clothes and release him!” Many of the **Jews believed** in Jesus **because of this miracle**.

Kwa hivyo **Lazaro akatoka!** Alikuwa bado amefungwa **katika sanda**. **Yesu** akawaambia, "wamsaidie kuvua zile nguo za kaburi, kisha waache aende! " Wengi wa **Wayahudi walimuamini ** Yesu **kwa sababu ya ishara hiyo**.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lazarus came out!**  - Some languages should say, "Lazarus came out of the tomb!"
  * **Lazaro akatoka!**  - Itabidi baadhi ya lugha kusema, "Lazaro alitoka kwa kaburi!"
  * **grave clothes**  - That is, "burial clothes." This can also be translated as, "burial bandages" or, "strips of cloth."
  * **nguo za kaburi**  - Hiyo ni, "nguo za mazishi." Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "bandeji za mazishi" ama, "ukanda a nguo."
  * **because of this miracle**  - That is, "because God had done this amazing miracle" or, "because Jesus had made Lazarus come alive again."
  * **kwa sababu ya muujiza huu**  - Hiyo ni, "kwa sababu Mungu alikuwa amefanya ishara hiyo ya ajabu" ama, "kwa sababu Yesu alikuwa amefanya Lazaro kuwa hai tena."
