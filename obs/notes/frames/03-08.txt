===== Safe from the Flood [03-08] Salama kutokana na mafuriko =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Everything that lived on the dry land **died**, except the people and animals that were in the boat. The boat floated on the water and kept everything inside the boat safe from drowning.

Kili kitu kiluchokuwa kinaishi katika nchi kavu **kilikufa**, isipokuwa watu na wanyama waliokuwa ndani ya ile mashua. Ile mashua ikaelea juu ya maji na ikalinda wote na lika kitu kilichokua ndani yake isizame.
