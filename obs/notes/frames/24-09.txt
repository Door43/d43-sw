===== Jesus Is the Son of God [24-09] Yesu ni Mwana wa Mungu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-09.jpg?nolink&}}

**God had told John**, “**The Holy Spirit will come down and rest on** someone you **baptize**. That person is the **Son of God**.” There is only one God. But when John baptized Jesus, he heard **God the Father** speak, he saw God the Son, who is **Jesus**, and he saw God the Holy Spirit.

**Mungu alikuwa amemwambia Yohana**, "**Roho Mtakatifu atashuka na kutua kwa** mmoja wa wale **utabatiza**. Huyo mtu ndiye **Mwana wa Mungu**." Kuna Mungu mmoja tu. Lakini wakati Yohana alibatiza Yesu, alisikia **Mungu Baba** akinena, akaona Mungu Mwana, ambaye ni **Yesu**, na akaona Mungu Roho Mtakatifu.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **God had told John**  - That is, "Before this, God had told John" or, "Before Jesus came to be baptized, God had told John."
  * **Mungu alikuwa amemwambia Yohana**  - Hiyo ni, "Kabla ya hii, Mungu alikuwa amemwambia Yohana" ama, "Kabla ya Yesu kuja kubatizwa, Mungu alikuwa ameambia Yohana."
