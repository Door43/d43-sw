===== Elijah Challenged Ahab [19-05]- Elia Alimpatia Ahab Changamoto =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-05.jpg?nolink&}}

After three and a half years, **God** told **Elijah** to return to the **kingdom of Israel** and **speak with Ahab because he was going to send** rain again. When Ahab saw Elijah he said, “There you are, **you troublemaker**!” Elijah replied to him, “You are the troublemaker! **You have abandoned Yahweh**, the true God, and **worshiped Baal**. Bring all the people of the kingdom of Israel to **Mount Carmel**.” Baada ya miaka mitatu na nusu, **Mungu ** akamwambia **Elia ** arudi kwa **ufalme wa Israeli** na **anene pamoja na Ahabu kwa sababu alikuwa anaenda kutuma** mvua tena. Wakati Ahabu alimuona Elia akasema, "uko hapo wewe, **wewe mtaabishaji!"**Elia akamjibu, "wewe ndiwe mtaabishaji! **wewe umeamuacha Yahweh**, mungu wa kweli, na **Kuabudu Baali**. **Leta watu wote wa ufalme wa Israeli Mlimani Kameli**."

==== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) ====

  * **speak with Ahab because he was going to send**  – This could be translated as, "tell Ahab that God was going to send."
  * **Anene na Ahabu kwa sababu alikuwa anatuma**  - Hii inaweza tafsiriwa kama, " mwambie Ahab kuwa Mungu alikuwa anaenda kutuma."
  * **you troublemaker**  - This means, "You are a troublemaker!" Ahab was accusing Elijah of causing trouble by telling the king that he was doing wrong and also by stopping the rain.
  * **wewe mtaabishaji**  - Hii inamaanisha, "Wewe ni mtaabishaji!" Ahab alikuwa anashitaki Elia kwa kusababisha taabu kwa kumwambia mfalme ya kuwa alikuwa anafanya mabaya na pia kusimamisha mvua.
  * **You have abandoned Yahweh**  - That is, Ahab had led Israel to stop worshiping and obeying Yahweh.
  * Wewe umemuacha Yahweh - Hiyo ni kuwa, Ahabu alikuwa ameongoza Israeli waache kuabudu na kutii Yahweh.
  * **Mount Carmel**  - Mount Carmel is the name of a mountain located in northern Israel. It is over 500 meters high.
  * **Mlima Kameli**  - Mlima Kameli ni jina ya mlima ulioko kaskazini mwa Israeli.  uko zaidi ya mita 500 kwa urefu.
