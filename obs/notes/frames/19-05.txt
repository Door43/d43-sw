===== Elijah Challenged Ahab [19-05]- Elia Alimpa Ahabu Changamoto =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-05.jpg?nolink&}}

After three and a half years, **God** told **Elijah** to return to the **kingdom of Israel** and **speak with Ahab because he was going to send** rain again. When Ahab saw Elijah he said, “There you are, **you troublemaker**!” Elijah replied to him, “You are the troublemaker! **You have abandoned Yahweh**, the true God, and **worshiped Baal**. Bring all the people of the kingdom of Israel to **Mount Carmel**.”

Baada ya miaka mitatu na nusu, **Mungu ** akamwambia **Elia ** arudi kwa **ufalme wa Israeli** na **anene na Ahabu kwa sababu alikuwa anaenda kutuma** mvua tena. Wakati Ahabu alimuona Elia akasema, "ndiyo wewe huyo, **wewe mtaabishaji!"**Elia akamjibu, "wewe ndiwe mtaabishaji! **wewe umemuacha Yehova**, Mungu wa kweli, na **k****uabudu**** Baali**. **Leta watu wote wa ufalme wa Israeli kwa Mlima wa Karmeli**."

==== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) ====

  * **speak with Ahab because he was going to send**  – This could be translated as, "tell Ahab that God was going to send."
  * **Anene na Ahabu kwa sababu alikuwa atume**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "mwambie Ahabu kuwa Mungu alikuwa anaenda kutuma."
  * **you troublemaker**  - This means, "You are a troublemaker!" Ahab was accusing Elijah of causing trouble by telling the king that he was doing wrong and also by stopping the rain.
  * **wewe mtaabishaji**  - Hii inamaanisha, "Wewe ni mtaabishaji!" Ahabu alikuwa anashitaki Elia kwa kusababisha taabu kwa kumwambia mfalme ya kuwa alikuwa anafanya mabaya na pia kusimamisha mvua.
  * **You have abandoned Yahweh**  - That is, Ahab had led Israel to stop worshiping and obeying Yahweh.
  * **Wewe umemuacha Yehova**  - Hiyo ni, Ahabu alikuwa ameongoza Israeli waache kuabudu na kutii Yehova.
  * **Mount Carmel**  - Mount Carmel is the name of a mountain located in northern Israel. It is over 500 meters high.
  * **Mlima wa Karmeli**  - Mlima wa Karmeli ni jina la mlima ulioko kaskazini mwa Israeli. Iko na zaidi ya mita 500 kwa urefu.
