===== The Brothers Were Reunited [07-10] Ndugu waliunganishwa *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-10.jpg?nolink&}}

But **Esau** had already **forgiven Jacob**, and they were happy to see each other again. Jacob then **lived peacefully** in **Canaan**. Then **Isaac** died, and Jacob and Esau **buried him**. The **covenant promises God** had promised to **Abraham** now **passed on from Isaac to Jacob**.

Lakini **Esau** alikuwa tayari **amemsamehe Yakobo**, na walifurahia kuonana tena. Basi Yakobo **akaishi kwa amani Kanani.** Halafu **Isaka** akafa, na Yakobo wakiwa na Esau **wakamzika**. Na **ahadi za agano** ambazo Mungu alikuwa amemuahidi Ibrahimu **zikapitishwa kutoka kwa Isaka hadi kwa Yakobo**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **lived peacefully**  - This refers to the fact that Esau and Jacob were not angry at each other and did not fight with each other.
  * **waliishi kwa amani **- Hii inaeleza ukweli kuwa Esau na Yakobo hawakua na kukasirikiana na hawakupigana.
  * **buried him**  - This could mean that they dug a hole in the ground, placed Isaac's body in it, and covered the hole with dirt or stones. Or it could mean that they placed Isaac's body in a cave and covered the opening.
  * **walimzika**  - Hii inaweza kumanisha kuwa walichimba shimo kwa ardhi, wakaweka mwili wa Isaaka ndani yake, na wakarudisha mchanga kwa ile shimo. Ama inaweza kumanisha kuwa waliweka mwili wa Isaka katika pengo na kufunika mlango yake.
  * **covenant promises**  - These were the promises that God made in his covenant with Abraham.
  * **Ahadi za agano**  - Hizi ni zile agano ambazo Mungu alifanya katika agano yake na Ibrahimu.
  * **passed on from Isaac to Jacob**  - The promises went from Abraham to his son Isaac, and now to Isaac's son Jacob. Esau did not receive the promises. See also **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:06-04|[06-04]]]**.
  * **zikapita kutoka kwa Isaka hadi kwa Yakobo**  - Hizo ahadi zilitoka kwa Ibrahimu hadi kwa mwanawe Isaka, na sasa mwanawe Isaka, Yakobo. Esau hakupokea zile ahadi. Ona pia **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:06-04|[06-04]]]**. 
  * //A Bible story from//  - These references may be slightly different in some Bible translations.
  * //Hadithi ya Biblia kutoka//  - Marejeleo haya yaweza kuwa tofauti kiasi katika tafsiri zingine za Biblia.
