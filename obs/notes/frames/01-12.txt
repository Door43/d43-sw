===== Adam Was Alone [01-12] Adamu alikuwa peke yake =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-12.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **God** said, “It is **not good** for man to be **alone**.” But none of the animals could be **Adam's helper**.

**Mungu** akasema, "**si vyema** mwanaume kuwa **peke yake**." Lakini hakuna mnyama yeyote angefaa kama **msaindizi wa Adamu**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **not good**  - This is the first time that anything in creation was not good. It means it was, "not yet good" because God was not finished with the creation of humans.
  * **Si vyema**  - Hii ndio mara ya kwanza ya vitu vilivyoumbwa kuonekana kama si nzuri. Inamanisha bodo haikuwa imefanyika zuri sababu Mungu hakuwa amemalizia uumbaji wa wanadamu.
  * **alone**  - Adam was the only human, with no possibility of a relationship with another person, and unable to reproduce children and multiply.
  * **Pekee**  - Adamu ndiye alikuwa mwanadamu peke yake, na kusiwe na uwezekano wa uhusiano na mtu mwingine, na hangeweza kuzaliana watoto wala kuzidisha.
  * **Adam's helper**  - There was no one who was similar enough to Adam to join with him to accomplish the task that God gave to him. None of the animals could do this.
  * **Msaidizi wa Adamu**  - Hakukua na aliyefanana la kutosha na Adamu kumuwezesha kutimiza majukumu aliyompa Mungu. Hakuna mnyama angeweza hiyo.
