===== Wicked King Saul [17-01] Sauli mfalme mbaya =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-01.jpg?nolink&}}

**Saul** was the **first king of Israel**. He was tall and handsome, just like the people wanted. Saul was a good king for the first few years that he ruled over Israel. But then he became a wicked man who did not **obey God**, so God chose a different man who would **one day be king in his place**.

**Sauli** ndiye alikuwa **mfalme wa kwanza wa Israeli**. Alikuwa mrefu na mwenye maumbile mazuri, kama walivyotaka watu. Sauli alikuwa mfalme mzuri katika miaka yake ya kwanza ya kutawala Israeli. Lakini alifanyika mtu mbaya ambaye **hakutii Mungu**, kwa hivyo Mungu akachagua mtu mwingine ambaye **siku moja atakuwa mfalme mahali pake**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **first king of Israel**  – This could be translated as, "first king to rule over Israel."
  * **mfalme wa kwanza wa Israeli **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "mfalme wa kwanza kutawala Israeli."
  * **one day**  – This could be translated as, "some time in the future" or "years later."
  * **siku moja**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "wakati fulani siku sijazo" ama, "miaka kadhaa baadaye."
  * **be king in his place**  – Another way to say this would be, "take his place as king over Israel" or, "rule as king instead of him."
  * **awe mfalme mahali pake**  - Njia ingine wa kusema hii yaweza kuwa, "achukue nafasi yake kama mfalme wa Israeli" ama, "atawale kama mfalme mahali pake."
