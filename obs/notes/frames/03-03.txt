===== The Big Boat [03-03] Mashua kubwa *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-03.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** told **Noah** to make **the boat** about 140 meters long, 23 meters wide, and 13.5 meters high. Noah was to build it with wood and to make three levels, many rooms, a roof, and a window. The boat would keep Noah, his family, and every kind of land animal safe during the flood.

**Mungu** akamwambia **Nuhu** aitengeneze **mashua** kama mita 140 kwa urefu, na mita 23 kwa upana, na mita 13.5 kwenda juu. Nuhu alikuwa aijenge akitumia mbao na atengeneze ghorofa tatu, vyumba nyingi, paa na ndirisha. Ile mashua ingewaweka salama Nuhu, jamii yake, na wanyama wote wa nchi kavu wakati wa mafuriko.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsri): =====

  * **the boat**  - The boat was big enough to carry eight people, two of every kind of animal, and their provisions for almost a year.
  * **Mashua**  - Ile mashua ilikuwa kubwa kiasi ya kutoshea watu wanane, wawili wa kila aina ya mnyama, na chakula yao ya karibu mwaka.
