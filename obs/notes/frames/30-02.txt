===== The Crowd Followed Jesus [30-02] Umati Ulifuata Yesu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-02.jpg?nolink&}}

But there were many people who saw **Jesus** and the **disciples** leave in the boat to cross the lake. These people ran along the shore of the lake to get to the other side ahead of them. So when Jesus and the disciples **arrived**, a large group of people was already there, waiting for them.

Lakini kulikuwa na watu wengi ambao waliona **Yesu** na **wanafunzi** wakienda kwa mashua kuvuka bahari. Hawa watu walikimbia kando ya pwani ya bahari ili wafike pande ile ingine ya bahari mbele yao. Kwa hivyo wakati Yesu na wanafunzi wake **walifika**, umati mkubwa wa watu walikuwa tayari wamefika na wanawangojea.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **arrived**  - That is, "got to the other side of the lake" or, "got there."
  * **walifika**  - Hiyo ni, "walifika pande ile ingine ya bahari" ama, "walifika pale."
