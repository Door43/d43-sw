===== God Blessed Abram [04-06] Mungu alimbariki Abrahamu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-06.jpg?nolink&}}

When **Abram** arrived in **Canaan**, **God** said, “Look all around you. I will give to you and your **descendants all the land that you can see as an inheritance**”. **Then Abram settled in the land**.

Wakati **Abrahamu** alifika **Kanani**, **Mungu** akasema, "Tazama kila upande karibu na wewe. Nitakupa wewe pamoja na vizazi vyako ardhi yote unyoweza kuiona kama urithi." **Basi, Abrahamu akafanye pale makao yake.**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **all the land that you can see**  - If Abram was standing on a mountain, he could have seen a very large area. On several occasions God promised to give the entire land of Canaan to Abram and his descendants.
  * **Ardhi yote unayoweza kuiona**  - Kama Abrahamu alikuwa anasimama kwa mlima, angeweza kuona eneo kubwa sana. Mara kadhaa Mungu aliahidi kupeana nchi yote ya Kanani kwa Abrahamu na vizazi vyake.
  * **as an inheritance**  - God promised to give land to Abram and his descendants as a father gives land and possessions to his children.
  * **Kama uridhi**  - Mungu aliahidi kupea ardhi Abrahamu na vizazi vyake kama vile baba anavyowapea ardhi na mali watoto wake.
  * **Then Abram settled in the land**  - Abram lived there along with all those who had gone there with him.
  * **Basi! Abrahamu akafanya pale makao yake**  - Abrahamu aliishi pale muda mrefu pamoja na wale wote walienda pale naye.
