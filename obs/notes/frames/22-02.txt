===== You Will Have a Son [22-02] Utakuwa na Mwana =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-02.jpg?nolink&}}

The **angel** said to **Zechariah**, “Your wife will have a son. You will name him **John**. He will be **filled with the Holy Spirit**, and will **prepare the people for the Messiah**!” Zechariah responded, “My wife and I are too old to have children! How will I know this will happen?”

**Malaika** akasema kwa **Zakaria**, "Mke wako atakuwa na mwana. Utamuita jina lake **Yohana**. **Atajawa na Roho Mtakatifu**, na **atawaandaa watu kwa sababu ya Masihi!"** Zakaria akajibu, "Mke wangu na mimi tumekuwa na umri sana kuweza kupata watoto! Nitajua aje hili litatendeka?"

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **The angel**  - This refers to the angel that came to Zechariah in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:22-01|[22-01]]]**.
  * **malaika **- Hii inaelezea kuhusu malaika ambaye alikuja kwa Zakaria pale **[22-02]. **
  * **filled with the Holy Spirit**  - That is, "controlled by the Holy Spirit" or, "given wisdom and power by the Holy Spirit."
  * **kujawa na Roho Mtakatifu**  - Hiyo ni, "anaongozwa na Roho Mtakatifu" ama "amepewa hekima na nguvu na Roho Mtakatifu."
  * **How will I know this will happen?**  - Another way to translate this would be, "How can I be sure that this will truly happen?"
  * **Nitajua aje hii itafanyika?**  - Njia ingine ya kutafsiri hii inaweza kuwa, "nikuwa aje na uhakika kuwa hii kabisa itafanyika?"
