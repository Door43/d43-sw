===== Test of Faith [05-06] Kujaribiwa kwa Imani =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-06.jpg?nolink&}}

When **Isaac** was a young man, **God tested Abraham's faith** by saying, “Take Isaac, your only **son**, and **kill him** as a **sacrifice** to me.” Again Abraham **obeyed** God and **prepared to sacrifice** his son.

Wakati Isaka alipokuwa kijana mdogo, Mungu allijaribu imani ya Abrahamu kwa kusema , mchukue Isaka mwanao wa pekee, umuue kama dhabihu kwangu. Tena Abrahamu akamtii Mungu na akajiandaa kumchinja mwanae kama sadaka.

===== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri =====

  * **God tested Abraham's faith**  - God wanted Abraham to show that he was completely submitted to God, that he would obey whatever God told him to do.
  * **Mungu akaijaribu Imani ya Abrahamu**  - Mungu alitaka Abrahamu aoshe kwamba alikua amejipeana kwa Mungu, kwamba angetii chochote Mungu alimuambia atende.
  * **kill him**  - God did not want human sacrifice. God wanted to see that Abraham loved God more than he loved his son and would obey God even when God told him to give his son back to God.
  * **Umuue**  - Mungu hakutaka sadaka ya binadamu . Mungu alitaka kuona kwamba Abrahamu alimpenda zaidi ya mwanae na angemtii Mungu hata Mungu alipomuambia amurudishie .
  * **prepared to sacrifice**  - Abraham got ready to sacrifice his son. God stopped him before he killed his son.
  * **Kujiandaa kutoa dhabihu **- Abrahamu alijiandaa kumtoa mwanae kama dhabihu. Mungu akamzuia kabla hajamuua mwanae.
