===== Test of Faith [05-06] Kujaribiwa kwa Imani *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-06.jpg?nolink&}}

When **Isaac** was a young man, **God tested Abraham's faith** by saying, “Take Isaac, your only **son**, and **kill him** as a **sacrifice** to me.” Again Abraham **obeyed** God and **prepared to sacrifice** his son.

Wakati **Isaka ** alipokuwa kijana, **Mungu alijaribu imani ya Ibrahimu** kwa kusema , "Mchukue Isaka **mwanao** wa pekee, **umuue** kama **dhabihu** kwangu." Tena Ibrahimu **akamtii** Mungu na **akajiandaa kumtoa kama dhabihu ** mwanawe.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **God tested Abraham's faith**  - God wanted Abraham to show that he was completely submitted to God, that he would obey whatever God told him to do.
  * **Mungu alijaribu imani ya Ibrahimu**  - Mungu alitaka Ibrahimu aonyeshe kwamba alikuwa amejipeana kabisa kwa Mungu, kwamba angetii chochote Mungu angemwambia atende.
  * **kill him**  - God did not want human sacrifice. God wanted to see that Abraham loved God more than he loved his son and would obey God even when God told him to give his son back to God.
  * **umuue**  - Mungu hakutaka sadaka ya binadamu . Mungu alitaka kuona kwamba Ibrahimu alimpenda zaidi ya mwanawe na angemtii Mungu hata Mungu alipomuambia amurudishie .
  * **prepared to sacrifice**  - Ibriham got ready to sacrifice his son. God stopped him before he killed his son.
  * **kujiandaa kutoa dhabihu **- Ibrahimu alikuwa tayari kumtoa mwanawe kama dhabihu. Mungu akamzuia kabla hajamuua mwanawe.
