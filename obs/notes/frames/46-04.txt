===== God Sent Ananias to Saul [46-04] Mungu Alimtuma Anania kwa Sauli =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-04.jpg?nolink&}}

There was a **disciple** in Damascus named Ananias. **God** said to him, “Go to the house where **Saul** is staying. Place your hands on him so that he can see again.” **But Ananias said**, “**Master**, I have heard how this man has **persecuted** the believers.” **God answered him**, “Go! I have chosen him **to declare my name** to the Jews and to people from other people groups. He will **suffer** many things **for my name**.”

Kulikuwa na **mwanafunzi ** katika Damesiki aliyeitwa Anania.  **Mungu ** akamwambia, "Enenda kwa nyumba ambapo **Sauli ** anakaa. Wekelea mikono yako juu yake ndiposa aone tena."**Lakini Anania akasema, "Bwana,** nimesikia vile mtu huyu **aliwatesa ** waaminio.**"Mungu akamjibu**, "Enenda! nimemchagua **atangaze jina langu** kwa Wayahudi na watu kutoka makundi mengine.  **Atateseka ** kwa mambo mengi **kwa ajili ya jina langu**.

===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **But Ananias said**  - If it is not clear why the word "But" is used here, this could be translated as, "But Ananias was afraid, so he said."
  * **Lakini Anania akasema**  - kama si wazi ni kwa nini neno "lakini" linatumika hapa, hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Lakini Anania alikuwa anaogopa, kwa hyivo akasema."
  * **God answered him**  - This could be translated as, "To reassure Ananias, God said" to make clear why God said this.
  * **Mungu akamjibu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kumhakikishia Anania, Mungu alisema" ndio kuifanya wazi Mungu alisema hivi.
  * **to declare my name**  - That is, "to teach about me" or, "to make me known."
  * **kutangaza jina langu**  - Hio ni, "kufundisha juu yangu" ama, "kunifanya nijulikane."
  * **for my name**  - That is, "for me" or, "because of me" or, "because he serves me" or, "because he teaches others about me."
  * **kwa ajili ya jina langu**  - Hio ni, "kwa ajili yangu" ama, kwa sababu yangu" ama, "kwa sababu ananitumikia" ama, "kwa sababu anafundisha wengine juu yangu."
