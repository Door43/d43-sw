===== Jesus Will Reign Forever [50-17] Yesu Atatawala Milele =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-17.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** and his people will live on the new earth, and he will reign forever over everything that exists. He will **wipe away every tear** and **there will be no more suffering**, **sadness**, **crying**, **evil**, **pain**, **or death**. Jesus will **rule his kingdom with peace and justice**, and he will be with his people forever.

**Yesu** na watu wake wataishi katika dunia mpya, naye atatawala milele juu ya kila kitu kilichopo. Yeye **atayafuta machozi ** na **hakutakuwa na mateso tena, huzuni, kilio, maovu, maumivu, au kifo. ** Yesu **atatawala ufalme wake kwa amani na haki**, naye atakuwa pamoja na watu wake milele.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **wipe away every tear**  - This could also be translated as, "bring an end to all of our grief" or, "make trouble end" or, "tenderly take away any sadness people have."
  * **atayafuta machozi yote**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama "kumaliza huzuni yetu yote" ama, "kufanya taabu ifike mwisho" ama, "kwa upole kuondoa huzuni yoyote walio nayo watu."
  * **there will be no more suffering, sadness, crying, evil, pain, or death**  - This could also be translated as, "people will no longer suffer, be sad, cry, do evil things, feel pain, or die."
  * **hakutakuwa tena mateso, huzuni, kilio, maovu, maumivu, au kifo**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama "watu hawatateseka tena, kuwa na huzuni, kilio, kufanya maovu, kuhisi maumivu, au kufa."
  * **rule his kingdom with peace and justice**  - This could also be translated as, "rule over his people fairly, in a way that brings them peace."
  * **kutawala ufalme wake kwa amani na haki**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "atatawala juu ya watu wake kwa haki, kwa njia ambayo itawaletea amani."
