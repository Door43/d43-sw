===== The Blessing [05-10] Baraka =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-10.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **God** said to **Abraham**, “Because you were willing to give me everything, even **your only son**, I **promise** to **bless** you. Your **descendants** will be more than **the stars in the sky**. Because you have **obeyed** me, **all the families of the world** will be **blessed through your family**.”

Halafu **Mungu** akamwambia **Ibrahimu**, "Kwa sababu ulikuwa tayari kunipea kila kitu, hata **mwanao wa pekee**, **ninaahidi kukubariki.** **Vizazi** vyako zikuwa zaidi ya **nyota wa angani.** Sababu **umenitii**, **jamii zote za dunia** **zitabarikiwa kupitia kwa jamii yako**."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **your only son**  - See explanation in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:05-08|[05-08]]]**.
  * **Mwanao wa pekee**  - angalia maelezo katika [05-08]
  * **the stars in the sky**  - See explanation in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:04-05|[04-05]]]**.
  * nyota wa angani - angalia maelezo katika [04-05]
  * **all the families of the world**  - Here, "families" refers to the larger distinct groups of people on earth, rather than to each set of parents and children.
  * **Jamii zote za dunia**  - Hapa, "jamii" zinamaanisha makabila tofauti yaliyomo duniani, badala ya jamii iliyo na wazazi na watoto.
  * **blessed through your family**  - Here, "family" refers to the many descendants that Abraham would have. The future generations of the world would be blessed through Abraham's descendants. The greatest blessing would come many generations later through God's chosen servant, the Messiah.
  * kubarikiwa - Hapa, "jamii" inamaanisha wale vizazi nyingi ambazo Ibrahimu atakuwa nazo. Vizazi vikazofuata katika dunia zitabarikiwa kupitia kwa vizazi vya Ibrahimu. Baraka iliyo kubwa zaidi itakuja vizazi nyingi baadaye kupitia mutumishi wa Mungu aliye chaguliwa, yule mashihi.
