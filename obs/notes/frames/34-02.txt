===== The Large Tree [34-02] Mti Mkubwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-02.jpg?nolink&}}

“But when the **mustard seed grows**, it becomes the largest of all of the **garden plants**, big enough that even the birds come and **rest in** its branches.”

"Lakini wakati ile **mbegu ya haradali inakua**, inafanyika **mumea** uliyo mkubwa zaidi kwa **bustani**, kubwa kiasi kuwa hata ndege wanakuja na **kupumzika** kwa matawi yake."

===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **mustard seed**  - See how you translated this term in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:34-01|[34-01]]]**.
  * **mbegu ya haradali **- Ona vile ulivyotafsiri neno hili pale **[34-01]. **
  * **grows**  - You may want to add, "into a mature plant."
  * **inakua **- Unaweza taka kungezea, "inafanyika mumea umekomaa."
  * **garden plants**  - This can also be translated as, "plants that would be planted in a garden."
  * **mimea ya bustani**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "mimea ambayo inaweza kupandwa katika bustani."
  * **rest in**  - This may also be translated as, "sit on" or, "perch on."
  * **pumzika ndani ya**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "keti kwa" ama, "sangara juu ya."
