===== Working for Nothing [35-12] Kufanya Kazi Bila Kupata Kitu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-12.jpg?nolink&}}

“The older son said to his father, ‘All these years I have worked **faithfully** for you! I never **disobeyed** you, and still you did not even give me **one small goat** so I could celebrate with my friends. But when **this son of yours** who **consumed your money** in **sinful** behavior came home, you **killed the best calf** for him!’”

"Yule mwana mkubwa akamwambia baba, 'Miaka hii yote nimekufanyia kazi kwa **uaminifu!** Kamwe **sijakosa kukutii**, na hujanipa **hata mbuzi moja mdogo** ili niweze kusherehekea na marafiki zangu. Lakini wakati huyu mwanao ambaye ameharibu fedha zako katika maisha ya dhambi amekuja nyumbani, **umechinja ndama aliye bora** kwake!"

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **one small goat**  - A small goat would have fed fewer people than the best calf, and would not be valued as much. The older brother is complaining that his father was treating the sinful younger son better than him.
  * **mbuzi moja mdogo**  - Mbuzi mdogo angeliwa na watu wachache kuliko ndama bora, na hangekuwa na thamana kubwa vile. Yule ndugu mkubwa analalamika kuwa baba yake anamtendea ndugu yake mdogo vyema kuliko yeye.
  * **this son of yours**  - This expression shows that the older son was angry. It shows his rejection of his younger brother and his disapproval of his father for welcoming back this wayward son. Other languages may also have an indirect way of communicating these things.
  * **huyu mwanao**  - Hii msemo inaonyesha kuwa yule mwana mkubwa alikuwa na hasira. Inaonyesha kuwa anakataa ndugu yake mdogo na kutoidhinisha babake kwa kumkaribisha yule mwana mpotevu. Lugha zingine zaweza kuwa na njia isiyo ya moja kwa moja ya kuwasiliana mambo haya.
  * **consumed your money**  - That is, "squandered the money that you gave him" or, "devoured your wealth." If possible, use an expression that shows the brother's anger.
  * **aliharibu pesa zako**  - Hiyo ni, "aliharibu fedha ambazo ulimpa" ama, "alikula fedha zako." Kama inawezekana, tumia msemo unaoonyesha hasira ya ndugu yake.
  * **killed the best calf**  - That is, "killed the best calf to eat at a feast."
  * **alichinja ndama bora**  - Hiyo ni, "alichinja ndama aliye bora aliwe katika karamu."
