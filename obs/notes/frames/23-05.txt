===== The Birth of Jesus [23-05] Kuzaliwa kwa Yesu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-05.jpg?nolink&}}

When they arrived in **Bethlehem**, there was **no place to stay**. The only room they could find was a **place where animals stayed**. The baby was born there and his mother laid him in a **feeding trough**, since they did not have a bed for him. They named him **Jesus**.

Wakati walifika **Bethlehemu**, **hakukuwa** na **mahali pa kukaa**. Chumba peke yake ambayo wangeipata ilikuwa katika makao ya wanyama. Mtoto alizaliwa pale na mama yake akamlaza **horini**, kwa sababu hawakuwa na kitanda yake. Wakamuita jina lake **Yesu**.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:bethlehem|Bethlehem (Bethlehemu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **no place to stay**  - That is, "no usual place to stay." Because Bethlehem was so crowded at that time, the usual rooms for guests were already full of people.
  * **hakukuwa mahali pa kukaa**  - Hiyo ni, "hakuna mahali ya kawaida ya kukaa." Kwa sababu Bethlehemu ilikuwa imejaa watu wakati ule kiasi kuwa, vyumba vya wageni tayari zilikuwa zimejaa watu.
  * **place where animals stayed**  - This was a place for sheltering animals, not a place where people lived. Translate this with a term that would normally be used for a place where animals were kept.
  * **mahali ambapo wanyama walikaa**  - Hii ilkuwa makazi ya wanyama, na si pahali walipoishi watu. Tafsiri hii ukitumia neno ambalo kwa kawaida linatumika kwa mahali ambapo wanyama wa mifugo wanawekwa.
  * **feeding trough**  - That is, "animal feed box" or, "wooden or stone box for feeding animals." The box could have been filled with hay to provide a padded surface for the baby to lie on.
  * **horini **- Hiyo ni, "saduku ya chakula ya mifugo" ama, "saduku ya mawe ama mbao ya kuweka chakula ya mifugo." Saduku yawezekana ilikuwa imejaa nyasi ili iwe pahali pameinyka ya mtoto kulazwa.
