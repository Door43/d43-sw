===== The Virgin Birth [21-09] - Kuzaliwa na Bikira =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-09.jpg?nolink&}}

**God's prophets** predicted many other things about the **Messiah**. The prophet **Malachi foretold** that a great prophet would come before the Messiah came. The prophet **Isaiah prophesied** that **the Messiah would be born from a virgin**. The prophet **Micah** said that he would be born in the town of **Bethlehem**. **Manabii wa Mungu** walitabiri mambo mengine mengi kuhusu Masihi. ** Nabii Malachi ** aliwaambia mapema kabla ifanyike ya kuwa nabii mkuu angekuja kabla ya kuja kwa masihi. ** Nabii Isaiya alitabiri ** ya kuwa** Masihi angezaliwa na bikira.  ** Nabii **Mica ** alisema ya kuwa angezaliwa katika mji wa **Bethlehemu**.

==== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri ====

  * **Malachi**  - Malachi was the last prophet in the Old Testament.
  * **Malachi **- Malachi alikuwa nabii wa mwisho katika agano la kale.
  * **foretold**  - This could be translated as, "predicted" or, "prophesied." It means to tell something that will happen in the future. Malachi spoke God's message to the people 400 years before the Messiah came.
  * **Kusema mapema ya kuwa jambo itakuwa kabla iwe**- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuona uwezekano wa jambo kufanyika," ama "kutabiri." Inamaanisha kusema eti jambo itafanyika katika siku za usoni.  Malachi alinena ujumbe wa Mungu kwa watu mika 400 kabla ya kuja kwa Masihi.

  * **Alitabiri **- katika kufungu hiki, "alitabiri" iko na maana sawa na "kusema mambo itafanyika kabla ifanyike
  * **the Messiah would be born from a virgin**  - Another way to say this would be, "a virgin would give birth to the Messiah."
  * **Masihi angezaliwa na Bikira**  - Njia ingine ya kusema jambo hii ingekuwa, "Bikira angemzaa Masihi."
  * **Micah**  - Micah was an Old Testament prophet of God who, like Isaiah, spoke his messages from God almost 800 years before the Messiah came.
  * **//Mica //**- Mica alikuwa nabii wa Mungu wa Agano la Kale ambaye, kama Isaya, alinena jumbe zake kutoka kwa mungu karibu miaka 800 kabla ya kuja kwa Masihi.
