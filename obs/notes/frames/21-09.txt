===== The Virgin Birth [21-09] - Kuzaliwa na Bikira =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-09.jpg?nolink&}}

**God's prophets** predicted many other things about the **Messiah**. The prophet **Malachi foretold** that a great prophet would come before the Messiah came. The prophet **Isaiah prophesied** that **the Messiah would be born from a virgin**. The prophet **Micah** said that he would be born in the town of **Bethlehem**.

**Manabii wa Mungu** walitabiri mambo mengine mengi kuhusu Masihi. ** Nabii Malachi ** aliwaambia mapema kabla ifanyike ya kuwa nabii mkuu angekuja kabla ya kuja kwa masihi. ** Nabii Isaiya alitabiri ** ya kuwa** Masihi angezaliwa na bikira. ** Nabii **Mica ** alisema ya kuwa angezaliwa katika mji wa **Bethlehemu**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **Malachi**  - Malachi was the last prophet in the Old Testament.
  * **Malaki **- Malaki alikuwa nabii wa mwisho katika agano la kale.
  * **foretold**  - This could be translated as, "predicted" or, "prophesied." It means to tell something that will happen in the future. Malachi spoke God's message to the people 400 years before the Messiah came.
  * **Kutabiri **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuona uwezekano wa jambo kufanyika," ama "kutoa unabii." Inamaanisha kusema kuhusu jambo litakalofanyika katika siku za usoni. Malaki alinena ujumbe wa Mungu kwa watu miaka 400 kabla ya kuja kwa Masihi.
  * **prophesied**  â€“ In this context, "prophesied" has the same meaning as "foretold" and "predicted" since it means the prophet told something that would happen far in the future.
  * **Alitoa unabii **- katika kifungu hiki, "alitoa unabii" iko na maana sawa na kutabiri kwa sababu inamaanisha kuwa yule nabii alieleza kuhusu jambo litakalo fanyika siku nyingi za usoni."
  * **the Messiah would be born from a virgin**  - Another way to say this would be, "a virgin would give birth to the Messiah."
  * **Masihi angezaliwa na bikira**  - Njia ingine ya kusema jambo hili ingekuwa, "bikira ndiye angemzaa Masihi."
  * **Micah**  - Micah was an Old Testament prophet of God who, like Isaiah, spoke his messages from God almost 800 years before the Messiah came.
  * **Mika **- Mika alikuwa nabii wa Mungu wa Agano la Kale ambaye, kama Isaya, alinena jumbe zake kutoka kwa Mungu karibu miaka 800 kabla ya kuja kwa Masihi.
