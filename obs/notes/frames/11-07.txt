===== Sorrow in Egypt [11-07] Huzuni Misri *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Every **Egyptian** firstborn male died, **from the firstborn of the prisoner in jail**, **to the firstborn of Pharaoh**. Many people in **Egypt** were crying and wailing because of their deep sadness.

Kila **Mumisri** ambaye mwanaume mzaliwa wa kwanza alikufa, **kutoka kwa muzaliwa wa kwanza aliyekuwa mfungwa gerezani mpaka mzaliwa wa kwanza wa Farao**. Watu wengi **Misri ** walikuwa wanalia na kuomboleza kwa sababu ya huzuni yao.
