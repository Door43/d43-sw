===== A Wife for Isaac [06-01] Mke wa Isaka *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-01.jpg?nolink&}}

When **Abraham** was very old, his **son**, **Isaac**, had grown to be a man. So Abraham **sent one of his servants back to the land** where his relatives lived **to bring back a wife for his son**, **Isaac**.

Wakati **Ibrahimu** alikuwa mzee sana, mwanawe Isaka akawa amefika umri wa kuoa. Basi Ibrahimu **akamtuma mmoja wa watumishi wake arudi kwenye nchi ** ambayo jamii yake walipoishi ili amletee mke mwanawe Isaka.
