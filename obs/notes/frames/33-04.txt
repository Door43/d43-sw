===== The Seed in the Thorns [33-04] Mbegu katika Miiba =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-04.jpg?nolink&}}

“Still other seeds fell among thorn bushes. Those seeds began to grow, but **the thorns choked them out**. So the plants that grew from the seeds in the **thorny ground** did not produce any grain.”

"Bado mbegu zingine zikaanguka kati ya kichaka ya miti ya miiba. Zile mbegu zikaanza kukua, lakini **miiba ikazisonga**. Kwa hivyo mimea yenye ilikua kutoka kwa hizi mbegu za **ardhi iliyo kati ya miiba** haikuzaa nafaka yoyote."
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **the thorns**  - That is, "the thorn plants" or, "the thorn bushes."
  * **miiba **- Hiyo ni, "miti ya miiba" ama, "vichaka via miti ya miiba."
  * **choked them out**  - This can also be translated as, "overshadowed them" or, "crowded them out."
  * **kuisonga**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "ikazifunika" ama, "ikaimaliza kwa kuisonga."
  * **thorny ground**  - That is, "ground that was covered with thorny bushes."
  * **ardhi yenye miiba**  - Hiyo ni, "ardhi ambayo ilifunikwa na vichaka vya miti ya miiba."
