===== A Promise for the Whole World [21-02] Ahadi ya Ulimwengu Wote =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** promised **Abraham** that **through him** all **people groups** of the world would receive a **blessing**. This blessing would be **fulfilled** when the **Messiah** would come sometime in the future. He would make it possible for people from every people groups in the world to be **saved**.

**Mungu** aliahidi **Ibrahimu** kuwa **kupitia kwake** **vikundi vyote vya watu** za ulimwengu zitapata **baraka**. Hii baraka **itatimizwa** wakati **Masihi** atakuja katika siku zijazo. Atahakikisha kuwa watu kutoka kwa kila kikundi ulimwenguni **wataokolewa**.
