===== Peter Denied Jesus [39-07] Petero Alikana Yesu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Then **Peter vowed**, saying, “**May God curse me if I know this man**!” Immediately, **a rooster crowed**, and **Jesus** turned around and looked at Peter.

Kisha **Petero aliapa** akisema, “**Mungu**** anilaani mimi kama ninajua mtu huyu!”** Mara **jogoo akawika**, na **Yesu** akageuka na kumtazama Petero.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **vowed**  - That is, "said emphatically" or, "said very strongly."
  * **aliapa**  - Hiyo ni, “alisema kwa kusisitiza” ama, “alisema kwa nguvu sana.”
  * **May God curse me if I know this man**  - This is a curse that means, "May God cause harm to me if what you say is true" or, "May God punish me if I am lying to you!" In this way Peter was saying very strongly that he did not know Jesus. He also referred to Jesus as, "this man" to make it sound like he did not know him.
  * **Naomba Mungu anilaani kama ninajua huyu mtu**  - Hii ni laana na inamaanisha, "na Mungu asababishe madhara kwangu kama mnasema kweli" ama, "na Mungu aniadhibu kama mimi ninawaambia uongo!" Kwa njia hii, Petero alikuwa akisema kwa nguvu sana kwamba hakujua Yesu. Pia alimuita Yesu, "Mtu huyu" ili aonekana kama hakumjua.
  * **a rooster crowed**  - "Crowing" is the loud sound that a rooster makes. Compare how you translated this in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:38-09|[38-09]]]**.
  * **jogoo akawika**  - "Kuwika" ni sauti kubwa anaye ifanya jogoo. Linganisha ulivyotafsiri hii pale **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:38-09|[38-09]]]**.
