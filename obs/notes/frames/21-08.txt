===== Messiah Will Rule Forever [21-08]- Masihi Atatawala Milele =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-08.jpg?nolink&}}

A **king** is someone who rules over a **kingdom** and **judges** the people. The **Messiah** would be the perfect king who would **sit on the throne** of his ancestor **David**. He would reign over **the whole world** forever, and would always judge honestly and make the right decisions. Mfalme ni mtu ambaye anatawala juu ya ufalme na kutoa hukumu kwa watu.  Masihi alikuwa awe mfalme mkamilifu ambaye atakalia kiti cha utawala cha babu  yake Daudi. Alikuwa atawale juu ya ulimwengu wote milele, na alikuwa daima atoe  hukumu kwa uaminifu na kufanya maamuzi yalio sahihi.

==== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri ====

  * **sit on the throne of his ancestor David**  - A throne is a ceremonial chair for a king or queen that represents their authority. This expression could also be translated as, "have the authority to reign as his ancestor King David did" or, "be the descendant of King David and continue his rule over God's people."
  * Keti kwenye kiti cha enzi cha babu yake Daudi - Kiti cha enzi ni kiti sherehe cha mfalme ama malkia  ambaye anaakilisha mamlaka yao.  Hii maelezo inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuwa na mamlaka kutawala kama vile babu yake Daudi alifanya," ama kuwa wa kizazi cha Mfalme Daudi na kuenea uongozi juu ya watu wa Mungu.
  * **the whole world**  - This could also be translated as, "everyone everywhere."
  * Ulimwengu wote - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, " Kila mtu kila mahali."
