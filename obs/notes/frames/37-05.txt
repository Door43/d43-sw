===== I Am the Resurrection [37-05] Mimi Ndiye Ufufuo =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-05.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** replied, “**I am the Resurrection and the Life**. Whoever **believes** in me **will live**, **even though he dies**. Everyone who believes in me **will never die**. Do you believe this?” **Martha** answered, “Yes, **Master**! I believe you are the **Messiah**, the **Son of God**.”

**Yesu** akamjibu, "**Mimi ndiye ufufuo na uzima**. Mtu **aliyeniamini** **ataishi, hata akifa**. Kila mtu aniaminiye **hatakufa kamwe**. Je, waamini hayo?" **Martha** akamjibu, "Ndiyo, **Bwana!** Naamini wewe ndiye **Masihi,** **Mwana wa Mungu**."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **I am the Resurrection and the Life**  - This is one of several very powerful "I am" statements in which Jesus says something about his essential nature. In this one, Jesus indicated that he is the "provider" or, "source" of resurrection and life. If possible, translate this phrase in a way that makes it clear that this is his essential nature. This can also be translated as, "I am the one who resurrects people and causes them to live.
  * **Mimi ndiye ufufuo na uzima - ** Hii ni moja ya sentensi kadhaa zenye nguvu sana za "Mimi ndiye" ambazo Yesu anasema kitu kuhusu asili yake muhimu. Kapitia kwa hii moja, Yesu alionyesha kwamba yeye ni "anayepeana" au, "chanzo" cha ufufuo na uzima. Kama inawezekana, tafsiri maneno haya kwa njia ambayo inafanya wazi kuwa hii ni asili yake muhimu. Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mimi ni mmoja ambaye anafufua watu na ninasababisha wao ya kuishi.
  * **will live, even though he dies.**  - That is, "will live forever, even if he dies." The English word, "he" does not only refer to men. Women who believe in Jesus will also live forever.
  * **ataishi, hata akifa**  - Hiyo ni, "ataishi milele, hata kama yeye atakufa. "Neno la Kiingereza, "yeye" halimaanishi tu wanaume. Wanawake ambao wanaamini Yesu pia wataishi milele.
  * **Martha**  - Martha was the sister of Lazarus and Mary. See **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:37-01|[37-01]]]**.
  * **Martha**  - Martha alikuwa dada ya Lazaro na Mariamu. Angalia **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:37-01|[37-01]]]**.
  * **will never die**  - This can also be translated as, "will live forever."
  * **kamwe hatakufa**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "ataishi milele.
