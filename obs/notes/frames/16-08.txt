===== Midian Attacked Israel Again [16-08] Midiani walishambulia Israeli tena *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Then the **Midianites came again to steal from the Israelites**. There were so many of them that **they could not be counted**. **Gideon** called the Israelites together to fight them. Gideon asked **God for two signs** so he could be sure **that God would use him to save Israel**.

Kisha **Wamidiani wakaja tena kuwaimbia Waisraeli**. Walikuwa wengi kiasi ya kuwa **wangehesabiwa**. **Gideoni** akawaita Waisreali pamoja ili wapigane na wao. Gideoni akumuuliza **Mungu ishara mbili** ili awe na uhakika **kuwa Mungu angemutumia kuokoa Israeli**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsri): ====

  * **came again to steal from the Israelites**  – This could be translated as, "came again to the Israelites' land to steal things from them."
  * **wakaja tena kuiba kutoka kwa Waisraeli **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "wakaja tena kwa nchi ya Waisraeli kuiba vitu kutoka kwao."
  * **they could not be counted**  - This could be translated as, "The number of the Midianites was too great to count" or, "it would be very difficult to count all of the Midianites."
  * **Hakuna mtu angeweza kuwahesabu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Idadi ya Wamidiani ilikuwa kubwa kiasi kuwa hakuna mtu angeweza kuwahesabu" ama, "ingekuwa ngumu sana kuwahesabu Wamidiani wote."
  * **for two signs**  - This could be translated as, "to do two miracles" or, "to make two impossible things happen."
  * **kwa ishara mbili**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "afanye miujiza mbili" ama, "afanye mambo mawili yasiyowezekana ifanyike."
  * **that God would use him**  – This could be translated as, "that God would enable him" or, "that God would help him" or, "that God was calling him."
  * **Kuwa Mungu angemutumia**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuwa Mungu atamuwezesha" ama, "kuwa Mungu atamsaidia" ama, "kuwa Mungu alikuwa anamuita."
  * **to save Israel**  - This could be translated as, "to rescue Israel from the Midianites."
  * **kuokoa Israeli **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuokoa Israeli kutoka kwa Wamidiani."
