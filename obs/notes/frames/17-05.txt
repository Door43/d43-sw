===== David Became King [17-05] Daudi alifanyika mfalme *F =====

Eventually, **Saul** died in battle, and **David** became **king** of **Israel**. He was a good king, and the people **loved** him. **God blessed** David and **made him successful**. David fought many battles and God helped him defeat Israel's enemies. David conquered **Jerusalem** and made it **his capital city**. **During Davidâ€™s reign**, Israel became powerful and wealthy.

Hatimaye, **Sauli** alikufa katika vita, na **Daudi** akafanyika **mfalme** wa **Israeli**. Alikuwa mfalme mzuri, na watu **walimpenda**. **Mungu alimbariki** Daudi na **akamfanya afaulu**. Daudi alipigana vita nyingi na Mungu alimsaidia kuwashinda maadui wa Israeli. Daudi alinyakua **Yerusalemu** na akaifanya **Mji wake mkuu**. **Wakati wa utawala wa Daudi**, Israeli ilipata nguvu na utajiri.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **made him successful**  - That is, "helped him accomplish the good things he wanted to do."
  * **alimfanya afaulu**  - Hiyo ni, "alimsaidia kukamilisha mambo mazuri aliyotaka kufanya."
  * **his capital city**  - That is, "the capital city of his kingdom." David lived in Jerusalem and ruled from there.
  * **Mji wake mkuu**  - Hiyo ni, "mji mkuu wa ufalme wake." Daudi aliishi Yerusalemu na akatawala kutoka huko.
  * **During David's reign**  - That is, "During the time when David was king over Israel" or, "During the time when David was ruling as king over Israel."
  * **Wakati wa utawala wa Daudi**  - Hiyo ni, "Katika muda ule Daudi alikuwa anatawala Israeli" ama, "Katika muda ule Daudi alikuwa anatawala kama mfalme kule Israeli."
