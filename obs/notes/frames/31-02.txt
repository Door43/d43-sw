===== The Storm [31-02] Dhoruba =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**Meanwhile**, the **disciples** were **rowing their boat**, but by late in the night they had only traveled as far as the middle of the lake. They were rowing with **great difficulty** because the wind was blowing hard against them.

**Wakati huo huo**, **wanafunzi** walikuwa **wanaendesha mashua yao**, lakini usiku wa manane walikuwa wamefika tu katikati ya bahari. Walikuwa na **shinda kubwa ** ya kuenda kwa sababu upepo ulikuwa unapepea vikali kinyume nao.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Meanwhile**  - That is, "while Jesus was praying on the mountainside."
  * **Wakati huo huo**  - Hiyo ni, "wakati Yesu alikuwa anaomba kwa upande wa mlima."
  * **rowing their boat**  - The boat had a sail, but it would not have worked when the wind was against them.
  * **wanaendesha mashua**  - Mashua ilikuwa inaenda, lakini hawakuendelea vizuri kwa sababu upepo ulikuwa kinyume nao.
  * **great difficulty**  - That is, "much difficulty" or, "a lot of difficulty."
  * **shinda kubwa**  - Hiyo ni, "shinda sana" ama, "shinda nyingi."
