===== This Is My Son [36-05] Huyu ni Mwanangu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-05.jpg?nolink&}}

As **Peter** was talking, a bright cloud came down and surrounded them and **a voice from the cloud said**, “This is my **Son** whom I **love**. I am pleased with him. **Listen to him**.” The three **disciples** were **terrified** and **fell on the ground**.

**Petero** alipokuwa akizungumza, wingu nyeupe lilishuka na kuwazunguka, **na sauti kutoka kwa wingu ikasema**, "Huyu ndiye **Mwanangu** **nimpendaye**. Ninafurahishwa naye. **Sikilizeni yeye**." Wale **wanafunzi** watatu wakawa na **hofu** na **wakaanguka chini.**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **a voice from the cloud said**  - This can also be translated as, "a voice spoke from the cloud saying" or, "God spoke from the cloud and said."
  * **Sauti kutoka kwa wingu ikasema**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Sauti ilizungumza kutoka mbinguni ikisema " au, "Mungu alizungumza kutoka kwa wingu akasema."
  * **Listen to him**  - This could be translated as, "He is the one you must listen to."
  * **Sikilizeni yeye**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Huyu ndiye mnapaswa kusikiliza."
  * **terrified**–That is, "extremely scared."
  * **hofu **- Yaani, "kuogopa mno kwa ghafla."
  * **fell on the ground**  - That is, "quickly bowed to the ground" or, "immediately got down on the ground." Make sure the translation of "fell" does not sound like an accident. They probably did this intentionally out of awe and fear.
  * **akaanguka chini**  - Yaani, "haraka akainama mpaka nchi" au, "mara moja akaenda chini kwa ardhi." Hakikisha tafsiri ya "akaanguka" haitaonekana kama ni ajali. Pengine walifanya hivyo kwa makusudi kwa sababu ya hofu na woga.
