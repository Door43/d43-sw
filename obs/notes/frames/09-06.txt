===== Moses in a Basket [09-06] Musa katika kikapu *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-06.jpg?nolink&}}

When the boy's parents could no longer hide him, they put him in a **floating basket among the reeds along the edge of the Nile River** in order to save him from being killed. His older sister watched to see what would happen to him

Wakati wazazi wa yule mvulana hawangemficha tena, walimuweka kwa **kikapu kinachoelea juu ya maji miongoni mwa mianzi karibu na mto wa Nile** ndiposa wamuokoe asiuawe. Dada yake mkubwa akachungulia kuona kile kitafanyika kwake.
