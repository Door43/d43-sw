===== The Canaanites Defeated Israel [14-10] Wakanani walishinda Israeli =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-10.jpg?nolink&}}

**God did not go with them into this battle**, so they were defeated and many of them were killed. Then the **Israelites turned back from Canaan** and **wandered through the wilderness** for forty years.

**Mungu hakwenda nao katika vita hii**, kwa hivyo walishindwa na wengi wao kuuawa. Kisha **Waisraeli wakarudi kutoka Kanani** na **wakazungukazunguka katika jangwa** kwa miaka arobanne.

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God (Mungu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:israel|Israelites (Waisraeli)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:canaan|Canaan (Kanani)]]**
==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **God did not go with them into this battle**  - In other words, God did not help them in this fight.
  * **Mungu hakwenda nao katika vita**  - Kwa maneno mengine, Mungu hakuwasaidia katika hii vita.
  * **turned back from Canaan**  - They left Canaan and went back into the wilderness where they were before.
  * **wakarudi kutoka Kanani **- Walitoka Kanani na wakarudi jangwani mahali walipotoka.
  * **wandered through the wilderness**  - They lived in the wilderness, and together they moved from place to place in that large, dry land, looking for food and water for themselves and their animals.
  * **wakazungazunguka katika jangwa**  - Waliishi jangwani, na pamoja wakaenda toka mahali moja hadi ingine katika ardhi hiyo kubwa na kavu, wakitafuta chakula na maji yao na ya mifugo yao.
