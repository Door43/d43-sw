===== This Is My Body [38-04] Huu Ndio Mwili Wangu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-04.jpg?nolink&}}

In Jerusalem, **Jesus celebrated** the **Passover** with his **disciples**. During the Passover meal, Jesus **took some bread** and **broke it**. He said, “Take and eat this. **This is my body**, which is **given for you**. **Do this to remember me**.” In this way, Jesus said that his body would be **sacrificed** for them.

Huko Yerusalemu, **Yesu alisherehekea Pasaka** pamoja na **wanafunzi ** wake. Wakati wa mlo wa Pasaka, Yesu **alitwaa mkate, akaumega**. Akasema, "Chukua na kula huu. **Huu ni mwili wangu**, **unaotolewa kwa ajili yenu**. **Fanyeni hivi kwa kunikumbuka**. "Kwa njia hii, Yesu alisema kwamba mwili wake itakuwa **sadaka** kwa ajili yao.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **celebrated**  - That is, "was celebrating."
  * **kusherehekea**  - Hiyo ni, "walikuwa wakiadhimisha."
  * **took some bread**  - This could be translated as, "picked up a piece of bread" or, "picked up a flat loaf of bread."
  * **alitwaa mkate**  -  Hiyo inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "alichukua kipande cha mkate" ama, "alichukua bapa ya mkate iliyo laini."
  * **broke it**  - Some languages should say, "tore it in pieces" or, "tore it in half" or, "tore off part of it."
  * **akaumega**  - Baadhi ya lugha zitasema, "akararua vipande vipande " ama, "akararua katika nusu" ama, "alipasua sehemu yake."
  * **given for you**  - This can also be translated as, "which I give for you."
  * **unaotolewa kwa ajili yenu**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "ninapeana kwa ajili yenu."
  * **Do this to remember me**  - That is, "Do this as a way to remind yourselves of what I am doing for you." Jesus was referring to his death, which would happen soon.
  * **Fanyeni hii ili kunikumbuka**  - Hiyo ni, "Fanyeni hii kama njia ya kuwakumbusha nyinyi ninachofanya kwa ajili yenu." Yesu alikuwa anamaanisha kifo chake, ambayo ingetokea hivi karibuni.
