===== Jesus Taught for Forty Days [42-09] Yesu Alifundisha kwa Siku Arobaini =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-09.jpg?nolink&}}

During the next forty days, **Jesus** appeared to his **disciples** many times. Once, he even appeared to more than 500 people at the same time! He proved to his disciples in many ways that he was **alive**, and he taught them about the **kingdom of God**.

Katika siku arobaini zilizofuata, **Yesu** aliwatokea **wanafunzi** wake mara nyingi. Mara baada, yeye hata alionekana zaidi ya watu 500 kwa wakati mmoja! Alithibitishia wanafunzi wake kwa njia nyingi kwamba alikuwa **hai, ** naye akaanza kuwafundisha kuhusu **Ufalme wa Mungu**.
