===== John Baptized Jesus [24-07] Yohana Alibatiza Yesu =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-07.jpg?nolink&}}

**John** said to **Jesus**, “**I am not worthy to baptize you**. You should baptize me instead.” But Jesus said, “You should baptize me, because **it is the right thing to do**.” So John baptized him, even though Jesus had never **sinned**.

**Yohana** akamwambia **Yesu**, "**Mimi sistahili kukubatiza wewe**. Wewe ndiye ungenibatiza." Lakini Yesu akasema, "Inabidi unibatize, kwa sababu **hili ndilo jambo la haki kufanya.**" Kwa hivyo Yohana akambatiza, hata ingawa Yesu hakuwa amewahi **kutenda dhambi**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **I am not worthy to baptize you**  - This could be translated as, "I am not good enough to baptize you" or, "I am sinful, so I should not baptize you."
  * **Mimi sistahili kukubatiza wewe**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mimi si mwema ya kutosha kukubatiza wewe" ama, "Mimi ni mwenye dhambi, kwa hivyo si sawa nikubatize."
  * **it is the right thing to do**  - This might also be translated as, "it is the proper thing to do" or, "this is what God wants me to do."
  * **Hili jambo ndio sawa kulifanya**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Hili ndilo jambo sahihi la kufanya" ama, "Hili ndilo Mungu anataka nifanye."
