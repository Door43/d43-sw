===== Israel and Judah at War [18-10] Israeli na Yuda katika vita =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-10.jpg?nolink&}}

The **kingdoms** of **Judah** and **Israel** became enemies and often fought against each other.

**Falme** za **Yuda** na** Israeli** wakafanyika maadui na mara nyingi wakapigana.

==== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **Judah and Israel**  - The people of Judah and Israel were all descendants of Jacob and part of God's people. Even so, they disobeyed God and fought and killed each other.
  * **Yuda na Israeli **- Watu wa Yuda na Israeli wote walikuwa uzao wa Yakobo na sehemu ya watu wa Mungu. Hata hivyo, walikosa kumtii Mungu na wakapigana na kuuana.
