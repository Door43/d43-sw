===== Joseph in Prison [08-05] Yosefu gerezani *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-05.jpg?nolink&}}

His master’s wife **tried to sleep with Joseph**, but Joseph refused to **sin against God** in this way. She became angry and falsely accused Joseph so that he was arrested and sent to prison. Even in prison, Joseph **remained faithful to God**, and God **blessed** him.

Mke wa bwana wake **alijaribu kulala na Yosefu,** lakini Yosefu alikataa kutenda **dhambi kwa Mungu** kwa njia hii. Akawa mwenye hasira nyingi na akamsingizia Yosefu na alikamatwa na kutupwa gerezani. Hata katika gereza, Yosefu **alibaki mwaminifu kwa Mungu** na Mungu **akambariki.**

==== Translation Notes: Maelezo ya Tafsiri ====

  * **tried to sleep with Joseph**  – Another way to say this would be, "tried to seduce Joseph to have sex with her." To "sleep with" is a way of expressing this in order not to be crude or offensive.
  * **alijaribu kulala na Yosefu**  - Njia nyingine ya kuelezea hii ni kusema, alijarubu kumtongoza Yosefu ili wafanye ngono na yeye." "Kulala naye" ni njia ya kuelezea kitendo hichi kwa njia inayofaa na pasipo kukera.
  * **sin against God**  - It is against God's law for people to have sex if they are not married to each other. Joseph did not want to sin by disobeying God's law. -
  * **Dhambi kwa Mungu-**  Ni kinyume cha sheria ya Mungu kufanya ngono kama watu hajaoana moja na mwingine. Yosefu hakutaka kutenda dhambi kwa kutotii sheria ya Mungu.
  * **remained faithful to God**  - Another way to say this would be, "continued to obey God."
  * **Alibaki mwaminifu kwa Mungu**  - Njia nyingine ya kusema hii ingekuwa, "kuendelea kumtii Mungu."
