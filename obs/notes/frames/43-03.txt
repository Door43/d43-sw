===== Speaking Other Languages [43-03] - Kunena Lugha Zingine =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-03.jpg?nolink&}}

While the **believers** were all together, suddenly the house where they were was filled with **a sound like a strong wind**. Then something that looked like flames of fire appeared over the heads of all the believers. They were all **filled with the Holy Spirit** and they began to speak **in other languages**.

Wakati **waaminio ** walikuwa wote pamoja, ghafla nyumba walimokuwa ikajawa na **sauti kama ya upepo mkali**. Harafu kitu iliyofanana na miali ya moto ilionekana juu ya vichwa vya waaminio. Wote **wakajazwa na Roho Mtakatifu** na wakaanza kunena na **lugha zinginezo**
===== Important Terms:(Maneno ya Muhimu) =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:believer|believers]](waaminio)**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:holy-spirit|Holy Spirit]](Roho Mtakatifu)**
===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **a sound like a strong wind**  - That is, "a noise that a strong wind makes" or, "the sound that the wind makes when it blows hard."
  * **Sauti kama ya upepo mkali**  - Hio ni, "sauti ambayo upepo mkali hufanya" ama, "sauti ambayo upepo hufanya unapovuma kwa nguvu."
  * **filled with the Holy Spirit**  - That is, "given ability by the Holy Spirit" or, "given power by the Holy Spirit."
  * **Walijazwa na Roho Mtakatifu**
 - Hio ni, "kupatiwa uwezo na Roho Mtakatifu" ama, "kupatiwa nguvu na Roho Mtakatifu."
  * **in other languages**  - This could be translated as, "in languages other than their own language" or, "in foreign languages" or, "as people from other places speak." The believers did not know these languages until the Holy Spirit gave them the power to speak them. Make sure the word used to translate "languages" refers to languages people actually speak and understand.
  * **katika lugha zingine**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "katika lugha zingine zaidi ya lugha zao wenyewe" ama, "katika lugha za kigeni" ama, kama vile watu wa sehemu zingine kunena." Waaminio hawakujua lugha hizi mpaka wakati Roho Mtakatifu aliwapatia uwezo wa kuzinena. Hakikisha neno lililotumiwa kutafsiri "Lugha" inaonyesha lugha ambazo watu hakika hunena na kuelewa.
