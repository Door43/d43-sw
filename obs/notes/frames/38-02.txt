===== Judas Made a Deal [38-02] Yuda Alifanya Mpango =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-02.jpg?nolink&}}

One of **Jesus' disciples** was a man named **Judas**. Judas was **in charge of the apostles' moneybag**, but he **loved money** and often stole money out of the bag. After Jesus and the disciples arrived in **Jerusalem**, Judas went to the **Jewish leaders** and offered to **betray** Jesus to them in exchange for money. He knew that the Jewish leaders denied that Jesus was the **Messiah** and that they were plotting to kill him.

Mmoja wa **wanafunzi wa Yesu** alikuwa mtu mmoja aitwaye **Yuda**. Yuda alikuwa **msimamizi wa mfuko ya pesa ya mitume**, lakini yeye **alipenda pesa** na mara nyingi aliiba fedha kutoka kwa ile mfuko. Baada ya Yesu na wanafunzi kufika **Yerusalemu**, Yuda alienda kwa **viongozi wa Wayahudi** na akajitolea **kumsaliti** Yesu ili apewe fedha. Alijua kwamba viongozi wa Wayahudi walipinga kuwa Yesu ni **Masihi** na kwamba walikuwa wanapanga njama za kumuua.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **in charge of the apostles' moneybag**  - That is, "responsible to hold the apostles' money" or, "responsible to hold the bag that had the disciples' money in it and to distribute funds from it."
  * **msimamizi wa mfuko ya pesa za mitume**  - Hiyo ni, "aliwajibika kushikilia fedha za mitume " ama, "aliwajibika kushikilia mfuko iliyokuwa na fedha za wanafunzi 'ndani yake na kusambaza fedha kutoka humo."
  * **loved money**  - That is, "highly valued money" or, "wanted money." Some languages would not use the same word that can be used for "loved people."
  * **anapenda fedha sana**  - Hiyo ni, "mwenye kuthamini fedha" ama, "alitaka fedha." Baadhi ya lugha hazitatumia neno hilo hilo ambalo linaweza kutumika kwa ajili ya "kupendwa kwa watu."
