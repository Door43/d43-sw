===== Two Nations [06-06] Mataifa mawili =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-06.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** told **Rebekah**, “Two nations will come **from the two sons** inside of you. **They will struggle with each other** and **the older son** will **serve the younger one**.”

**Mungu** akamwambia **Rebeka**, "mataifa mawili zitatoka **kutoka kwa wana wawili ** waliyomo ndani yako. **Watapambana moja na mwingine** na **mwanao aliye mkubwa** **atamutumikia aliye mdogo**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **from the two sons**  - That is, "from the descendants of the two sons."
  * **kutoka kwa wana wawili **- Hiyo ni, "kutoka kwa uzao wa wale wana wawili."
  * **They will struggle with each other**  - Both the two sons and the nations that will come from them will continually fight with each other. Compare to **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:06-05|[06-05]]]**.
  * **Watapambana moja na mwingine**  - Wana hawa wawili na mataifa yatakayotoka ndani yao watapigana moja na mwingine mbila kukoma. Linganisha na **[06-05]**
  * **the older son**  - Even though the babies were twins, the baby that came out first would be treated as the older son.
  * **mwana aliye mkuu**  - ingawa wale watoto walikuwa mapacha, mtoto aliyetoka mbele ndiye atahesabiwa kama mwana aliye mkuu.
  * **serve the younger one**  – This could be translated as, "the older son will have to do what the younger one tells him to do."
  * **Kumutumikia aliye mdogo**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "mwana aliye mkuu atalazimika kufanya kile aliye mdogo atamwambia afanye."
