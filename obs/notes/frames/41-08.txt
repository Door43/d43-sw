===== Jesus Appeared to the Women [41-08] Yesu Alionekana kwa Wanawake =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-08.jpg?nolink&}}

As the women were on their way to tell the **disciples** the good news, **Jesus** appeared to them, and they **worshiped** him. Jesus said, “Do not be afraid. Go and tell my disciples to go to **Galilee**. They **will see me** there.”

Wakati wanawake walikuwa wanaenda zao kuwaambia **wanafunzi** ile habari njema, **Yesu** alionekana, nao wakamuabudu. Yesu akawaambia, "Msiogope. Endeni muwaambie wanafunzi wangu waenda **Galilaya** na **wataniona** huko."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **will see me**  - That is, "will meet me" or, "can meet with me."
  * **wataniona**  - Hiyo ni, "watakutana na mimi" ama, "wanaweza kukutana na mimi."
