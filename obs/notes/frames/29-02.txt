===== The Huge Debt [29-02] Deni Kubwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Jesus said, “**The kingdom of God is like a king who** wanted to **settle accounts with his servants**. One of his servants owed a huge debt worth 200,000 years’ wages.”

Yesu akasema, **"Ufalme wa Mungu ni kama mfalme ambaye** alitaka **kukagua hesabu vya watumishi wake.** Mojawapo wa watumishi wake alikuwa na deni kubwa inayoweza kugharimu mshahara wa miaka 200,000."
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:kingdom-of-god|kingdom of God (ufalme wa Mungu)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:king|king (mfalme)]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:servant|servants (watumishi)]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **The kingdom of God is like**  - Another way to say this would be, "God's rule over people is like" or, "The way God rules people can be compared to."
  * **Ufalme wa Mungu unaweza kulinganishwa na**  - Njia ingine ya kusema hii inaweza kuwa, "Utawala wa Mungu juu ya watu unaweza linganishwa na" ama, "vile Mungu anavyotawala watu inaweza linganishwa na."
  * **is like a king who**  - This could be translated as, "is like the kingdom of a king who" or, "can be compared with the rule of a king who."
  * **Ni kama Mfalme aliye**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "ni kama ufalme wa Mfalme ambaye" ama, "inaweza linganishwa na ufalme wa mfalme ambaye."
  * **settle accounts with his servants**  - That is, "collect the debts his servants owed him" or, "collect the money his servants had borrowed from him."
  * **kagua hesabu za watumishi wake **- Hiyo ni, "kusanya madeni aliyo wadai watumishi wake" ama, "akusanye pesa ambazo watumishi wake walikuwa wamekopa kutoka kwake."
