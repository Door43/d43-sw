===== He Is Not Guilty [39-11] Yeye Hana Hatia =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-11.jpg?nolink&}}

After speaking with **Jesus**, **Pilate** went out to the crowd and said, “**I find no guilt in this man**.” But the **Jewish leaders** and the crowd shouted, “**Crucify** him!” Pilate replied, “**He is not guilty**.” But they shouted even louder. Then Pilate said a third time, “He is not guilty!”

Baada ya kuongea na **Yesu**, **Pilato** alienda nje kwa umati na kusema, **"Sioni kosa lolote kwa mtu huyu.**" Lakini viongozi wa Wayahudi na umati wa watu wakasema kwa sauti: "**Msulubishe**! "Pilato akajibu, **"Yeye hana hatia."** Lakini waliinua sauti zao hata zaidi. Hivyo Pilato akasema kwa mara ya tatu, "Yeye hana hatia! "

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **I find no guilt in this man**  - That is, "I do not find this man to be guilty" or, "I have examined this man, and I do not see that he has done anything wrong."
  * **Mimi sioni hatia na mtu huyu**  - Hiyo ni, "Sioni mtu huyu akiwa na hatia" ama, "Mimi nimechunguza mtu huyu, na sioni kwamba amefanya chochote kibaya."
  * **He is not guilty**  - That is, "He has done nothing wrong!"
  * **Hana hatia**  - Hiyo ni, "Yeye hakufanya chochote kibaya!"
