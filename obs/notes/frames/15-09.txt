===== God Fought the Amorites [15-09] Mungu alipiga Waamori *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-09.jpg?nolink&}}

**God fought for Israel** that day. He **caused the Amorites to be confused** and he sent **large hailstones** that killed many of the Amorites.

**Mungu aliwapigania Israeli** siku hiyo. **Aliwafanya Waamori kuchanganyikiwa** na akatuma **mvua ya mawe kubwa** ambazo ziliwauwa wengi wa Waamori.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **God fought for Israel**  - God fought on the side of Israel against Israel's enemies.
  * **Mungu alipigania Israeli **- Mungu alipigana pamoja na Waisraeli kinyume na maadui wa Israeli.
  * **caused the Amorites to be confused**  - This could be translated as, "made the Amorites panic" or, "caused the Amorites to be unable to fight together well."
  * **alifanya Waamori wachanganyikiwe**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "alifanya Waamori kuwa na hofu" ama, "alifanya Waamori washindwe kupigana vizuri pamoja."
  * **large hailstones**  - This could be translated as, "very large balls of ice to come down from the sky."
  * **mvua ya mawe kubwa**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "vipande vikubwa vya theluji kuanguka kutoka angani."
