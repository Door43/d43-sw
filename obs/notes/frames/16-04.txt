===== The Rule of Midian [16-04] Utawala wa Midiani *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-04.jpg?nolink&}}

The **Midianites** took all of the **Israelites' crops** for seven years. The Israelites were **so scared that** **they hid** in caves so the Midianites would not find them. Finally, they **cried out** to **God** to **save them**.

**Wamidiani** walichukua **mazao yote ya Waisraeli ** kwa miaka saba. Waisraeli **waliojawa na hofu hadi wakajificha** kwenye mapango ili Wamidiani wasiwapate. Hatimaye **wakamlilia Mungu ili awaokoe**.

==== Translation Notes: Tafsiri ya Maneno ====

  * **crops**  - This refers to plants that the Israelites were growing in their gardens or fields for food.
  * **Mazao**  - Hii inamaanisha mimea Waisraeli waliokuwa wakikuza katika bustani au mashamba yao kwa chakula
  * **were so scared that they hid**  – This could be translated as, "were very afraid of the Midianites, so they hid."
  * **walijawa na hofu hadi wakajificha**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, " waliwaogopa Wamidiani kwa hivyo wakajificha.
  * **cried out**  - This might be translated as, "they called out" or, "they prayed desperately."
  * **wakapiga yowe**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, " waliitana, ama "waliomba kwa kukata tamaa".
  * **save them**  – This might be translated as, "set them free" or, "rescue them from these enemies."
  * **Kuwaokoa**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuwaweka huru" ama " kuwaokoa kutoka kwa hawa maadui".
