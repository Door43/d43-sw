==== Translation Notes: (Tafsiri ya Maelezo) ====

  * **Throughout the history of**  - This expression means that God sent many prophets at many different times during all of the years that Israel and Judah existed.
  * Katika nyakati zote za historia ya - Hii maelezo inamaanisha ya kuwa Mungu aliwatuma manabii nyakati nyingi tofauti tofauti miaka yote ile Isareli na Yuda walikuwepo.
  * **Israelites**  - This could be translated here as, "the kingdoms of Israel and Judah." All of the descendants of Jacob, including those in the kingdom of Judah, continued to be called "Israelites."
  * Waisraeli - Hii inaweza klutafsiriwa hapa kama, "falme za Israeli na Yuda." Vizazi vyote vya Yuda, pamoja na wale wa ufalme wa Yudah, waliendelea kuitwa "Waisraeli."
