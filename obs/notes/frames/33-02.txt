===== The Seed on the Path [33-02] Mbegu Kwenye Njia =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-02.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** told this story. “A farmer went out to plant **some seed**. As he was **spreading the seeds by hand**, some seeds happened to fall on the path, and the birds came and ate all of those seeds.”

**Yesu** akawambia hadithi hii. "Mkulima alienda kupanda **mbegu**. Alipokuwa **anatawanya mbegu kwa mkono**, mbegu zingine zilianguka kando ya njia, na ndege zikaja na kukula zile mbegu zote."
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**

  * **[[:sw:obe:kt:jesus]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **spreading the seeds by hand**  - That is, "throwing the seeds on the soil" or, "covering all of his crop soil with seeds." This is the way that farmers in the ancient Middle East typically planted grain-bearing crops.
  * **kutawanya mbegu kwa mkono**  - Hiyo ni, "kutupa mbegu kwa ardhi" ama, "kupanda mbegu kwa mchanga yake ya kila aina." Kwa njia hii wakulima katika Mashariki ya kati ya siku za kale, kwa kawaida, walipanda mimea inayozaa nafaka.
