===== God Fought for Israel [12-10] Mungu alipigania Israeli *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-10.jpg?nolink&}}

So they followed the **Israelites** onto the **path through the sea**, but **God** caused the **Egyptians** to panic and caused their **chariots** to **get stuck**. They shouted, “Run away! God is fighting for the Israelites!”

Kwa hivyo wakawafuata **Waisraeli ** katika **njia ya katikati ya bahari**, lakini **Mungu** akawafanya **Wamisri** kuwa na hofu na **magari ** yao **kukwama**. Wakapasa sauti, "Kimbieni! Mungu anawapigania Waisraeli!"

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **path through the sea**  - This was the dry strip of land across the bottom of the sea, with a wall of water on each side.
  * **njia katikati ya bahari **- Hii ilikuwa ukanda mkavu wa ardhi kule chini ya bahari, na kuta za maji pande zote.
  * **to panic**  – This could be translated as, "to get fearful and confused."
  * **kuwa na hofu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kuogopa na kuchanganyikiwa."
  * **get stuck**  - The chariots could no longer move.
  * **kukwama**  - Zile magari hazingeweza kuenda tena.
