===== Peter Preached [44-05] Petero Alihubiri =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-05.jpg?nolink&}}

“You are the ones who told the **Roman** governor to kill **Jesus**. You killed the **author of life**, but **God raised** him from the **dead**. Although you did not understand what you were doing, God used **your actions** to **fulfill** the prophecies that the **Messiah** would **suffer** and die. So now, **repent** and **turn to God** so that **your sins will be washed away**.”

"Nyinyi ndio mliiambia gavana wa **Kirumi** amuue **Yesu**. Nyinyi mliua **mwanzilishi wa maisha**, lakini **Mungu akamfufua** kutoka kwa **wafu**. Ingawa hamukuelewa nini mlikuwa mnafanya, Mungu alitumia **matendo yenu kutimiza** unabii kuwa **Masihi atateswa** na kufa. Kwa hivyo sasa, **tubuni** na **kumgeukia Mungu ** ili dhambi zenu ziweze kuondolewa."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Peter continues to preach to the crowd.)
  * (Peter anaendelea kuhubiri kwa umati wa watu.)
  * **author of life**  - That is, "the one who created life" or, "the one who gives us life" or, "the one who causes people to live." This refers to Jesus.
  * **Mwanzilishi wa maisha**  - Hiyo ni, "yule aliye umba maisha" ama, "yule anayetupa maisha" ama, "mtu yeyote anayefanya watu kuishi." Hii inamaanisha Yesu.
  * **your actions **  - This can also be translated as, "the things you did." This refers to their asking Pilate to kill Jesus.
  * **matendo yenu**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mambo mliyo yatenda." Hii inamaanisha wao kuuliza Pilato amuue Yesu.
  * **turn to God**  - That is, "decide to obey God."
  * **kumrudia Mungu**  - Hiyo ni, "kuamua kumtii Mungu."
  * **your sin will be washed away**  - This could be translated as, "God will wash away your sins" or, "God will take away your sins and make you clean." This is talking about God purifying people in their spirit by completely removing their sins. It does not mean physical washing.
  * **dhambi zenu zitaondolewa**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mungu atawaondolea dhambi zenu" ama, "Mungu atawaondolea  dhambi zenu na kuwafanya wasafi." Hii inazungumza juu ya Mungu kuwataka watu katika roho zao na kuwaondolea kabisa dhambi zao. Haimaanishi kuosha mwili.
