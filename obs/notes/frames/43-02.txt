===== The Feast of Pentecost [43-02]  Sikukuu ya Pentekoste =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Every year, 50 days after the **Passover**, the **Jews** celebrated an important day called **Pentecost**. Pentecost was a time when the Jews **celebrated the wheat harvest**. Jews came from all over the world to **Jerusalem** to celebrate Pentecost together. **This year**, the time for Pentecost came about a week after **Jesus** had gone back to **heaven**.

Kila mwaka, siku hamsini baada ya **Pasaka**, **Wayahudi ** walisheherekea siku ya muhimu iliyoitwa **Pentekoste**. Pentekoste ilikuwa ni wakati ule Wayahudi **walisherehekea mavuno ya ngano**. Wayahudi walitoka kote duniani hadi **Yerusalemu ** kusheherehekea Pentekoste pamoja. Mwaka huu, wakati wa Pentekoste ulikuja karibu wiki moja baada ya **Yesu ** kurudi **mbinguni**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Pentecost**  - "Pentecost" means "fiftieth (day)." You could use the word "Pentecost" in your translation and let the text explain the meaning. Or you could use a term that means, "50th day."
  * **Pentekoste **- "Pentekoste" humaanisha "siku ya hamsini." unaweza kutumia neno "Pentekoste" katika tafsiri yako na uache kifungu kuelezea maana yake. Ama unaweza tumia neno linalomaanisha "siku ya hamsini"
  * **celebrated the wheat harvest**  - The Jews would thank God for the wheat harvest by bringing offerings and celebrate by having special meals. Wheat is a grain crop; if you have no word that means "wheat," you can use a more general word for grain. This happened in May; other crops were harvested at other times of the year.
  * **Walisherehekea mavuno ya ngano - Wayahudi wangemshukuru Mungu kwa mavuno ya ngano kwa kuleta sadaka na kusherehekea kwa chakula spesheli. Ngano ni mmea wa nafaka; kama hauna neno linalomaanisha "ngano," unaweza kutumia neno la jumla la fanaka. Hii ilifanyika mwezi ya Mei; mazao mengine ilivuniwa nyakati zingine za mwaka.**
  * **This year**  - That is, "In the year that Jesus died."
  * **Mwaka huu - Hio ni, "katika mwaka ule Yesu alikufa."**
