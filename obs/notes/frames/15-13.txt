===== Joshua Renewed the Covenant [15-13] Yoshua alifanya agano upya *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-13.jpg?nolink&}}

**When Joshua was an old man**, he called all the people of **Israel** together. Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to **obey** the **covenant** that **God** had made with the **Israelites** at **Sinai**. The **people promised** to **remain faithful to God** and **follow his laws**.

**Wakati Yoshua alikuwa mzee**, aliita watu wote wa **Israeli ** pamoja. Kisha Yoshua aliwakumbusha watu jukumu lao la **kutii agano** ambalo **Mungu** alifanya na **Waisraeli ** pale **Sinai**. **Watu waliahidi kudumu wakiwa waaminifu** kwa Mungu na **kufuata sheria zake**.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **When Joshua was an old man**  â€“ It may be clearer to say, "Many years later, when Joshua was an old man." Joshua was over 100 years old at this time.
  * **Wakati Yoshua alikuwa mzee **- Inaweza kuwa wazi zaidi kusema, "miaka mingi baadaye, wakati Yoshua alikuwa mzee." Yoshua alikuwa zaidi ya miaka 100 wakati huo.
  * **remain faithful to God**  - In other words, they would be loyal to God. They would worship and serve only God; they would not worship or serve any other gods.
  * **Kuwa waaminifu kwa Mungu**  - Kwa maneno mengine, watakuwa waaminifu kwa Mungu. Wataabudu na kumutumikia Mungu peke yake; hawataabudu ama kutumikia miungu mingine.
  * **follow his laws**  - This means that the people would obey the laws that God had given them already as part of the covenant.
  * **kufuata sheria zake**  - Hii inamaanisha kuwa watu watatii sheria ambazo Mungu tayari alishawapea kama sehemu ya agano.
