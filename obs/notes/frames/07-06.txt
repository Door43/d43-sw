===== Isaac Sent Jacob Away [07-06] Isaka alimtuma Yakobo mbali *F =====

But **Rebekah** heard of **Esau’s plan**. So **she and Isaac sent Jacob far away to live with her relatives**.

Lakini **Rebeka** akasikia kuhusu **mpango wa Esau**. Kwa hivyo **yeye na Isaka wakatuma Yakobo mbali kuishi na watu wa jamaa zao.**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **Esau's plan**  - Esau's plan was to kill Jacob after his father died.
  * **mpango wa Esau**  - Mpango wa Esau ulikuwa kumuua Yakobo baada ya baba yao kufa.
  * **she and Isaac sent Jacob**  – Rebekah wanted to protect Jacob from Esau so she talked to Isaac about sending Jacob away.
  * **yeye na Isaka walituma Yakobo**  - Rebeka alitaka kulinda Yakobo kutoka kwa Esau na kwa hivyo akaongea na Isaka kuhusu kumtuma Yakobo mbali.
  * **far away to live with her relatives**  - This was the same place where Rebekah was living when Abraham's servant got her to be Isaac's wife. To make this clear, you could add, "relatives in the same land where she used to live." The land was to the east, several hundred miles away.
  * **mbali kuishi na watu wa jamaa zao**  - Hii mahali ni sawa na ile mahali Rebeka alikuwa anaishi wakati mtumishi wa Ibrahimu alimchukua aje awe mke wa Isaka. Ndio hii ieleweke vizuri, unawaza kuongeza, "watu wa jamii yake mahali alikuwa anaishi." Ile nchi ilikuwa upande wa mashariki umbali wa mamia ya maili.
  * **her relatives**  - This could also be translated as, "their relatives." Since Abraham's brother was Rebekah's grandfather, her relatives were also Isaac's relatives.
  * **watu wa jamaa yake**  - Hii pia inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "jamaa zao." Ndugu yake Ibrahimu alikuwa babu wa Rebeka, jamaa zake walikuwa pia jamaa wa Isaka.
