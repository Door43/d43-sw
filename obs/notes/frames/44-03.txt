===== The Crippled Man Was Healed [44-03] Mtu yule Kilema Alipona =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Immediately, **God healed** the lame man, and he began to walk and jump around, and to **praise** God. The people in the **courtyard of the Temple** were amazed.

Mara moja, **Mungu akamponya** yule mtu kilema, naye akaanza kutembea na kurukaruka, na **kumsifu ** Mungu. Watu katika **uwanja wa hekalu** wakashangaa.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **courtyard of the Temple**  - Only priests could enter the Temple, but ordinary Jews were allowed to come into this area that surrounded the Temple.
  * **uwanja wa hekalu**  - Makuhani peke yao ndio waliweza kuingia hekaluni, lakini Wayahudi wa kawaida waliruhusiwa kuja katika eneo hili ambalo linazunguka Hekalu.
