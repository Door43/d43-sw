===== One People [04-01] Watu moja =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-01.jpg?nolink&}}

**Many years after the flood**, there were **again many people** in the world, and they all spoke the **same language**. Instead of filling the earth as **God** had commanded, they gathered together and built **a city**.

**Miaka mingi baada ya yale mafuriko**, kukawa na **watu wengi tena** katika dunia, na wote waliongea **lugha moja**. Badala ya kujaza dunia kama alivyoamuru **Mungu**, wakakusanyika pamoja na kujenga **mji. **
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya muhimu): =====

  * ****[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God (Mungu)]]****

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **many years after the flood**  - Many generations had passed since the end of the flood.
  * **miaka mingi baada ya mafuriko**  - vizazi nyingi zilikuwa zimepita kutoka wakati wa yale mafuriko.
  * **again many people**  - Noah's family multiplied into enough people to fill a city.
  * **Tena watu wengi**  - Jamii ya Nuhu ikawa imezidi kuongezeka ikafikia watu wanaweza kujaza mji.
  * **same language**  - This means there was only one language, so they could all understand each other.
  * **Lugha moja**  - Hii inamanisha kuikuwa na lugha moja tu, na kwa hivyo wote walielewana.
  * **a city**  - It is best to use a general word for "city" since the text does not give a specific name.
  * **Mji**  - Ni vizuru kutumia neno la kijumla la "mji" kwa sababu ilionakiliwa haipeani jina maalum.
