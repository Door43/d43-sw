===== What Else Must I Do? [28-03] Kitu Gani Kingine Lazima Nifanye? =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-03.jpg?nolink&}}

But the young man said, “I have **obeyed** all these **laws ever since I was a boy**. **What do I still need to do** in order to live forever?” **Jesus** looked at him and **loved him**.

Lakini yule kijana akasema, "**Nimetii ** sheria hizi zote **tangu nikiwa kijana mdogo**. **Je! Bado nina haja ya kufanya nini** ili niiishi milele?" **Yesu** akamtazama na **akampenda**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **ever since I was a boy**  - Another way to say this would be, "from the time I was a child until now."
  * **tangu nilipokuwa kijana mdogo**  - Njia ingine ya kusema hii inaweza kuwa, "tangu nilipokuwa mtoto hadi sasa."
  * **What do I still need to do?**  - That is, "What else do I need to do" or, "What do I need to do in addition to these?"
  * **Ninabidi bado nifanye nini?**  - Hiyo ni, "Kitu gani kingine nina haja ya kufanya" ama, "Ninahitaji kufanya nini pamoja na haya."
  * **loved him**  - Jesus had compassion on him. Choose a word for love that is consistent with the kind of love God has for people.
  * **akampenda**  - Yesu alimuonea huruma. Tafuta neno la upendo ambalo linaonyesha vyema upendo Mungu alio nao kwa watu wake.
