===== The Joyful News [41-07] Habari ya Furaha =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-07.jpg?nolink&}}

The women were **full of fear and great joy**. They ran to tell the **disciples** the **good news**.

Wale wanawake wakawa **wamejaa hofu na furaha kubwa**. Walikimbia kwenda kuwaambia **wanafunzi** **habari njema**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **full of fear and great joy**  - That is, "experiencing emotions of fear and also great joy."
  * **wamejaa hofu na furaha kubwa**  - Hiyo ni, "kuwa na hisia ya hofu na pia furaha kuu."
  * **good news**  - This can also be translated as, "the good news that Jesus was alive again." This good news refers to the fact that Jesus had risen from the dead.
  * **habari njema**  - Hili linaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "habari njema kuwa Yesu yuko hai tena." Hii habari njema ina maana ya ukweli kwamba Yesu amefufuka kutoka wafu.
