===== Jesus Opened their Minds [42-07] Yesu Alifungua Mawazo Yao =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-07.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** said, “I told you that everything written about me in **God’s word** must be **fulfilled**.” Then he **opened their minds so that they could understand** God’s word. He said, “It was written long ago that the **Messiah** would **suffer**, **die**, and **rise** from the dead **on the third day**.”

**Yesu** alisema, " Mimi niliwaambia kwamba kila kitu kilichoandikwa kunihusu katika neno la Mungu ni itatimia. "Kisha, akaangazia wawazo yao ili waweze kuelewa neno la Mungu. Alisema, "Hiyo ilikuwa imeandikwa muda mrefu uliopita kwamba Masihi atateswa, kufa, na kufufuka kutoka kwa wafu siku ya tatu."

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **must be fulfilled**  - This can be translated as, "must happen."
  * **lazima kutimia**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama , "Ni lazima itatokea."
  * **opened their minds so that they could understand**  - This could also be translated as, "caused them to be able to understand" or, "made them able to comprehend."
  * **akaziangazia mawazo yao ili waweze kuelewa**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "alisababisha wao wapate uwezo wa kuelewa" ama, "aliwafanya waweze kufahamu."
  * **on the third day**  - That is, "after three days" or, "three days later."
  * **siku ya tatu**  - Hiyo ni, "baada ya siku tatu" ama, "siku tatu baadaye."
