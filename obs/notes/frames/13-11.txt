===== Make Us an Idol for Us [13-11] Fanya sanamu kwa ajili yetu *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-11.jpg?nolink&}}

For many days, **Moses** was on top of **Mount Sinai** talking with **God**. **The people became tired of waiting** for him to return. So they **brought gold** to **Aaron** and asked him to make an **idol** for them!

Kwa siku nyingi, **Musa** alikuwa juu ya **Mlima wa Sinai** akiongea na **Mungu**. **Watu wakachoshwa na kumngojea** arudi. Kwa hivyo **wakaleta dhahabu** kwa **Haruni** na wakamuuliza awatengenezee **sanamu**!

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **The people became tired of waiting**  - This could be translated as, "The people became impatient because he did not return quickly" or, "The people didn't want to wait any longer for him to return."
  * **Watu walichoswa na kungoja**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Watu wakakosa uvumilivu kwa sababu hakurudi haraka" ama, "Watu hawakutaka kungoja tena kurudi kwake."
  * **brought gold**  - These were objects and jewelry made of gold, which could be melted and formed into other things.
  * **walileta dhahabu **- Hizi zilikuwa vitu na zana zimetengenezwa na dhahabu, ambazo zingeyeyushwa na kuundwa kuwa vitu zingine."
