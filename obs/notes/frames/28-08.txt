===== All Things Are Possible [28-08] Mambo Yote Yawezekana =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-08.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** looked at the **disciples** and said, “**With people this is impossible**, but **with God**, **all things are possible**.”

**Yesu** aliwatazama** wanafunzi** na kusema, **"Kwa wanadamu hii haiwezekani,** lakini **kwa Mungu, mambo yote yawezekana."**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **With people this is impossible**  - That is, "It is not possible for people to do this" or, "Mere humans cannot save themselves."
  * **Kwa wanadamu hiyo haiwezekani**  - Hiyo ni, "haiwezekani watu kufanya hivi" ama, "binadamu tu hawawezi kujiokoa."
  * **with God, all things are possible**  - This could also be translated as, "God is able to do anything, even save a rich person" or, "God is able to do impossible things, so he can save even a rich person."
  * **kwa Mungu, mambo yote yawezekana**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mungu anaweza kufanya lolote, hata kuokoa tajiri" ama, "Mungu anaweza mambo ambayo hayawezekani, kwa hivyo anaweza kukoa hata mtu aliye tajiri."
