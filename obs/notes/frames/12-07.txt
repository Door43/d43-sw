===== Moses Divided the Sea [12-07] Musa aligawanya bahari *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-07.jpg?nolink&}}

**God** told **Moses** to **raise his hand over the sea** and divide the waters. Then God caused wind to push the water in the sea to the left and the right, so that a path formed through the sea.

**Mungu** akamwambia **Musa** **ainue mkono wake juu ya bahari** na kugawanya maji. Halafu Mungu akafanya upepo usukume maji baharini upande wa kushoto na wa kulia, kwa hivyo njia ikawa katikati ya bahari.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **raise his hand over the sea**  - This could be translated as, "held out his hand over the sea". This was a gesture to show that God was doing this miracle through Moses.
  * **inua mkono juu ya bahari **- Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "akanyoosha mkono wake juu ya bahari." Hii ilikuwa ishara ya kuonyesha kuwa Mungu alikuwa anafanya muujiza kupitia kwa Musa.
