===== The Hail [10-08] Mvua ya mawe *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-08.jpg?nolink&}}

**After that**, **God sent hail** that destroyed most of the crops in **Egypt** and killed anybody who went outside. **Pharaoh** called **Moses** and **Aaron** and told them, “I have **sinned**. **You may go**.” So Moses **prayed**, and the hail stopped falling from the sky.

**Baadaye**, **Mungu akaleta** **mvua ya mawe** iliyoharibu mimea mingi kule **Misri ** na kuua kila mmoja alieenda nje. Farao akaita **Musa** na **Haruni ** na akawaambia, "**nimetenda dhambi**. **Munaweza enda**." Kwa hivyo Musa **akaomba** na ile mvua ya mawe ikaacha kunyesha kutoka angani.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya tafsiri): ====

  * **After that**  - This means, after God caused the painful sores to appear on the Egyptians' skin.
  * **baadaye**  - Hii inamaanisha, baada ya Mungu kuleta vidonda vyenye uchungu kwa ngozi ya Wamisiri.
  * **God sent hail**  - God caused hail to fall from the sky.
  * **Mungu akaleta mvua ya mawe**  - Mungu alifanya mvua ya mawe inyeeshe kutoka kwa anga.
  * **hail**  - Hail is like chunks of ice that fall down from the clouds as rain does. These chunks can be very small or very large. The larger ones will hurt or kill whatever they fall on.
  * **mvua ya mawe**  - Mvua ya mawe ni kama vipande vya theluji zinazoanguka kutoka kwa mawingu kama mvua. Hizi vipande zaweza kuwa ndogo ama kubwa. Zilizo kubwa zitaumiza ama kuua kila kitu zinaangukia.
  * **You may go**  - The word "you" refers to Moses, Aaron, and the Israelites.
  * **munaweza kwenda**  - Neno "nyinyi" linaongea kuhusu Musa, Haruni na Waisraeli.
