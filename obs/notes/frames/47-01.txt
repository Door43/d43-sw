===== Paul and Silas in Philippi [47-01] Paulo na Sila huko Filipi =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-47-01.jpg?nolink&}}

As Saul traveled throughout the **Roman Empire**, **he began to use his Roman name**, “**Paul**.” One day, Paul and his friend **Silas** went to the town of **Philippi** to **proclaim** the **good news** about **Jesus**. They went to a place by the river outside the city where people gathered to pray. There they met a woman named Lydia who was a **merchant**. She **loved** and **worshiped God**.

Kama vile Saulo aliendelea kusafiri akipitia katika Dola ya Kirumi, akaanza kutumia Jina la Kirumi, "Paulo." Siku moja, Paul na rafiki yake Sila walienda kwa mji wa Filipi kutangaza habari njema za Yesu.  Walienda pahali karibu na mto nje ya mji ambapo watu walikusanyinka kuomba.  Pale walikutana na mwanamke aliyeitwa Lydia aliyekuwa mfanyabiashara.  Alipenda na kumwabudu Mungu.
