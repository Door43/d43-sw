===== God Made Eve [01-13] Mungu Alimuumba Hawa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-13.jpg?nolink&}}

So **God** made **Adam** fall into **a deep sleep**. Then God **took one of Adam's ribs and made** it into **a woman** and **brought her to him**.

Basi **Mungu** akamfanya **Adamu** aingie kwa **usingizi mzito**. Na Mungu **akachukua umbavu mmoja wa Adamu** na akaiunda iwe **mwanamke ** na **akamleta kwake.**
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God]]**
  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:adam|Adam]]**

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya tafsiri): ====

  * **a deep sleep**  - This was a deeper sleep than normal.
  * **Usingizi mzito**  -Hii ilikuwa usingizi mzito kuliko ya kawaida.
  * **took one of Adam's ribs and made**  - The verbs indicate God's very personal act of removing the rib from Adam and shaping it into a woman.
  * **Akachukua mojawapo ya Mbavu za Adamu.**  Maneno katika sentensi inaonyesha Mungu binafsi akitoa umbavu nakuiunda ikafanyika Mwanamke.
  * **a woman**  - She was the first woman, the female version of human beings that was missing until now.
  * **Mwanamke**  - Yeye ndiye alikuwa Mwanamke wa kwanza, jinsia ya mwanadamu ya kike, ambayo haikuweko hadi wakati ule.
  * **brought her to him**  - God personally introduced them. He presented the woman to Adam, almost like offering a special gift.
  * **Akamleta kwake**  - Mungu mwenyewe aliwajulisha kwa moja na mwingine. Akampeana mwanamke kwa Adamu, kama vile kumpea zawadi maalum.
