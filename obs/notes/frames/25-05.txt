===== Do Not Test God [25-05] Usimjaribu Mungu =====

But **Jesus** replied to **Satan** by quoting from the **scriptures**. He said, “**In God's word**, **he commands his people**, ‘**Do not test the Lord your God**.’”

Lakini **Yesu ** akamjibu **Shetani ** kwa kunukuu kutoka kwa **maandiko**. Akasema, "**Katika neno la Mungu, ameamuru watu wake, "Msimjaribu Bwana Mungu wenu."**

===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **In God's word, he commands his people, 'Do not test the Lord, your God.'"**  - This could be translated as an indirect quote: "God commands us in his word that we should not test the Lord our God."
  * **Katika neno la Mungu, anaamuru watu wake, "msimjaribu Bwana, Mungu wenu.**" - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama nukuu isiyo ya moja kwa moja: "Mungu anaamuru sisi katika neno lake ya kwamba hatupaswi kumjaribu Bwana Mungu wetu.
  * **Do not test the Lord your God**  - This could be translated as, "Do not make the Lord your God prove himself to you" or, "Do not make the Lord your God prove that he is good."
  * **Usimjaribu Bwana Mungu wako**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Usimfanye Bwana Mungu wako ajithibitishe mwenyewe kwako" ama, "Usimfanye Bwana Mungu wako athibitishe ya kwamba yeye ni mwema."
  * **Lord your God**  - That is, "Yahweh, your God" or, "Yahweh, who is God and has authority over you."
  * **Bwana Mungu wako**  - Hiyo ni, "Yehova, Mungu wako" ama, Yehova, aliye Mungu na ana mamlaka juu yako."
