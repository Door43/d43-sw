===== The Small Debt [29-05] Ndeni iliyo Ndogo =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-05.jpg?nolink&}}

“But when the **servant** went out from the king, he found a **fellow servant** who owed him a debt worth four months’ wages. The servant grabbed his fellow servant and said, ‘Pay me the money that you owe me!’”

"Lakini wakati yule **mtumishi** alitoka kwa mfalme, alienda kwa **mtumishi mwenzake** aliyekuwa na deni yake iliyotoshana na mshahara wa miezi minne. Yule mtumishi alimkamata mtumishi mwenzake na kusema, 'Lipa deni ambayo ninakudai!'"

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * (Jesus continues the story.)
  * (Yesu anaendelea na hadithi.)
  * **fellow servant**  - This refers to another man who was also a servant of the king.
  * **mtumishi mwenzake**  - Hii inaeleza kuhusu mwanamme mwingine ambaye pia alikuwa mtumishi wa yule mfalme.
