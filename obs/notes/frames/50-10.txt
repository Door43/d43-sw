===== Evil People Will Be Judged [50-10] Watu Waovu Watahukumiwa =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-10.jpg?nolink&}}

“When the world ends, the **angels** will gather together all the people **who belong to the devil** and throw them into **a raging fire**, where they will cry and grind their teeth in terrible **suffering**. Then **the righteous ones** will **shine like the sun** in the **kingdom** of **God their Father**.”

"Mwisho wa dunia utakapofika, **malaika ** watawakusanya watu wote **ambao ni wa ibilisi** na kuwatupa katika **moto mkali**, ambapo wao watalia na kusaga meno yao katika **mateso** ya kutisha. Nao **wenye haki watang'aa kama jua** katika **ufalme ** wa **Mungu Baba yao."**

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **who belong to the devil**  - This could also be translated as, "who obey the devil" or, "who are ruled by the devil." This refers to those who do not believe in Jesus but follow the evil ways of the devil.
  * **ambao ni wa ibilisi **- Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "wanaotii ibilisi" ama, "ambao wanatawaliwa na ibilisi." Hii ina maana ya wale ambao hawamwamini Yesu lakini wanafuata njia ya uovu ya ibilisi.
  * **a raging fire**  - That is, "a very hot, burning fire" or, "a large, very hot fire."
  * **moto mkali **- Hiyo ni, "moto kali, unaochoma sana" ama, "moto kubwa na kali sana."
  * **the righteous ones**  - This refers to the people who belong to the Messiah. See **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:50-08|[50-08]]]**.
  * **wenye haki**  - Hii inamaanisha watu ambao ni wa Masihi. Angalia pale **[50-08].   **
  * **shine like the sun**  - This could also be translated as, "be glorious like the sun" or, "show pure goodness as the sun shows bright light."
  * **watang'aa kama jua **- Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama "kuwa na utukufu kama wa jua" ama, "kuonyesha wema msafi kama vile jua inaonyesha mwanga mkali."
