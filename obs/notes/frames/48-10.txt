===== The Passover Lamb [48-10] Mwana Kondoo wa Pasaka =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-10.jpg?nolink&}}

**Jesus** is **our Passover Lamb**. He was perfect and **sinless** and was killed at the time of the Passover celebration. When anyone **believes** in Jesus, the **blood of Jesus** pays for that person’s **sin**, and **God's punishment passes over that person**.

**Yesu ** ndiye **Mwana kondoo wetu wa ****Pasaka**. Alikuwa mkamilifu na **asiye na dhambi** na aliuwawa wakati wa sherehe ya Pasaka. Wakati mtu yeyote **anamwamini** Yesu, **damu ya Yesu** hulipia **dhambi ** ya mtu yule na **adhabu ya Mungu humpita yule mtu**.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **our Passover Lamb**  - That is, “the Passover Lamb that was killed for us” or, “the lamb that was killed so that God would pass over us” or, “the lamb that was killed so that God would spare us.”
  * **Mwana kondoo wetu wa Pasaka**  - Hiyo ni, "Mwana kondoo wa Pasaka ambaye aliuwawa kwa ajili yetu" ama, "mwana kondoo ambaye aliuwawa ndiposa Mungu atupite" ama, "mwana kondoo aliyeuwawa ndiposa Mungu atuokoe."
  * **sinless**  - This can also be translated as, “had never sinned.”
  * **asiye na dhambi**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "hakuwahi tenda dhambi."
  * **blood of Jesus**  – This could be translated as, “The sacrifice that Jesus made of himself when he died for sinners.” The word “blood” here also means “death.”
  * **damu ya Yesu**  - Hii inaweza pia kutafsiriwa kama, "Dhabihu ambayo Yesu alijitoa wakati alikufa kwa ajili ya wenye dhambi." Neno "damu" hapa pia linamaanisha "kifo."
  * **God's punishment passes over that person**  - This statement means, “God does not punish that person.” Translate it using a phrase that shows its relationship to the words for "Passover" and the "Passover Lamb." If the word "spare" is used in the name of the Passover, this could also be translated as, “God spares that person from punishment.”
  * **Adhabu ya Mungu hupita mtu ******huyo****- Mstari huu unamaanisha, "Mungu haadhibu mtu yule." Tafsiri ukitumia msemo ambao utaonyesha uhusiano wake na maneno ya "Pasaka" na "Mwana kondoo wa Pasaka." Kama neno "okoa" imetumika katika mahali pa jina Pasaka, hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "Mungu huokoa mtu yule kutoka kwa adhabu."
