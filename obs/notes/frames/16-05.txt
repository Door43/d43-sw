===== An Angel Spoke to Gideon [16-05] Malaika alinena na Gideoni *F =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-05.jpg?nolink&}}

One day, a man of Israel named **Gideon** was **threshing grain secretly** so the **Midianites** would not steal it. The **angel** of **Yahweh** came to Gideon and said, “**God is with you**, mighty warrior. Go and save **Israel** from the Midianites.”

Siku moja, mwanaume wa Israeli anayeitwa **Gideoni** alikuwa **anapuria ngano kisiri ** ili **Wamidiani** wasiibe. **Malaika** wa **Yehova** akaja kwa Gideoni na kusema, "**Mungu yuko pamoja na wewe**, mpiganaji hodari. Enda ukaokoe **Israeli** kutoka kwa Wamidiani."

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **One day**  - This phrase introduces an event that happened in the past, but does not state the specific time. Many languages have a similar way to begin telling a true story.
  * **Siku moja**  - Msemo huu unatanguliza tukio lililofanyika siku zilizopita, lakini halitaji wakati haswa. Lugha nyingi ziko na njia sawa ya kuanzia kueleza hadithi ya kweli.
  * **threshing grain**  - The grain was wheat, which has a head of many small grains, or seeds, on the top of a thin stalk. "Threshing" is separating the seeds of the plant from the stalks by beating the heads of grain. The seeds are food, but the stalks are not.
  * **kupuria nafaka**  - Nafaka ilikuwa ngano, ambayo iko na kichwa ya nafaka wengi wadogo, ama mbegu, juu ya bua nyembamba. "Kupuria" ni kutenganisha mbegu za mmea na bua zake kwa kupiga kichwa cha nafaka. Zile mbegu ni chakula lakini bua si chakula.
  * **secretly**  - Gideon was threshing the grain in a hidden location, so the Midianites would not see him.
  * **kisiri **- Gideoni alikuwa anasiaga nafaka mahali pamejificha, ili Wamidiani wasimuone.
  * **God is with you**  – This means, "God is present with you in a special way" or, "God has plans to use you in a special way."
  * **Mungu yoko pamoja na wewe**  - Hii inamaanisha, "Mungu yuko pamoja na wewe kwa njia spesheli" ama, "Mungu ana mpango wa kukutumia kwa njia maalum."
