===== Noah Found Grace [03-02] Nuhu akapata Neema =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-02.jpg?nolink&}}

But **Noah found favor** with **God**. He was a **righteous** man, living among wicked people. God told Noah about the flood He was **planning to send**. He told Noah to build a huge boat.

Lakini **Nuhu akapata neema** na **Mungu**. Yeye alikuwa mwenye** haki**, akiishi kati ya watu walio waovu. Mungu akamwambia Nuhu kuhusu mafuriko aliyokuwa **anapanga kuleta**. Akamwambia Nuhu alijenge mashua kubwa.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **found favor**  - God was pleased with Noah because he feared and obeyed God. So even though Noah was not sinless, God was gracious to him and made a plan to rescue his family from the devastating flood. Make sure this doesn't sound like Noah was lucky or that he just happened to escape. It was God's choice.
  * **Akapata Neema**  - Mungu alifurahishwa na Nuhu sababu alimcha na kumtii Mungu. Kwa hivyo ingawa Nuhu hakuwa bila dhambi, Mungu akampa neema na akafanya mpango wa kuiokoa jamii yake kutoka kwa hiyo mafuriko makubwa. Hakikisha hii haitatokea kana kwamba Nuhu alikuwa na bahati ama aliponyoka tu. Ilikuwa mpango wa Mungu.
  * **the flood**  - See how you translated this in **[[:sw:obs:notes:frames:03-01|[03-01]]]**.
  * **Mafuriko**  - Angalia vile ulivyoitafsri hii pale [03-01]
  * **planning to send**  - God planned to cause deep water to cover the earth by sending a lot of rain. That is, he planned to cause the flood by causing a lot of rain to fall.
  * **Alipanga kuleta**  - Mungu alipanga kufanya maji ya kina ikafunike dunia kwa kuleta mvua nyingi. Hivyo ni kusema, alipanga kuleta mafuriko kwa kuileta mvua nyingi.
