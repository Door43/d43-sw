===== Paul and Silas in Prison [47-08] Paulo na Sila Gerezani =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-47-08.jpg?nolink&}}

They put **Paul** and **Silas** in the most secure part of the prison and even locked up their feet. Yet **in the middle of the night**, they were **singing songs of praise to God**.

Waliwatia **Paulo ** na **Sila ** katika sehemu iliyo salama zaidi pale gerezani na hata wakaifunga miguu yao. Hata hivyo **usiku wa manane** walikuwa** wakiimba nyimbo za kumsifu Mungu**.

===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **in the middle of the night**  - This could be translated as, "very late at night" or, "very early in the morning." This was when it was completely dark outside, when people would normally be asleep.
  * **Usiku wa manane**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "usiku sana" au, alfajiri na mapema." Hii ilikuwa wakati ambao kulikuwa kuna giza kabisa nje, wakati watu kawaida wangekuwa wamelala.
  * **singing songs of praise to God**  - This can also be translating as, "praising God by singing" or, "singing songs to praise God" or, "singing songs that gave praise to God."
  * **wakiimba nyimbo za sifa kwa Mungu**  - Hii inaweza kutafsiriwa kama, "kumsifu mungu kwa kuimba" au, "kuimba nyimbo za kumsifu Mungu" au, "kuimba nyimbo ambazo zinampatia Mungu sifa."
