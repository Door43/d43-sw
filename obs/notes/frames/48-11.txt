===== Translation Notes: (Maelezo ya Tafsiri) =====

  * **the Israelites, who were his chosen people**  - That is, "the Israelites, the people he had chosen" or, "his chosen people, the Israelites."
  * **Waisraeli, ambao walikuwa watu wake wateule**  - Hio ni, "Waisraeli, watu aliokuwa amewachagua" au, watu wake wateule, Waisraeli."
