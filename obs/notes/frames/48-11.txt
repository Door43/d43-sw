===== God's New Covenant [48-11] Agano Jipya ya Mungu =====

**God** made a **covenant** with **the Israelites**, **who were his chosen people**. But God has now made a **New Covenant** that is available to everyone. Because of this New Covenant, anyone from any **people group** can become part of Godâ€™s people by **believing** in Jesus.

**Mungu ** alifanya **agano ** na **Waisraeli,** **ambao walikuwa watu wake wateule**. Lakini sasa Mungu amefanya **Agano Jipya** ambalo linapatikana na kila mtu. Kwa sababu ya hili Agano Jipya, mtu yeyote kutoka kwa kila **kikundi cha watu** anaweza kuwa sehemu ya watu wa Mungu kwa **kumwamini ** Yesu.

===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **the Israelites, who were his chosen people**  - That is, "the Israelites, the people he had chosen" or, "his chosen people, the Israelites."
  * **Waisraeli, ambao walikuwa watu wake wateule**  - Hiyo ni, "Waisraeli, watu aliokuwa amewachagua" ama, watu wake wateule, Waisraeli."
