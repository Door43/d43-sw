===== Not Willing to Obey [28-05] Hakuwa Tayari Kutii =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-05.jpg?nolink&}}

When the young man **heard what Jesus said**, he became very sad, because he was very rich and did not want to give away all the things he possessed. He turned and went away from Jesus.

Wakati yule kijana **alisikia kile Yesu alisema**, alikasirika sana, kwa sababu alikuwa tajiri sana, na hakutaka kutoa zile vitu vyote alikuwa anamiliki. Akageuka na akaenda mbali na Yesu.
===== Important Terms (Maneno ya Muhimu): =====

  * **[[:sw:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus (Yesu)]]**

  * **[[:sw:obe:kt:jesus]]**
===== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): =====

  * **heard what Jesus said**  - That is, "heard Jesus tell him to give away what he owned."
  * **alisikia kile Yesu alisema**  - Hiyo ni, "alisikia Yesu akimwambia apeana kile anamiliki."
