===== Assyria Destroyed Israel [20-02] Ashuru Iliharibu Israeli =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-02.jpg?nolink&}}

So **God punished both kingdoms** by allowing their enemies to destroy them. The **Assyrian Empire**, a powerful, cruel nation, destroyed the **kingdom of Israel**. The Assyrians killed many people in the kingdom of Israel, **took away** everything of value, and burned much of the country.

Kwa hivyo **Mungu aliadhibu falme zote mbili** kwa kuruhusu maadui wao kuwaangamiza. **Ufalme wa Ashuru**, uliyokuwa mwenye nguvu na umejaa ukatili, ukaangamiza **ufalme wa Israeli**. Waashuri wakaua watu wengi katika ufalme wa Israeli, **wakabeba** kila kitu cha dhamana, na wakachoma sehemu kubwa ya nchi yao.

==== Translation Notes (Maelezo ya Tafsiri): ====

  * **both kingdoms**  - This refers to both the kingdoms of Israel and Judah.
  * **falme zote mbili **- Hii inaeleza kuhusu falme zote mbili ya Israeli na ya Yuda.
  * **Empire**  - A country was called an "empire" when it was powerful enough to extend its authority over many other countries.
  * **Dola**  - Nchi iliitwa "Dola" wakati iko na nguvu kiasi ya kuwa inaweza kueneza mamlaka yake juu ya mataifa mengine mengi.
  * **took away**  - That is, "stole." They stole these valuable things and carried them back to Assyria.
  * **walibeba**  - Hiyo ni, "waliiba." Waliiba zile vitu vya dhamana na wakazibeba kurudi nazo Ashuru.
