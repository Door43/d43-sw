====== 5. Mwana wa Ahadi ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-01.jpg?direct&}}

Miaka kumi baada ya Abramu na Sarai kufika Kanani, bado hawakuwa na mtoto. Kwa hivyo mke wa Abramu, Sarai akamwambia, "Kuona Mungu hajaniruhusu kuwa na watoto na sasa nimezeeka kiasi kuwa siwezi kupata watoto, ndiye huyu hapa mtumishi wangu wangu Hajiri. Muoe pia na ndiyo akanizalie mtoto."

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-02.jpg?direct&}}

Basi Abramu alimuoa Hajiri. Hajiri akamzaa mtoto wa kiume naye Abramu akampa jina Ishmaeli. Lakini Sarai alimwonea wivu Hajiri. Wakati Ishmaeli alipotimiza umri wa miaka kumi na tatu, Bwana alizungumza na Abramu tena.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-03.jpg?direct&}}

God said, "I am God Almighty. I will make a covenant with you." Then Abram bowed to the ground. God also told Abram, "You will be the father of many nations. I will give you and your descendants the land of Canaan as their possession and I will be their God forever. You must circumcise every male in your family."

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-04.jpg?direct&}}

"Your wife, Sarai, will have a sonâ€”he will be the son of promise. Name him Isaac. I will make my covenant with him, and he will become a great nation. I will make Ishmael a great nation, too, but my covenant will be with Isaac." Then God changed Abram's name to Abraham, which means "father of many." God also changed Sarai's name to Sarah, which means "princess."

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-05.jpg?direct&}}

Siku hiyo Ibrahimu akawatahirisha wanaume wote wa nyumba yake. Baada ya mwaka mmoja baadae, Ibrahimu akiwa na umri wa miaka 100 naye Sarai alikuwa 90. Sarai alimzalia Abrahamu mwana. Wakamuita Isaka kama Mungu alivyowaambia.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-06.jpg?direct&}}

Wakati Isaka alipokuwa kijana, Mungu allijaribu imani ya Ibrahimu kwa kusema , "Mchukue Isaka mwanao wa pekee, umuue kama dhabihu kwangu." Tena Ibrahimu akamtii Mungu na akajiandaa kumtoa kama sadaka mwanawe.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-07.jpg?direct&}}

Wakati Ibrahimu na Isaka walipokuwa wanatembea kuelekea mahali pa kutoa dhabihu, Isaka akauliza, "Baba, tuko na kuni za kuchoma dhabihu, lakini kondoo yuko wapi? Ibrahimu akajibu, "Mungu atampeana kondoo wa kutoa dhabihu, mwanangu."

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-08.jpg?direct&}}

Walipofika mahali pa kutolea dhabihu, Ibrahimu akafungilia mwanawe Isaka na akamuwekelea juu ya madhabahu. Alikuwa karibu kumuuwa mwanawe wakati Mungu alisema, "Wacha! Usimuumize huyo kijana! Sasa ninajua kuwa unaniogopa sababu hukunizuilia mwanao wa pekee."

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-09.jpg?direct&}}

Hapo karibu Ibrahimu akamuona kondoo wa kiume amekwama katika kichaka. Mungu alikuwa amepeana kondoo awe dhabihu badala ya Isaka. Ibrahimu kwa furaha akamtoa kondoo kama dhabihu.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-10.jpg?direct&}}

Halafu Mungu akamwambia Ibrahimu, "Kwa sababu ulikuwa tayari kunipea kila kitu, hata mwanao wa pekee, ninaahidi kukubariki. Vizazi vyako vitakuwa zaidi ya nyota wa angani. Sababu umenitii, jamii zote za dunia zitabarikiwa kupitia kwa jamii yako."
