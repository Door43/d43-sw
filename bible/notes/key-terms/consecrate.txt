====== consecrate ======

===== Definition: =====

To consecrate means to dedicate something or someone to serve God. <font 13.63636302948px line-height: 1.4/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The person or object that is consecrated is considered holy and set apart for God.</font>

  * The meaning of this term is similar to "sanctify" or "to make holy", but with the added meaning of formally setting apart someone for service to God.
  * The term "consecrate" can also be translated as "set apart for God" or "purify for service to God".

(See also: [[:sw:bible:notes:key-terms:holy]], [[:sw:bible:notes:key-terms:sanctify]])
