====== ungodly, godlessness ======

===== Definition: =====

People or nations that are ungodly or godless do not obey God and do not even acknowledge God in their lives. They are said to be in a state of ungodliness, or godlessness.

  * God pronounces judgment and wrath on ungodly and godless people, on everyone who rejects him and his ways.
  * Through Jesusâ€™ death and resurrection, God provided a way for ungodly sinners to be forgiven and declared righteous.
  * God gives Christians the power and ability to turn away from ungodliness.
  * Other ways to translate "ungodliness" or "godlessness" could be, "wickedness" or "evil" or "rebellion against God".
