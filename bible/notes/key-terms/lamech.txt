====== Lamech ======

===== Facts: =====

Lamech is the name of two men in the book of Genesis.

  * The first Lamech mentioned was a descendant of Cain (Adam's son); he married two women and boasted of killing two men.
  * The second Lamech was a descendant of Seth (Adam's son); he was the father of Noah.
