====== cut to the heart ======

===== Definition: =====

The term  "cut to the heart" is an idiom that expresses very strong emotion, often in response to being confronted with sin.

  * Sometimes this idiom literally means,  "pierced to the heart" and refers to a very strong emotion of sorrow because of sin. This could be translated as, "deeply sorrowful for their sin" or "strongly convicted because of their sin."
  * Sometimes this idiom literally means, "cut in two" and refers to a very strong emotion of anger because of something someone did or said.This could be translated as "furious" or "extremely angry" or "enraged."
  * Other languages may also have idioms to translate these expressions.
