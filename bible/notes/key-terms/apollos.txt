====== Apollos ======

===== Facts: =====

Apollos was a Jew from <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>the city of Alexandria in Greece</font>    who was well-educated in the Hebrew Scriptures and who was an eloquent speaker. 

  * Apollos was instructed by two Christians in Ephesus named Aquila and Priscilla.
  * Paul emphasized how he (Paul), Apollos, and other teachers were part of a team working together in God's harvest.
