====== Ai ======

===== Facts: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In Old Testament times, Ai was the name of a Canaanite town located about 8 km northwest of Jericho. Ai was also near Bethel.</font>

  * After defeating Jericho, Joshua led the Israelites on an attack of Ai, but Israel was defeated.
  * God revealed Achan's disobedience <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>in stealing plunder from Jericho and ordered the Israelites</font> to kill him and his family. After they had done that, God allowed the Israelites to defeat the town of Ai when they attacked it a second time.
