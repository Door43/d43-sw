====== house of God ======


===== Definition: =====


In the Bible, the phrase "house of God" refers to the place where God resides or is worshipped. 

  * "House of God" is also used for the tabernacle or the temple.
  * This phrase is also sometimes used to refer to the people of God.


(See also: [[:sw:bible:notes:key-terms:tabernacle]], [[:sw:bible:notes:key-terms:temple]], [[:sw:bible:notes:key-terms:peopleofgod]])
