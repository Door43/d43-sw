====== watch ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "watch" means to look intently at something or to pay attention to something very closely. A "watch" also can refer to a time period of a few hours (often at night) when guards are on duty to look out for an enemy who might come to attack.

  * In the Bible, "watch" or "keep watch" often means to take special care to avoid sin and to be on guard against evil.
  * Jesus said to his disciples, “Watch and pray so that you will not fall into temptation” (Matthew 14:28) which means, "Be on guard against evil and pray, so that you will not sin when you are tempted".
  * Paul told Timothy, “Watch your life and doctrine closely” (1 Timothy 4:16) which means, "Be diligent to not do sinful things and to not believe false teachings.
  * Other ways of translating "to watch" include, "to pay close attention to" or "to be diligent" or "to be very careful" or "to be on guard".
