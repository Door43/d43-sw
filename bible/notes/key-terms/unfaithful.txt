====== unfaithful ======


===== Definition: =====


An unfaithful person is one who does not fulfill his responsibilities and duties.


    * In marriage, someone who commits adultery is "unfaithful" to his or her spouse.
    * Israel was called "unfaithful" when the people began to worship idols and disobeyed him in other ways.

(See also: [[sw:bible:notes:key-terms:faithful]], [[sw:bible:notes:key-terms:adultery]])
