====== governor ======


===== Definition: =====


A governor is a person who governs or rules over a state, region, or territory. 

  * Governors in the Bible were appointed by a king or emperor.  
  * Governors were under the authority of the king or emperor. 
  * The word "governor" can also be translated as “ruler” or “overseer”.
