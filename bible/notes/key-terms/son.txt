====== son ======

===== Definition: =====

A son is someone's direct male offspring. This is a relationship term and does not depend on the person's age.

  * "Son" usually refers to a boy or man in relation to his parents.
  * Sometimes the word "son" refers to a boy or man in relation to someone who cares for him as if he were his or her offspring. This can happen when someone adopts a boy to be his or her son.
  * The term "son" is often used figuratively in the Bible to refer to a male descendant. So when Jesus was called  the "Son of David" it meant that he was a "descendant of David."
  * Sometimes "son" is used figuratively by the apostle Paul to refer to a man who became a believer in Jesus as a result of Paul's teaching or who received spiritual training from Paul.
  * Another figurative use of son means "having the nature or characteristics of" as in:
      * "sons of the light"
      * "sons of disobedience"
      * "son of peace"
      * "sons of thunder"
