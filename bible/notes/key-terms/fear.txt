====== fear, afraid ======

===== Definition: =====


Fear is what a person feels when he knows that he, or someone he cares about, is in danger or could be be harmed. 

    * The word "fear" can refer to extreme terror or to a respect and carefulness around the thing or person that can cause the harm.
    * "To fear" can be translated as "to be afraid" or "to respect" or, "to revere" or "to be in awe."
    * The “fear of God” is something that people feel about him when they know that God is perfectly holy and hates sin. This fear motivates people to obey God and to hate and shun all form of evil.
