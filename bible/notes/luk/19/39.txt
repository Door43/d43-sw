===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **rebuke your disciples**  - This can be translated as "tell your disciples to stop doing these things."
  * **I tell you** - Jesus said this to emphasize what he would say next.
  * **If these were silent** - This is a hypothetical situation. (See: [[en:ta:discourse:hypothetical]]) Some translators may be need make it clear what Jesus was implying when he said this: "No, I will not rebuke them, for if these people were to be silent..."  (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:implicitexplicit]])
  * **the stones would cry out**  - "the stones would call out praises"
